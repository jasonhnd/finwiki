# megabanks — Japan megabanks and financial-group holding companies index

## Scope

`megabanks/` is a **standalone top-level domain**, split out of the former `JapanFG` entity umbrella. It covers Japan's **mega-bank groups and financial-group holding companies**: the three mega-bank groups (MUFG / SMFG / Mizuho) and their core banks, the Resona group, telecom / platform financial groups, the postal financial group, and the independent internet financial group. It holds **19 entity pages** (excluding this INDEX), each physically resident in `megabanks/`.

This page is the index of record for the domain. List the question as **"which megabanks and FG holding-company entities does FinWiki cover?"** and route from here.
