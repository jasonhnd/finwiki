# 主観内容の隔離 — Franklin Templeton FOBXX (BENJI) · Stellar 単一チェーンからマルチチェーン RWA へ · 「トークン化ファンド ≠ ステーブルコイン」のパラダイム

## Counterpoints

The 8-chain expansion has measurable operational overhead — each chain requires its own custody integration, transfer-agent function, audit attestation, and regulatory disclosure. Franklin has not disclosed per-chain attribution of AUM, but practitioner reports suggest **Stellar still holds the majority of BENJI** (~50-60%) with Polygon + Solana + Aptos splitting most of the rest; the late additions (Avalanche, Base, Sui) may have very thin actual AUM despite formal availability. This raises the question of whether the multi-chain expansion is **branding** more than substantive distribution. BENJI's AUM growth pace is meaningfully slower than BUIDL's — BUIDL went from $0 to $2.5B+ in ~24 months while BENJI took ~24 months to add $290M; Franklin's institutional Rolodex is smaller than BlackRock's. The yield-distribution-as-rebase model is **operationally clean but creates fragmentation across chains** when wallets move between them mid-month (Franklin reconciles, but the user-experience friction is real). **The tokenized-fund category itself faces competitive pressure from yield-bearing synthetic dollars** (Ethena sUSDe, Sky sUSDS) and from direct DeFi yield strategies (sFRAX, Pendle PT-tokens) — none of which need SEC registration but offer comparable yield. Franklin's edge is the **regulatory-clean and institution-eligible** wrapper, but if smaller institutions decide they prefer yield over compliance, BENJI's addressable market shrinks.

## Open questions

Does BENJI cross $1B AUM in 2026-2027, or does BUIDL fully dominate the institutional tokenized MMF lane? Does Franklin launch BENJI's "second product" — a tokenized bond ETF or international equity fund using the same transfer-agent-on-chain platform? Does Franklin become a Tier-1 validator on [[fintech/ondo-finance-rusdy-adoption|Ondo Chain]] or build its own RWA chain? Does the 8-chain footprint expand further (zkSync, Linea, Sei, Berachain rumored as 2026 candidates) or consolidate? Does the eventual GENIUS Act × 1940 Act intersection (rumored 2026-2027 guidance) reshape how BENJI and stablecoins interact at the prime-broker collateral level? Will Franklin use its [[exchanges/jp-cex-deposit-token-stablecoin-integration|Japan distribution channels]] (via SBI / Nomura relationships) to bring BENJI to Japanese institutional buyers under FSA recognition?
