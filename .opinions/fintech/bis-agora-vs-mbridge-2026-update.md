# 主観内容の隔離 — BIS Agora vs mBridge · 2026 Update — Three-Pole CBDC Map & Regulatory Friction

> 出典エントリー: `fintech/bis-agora-vs-mbridge-2026-update.md`

## Counterpoints

- **mBridge volume claims are not transparent** — central banks publish aggregate ranges but not transaction-level data; growth-rate claims should be heavily discounted.
- **Agora's "complete capital markets stack" framing is BlackRock + Citi + Morgan Stanley marketing** — actual production tokenized-deposit volume on Agora is still single-digit billions cumulative.
- **The three-pole map oversimplifies** — Brazil's DREX, India's e-Rupee, EU's digital euro all sit somewhat outside this map and could become a fourth pole or align with one of the existing three.
- **US Treasury sanctions friction may be reversible** — a future US administration could exempt central bank tokenized money from OFAC obligations, removing the chilling effect.
- **CBDC vs tokenized-deposit architecture distinction may collapse** — Agora-2027 could include both reserve-CBDC and tokenized-deposit, blurring the architectural distinction; mBridge-2028 could add a commercial-bank deposit-token layer.
- **The "BIS exit from mBridge" framing should not be read as BIS abandoning** — BIS Innovation Hub Hong Kong center still operates; cross-staff continuity exists.

## Open questions

- Does Brazil BCB formally join mBridge in 2026-H2 (DREX-mBridge interop), in 2027, or never?
- Does the US Treasury issue specific OFAC guidance for tokenized-asset settlement that resolves the chilling effect?
- Does an Agora-mBridge interop pilot launch before 2028?
- Does the digital euro launch align with Agora or sit independently as a fourth pole?
- Does India's e-Rupee + RBI public-stack DPI strategy create a fourth pole anchored on UPI rather than tokenized-banking (see [[fintech/india-anti-dollar-dpi-alliance|印度 anti-dollar DPI alliance]])?
- Does Russia formally apply to mBridge, and if so does the US trigger secondary sanctions on participating commercial banks?
- Does BlackRock's BUIDL + Agora integration become the **template** for institutional tokenized-MMF cross-border distribution?
- Does the GENIUS Act federal stablecoin framework (see [[fintech/genius-act-501-denylist-mandate|GENIUS Act §501]]) push private USD stablecoins into the Agora architecture or keep them on a parallel commercial track?
- Does the FX-onchain final-boss thesis (see [[fintech/fx-onchain-as-stablecoin-final-boss|FX オンチェーン化が stablecoin の final boss となる]]) play out within Agora or outside it?
