# 主観内容の隔離 — Paxos · BUSD 停止後のマルチプロダクト + マルチライセンス pivot · PYUSD / USDG / USDL / PAXG

> 出典エントリー: `fintech/paxos-paxg-post-binance-pivot.md`

## Counterpoints

Paxos's pivot left it with several structural exposures. **PYUSD revenue concentration**: ~70% of Paxos's stablecoin revenue still comes from PYUSD; if PayPal in-houses issuance (the Stripe × Bridge precedent, or PayPal acquiring its own trust company) Paxos loses its anchor. **USDG dependency on distributor goodwill**: the multi-distributor alliance only works if Robinhood / Kraken / etc. keep promoting USDG; any one defection (especially Robinhood) could collapse USDG share. **USDL at $80M** has not demonstrated whether Middle East / Asia retail demand is durable or just a launch effect. **PAXG's correlation to gold prices** means revenue is cyclical and could compress sharply if gold corrects 20-30%. **ADGM and Singapore licenses are valuable but expensive to maintain** — Paxos has been quietly reducing headcount on the BUSD-era infra teams while building up its multi-jurisdiction compliance teams, a transition that has visible operational stress. **Competitive threat from M^0 + Bridge**: both are explicitly going after the "issuance-as-a-service" market that Paxos's USDG model creates — Paxos may find itself with fewer brand partners than the model assumes if M^0 / Bridge undercut on fees or offer faster onboarding.

## Open questions

Does PayPal in-house PYUSD issuance in 2027-2028, and if so does Paxos pivot a second time? Does USDG reach 10+ distributor members and approach $1B MCap, validating the multi-distributor alliance model? Does the UAE Central Bank Token Service Provider license complete and let Paxos issue a UAE-Dirham-pegged product? Does USDL ever cross $500M, or does it remain a Middle East niche? Does Paxos eventually IPO or get acquired by a TradFi institution (BNY Mellon? State Street?) given the value of its multi-jurisdiction license portfolio? Does Paxos compete directly with [[fintech/m-network-m0-neutral-infrastructure|M^0]] on long-tail issuance, or do they coexist?
