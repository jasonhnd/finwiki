# 主観内容の隔離 — Brazil DREX Timeline 2024-2026 (BCB CBDC Pilot Phases, Hyperledger Besu, Pix Integration, BRL Stablecoin Competition)

> 出典エントリー: `fintech/brazil-drex-timeline-2026.md`

## Counterpoints

- **The "Pilot 2 in progress" framing may understate slippage.** BCB has been deliberately cautious; multiple reported "next phase" deadlines have been revised. A 2026 production launch is **not** committed.
- **Privacy is the binding constraint.** Without a credible privacy architecture for retail tokenized deposits on a permissioned EVM, BCB will not push to production. The technical challenge is significant.
- **Pix's success makes DREX's retail value proposition narrow.** The realistic adoption path is **B2B and programmable use cases first**, retail later (or never as a separate-token product).
- **The private-BRL-stablecoin segment may stay marginal.** BRLA + MBRL are small in volume vs USDT and USDC retail flow in Brazil; the bulk of crypto-native demand is still USD-pegged.
- **Cross-CBDC ambitions depend on geopolitical alignment.** Brazil's BRICS membership creates a structural pull toward mBridge / non-USD settlement rings, but the BCB's institutional posture is more closely aligned with BIS / Agora / Ensemble than with mBridge — the [[fintech/mbridge-non-usd-settlement-ring-scale]] analysis applies.
- **§501(d) US MRA participation** under the GENIUS Act framework (see [[fintech/genius-act-501-denylist-mandate]]) is not currently on Brazil's agenda; the divergence with Mexico (more US-aligned) and Argentina (currently US-aligned under Milei) on this axis is structural.

## Open questions

- Does Pilot 2 complete by end-2026, and does Pilot 3 / production launch become the announced next phase?
- Does BCB pick a specific privacy-overlay technology stack (Aztec, Zether-variant, ZK-rollup pattern, EVM-precompile approach) for retail tokenized deposits?
- Does the first programmable-money use case to reach production adoption come from real-estate escrow, trade finance, or tokenized-securities DvP?
- Does BRLA Digital or Mercado Bitcoin MBRL scale to a level where BCB considers regulatory boundary clarification between DREX-platform tokenized deposits and non-platform BRL stablecoins?
- Does Brazil sign on to any specific BIS interoperability framework (Agora, Ensemble, Nexus) at full-participation level in 2026-2027?
- Does the Brazilian Real Digital project ever convert into a true retail CBDC, or does the two-tier tokenized-deposit framing remain permanent?
