# 主観内容の隔離 — Japan policy-finance institution mandate matrix (JFC / DBJ / JBIC / JICA / NEXI / JOGMEC / JHF / ODFC / JASSO)

> 出典エントリー: `policy-finance/japan-policy-finance-institution-mandate-matrix.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Headline portfolio scale ≠ policy weight.** JOGMEC's headline equity book is smaller than JFC's loan book by an order of magnitude, but JOGMEC's strategic-impact-per-yen is high because it sits at the upstream-equity gate that determines whether large resource projects ever reach bankability.
- **Insurance commitment ≠ insurance loss.** NEXI's outstanding commitment is large, but realised losses depend on country-risk classification, premium adequacy, and claims experience — comparing a yen-of-NEXI-commitment to a yen-of-JFC-loan is misleading.
- **特殊会社 vs 独立行政法人 status is not "more privatised vs less privatised".** Both are 100% state-owned in capital terms; the legal form mostly controls governance and disclosure shape, not commercial freedom.
- **Co-financing ≠ identical risk-taking.** A megabank syndicated tranche alongside a JBIC direct loan and a NEXI insurance wrap is a layered risk structure, not three identical risk-takings. Pricing and tenor differ by tranche.

## Open questions

- How will the GX-transition mandate split between JBIC (overseas), DBJ (domestic large-cap), JFC (domestic SME), and NEXI (insurance) settle into a coherent stack vs continue as overlapping institutional mandates?
- How will JOGMEC's critical-minerals equity portfolio perform through the next commodity-cycle downturn, and what does that imply for the post-2022 mandate expansion?
- Will DBJ's privatisation ever resume, and if so under what mandate split with the crisis-response window?
- How will JFC's 利子補給-driven 特別貸付 menu structure adjust post-Covid as supplementary budgets normalise?
- Will JASSO's income-contingent repayment expansion be sufficient to manage demographic / labour-market hardship pressure on the student-loan portfolio?
- How will ODFC adapt to the post-Covid normalisation of Okinawa's tourism economy?
- Whether the four-minister 共管 structure of JFC will be preserved or partially consolidated in any future policy-finance reform.
