# 主観内容の隔離 — 福岡県信用保証協会 (Fukuoka Credit Guarantee Corporation)

> 出典エントリー: `policy-finance/fukuoka-credit-guarantee-corp.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Inbound-tourism geopolitical risk**: Fukuoka's tourism SME book is structurally exposed to Japan-Korea, Japan-China, and Japan-Taiwan political tensions in a way that other prefectures' books are not. A diplomatic incident can move tourism volume sharply within a single quarter.
- **Semiconductor-cycle exposure**: the new TSMC-JASM-driven supplier cluster is a positive growth story but creates fresh cyclical exposure to global semiconductor capex cycles — historically among the most volatile of any industrial segment.
- **Fukuoka FG concentration**: the dominance of [[JapanFG/fukuoka-fg|Fukuoka FG]] in the regional-bank market means Fukuoka CGC's loss correlation with a single financial-group counterparty is high.
- **Kitakyushu vs. Fukuoka City split**: Fukuoka prefecture's economic structure is bifurcated between the heavy-industry north (Kitakyushu) and the services-driven south (Fukuoka City / Hakata / Tenjin). A book-level analysis that treats the prefecture as homogeneous misses this internal heterogeneity, similar to the Mikawa-vs-Owari split in Aichi.
- **Multi-prefecture FG spillover**: [[JapanFG/fukuoka-fg|Fukuoka FG]]'s coverage of Kumamoto Bank and [[JapanFG/18-shinwa-bank|Eighteenth-Shinwa Bank]] means that Fukuoka-based guarantee operations partly reach into neighboring CGCs' counterparty footprints, complicating clean prefectural attribution of risk.

## Open questions

- What share of Fukuoka CGC's guarantee balance is now concentrated in semiconductor-supplier SMEs vs. inbound-tourism SMEs vs. general SME?
- How is the TSMC-JASM Kumamoto investment spilling over into Fukuoka prefecture's guarantee book through Kitakyushu / Kurate supplier capex?
- How did the 2020-vintage ZeroZero loans perform through full payoff in 2026–2028 specifically in the inbound-tourism segment?
- What is the long-term effect of Fukuoka City's startup ecosystem on 創業保証 utilization compared to Tokyo / Osaka peers?
- How does Fukuoka CGC's coverage of Kitakyushu's heavy-industry SME tail compare to other industrial-belt prefectures' guarantee profiles?
