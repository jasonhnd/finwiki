# 主観内容の隔離 — Japan Federation of Credit Guarantee Corporations

> 出典エントリー: `policy-finance/national-federation-credit-guarantee-corporations.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Federation vs single national CGC trade-off**: Japan's choice of 51 local CGCs + federation rather than a single national CGC reflects historical regional autonomy and local-government engagement. Critics argue a national-CGC structure would be more efficient and reduce inter-regional cost variation; defenders argue local CGCs maintain local-economy fit, local lender relationships, and local-government policy alignment that a national CGC could not replicate.
- **Federation coordination latency**: in crisis-mode programs (ZeroZero, GFC 緊急保証), federation-level standardization can lag the actual frontline lending speed at individual CGCs. JFG's response in 2020 was operationally fast by historical standards but still produced some inter-CGC variance in initial processing.
- **Loss-pooling fairness**: federation-pooled funds and credit-insurance redistribute risk across regions; this means urban CGCs effectively subsidize rural CGCs' higher loss rates. This is policy-intentional but politically sensitive.
- **City CGC vs prefecture CGC duplication**: the 4 city CGCs (Yokohama / Kawasaki / Nagoya / Gifu) operate in parallel with the same-region prefecture CGCs. Operationally workable but a structural anomaly that no other Japanese policy-finance system has.
- **ZeroZero unwind discipline**: the "粘り強い支援" framing risks delaying loss recognition. JFG's exit framework aims to balance preservation of viable SMEs against avoiding zombie-borrower accumulation; the actual outcome will only be visible through 2026–2028 statistical tracking.

## Open questions

- What is the current federation-pooled fund balance and how has it been deployed through the ZeroZero unwind?
- What is the inter-CGC variance in ZeroZero subrogation rates, and what does it reveal about local-economy vs federation-design factors?
- How will the 4 city CGCs evolve over time — consolidation with prefectural CGCs, continued parallel operation, or expansion?
- What is the federation's long-run statistical projection for SME guarantee demand as Japan's SME population contracts with demographics?
- How does JFG plan to integrate the 経営者保証ガイドライン shift away from owner-personal-guarantees into the responsibility-sharing system economics?
