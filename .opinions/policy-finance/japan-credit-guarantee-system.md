# 主観内容の隔離 — Japan credit guarantee system

> 出典エントリー: `policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Moral hazard from 100% guarantees**: critics argue 100% guarantee programs (危機関連, セーフティネット extensions, ZeroZero) weaken lender screening and create over-extension of credit to fundamentally unviable SMEs. The 2007 責任共有 reform partially addresses this, but crisis-era programs reinstate full guarantee. Counterargument: in genuine systemic crisis, the priority is system-wide liquidity preservation, and screening can be restored ex post via workout-stage discipline.
- **ZeroZero post-mortem**: the 2020 ZeroZero program is structurally analogous to the 1998 安定化特別保証 (Asian-crisis-era emergency program); both produced large subsequent subrogation waves. The systemic lesson — that crisis guarantees produce delayed loss recognition — is well understood but politically difficult to constrain in real time.
- **Regional CGC capital adequacy**: rural CGCs face structural challenges (lower recovery values, smaller capital base, higher loss-given-default) that pressure regional equity. The system mitigates this via federation pooling and JFC reinsurance, but local CGC reserves can be stressed in concentrated regional shocks.
- **Lender risk-transfer overuse**: with 80% guarantee available on most ordinary loans, some lenders use CGC guarantees as default risk-mitigation rather than as a public-credit instrument for marginal borrowers. The 責任共有 system pushes back but does not eliminate this dynamic.

## Open questions

- What is the consolidated current subrogation volume across all 51 CGCs from ZeroZero-vintage loans, and how does it compare to the 1998 安定化特別保証 unwind?
- How does the ZeroZero subrogation rate distribute across sectors (hospitality / restaurants / construction / manufacturing / retail)?
- What is the current breakdown of CGC reserves vs JFC insurance payments vs national / local government backstop contributions absorbing the ZeroZero loss tail?
- How does CGC subrogation interact with the 経営者保証ガイドライン (personal-guarantee guideline) shift away from owner-personal-guarantees on SME loans?
- What is the current ratio of 80% responsibility-sharing guarantees to 100% policy-program guarantees in the active book?
