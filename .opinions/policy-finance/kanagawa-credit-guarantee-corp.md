# 主観内容の隔離 — 神奈川県信用保証協会 (Kanagawa Credit Guarantee Corporation)

> 出典エントリー: `policy-finance/kanagawa-credit-guarantee-corp.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Tokyo-correlation question**: critics argue Kanagawa CGC's book is structurally a satellite of [[policy-finance/tokyo-credit-guarantee-corp|Tokyo CGC]] given the commuter-economy SME overlap, which would suggest a higher loss correlation than diversified prefectures. The counterargument is that the Keihin industrial / port-logistics cluster gives Kanagawa a distinct risk profile independent of Tokyo's service-economy cycle.
- **Port-cycle exposure**: container-shipping and bulk-freight cycles can move very fast, and SME logistics counterparties have thin margins. Subrogation volume in the port-logistics segment can spike with limited warning.
- **Heavy-industry decline**: long-run decline in Keihin heavy-industry footprints (steel, petrochemicals, shipbuilding) erodes the Tier-2/3 supplier base, gradually shrinking one of Kanagawa CGC's traditional guarantee pools.
- **Yokohama Bank concentration**: [[regional-banks/yokohama-bank|Yokohama Bank]]'s dominance of the Kanagawa regional-bank market means Kanagawa CGC's guarantee book is heavily concentrated against a single dominant lending counterparty. This is partly mitigated by megabank in-prefecture branch participation.
- **Nissan exposure**: the Nissan-anchored automotive supplier cluster in Atsugi / Yokosuka adds a layer of single-OEM risk in a smaller but structurally similar pattern to Aichi's Toyota dependence.

## Open questions

- What share of Kanagawa CGC's guarantee balance is concentrated in the port-logistics SME segment vs. the Keihin industrial belt vs. the commuter-economy retail / service tail?
- How tightly correlated are Kanagawa CGC subrogation losses with [[policy-finance/tokyo-credit-guarantee-corp|Tokyo CGC]] over a full economic cycle?
- What was the ultimate subrogation loss profile of Kanagawa's 2020-vintage ZeroZero loans through full payoff?
- How does the Concordia FG ([[regional-banks/yokohama-bank|Yokohama Bank]]) relationship-banking model affect guarantee utilization vs. peer prefectures with weaker dominant regional banks?
- What is the trend in 創業保証 utilization in Shin-Yokohama and Kawasaki innovation zones, and how does it compare to Tokyo's startup-finance flow?
- How does the Nissan-anchored Atsugi / Yokosuka automotive supplier base behave through the EV transition, and how does it differ in scale and dynamics from the Toyota cluster covered by [[policy-finance/aichi-credit-guarantee-corp|Aichi CGC]]?
- What is the long-term trajectory of Yokohama Port and Kawasaki Port SME logistics under the global container-shipping share-shift from intra-Asia to trans-Pacific?
- How does the dense Yokohama / Kawasaki / Sagamihara / Shonan shinkin network split its guarantee-backed lending vs. unsecured SME lending within Kanagawa CGC's footprint?
