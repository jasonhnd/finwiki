## Counterpoints

- "PayPay's 70% share number is precise." It is **indicative** — exact share calculations vary by metric (TPV vs registered users vs MAU vs merchant locations) and by reporting period. METI and Cashless Promotion Council publish aggregate code-payment-class numbers but do not always publish per-operator splits.
- "Merpay is smaller than its strategic importance." TPV-only ranking understates Mercari's structural role in code-payment because the marketplace-anchored balance funding mechanism and the バーチャル credit + BNPL adjacency create cross-product LTV.
- "FamiPay is just a FamilyMart product." FamiPay external expansion through 2024-2025 has materially broadened the merchant footprint beyond FamilyMart-internal — though still narrower than the general-purpose wallets. See [[payments/famipay-valucreate-strategy|FamiPay ValuCreate strategy]] for the broader strategic frame.
- "J-Coin Pay failed." Operating, not failed — but at a meaningfully smaller scale than the leading wallets, reflecting the absence of point-driven user-acquisition machinery and a narrower distribution channel.
- "Seven Pay = Seven Bank." Different — [[payment-firms/seven-payment-service|Seven Payment Service]] / Seven Bank are operating financial entities; Seven Pay was a specific code-payment app that was shut down in 2019 due to security breach.
- "Code-payment will replace card payment." Code-payment value (16.6T yen) remains far below card payment value (~100T+ yen) — see [[payments/japan-payment-scheme-economics-matrix|Japan payment scheme economics matrix]]. Even at high growth rates, code-payment is a complementary lane, not a replacement.
- "All code-payment is equivalent." Per the matrix: deposit attachment, point integration, BNPL adjacency, banking-license status, and merchant-fee structure differ materially per operator.

## Open questions

- Will [[payment-firms/paypay|PayPay]]'s majority share stabilize, or do ecosystem-anchored competitors (Rakuten, docomo, KDDI, Mercari, FamiMa) chip away through superior cross-product economics?
- Does the **LY Corporation merger / LINE Pay JP migration into PayPay** complete cleanly through 2026, and what residual user-base does LINE retain?
- Will [[payment-firms/merpay|Mercari]] achieve its banking license ambition (Mercari Bank), and how does that re-shape Merpay's funding-source economics?
- Will [[payment-firms/famima-digital-one|FamiMa Digital One]] / FamiPay extend beyond ITOCHU-group retail to broader external merchants at scale?
- Will [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]] expand J-Coin Pay through regional-bank consortium participation, or does the bank-anchor model remain structurally niche?
- Will [[regional-banks/yucho|Japan Post Bank]] re-invest in ゆうちょPay growth, or does the postal-customer-anchor segment remain a code-payment laggard?
- Does code-payment merchant-fee disclosure pressure (JFTC extension from card to code) re-shape merchant economics?
- Will Visa Direct / Mastercard Send / Visa Agentic Commerce / Mastercard Agent Pay flow over code-payment-wallet rails or directly to card rails?
- How does NTT docomo FG's bank-side strategy (post-2026 reorganization) re-shape d-Barai economics?
