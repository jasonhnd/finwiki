# 主観内容の隔離 — Google Play Gift Card Japan: closed-loop Google-ecosystem prepaid

> 出典エントリー: `payments/google-play-gift-card-japan.md`

## Counterpoints

- "Google Play Gift Card is just for apps." It also funds in-app purchases (the larger spend category), Google Play Books, Movies, and Google Play Pass — not apps alone.
- "Carrier billing replaces gift card." Carrier billing is the larger volume rail but does not cover users outside major-carrier contracts; gift card serves the residual cohort and minors.
- "Closed-loop is less valuable than open-loop." Closed-loop platform gift cards capture a structural cardless on-ramp need that open-loop gift cards do not serve as cleanly.
- "Scam exploitation will end gift cards." Convenience-store warning and consumer education are deployed countermeasures; banning gift cards would harm legitimate cardless on-ramp use.
