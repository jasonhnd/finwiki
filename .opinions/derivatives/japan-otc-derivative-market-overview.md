# 主観内容の隔離 — Japan OTC derivative market overview

> 出典エントリー: `derivatives/japan-otc-derivative-market-overview.md`

## Counterpoints

- "OTC is opaque" — Aggregate-level public data is rich, but trade-level pricing and counterparty-specific exposures remain private. The opacity is at the trade level, not the market level.
- "Clearing eliminates risk" — Central clearing transforms bilateral counterparty risk into CCP risk; the CCP default-fund waterfall and capital adequacy are the residual risk surface.
- "FIEA covers everything" — FIEA covers Japan-located dealers and trades involving regulated counterparties. Offshore bilateral trades may sit outside the mandate.
- "Notional = exposure" — Notional outstanding overstates economic exposure dramatically. Gross market value is the more relevant metric for credit risk.
- "Retail FX is small" — Japanese retail FX margin trading is one of the largest retail FX markets globally by turnover, contributing meaningfully to aggregate FX OTC turnover statistics.
