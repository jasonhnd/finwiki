# 主観内容の隔離 — 会社分割 (Japan corporate split) — 吸収 / 新設 split types, 適格 tax regime, and employee succession

> 出典エントリー: `corporate-strategy/japan-kaisha-bunkatsu-tax-regime.md`

## Counterpoints

- The clean 適格 / 非適格 binary obscures multiple sub-categories — wholly-owned, majority-owned, joint-business — each with different test sets. Practitioners frequently misclassify the applicable test set.
- The 労働契約承継法 automatic-transfer rule is often described as "no consent needed", which understates the consultation burden — properly executed 7条 / 5条 consultation is procedurally heavy.
- Cross-border buyers attempting to apply Japanese 会社分割 mechanics to a Japanese subsidiary inside a global carve-out frequently underestimate the difference between Japanese 適格 tests and US Sec. 355 / UK demerger tests; the regimes do not map cleanly.
- "適格 means tax-free" is wrong — 適格 means tax-**deferred**, with carryover basis preserving the latent gain in the successor's hands for eventual recognition.
