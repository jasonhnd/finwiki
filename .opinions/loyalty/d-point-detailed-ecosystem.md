# 主観内容の隔離 — d Point detailed ecosystem (NTT docomo) — accrual mechanics, d Card / d払い convergence, 2025 reform

> 出典エントリー: `loyalty/d-point-detailed-ecosystem.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Telco moat is weakening**: ahamo, irumo, povo, LINEMO MVNO competition has materially reduced mobile-line switching cost. dポイント's anchor value depends on docomo subscriber retention, which is structurally pressured.
- **d払い lags PayPay in code-payment share**: despite the 2025 dポイントクラブ rank reform's d払い-weighting, d払い MAU and TPV remain materially below PayPay — see [[payments/japan-code-payment-operator-2025-market-share-matrix|2025 code-payment market share matrix]]. The reform is necessary but may not be sufficient.
- **Lawson partnership uncertain post-KDDI ownership**: as detailed in [[retail/lawson-mitsubishi-corporation-tie-up-2024-deep|Lawson + Mitsubishi 2024 deep tie-up]], KDDI's strategic incentive favours Pontaポイント at Lawson; dポイント retention at Lawson over the medium term is a partnership-not-equity question.
- **NDFG consolidation execution risk**: the planned 2026-07 NDFG holding company has not yet produced consolidated financial reports; the integrated narrative depends on execution that is still forward-looking.
- **Accounting opacity**: NTT docomo's contract-liability disclosure combines dポイント with telecom-revenue deferred amounts, making the standalone dポイント liability less observable than for pure-play loyalty operators.

## Open questions

- Will the 2025 dポイントクラブ rank reform achieve its strategic goal of materially increasing d払い MAU and TPV share against PayPay?
- How will the [[megabanks/ndfg|NDFG]] consolidation announce dポイント's role as a cross-product loyalty currency — will the rank system extend to bank / securities / insurance / trust products inside NDFG?
- Does the partnership with **Lawson** survive at parity with Pontaポイント, or does dポイント lose retail surface progressively as KDDI integrates Ponta into Lawson's POS strategy?
- How does d Card GOLD's economic break-even change if docomo mobile prices continue downward pressure from MVNO competition?
- Will dスマートバンク (the digital-bank brand) merge into a renamed bank vehicle under NDFG, and does dポイント accrual extend to deposit / lending / NISA products?
- How does the IFRS 15 contract-liability disclosure evolve as dポイント liability grows in absolute terms — at what scale does separate disclosure become material enough for granular IR breakout?
