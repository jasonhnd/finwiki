## Counterpoints

- **au PAY trailing PayPay in wallet share**: despite the integrated ecosystem, au PAY's code-payment wallet share is materially below PayPay's. The [[payments/japan-code-payment-operator-2025-market-share-matrix|2025 market share matrix]] shows PayPay maintaining a dominant lead.
- **Pontaポイント governance limits**: Loyalty Marketing is Mitsubishi Corp-affiliated, not KDDI-controlled. KDDI's strategic leverage over Pontaポイント program design (rate structure, expiry rules, merchant onboarding) is contractual rather than equity.
- **50/50 JV friction risk at Lawson**: the Lawson governance design requires KDDI ↔ Mitsubishi Corp agreement on retail strategy. Decision-paralysis risk is documented in [[retail/lawson-mitsubishi-corporation-tie-up-2024-deep|Lawson + Mitsubishi 2024 deep tie-up]].
- **au じぶん銀行 deposit-rate sensitivity**: as deposit balance grew through the negative-interest-rate era and into the BOJ rate-normalization era, au じぶん銀行's deposit-margin economics may compress under competitive pressure from megabanks raising deposit rates.
- **UQ Mobile cannibalization of au-mainline**: the multi-line strategy preserves ecosystem attachment but compresses ARPU. au PAY ゴールドカード hurdle economics weaken if customers downgrade to UQ Mobile.
- **2025 dポイントクラブ reform pressure**: NTT docomo's 2025 dポイントクラブ reform shifts dポイント accrual weighting toward d払い, potentially eroding the dポイント vs au PAY ポイント (Ponta) parity. KDDI's countermove may be needed.
- **au FH IPO pricing risk**: a 2026-2027 [[JapanFG/au-fh|au FH]] IPO will be tested against PayPay 銀行 / 楽天銀行 ネット銀行 valuation comparables, which trade at variable multiples.
