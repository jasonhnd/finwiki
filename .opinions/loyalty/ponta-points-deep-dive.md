# 主観内容の隔離 — Ponta points deep dive — Loyalty Marketing Inc., KDDI au PAY integration, Lawson + Mitsubishi anchor

> 出典エントリー: `loyalty/ponta-points-deep-dive.md`

## Counterpoints

- **Coalition governance complexity**: with KDDI, Mitsubishi Corp, and Recruit as the three principal commercial stakeholders, strategic decisions on point economics or partner integration require multi-party alignment. This is structurally slower than single-anchor programmes.
- **Telco moat erosion**: ahamo, irumo, povo, LINEMO MVNO competition reduces au's mobile-line stickiness, weakening the telco-anchor leg of the Ponta coalition. Mitsubishi's retail anchor (Lawson) and Recruit's service anchor compensate partially.
- **Lawson POS competition**: Lawson POS continues to accept PayPay, dポイント (partnership), and Pontaポイント. KDDI's incentive to favour Ponta cannot translate into exclusion of competing wallets without alienating customers.
- **PayPay scale dominance at wallet layer**: PayPay's wallet share (per [[payments/japan-code-payment-operator-2025-market-share-matrix|2025 share matrix]]) materially exceeds au PAY. Ponta's coalition reach doesn't compensate for au PAY's wallet-share gap.
- **Data-marketing depth**: Loyalty Marketing's data-marketing operation is mature but doesn't match the depth of CCC's historical T-Point database or LY's combined Yahoo + LINE data assets that PayPay leverages.

## Open questions

- How will the 2024 KDDI + Mitsubishi Lawson take-private translate into Ponta-Lawson integration depth over the medium term? Will dポイント lose accrual surface at Lawson progressively?
- Does the 2024-12 au PAY ポイント / Ponta wallet-side unification accelerate Ponta-ID growth or simply consolidate existing accrual under a single brand?
- Will the Recruit ecosystem partnership remain stable as Recruit's strategic priorities evolve (e.g., toward Indeed Hire-side international expansion vs domestic services)?
- How does Pontaポイント's JAL alliance evolve relative to the more mature dポイント ↔ JAL exchange (a competing carrier pair within the same airline)?
- Can Loyalty Marketing's data-marketing operation under Mitsubishi parent monetise the 100M+ Ponta-ID database at scale comparable to CCC's historical T-Point data business?
- What is the regulatory ceiling on telco-retail-finance data sharing under personal-information protection rules, and how does it apply to the KDDI-Mitsubishi-Recruit coalition structure?
