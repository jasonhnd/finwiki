## TL;DR

多摩信用金庫（たましん）は、東京都立川市に本店を置く、東京都多摩地域（立川・武蔵野・八王子・三鷹・調布など）を主たる営業地盤とする大手地域信用金庫。多摩地区の中小企業・住宅ローン・個人預金を中核とし、ベンチャー支援・産学連携・地域 CRE 金融でも知られる協同組織金融機関。

## 2. Institution-type business model

As a **信用金庫 (shinkin bank)**, 多摩信用金庫 is a *member-based cooperative deposit-taking institution* under 信用金庫法, not a joint-stock bank. Membership and lending are anchored to a defined 営業地区 — Tokyo's 多摩 region — and credit is statutorily oriented to member SMEs and retail households. Its region-development overlay (ベンチャー支援・産学連携・地域 CRE) is an expression of the cooperative mandate to serve the local member economy rather than maximise external-shareholder returns, the defining feature of Japan's 協同組織金融 (cooperative finance) tier it shares with [[cooperative-banks/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]] and [[cooperative-banks/asahi-shinkin|朝日信用金庫]].

多摩信用金庫は、多摩地域の中小企業向け SME 融資・住宅ローン・個人預金を中核業務としつつ、ベンチャー支援（多摩信用金庫ビジネスフェアなど）、地元大学（中央大・東京農工大など）との産学連携、地域 CRE/不動産金融に強みを持つ。東京 23 区中心の [[cooperative-banks/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]]・[[cooperative-banks/asahi-shinkin|朝日信用金庫]] と地理的に役割分担する Tama 地区アンカー shinkin。

## 3. System & regulatory positioning

- **Central institution**: sits beneath [[cooperative-banks/shinkin-central|信金中央金庫 (Shinkin Central Bank)]] for liquidity, surplus-fund investment, and settlement — the shinkin analogue of [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin|農林中央金庫]] for the JA system.
- **Supervision**: licensed under 信用金庫法 by [[financial-regulators/fsa|the FSA]] with day-to-day oversight delegated to the regional 財務局; deposits are insured by [[financial-regulators/dic|the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC)]].
- **System context**: see [[banking/cooperative-banking-japan|cooperative banking in Japan]] for the shinkin tier's place within 協同組織金融 and [[banking/shinkin-bank-registry-japan|the shinkin registry]] for the national 信用金庫 population.
