## TL;DR

朝日信用金庫は、東京都台東区に本店を置く、東京都心部（台東・中央・千代田・墨田など）と埼玉県・千葉県の一部を主たる営業地盤とする大手都市型信用金庫。中小企業・問屋・商店・個人事業主向けの融資を中核とする協同組織金融機関。

## 2. Institution-type business model

As a **信用金庫 (shinkin bank)**, 朝日信用金庫 is a *member-based cooperative deposit-taking institution* under 信用金庫法, not a joint-stock bank. Lending is statutorily directed at members within a defined 営業地区 — here the dense 問屋街 (wholesale district) of central Tokyo plus 中小製造業・サービス業 — rather than at the open corporate market a 都市銀行 serves. Deposits are taken from the general public, but credit and surplus are oriented to the cooperative membership of local SMEs and individual proprietors, the defining feature of Japan's 協同組織金融 tier shared with [[cooperative-banks/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]] and the wider shinkin population.

朝日信用金庫は、東京都心の問屋街（浅草橋・蔵前・日本橋など）の中小卸売業・小売業・個人事業主、ならびに台東・中央・千代田・墨田区の中小製造業・サービス業向け融資を中核業務とする。東京の shinkin landscape では [[cooperative-banks/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]] と並ぶ大手都市型 shinkin の一つで、預金規模で全国信用金庫の上位に位置する。

## 3. System & regulatory positioning

- **Central institution**: sits beneath [[cooperative-banks/shinkin-central|信金中央金庫 (Shinkin Central Bank)]] for liquidity, surplus-fund management, and nationwide settlement — the shinkin analogue of [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin|農林中央金庫]] for the JA system.
- **Supervision**: chartered under 信用金庫法 and supervised by [[financial-regulators/fsa|the FSA]] with day-to-day oversight delegated to the regional 財務局; deposits are protected by [[financial-regulators/dic|the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC)]].
- **System context**: see [[banking/cooperative-banking-japan|cooperative banking in Japan]] for the position of the shinkin tier within 協同組織金融, and [[banking/shinkin-bank-registry-japan|the shinkin registry]] for the national 信用金庫 population.
