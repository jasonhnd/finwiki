# 大阪信用金庫

## Wiki route

Read it against [[cooperative-banks/kyoto-chuo-shinkin|京都中央信用金庫]] for peer / contrast context within the Kansai shinkin block, [[banking/shinkin-bank-registry-japan|Japan shinkin bank registry]] for the system landscape, [[cooperative-banks/shinkin-central|信金中央金庫]] for the cooperative central institution above it, and [[financial-regulators/fsa|the FSA]] for the supervisory / licensing boundary over 信用金庫.

## TL;DR

大阪信用金庫（だいしん）は、大阪市天王寺区に本店を置く、大阪市・大阪府を主たる営業地盤とする大手信用金庫の一つ。大阪の中小企業・商店・個人事業主への融資、住宅ローン、相続・事業承継相談を中核業務とする。

## 2. Institution-type business model

As a **信用金庫 (shinkin bank)**, 大阪信用金庫 is a *member-based cooperative deposit-taking institution* under 信用金庫法, not a joint-stock bank. Membership and lending are tied to a defined 営業地区 — 大阪市・大阪府 — and credit is statutorily oriented to member SMEs, 商店, and individual proprietors rather than the open corporate market a 都市銀行 serves. Surplus serves member return and reserves, the defining feature of the 協同組織金融 (cooperative finance) tier shared with [[cooperative-banks/kyoto-chuo-shinkin|京都中央信用金庫]] and the broader shinkin population. Within Osaka it also runs alongside the 業域型 [[cooperative-banks/osaka-shogin-shinkumi|大阪商銀信用組合]] in the adjacent 信用組合 cooperative tier.

大阪信用金庫は、大阪都市部の中小企業・小規模事業者向けの SME 融資・運転資金・設備資金・住宅ローン・個人預金を中核とする協同組織金融機関。大阪府下に多数の店舗網を展開し、地域密着型の融資判断・経営相談・ビジネスマッチングを強みとする。大阪府内では [[cooperative-banks/kawasaki-shinkin|川崎信用金庫]] のような首都圏大手 shinkin と相似する都市部 shinkin モデルを構成する。

## 3. System & regulatory positioning

- **Central institution**: sits beneath [[cooperative-banks/shinkin-central|信金中央金庫 (Shinkin Central Bank)]] for liquidity, surplus-fund investment, and settlement — the shinkin analogue of [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin|農林中央金庫]] for the JA system.
- **Supervision**: licensed under 信用金庫法 by [[financial-regulators/fsa|the FSA]] with day-to-day oversight delegated to the regional 財務局; deposits are insured by [[financial-regulators/dic|the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC)]].
- **System context**: see [[banking/cooperative-banking-japan|cooperative banking in Japan]] for the shinkin tier's place within 協同組織金融 and [[banking/shinkin-bank-registry-japan|the shinkin registry]] for the national 信用金庫 population.
