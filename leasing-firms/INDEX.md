# leasing-firms — Japanese leasing / auto-finance / captive-lessor index

## TL;DR

`leasing-firms/` contains **23 active entries** (excluding INDEX, 24 total) covering Japanese leasing companies, auto-finance / fleet-leasing operators, and captive lessors — the independent leasing majors, the megabank- and trust-affiliated leasing arms, the vendor / manufacturer-captive lessors, and the regional-bank leasing subsidiaries. This domain was split out of the former `JapanFG/` umbrella alongside [[consumer-finance/INDEX|consumer-finance]]. Use it when a bank, trading-house, manufacturer, or regional-bank page needs the underlying leasing / auto-finance operating company rather than the parent group entity.

> **Scope / disambiguation.** This domain is **Japanese leasing / auto-finance / captive-lessor operating companies** — the general-leasing majors (ORIX, Tokyo Century), bank-affiliated leasing arms (Mitsubishi HC Capital, Mizuho Lease, SMFL, Fuyo Lease), auto / fleet finance (Toyota Financial Services, Sumitomo Mitsui Auto Service), vendor-captive lessors (NEC, Ricoh, NTT TC, Hitachi Capital legacy), and regional-bank leasing subsidiaries. It is **not** the *thematic* manufacturer captive / vendor / export / floorplan finance MECHANISM layer ([[manufacturer-finance/INDEX|manufacturer-finance]]), **not** card / installment-credit issuers (信販・カード → see [[card-issuers/jaccs|株式会社ジャックス (Jaccs)]] and the card-issuers domain), and **not** the 貸金業 consumer-lender split sibling ([[consumer-finance/INDEX|consumer-finance]]). For the parent financial-group *entity* pages and the umbrella that this domain was split from, see [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]].

## How the domain reads

- **Independent vs bank-affiliated split** — ORIX and Tokyo Century run as independent diversified financial-services groups, while Mitsubishi HC Capital, Mizuho Leasing, SMFL, and Fuyo Lease sit inside megabank / financial-group families. The governance route is the main differentiator.
- **Consolidation lineage** — the bank-affiliated majors are merger products: Mitsubishi HC Capital = 三菱UFJリース + 日立キャピタル ([[leasing-firms/hitachi-capital|Hitachi Capital]] legacy), Mizuho Leasing absorbed [[leasing-firms/ibj-leasing|IBJ Leasing]]. Legacy pages are kept as redirect-style anchors.
- **Captive / auto / vendor layer** — Toyota Financial Services and Sumitomo Mitsui Auto Service cover the auto / fleet lane; NEC, Ricoh, NTT TC, and Kanematsu cover vendor / manufacturer-captive equipment finance. The *thematic mechanism* view of captive / vendor / floorplan finance lives in [[manufacturer-finance/INDEX|manufacturer-finance]].
- **Regional-bank subsidiaries** — Chibagin, Mebuki, Iyogin, Yamaguchi, Yokohama Bank, and Hokkaido leasing arms are the operating-company route from a regional-bank page into equipment / vehicle leasing.
