# manufacturing — Japanese manufacturing company index

## TL;DR

`manufacturer-finance/` contains **13 active entries** (excluding INDEX, 14 total). The domain scope is **manufacturer-*finance*: the captive, vendor, export, and floorplan finance MECHANISMS plus the captive / vendor / export finance arms run by industrial groups, and the operating-company anchors they sit behind** — not a general manufacturer encyclopedia. The domain now layers three mechanism pages (captive/vendor, export, floorplan) under company profiles for the Japanese-OEM auto captives (Toyota Financial Services, Honda Finance, Nissan Financial Services) and the industrial-conglomerate finance arms (Sony Group finance arm, Hitachi industrial finance, Panasonic captive finance, Mitsubishi Heavy export finance, Komatsu captive finance), with operating-company anchors (Toyota Motor, Seiko Epson) behind them. Use this domain when a bank / finance page needs the underlying industrial borrower, captive-finance arm, finance mechanism, parent company, or regional manufacturing anchor.

> **Scope / disambiguation.** This domain is manufacturer-finance, not corporate profiles. For financial-group *entity* pages see [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]]; for the asset-backed securitisation (ABS) side of captive finance see [[structured-finance/INDEX|structured-finance]]. The pages here are the manufacturer-side complement to those two homes.
