# Agriculture credit guarantee system in Japan

## TL;DR

Japan's agriculture credit guarantee system supplements farmers' and agriculture-related borrowers' credit by combining prefectural agriculture credit fund associations with insurance / support from the Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations. It is adjacent to [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan|JA Bank system]], but it is a public-credit guarantee-insurance route rather than a JA Bank deposit / lending system page.

## 1. System boundary

| Layer | Role | FinWiki route |
|---|---|---|
| Agricultural borrower | Farmer, agriculture corporation, agriculture worker, or eligible agriculture-related organization. | System route here |
| Lending institution | JA, bank, and other eligible financial institutions extending agriculture-related credit. | [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan]] / bank pages as relevant |
| Agriculture credit fund association | Prefectural association that guarantees eligible borrower obligations. | System route here; local association pages only if strategic |
| Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations | Provides guarantee insurance and direct loan insurance / lending support where relevant. | System route here |
| MAFF policy lane | Public agriculture-finance and guarantee-insurance policy. | [[policy-finance/INDEX]] |

## 2. Operating logic

- Prefectural agriculture credit fund associations guarantee debt incurred by eligible agricultural borrowers.
- The Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations insures those guarantees and supports the guarantee capacity of the associations.
- If a guaranteed borrower defaults, the association performs subrogation and the national credit-fund layer pays insurance according to the applicable scheme.
- Separate direct insurance can apply when the agriculture credit fund association does not guarantee a large loan.

## 3. Why it matters

- This page prevents agriculture guarantees from being mixed into ordinary SME credit guarantees.
- It clarifies the relationship between [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan|JA Bank system]], [[JapanFG/norinchukin]], and agriculture public-credit support.
- It gives a policy-finance route for agriculture modernization, disaster support, and regional farming credit.

## 4. Boundary cases

- **Not JA Bank**: JA Bank may be a lender route, but this page is the guarantee-insurance system.
- **Not the SME guarantee system**: SME guarantees route to [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]].
- **Not fisheries**: fisheries guarantee-insurance belongs to [[policy-finance/fisheries-credit-guarantee-system]].
