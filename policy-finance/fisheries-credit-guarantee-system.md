# Fisheries credit guarantee system in Japan

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance index]]. Read it against [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system|Japan credit guarantee system]] for SME-side contrast, [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system|agriculture credit guarantee system]] for the sister sector (same JAFFIC parent), and [[policy-finance/japan-policy-finance-system|Japan policy finance system]] for the broader public-credit boundary. The fisheries deposit-and-lending counterpart is [[banking/jf-marine-bank-system-japan|JF Marine Bank system]] and the cooperative apex is [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin|Norinchukin]] (which serves agriculture, forestry, and fisheries cooperatives jointly).

## TL;DR

Japan's fisheries credit guarantee system (漁業信用保証保険制度) supplements small / medium fisheries operators' credit through a two-tier structure: (1) prefectural-and-broad-area-level **漁業信用基金協会** (Fisheries Credit Fund Associations) that guarantee fisheries-borrower debt to lending institutions, with the **全国漁業信用基金協会連合会** as national federation, reinsured by (2) the national-level **独立行政法人農林漁業信用基金** (JAFFIC) fisheries-credit-insurance business. The system parallels the [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system|agriculture credit guarantee system]] but with sector-specific borrower targets — fishing vessel finance, aquaculture, seafood processing — and is geographically concentrated in Japan's coastal prefectures with Hokkaido, Kyushu (Nagasaki / Kagoshima), Tōhoku Pacific coast, and Sanriku coast accounting for the bulk of guarantee volume.

Japanese fisheries finance flows through a **fisheries 系統金融** stack that parallels the agriculture stack:

## 3. 機関概要 — JAFFIC 漁業信用保険業務

Same parent agency as agriculture (independent administrative agency 農林漁業信用基金 / JAFFIC). Separate **fisheries credit insurance** account within JAFFIC, with separate reinsurance pool and separate prefectural-association interface. See [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system]] for the agriculture-account counterpart.

## 4. 主要保証商品

- **漁業近代化資金保証** — modernization-loan guarantee for fishing-vessel construction, engine replacement, gear upgrade, refrigeration, on-board processing.
- **漁業経営改善支援資金保証** — fisheries-management improvement loan guarantee.
- **沿岸漁業改善資金保証** — coastal-fisheries improvement loan guarantee.
- **新規就業者支援資金保証** — new-entrant fisher / aquaculture-operator support guarantee.
- **養殖関連融資保証** — aquaculture facility / feed / fingerling working-capital guarantee (heavy use in scallop / oyster / yellowtail / kombu / pearl segments).
- **水産加工 / 流通融資保証** — seafood processing and distribution working-capital guarantee.
- **災害復旧保証** — disaster-recovery guarantee (typhoon damage, marine pollution, red tide, vessel loss, harbor damage).
- **燃油価格高騰対策保証** — fuel-price-shock liquidity guarantee.
- **借換保証** — refinancing guarantee.

## 5. 製品ミックス characteristics

The fisheries guarantee book has a distinct product-mix profile vs the agriculture book:

- **Fishing vessel finance** is heavily collateralized but with depreciating asset values; guarantee terms reflect the vessel-life cycle and resale-market thinness for specialized vessels.
- **Aquaculture finance** is highly geographically concentrated (scallop / kombu in Hokkaido and Sanriku; oyster / pearl in Hiroshima and Mie; yellowtail in Kagoshima and Ehime; tuna farming in Wakayama and Nagasaki) and exposed to environmental shocks (red tide, marine heatwave, disease outbreaks).
- **Processing finance** overlaps with general SME working-capital needs but with seasonality tied to catch cycles and inventory build for export markets.

## 6. Geographic concentration

Unlike agriculture, which is geographically distributed across nearly all prefectures, the fisheries guarantee system is **heavily concentrated in coastal prefectures**:

| Region | Fisheries character | Guarantee book emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| Hokkaido | Pacific salmon, scallop, kelp, sea urchin, crab — Japan's largest fisheries prefecture by volume and value. | Vessel finance + scallop / kelp aquaculture + processing. |
| Sanriku coast (Iwate, Miyagi) | Coastal fisheries; oyster, scallop, kelp aquaculture; salmon; saury. | Post-2011-earthquake reconstruction guarantee + aquaculture. |
| Kyushu (Nagasaki, Kagoshima, Fukuoka, Saga) | Tuna farming, yellowtail aquaculture, coastal fisheries, pelagic fishing fleet. | Aquaculture + vessel finance + export-processing. |
| Sea of Japan (Tottori, Shimane, Toyama) | Snow crab, glow squid, yellowtail. | Vessel + seasonal-processing guarantee. |
| Pacific central (Chiba, Shizuoka, Kochi) | Bonito, tuna, mackerel; distant-water fleet. | Vessel finance for distant-water fleet. |
| Inland-sea (Hiroshima, Ehime, Mie) | Oyster, pearl, yellowtail. | Aquaculture-heavy guarantee book. |

The geographic concentration means the fisheries guarantee system's portfolio risk is exposed to **regional marine-environment shocks** that can simultaneously affect multiple guarantees in a single coastal area.

## 7. Year evolution

| Year | Event |
|---|---|
| 1952 | 中小漁業融資保証法 enacted; prefectural fisheries credit fund associations established. |
| 1972 | 漁業信用基金 established at national level. |
| 1990s | 200海里EEZ regime / pelagic fishery restructuring; vessel reduction policy supported by guarantee restructuring. |
| 2003-10-01 | 独立行政法人農林漁業信用基金 (JAFFIC) created by integrating agriculture, forestry, and fisheries credit-insurance bodies. |
| 2011-03-11 | Tōhoku earthquake / tsunami devastates Sanriku coastal fisheries — fisheries guarantee system core to reconstruction lending. |
| 2018 | 漁業法 70-year fundamental revision — TAC / IQ resource-management modernization; aquaculture license reform; guarantee system adapts to new resource framework. |
| 2020–2022 | COVID-era food-service collapse hits tuna / yellowtail / scallop / oyster export markets — guarantee system supports liquidity. |
| 2022 | Energy / fuel price shock; 燃油価格高騰 guarantee activated; coastal-fisheries operating margins collapse. |
| 2023 | Fukushima ALPS-treated-water release → Chinese seafood-import ban → scallop / sea-cucumber export shock — guarantee system supports working-capital bridge for affected operators (Hokkaido / Aomori especially). |
| 2024 | Continued ALPS-related export-market recovery; guarantee book absorbs ongoing reconstruction. |
| 2024–2025 | Marine-heatwave shocks affecting kelp / scallop / shellfish; guarantee system covers operating-loss bridge financing. |
| 2025 | Continued policy focus on aquaculture expansion (国産養殖振興) as food-security strategy; guarantee system aligned. |

## 8. Comparison vs agriculture credit guarantee system

| Dimension | Fisheries credit guarantee system | [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system\|Agriculture credit guarantee system]] |
|---|---|---|
| Borrower target | 漁業者 / 漁業法人 / 養殖業者 / 水産加工業者 | 農家 / 農業法人 / 関連事業者 |
| Local guarantor | 漁業信用基金協会 (prefectural / 広域) | 農業信用基金協会 (47 prefectural) |
| National federation | 全国漁業信用基金協会連合会 | 全国農業信用基金協会連合会 |
| National reinsurer | JAFFIC 漁業信用保険業務 | JAFFIC 農業信用保険業務 |
| Supervisor | 水産庁 / MAFF | MAFF |
| Lender partners | JF / 信漁連 / Norinchukin / 銀行 / JFC ag-forestry-fishery arm | JA / 信連 / Norinchukin / 銀行 / JFC ag-forestry-fishery arm |
| Geographic scope | Coastal-prefecture concentrated | Nationwide |
| Asset characteristic | Vessels (depreciating, specialized) + aquaculture (environment-exposed) | Land + farm equipment + livestock |
| Crisis vectors | Resource collapse / red tide / typhoon / oil-spill / export-market shock (ALPS-water incident) | Weather / disease / TPP-FTA / food-price |
| Typical scale | Smaller individual loan sizes; concentrated portfolio | Broader borrower base; more diverse loan-size distribution |

Both systems share the same JAFFIC reinsurance parent but operate **separate insurance accounts** with different actuarial assumptions and different prefectural-network coverage.

## 9. Boundary cases

- **Not JF Marine Bank** — JF Marine Bank is a lender route; this page is the guarantee-insurance mechanism layered on top.
- **Not agriculture** — agriculture guarantee-insurance belongs to [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system]] (sister sector, same JAFFIC parent).
- **Not ordinary SME guarantees** — ordinary SME guarantees belong to [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]].
- **Not 森林 (forestry)** — forestry credit support is also under JAFFIC but a separate account.
- **Not JFC fisheries lending** — JFC's [[financial-regulators/jfc|agriculture-forestry-fisheries arm]] lends directly; this page is about guarantee insurance for fisheries borrowing.
