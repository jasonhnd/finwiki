# Fisheries credit guarantee system in Japan


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance index]]. Read it against [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system|Japan credit guarantee system]] for peer / contrast context and [[policy-finance/japan-policy-finance-system|Japan policy finance system]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Japan's fisheries credit guarantee system supplements small and medium fisheries operators' credit through fishery credit fund associations, with guarantee insurance and related support from the Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations. It is adjacent to [[banking/jf-marine-bank-system-japan|JF Marine Bank system]], but it is a public-credit guarantee-insurance route rather than the JF credit-business system itself.

## 1. System boundary

| Layer | Role | FinWiki route |
|---|---|---|
| Fisheries borrower | Small / medium fisheries operator, fisheries worker, fisheries corporation, seafood processor, or eligible cooperative / organization. | System route here |
| Lending institution | Norinchukin, JF / Shingyoren, banks, shinkin banks, credit cooperatives, and other eligible lenders. | [[banking/jf-marine-bank-system-japan]] / bank pages as relevant |
| Fishery credit fund association | Guarantees eligible borrower obligations. | System route here; local association pages only if strategic |
| National fisheries association route | Nationwide support / branch route for fisheries credit guarantee. | This page |
| Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations | Provides guarantee insurance and related support to fishery credit fund associations. | System route here |

## 2. Operating logic

- Fishery credit fund associations guarantee debt incurred by eligible fisheries borrowers.
- The Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations insures those guarantees and supports association guarantee capacity.
- When a borrower defaults, the association performs subrogation and the national credit-fund layer pays insurance under the applicable coverage.
- Covered lending institutions include cooperative financial institutions and ordinary financial institutions, depending on the scheme.

## 3. Why it matters

- It closes the fisheries public-credit lane after [[banking/jf-marine-bank-system-japan|JF Marine Bank system]].
- It prevents fishery guarantee-insurance from being misclassified as ordinary SME guarantees or JA Bank agriculture finance.
- It creates the future route for fisheries modernization loans, disaster credit support, and fisheries-policy finance.

## 4. Boundary cases

- **Not JF Marine Bank**: JF Marine Bank may be a lending route, but this page is the guarantee-insurance mechanism.
- **Not agriculture**: agriculture guarantee-insurance belongs to [[policy-finance/agriculture-credit-guarantee-system]].
- **Not ordinary SME guarantees**: ordinary SME guarantees belong to [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]].
