# Japan Federation of Credit Guarantee Corporations

## TL;DR

The Japan Federation of Credit Guarantee Corporations is the national federation for Japan's 51 local credit guarantee corporations. It is not itself the whole guarantee system; it is the coordinating, research, training, support, and guarantee-business support body behind [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]].

## 1. Institutional boundary

| Item | Reading |
|---|---|
| Japanese name | 一般社団法人全国信用保証協会連合会 |
| Common English | Japan Federation of Credit Guarantee Corporations |
| Members | 51 local credit guarantee corporations |
| Legal / operating lane | Public-credit support body for SME finance |
| FinWiki treatment | Federation page under [[policy-finance/INDEX]], not a bank / lender page |

## 2. Role map

| Role | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Member support | Supports the 51 local credit guarantee corporations. |
| Research / training / publications | Maintains guarantee-business knowledge and SME finance research. |
| Financial support to local associations | Supports local associations' financial base through loans, contributions, and guarantee-fee subsidies. |
| Responsibility-sharing administration | Handles calculation and receipt / payment of financial-institution burdens under the responsibility-sharing system. |
| Coordination | Coordinates among guarantee corporations, [[JapanFG/jfc|JFC]], financial institutions, and government agencies. |

## 3. Why it matters

- It is the national node for a system that otherwise looks like 51 local entities.
- It explains why the credit guarantee system can be treated as a national public-credit infrastructure while remaining locally operated.
- It is the clean routing page for national-level SME guarantee policy, while [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]] remains the mechanism page.

## 4. Boundary cases

- **Not JFC**: JFC is connected through credit insurance, but the federation is a separate general incorporated association.
- **Not a local credit guarantee corporation**: local corporations perform borrower-facing guarantees; the federation supports and coordinates them.
- **Not agriculture / fisheries guarantees**: agriculture and fisheries have separate sector-specific systems.
