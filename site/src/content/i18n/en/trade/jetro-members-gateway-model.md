# JETRO Members paid-membership system is the entry gate to B2B proposals


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[trade/jetro-organization-structure|JETRO は会員組織ではなく経産省傘下の独立行政法人]] and should be read with [[INDEX|FinWiki index]].

> [!info] TL;DR
> The statement that JETRO "is not a membership organization" is legally correct, but in practice joining JETRO Members (paid) is the entry condition to the B2B proposal channel. When export finance or overseas project lending is involved, the standard approach is to incorporate [[financial-regulators/jbic|JBIC]]・[[policy-finance/nexi|NEXI]]・[[policy-finance/japan-project-finance-stack-diagram|プロジェクトファイナンスのスタック]] from the outset in addition to JETRO-mediated sales-channel support.

## Scope of use after joining

Available after joining:
- Posting information in the member newsletter
- Sales opportunities and introductions at member networking events held roughly 2  times a year

Confirmed via METI's Trade Policy Bureau. If you respond with an intention to join, they will introduce a JETRO contact desk. For resource-development-related projects, in addition to JETRO's market information, [[policy-finance/jogmec|JOGMEC]] becomes the lead for equity investment and loan-investment.

The existing wiki [[trade/jetro-organization-structure]] description that it "has no member companies" is accurate as a legal fact, but it should be supplemented with the point that, as a practical proposal channel, Members enrollment is a prerequisite. Note that export-credit terms are governed by the OECD framework ([[policy-finance/oecd-export-credit-arrangement|OECD Export Credit Arrangement]]), and JETRO's sales-channel support is combined with NEXI insurance and JBIC lending within that framework.
