# Japan Research Grant map (FY 2026 年)

> A list of the main grants that [[non-profit/japan-general-foundation-establishment-requirements|一般財団法人]] / research institutions / public-interest research projects can target. Because grant amounts vary by fiscal year, this is recorded as a snapshot of "FY 2026 年."

## Important notes

### 1. Amounts vary by fiscal year

The case of the Suntory Foundation for the Arts:
- **FY 2025 年**: 300 万円 per 1 件
- **FY 2026 年**: 50-200 万円 per 1 件

→ When writing amounts in a proposal, **always refer to that fiscal year's official solicitation guidelines**. Copying old figures lowers the proposal's credibility.

### 2. When there is no stated "cap per 1 件"

As with the Mitsubishi Foundation Social Welfare Activities and Research Grant, when the guidelines do not specify it, calculate from **the track record of the list of accepted grants**:
- The 56 th round (FY 2024 年) list-of-accepted PDF
- The most recent 3  years (2023-2025) track record

### 3. Application periods concentrate from 12  to the following 4 

Research-grant solicitations generally concentrate from 12  of the previous year to 4  of the current year, so **completing the proposal by 11  of the previous year** is the practical schedule. Scholarships and support systems for students and early-career researchers are organized separately by [[policy-finance/japan-student-services-organization|JASSO]], and combining them with research grants can secure the living foundation of the research representative.

| When the research representative is early-career | Toyota Foundation | When the 45 歳-or-younger requirement is met |
