# Japan Research Grant Map (FY2026 年)

> [!info] TL;DR
> A consolidated list of the major grants that [[non-profit/japan-general-foundation-establishment-requirements|一般財団法人]] / research institutions / public-interest research projects can target. Grant amounts vary by fiscal year, so this is recorded as a snapshot of "FY2026 年."

|---------|-----------|---------|------|------|

## Important Notes

### 1. Amounts vary by fiscal year

The case of the Suntory Foundation for the Arts:
- **FY2025 年**: 300 万円 per 1 件
- **FY2026 年**: 50-200 万円 per 1 件

→ When writing amounts in a proposal, **always refer to that fiscal year's official call for applications**. Copying old figures verbatim lowers the proposal's credibility.

### 2. When there is no "cap per 1 件" stated

As with the Mitsubishi Foundation Social Welfare Activities and Research Grant, when the guidelines give no explicit figure, calculate from **the track record of the list of adopted projects**:
- Round 56  (FY2024 年) adopted-project list PDF
- Most recent 3  years (2023-2025) track record

### 3. Application periods cluster from 12  to the following 4 

Calls for research grants generally cluster from 12  of the prior year to 4  of the current year, so **completing the proposal by 11  of the prior year** is the practical arrangement. Scholarships / support schemes for students and young researchers are organized separately in [[policy-finance/japan-student-services-organization|JASSO]], and combining them with research grants can secure the living foundation of the research representative.

## Application Plan

| Activity | Primary candidate grant | Reason |
|------|-------------|------|
| Data-statistics activity | Health and Labour Sciences Research Grant | Directly tied to health-and-medical policy |
| Policy-advocacy activity | Mitsubishi Foundation Social Welfare | Investigative research + policy advocacy |
| Public-education activity | Suntory Foundation for the Arts / Telecommunications Advancement | Humanities/social-science research type |
| When the research representative is young | Toyota Foundation | When meeting the requirement of 45 歳 or younger |

When applying for grants on themes in the finance / policy-finance field, using [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance INDEX]] and [[finance/INDEX|finance INDEX]] to inventory primary materials, and referring to [[business/INDEX|business INDEX]] for cross-industry operator mapping, raises the resolution of problem-setting and prior-art organization.
