## TL;DR

Osaka Shinkin Bank, also known as Daishin, is one of the major credit cooperatives headquartered in Osaka City, with Osaka City and Osaka Prefecture as its main operating base. Its core businesses are lending to Osaka SMEs, merchants, and sole proprietors, mortgage loans, deposits, and consultation on inheritance and business succession.

## 2. Operating model

Osaka Shinkin Bank is a cooperative financial institution serving SMEs and small businesses in urban Osaka. Its core products are SME loans, working-capital and capex loans, mortgages, and retail deposits. It operates a broad branch network in Osaka Prefecture and emphasizes locally rooted credit assessment, management consultation, and business matching. Within Osaka Prefecture, it represents an urban shinkin model similar to major metropolitan shinkin such as [[JapanFG/kawasaki-shinkin|川崎信用金庫]].
