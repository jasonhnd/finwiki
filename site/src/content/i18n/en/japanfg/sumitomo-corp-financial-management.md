## 4. Control and policy points

- **Supervision**: finance bureau perimeter for money lending and FX-related business.
- **Tax / control issues**:
  - Transfer-pricing treatment of intra-group financial transactions.
  - Boundary between group factoring and installment-sales / money-lending rules.
  - Consolidated-accounting treatment of internal transactions.
- **Immediate policy issues**:
  - 2024 and [[JapanFG/boj-monetary-policy|日銀政策金利正常化]]: repricing of group interest-rate grants.
  - AML/CFT strengthening for overseas remittance / cross-border funding movement.
