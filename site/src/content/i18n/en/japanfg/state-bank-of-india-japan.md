## Overview

State Bank of India Japan is recorded as India-Japan wholesale banking infrastructure: corporate lending, trade finance, project / infrastructure finance adjacency, deposits, remittance, FX, and foreign-bank agency activity where publicly disclosed. It is unrelated to Japan's [[JapanFG/sbi-hd|SBI Holdings]] except for the confusing shared abbreviation "SBI."

Registration is observed in the checked FSA source as of 2026-05-13; this page does not independently opine on current licensing beyond the cited public source.
