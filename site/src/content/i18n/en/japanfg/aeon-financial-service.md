## TL;DR

Aeon Financial Service is [[retail/aeon-group|AEON Group]]'s financial service platform. Treated as the parent page of [[JapanFG/aeon-bank|イオン銀行]], AEON Card, and retail finance, which connects insurance, electronic money, and member store services.

## 2. Business role

- Turns AEON's store, mall, and app distribution into financial accounts and payment usage.
- Links retail loyalty, card issuing, bank deposits, and merchant payment services.
- Useful for comparing retail-originated finance with [[retail/seven-and-i-hd|Seven & i]], PayPay, and Rakuten.

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

AEON Bank alone does not explain AEON's financial-services footprint. AFS is the group-level operating platform that connects card, bank, and retail payment rails.
