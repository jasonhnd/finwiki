## TL;DR

General Incorporated Foundation Nippon Kaiji Kyokai (commonly ClassNK) is one of the world's largest **classification societies that inspect and register ships**. Founded in 1899 年, or Meiji 32 , it is one of the 1  full members among the 11 社 IACS (International Association of Classification Societies) members. It is **not an insurance company but an inspection and certification body**. Because marine insurance, P&I cover, and ship finance usually cannot proceed without the class certificate issued by ClassNK, the page is placed in JapanFG as **adjacent infrastructure** for marine insurance and ship finance.

## 2. Business lines

- **Classification registration** — rule-compliance surveys for the design, construction, and operation of merchant ships, tankers, LNG carriers, container ships, bulkers, and similar vessels.
- **Statutory survey delegation** — IMO treaty surveys such as SOLAS and MARPOL under delegation from flag-state governments, including Japan and other delegating countries.
- **ISM / ISPS / MLC certification** — management-system certification for ship safety management, security, and maritime labor conventions.
- **Licensing / industrial certification** — marine industry standards and software certification.
