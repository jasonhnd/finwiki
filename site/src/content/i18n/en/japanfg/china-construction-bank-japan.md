## 2. Operating model

CCB Tokyo states that the Tokyo representative office was established in 1993 and promoted to branch status in 2003. The Japanese branch page describes deposits, lending, trade finance, FX, funds settlement, domestic and overseas remittances, yen settlement participation, and personal banking services.

The branch also has Osaka secondary-branch context, which makes CCB part of the broader Chinese bank footprint in Japan rather than a single-office liaison presence.
