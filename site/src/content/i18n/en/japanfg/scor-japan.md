## TL;DR

This is the Japan branch of SCOR SE, a major European reinsurer listed in Paris. It is one of Europe's 4  major reinsurers, alongside Munich Re / Swiss Re / Hannover Re / SCOR, and a global reinsurance player with French origins. It appears on the FSA non-life insurance company license list under foreign non-life insurers and covers the Japanese market across both P&C and Life & Health segments.

## 2. Business Lines in Japan

- **P&C Treaty Reinsurance** — treaty reinsurance for earthquake, typhoon catastrophe, property, and liability risks
- **Specialty Insurance Reinsurance** — Marine, Aviation, Energy, Credit & Surety, Engineering, Cyber
- **L&H Reinsurance** — life reinsurance for mortality, medical, nursing-care, and longevity risks
- **Alternative Solutions** — capital-market-linked structures such as Insurance-Linked Securities and sidecars
