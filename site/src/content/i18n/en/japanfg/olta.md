## TL;DR

OLTA is an independent fintech leading **online-complete cloud factoring**. By immediately purchasing SMEs' **uncollected trade receivables** and turning them into cash, it provides a third working-capital channel alongside bank lending and credit-sales finance. In a BtoB context, it belongs to the "BNPL-like cash-flow acceleration" category and competes with NP Kakebarai from [[JapanFG/net-protections-hd|Net Protections HD]], Biz Forward from [[JapanFG/money-forward|Money Forward]], Yup, and bank-affiliated factoring services.

## 2. Business role

- Purchases trade receivables held by SMEs through online application and same-day review, providing cash conversion. Review is shorter than bank lending and uses different credit-information standards. ^[extracted]
- Responds to SMEs' **cash-flow acceleration** needs that are hard to capture through bank and regional-bank credit lines.
