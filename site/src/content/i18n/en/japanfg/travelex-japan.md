# Travelex Japan

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/wise-payments-japan|Wise Payments Japan]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Travelex Japan K.K. is a foreign-exchange retail network and money-transfer operator. In the FSA registry it appears as Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director 第00001号, and it provides points of contact for airport and urban foreign-currency exchange as well as international money transfer.

## 2. Business role

- Provides a branch / physical-network model for FX and remittance-adjacent services.
- Distinct from app-first fintechs because airport and retail storefront distribution matter.
- Useful comparator against [[JapanFG/western-union-japan|Western Union]] and [[JapanFG/sbi-remit|SBI Remit]], as well as digital-first peers like [[JapanFG/wise-payments-japan|Wise]].
