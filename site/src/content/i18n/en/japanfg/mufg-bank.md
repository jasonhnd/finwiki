## TL;DR

MUFG Bank is the core commercial-bank operating company of [[JapanFG/mufg|MUFG]]. In the FSA bank-license list, it is listed as "MUFG Bank, Ltd.", and it has a bank-license layer separate from [[JapanFG/mitsubishi-ufj-trust-bank|三菱UFJ信託銀行]], the securities holding company, and card / consumer-finance subsidiaries.

## 2. Business role

- Core operating company responsible for MUFG Group's deposits, lending, payments, foreign exchange, and overseas commercial-banking network.
- Many businesses attach to the standalone bank, including Asia commercial-bank investments, corporate payments, foreign-bank agency operations, and trust-contract agency.
- [[JapanFG/mitsubishi-ufj-trust-bank|三菱UFJ信託銀行]] handles the trust / asset administration / real estate / pension side and is functionally separate from MUFG Bank.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

[[JapanFG/mufg|MUFG]] is the listed holding company and the unit for viewing earnings, capital policy, and the Morgan Stanley alliance. MUFG Bank is the unit for tracing the Banking Act license, deposit base, corporate payments, overseas branches, foreign-bank agency operations, and trust agency. Especially around stablecoin / Progmat / EPI, the boundary between the trust bank and commercial bank needs to be separated.
