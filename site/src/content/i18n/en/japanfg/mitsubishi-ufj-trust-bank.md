# Mitsubishi UFJ Trust and Banking (MUTB)

## TL;DR

Mitsubishi UFJ Trust and Banking is [[JapanFG/mufg|MUFG]]'s trust-bank operating company. It is a banking entity separate from the commercial bank [[JapanFG/mufg-bank|三菱UFJ銀行]], and handles trust property, pensions, real estate, securities agency services, and entrusted-asset domains.

## 2. Business role

- MUFG's trust platform handling trust property, pensions, asset management, real estate, and securities agency services.
- Serves as the reference point when looking at relationships with asset-administration institutions such as [[JapanFG/master-trust-bank|日本マスタートラスト信託銀行]].
- [[JapanFG/mufg-bank|三菱UFJ銀行]] handles deposits / lending, foreign exchange, and the commercial banking network, while MUTB handles trust, fiduciary, real estate, and pension functions.
