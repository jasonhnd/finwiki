# Hyakujushi Bank

## TL;DR

Hyakujushi Bank is an independent regional bank based in Kagawa Prefecture. It traces its origin to The 114th National Bank, founded in Takamatsu on December 23, 1878, and remains a standalone listed bank rather than a bank holding-company group.
