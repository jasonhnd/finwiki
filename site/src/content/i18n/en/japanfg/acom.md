### Overpayment issue and MUFG TOB (2006-2008）★

- **Supreme Judgment (2006-01-13)**: Virtually negates the “deemed payment” provisions of the Money Lending Business Act → Interest Restriction Act (upper limit)15-20%) Excess amount must be returned as unjust enrichment.
- **2006 Amended Money Lending Business Act**: Upper interest rate limit29.2%→20%**total amount regulation** (annual income1/3)introduction
- **Refund of overpayments**: The industry as a whole faces trillions of yen, and Acom is also under pressure due to huge reserves.
- **2008-10-22MUFG TOB**: Bank of Mitsubishi UFJ (then Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi UFJ)40%Capital injection and bank affiliated reorganization were realized through acquisition and conversion into an equity method company.^[extracted]
