## TL;DR

This is the Japan branch of Swiss Reinsurance Company Ltd. / Schweizerische Rückversicherungs-Gesellschaft AG, a SIX-listed company. Alongside Munich Re, it is one of the **world's 2  major reinsurance companies**. It is listed in the FSA insurance-company license list under foreign non-life insurers and operates both Property & Casualty Reinsurance and Life & Health Reinsurance segments in the Japanese market.

## 2. Business lines in Japan

- **P&C Reinsurance** — non-life reinsurance for earthquake / typhoon catastrophe, property, liability, and related risks
- **Life & Health Reinsurance** — life reinsurance for mortality, medical, longevity, disability, and nursing-care risks
- **Corporate Solutions** — specialty insurance written directly for large enterprises, such as D&O, cyber, marine, and aviation; this is a direct / large corporate line rather than reinsurance
- **Public Sector Solutions** — parametric insurance for governments and municipalities, including earthquake and weather-triggered products
- **Reinsurance solutions** — Insurance-Linked Securities (CAT bond) arrangement and risk-transfer solutions
