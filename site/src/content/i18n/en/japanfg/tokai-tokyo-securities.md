# Tokai Tokyo Securities

## TL;DR

Tokai Tokyo Securities Co., Ltd. is a **Nagoya-headquartered mid-tier independent securities company** and the core wholly owned subsidiary of TSE PRIME-listed [[JapanFG/tokai-tokyo-fh|Tokai Tokyo Financial Holdings (8616)]]. The current structure was formed in 2008-10 through the business spin-off of the former Tokai Tokyo Securities, a Tokai Bank-affiliated firm, and its origin traces back to Maruman Securities, founded in 1929. It is a **Type I financial instruments business operator registered with the Tokai Local Finance Bureau Director (Kinsho) No. 140**. The head office is in Midland Square, Meieki, Nakamura-ku, Nagoya; capital is JPY 6.0 billion and employees total 1,869. As a **face-to-face retail-centered mid-tier independent securities firm based in the Tokai region of Aichi, Gifu, Mie, and Shizuoka**, it develops regional-bank alliance models, IPO underwriting, and consulting-style sales. Its characteristic model is regional joint securities companies and joint branch brands with regional banks under the "Tokai Tokyo XX Securities" pattern. Competitors include [[JapanFG/iwai-cosmo-securities|IwaiCosmo Securities]], [[JapanFG/okasan-sg|Okasan Securities Group]], [[JapanFG/aizawa-sg|Aizawa Securities Group]], and [[JapanFG/matsui-sec|Matsui Securities]].

## 1. Legal entity / shareholders

- **Company name**: Tokai Tokyo Securities Co., Ltd.
- **Established**: 2008-10-08, spun off when the group shifted to the holding-company structure at [[JapanFG/tokai-tokyo-fh|Tokai Tokyo FH]].
- **Founding origin**: Maruman Securities, founded in 1929.
- **Head office**: Midland Square, 4-7-1 Meieki, Nakamura-ku, Nagoya 450-6212.
- **Capital**: JPY 6.0 billion.
- **Parent company**: [[JapanFG/tokai-tokyo-fh|Tokai Tokyo Financial Holdings]], TSE PRIME 8616, headquartered in Nagoya, with 100% ownership.
- **Representative**: President and representative director.
- **Employees**: 1,869 on an HD consolidated basis as of 2024.

## 2. License / registration

- **Type I financial instruments business**: Tokai Local Finance Bureau Director (Kinsho) No. 140.
- **Industry associations**:
  - Japan Securities Dealers Association (JSDA), regular member
  - Financial Futures Association of Japan
  - Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association
- **Trading rights**: participant in [[securities/tokyo-stock-exchange|TSE]] and [[securities/osaka-exchange|OSE]], clearing participant in [[securities/japan-securities-clearing-corp|JSCC]].

## 3. Sales base

- **Face-to-face sales**: head office plus more than 130  branches / sales offices nationwide.
- **Regional-bank alliance model**: has regional securities companies established as JVs with the following regional banks:
  - "**Hokuhoku TT Securities**" (JV with [[JapanFG/hokuhoku-fg|Hokuhoku FG]], in the [[JapanFG/hokuriku-bank|Hokuriku Bank]] / [[JapanFG/hokkaido-bank|Hokkaido Bank]] lineage)
  - "**Tokai Tokyo & City Securities**" (JV with [[JapanFG/oita-bank|Oita Bank]] / FFG lineage)
  - "**Hamagin TT Securities**" (JV with [[JapanFG/yokohama-bank|Bank of Yokohama]] / [[JapanFG/concordia-fg|Concordia FG]] lineage)
  - "**Nishi-Nippon City TT Securities**" (JV with [[JapanFG/nishi-nippon-city-bank|Nishi-Nippon City Bank]])
  - Other multiple regional-bank alliances
- **Online trading services**: "Anshin Sogo Service" and "Tokai Tokyo Net Fish"
- **Call centers**: multiple locations

## 4. Products handled

- **Domestic equities**: cash and margin trading; 3  major-market line as a TSE / OSE / Nagoya Stock Exchange (NSE) trading participant
- **Foreign equities**: U.S., European, and Asian equities
- **Investment trusts**: in-house management + third-party funds
- **Bonds**: JGBs, municipal bonds, corporate bonds, and foreign bonds
- **NISA**: growth investment quota and tsumitate investment quota
- **iDeCo**: individual defined-contribution pension plan
- **Derivatives**: futures, options, and FX
- **IPO underwriting**: lead manager / underwriter for mid-tier company IPOs centered on the Tokai region
- **M&A advisory**: business succession / sale / acquisition consultation for mid-tier companies

## 5. Industry position

- As a **Nagoya / Tokai-region-based mid-tier independent securities company**, it occupies a distinctive position by developing a "JV securities" model with megabank-FG securities firms and regional banks.
- A hybrid face-to-face + regional-bank alliance strategy sometimes called "**the Nomura of the Tokai region**."
- Competitors: [[JapanFG/iwai-cosmo-securities|IwaiCosmo Securities]] in Kansai, [[JapanFG/okasan-sg|Okasan Securities]] as a Nagoya peer, [[JapanFG/aizawa-sg|Aizawa Securities]], [[JapanFG/matsui-sec|Matsui Securities]], and [[JapanFG/marusan-securities|Marusan Securities]].
- **Strengths**: deep relationships with mid-tier / large companies in the Tokai region, expansion of retail customers through regional-bank channels, and early-mover status in the regional-bank securities JV model.
- **Special business**: differentiated from [[securities/japan-online-brokerage-competition|online brokerage competition]] through a face-to-face and regional-bank alliance model.
- **Strategy**: deepen regional-bank alliances and develop overseas securities business in Southeast Asia, especially Vietnam / Indonesia.
