## TL;DR

**IBJ Leasing** was established in 1969 年 as a leasing company affiliated with the former Industrial Bank of Japan (IBJ), and has been active as a representative leasing company of the Mizuho/former IBJ affiliated companies for many years. ** 2019-04-01 changed its name to ``Mizuho Lease'' → Effectively became [[JapanFG/mizuho-lease|Mizuho Leasing / みずほリース]], and ``Ikobank Lease'' as an independent business entity ended. ** Therefore, this page is positioned as **Historical Entity + Naming Integration Note**. Please refer to [[JapanFG/mizuho-lease|みずほリース]] for current operations/financial status.

## 2. Merger history & business lines (old)

The former IBJ Lease was a semi-major leasing company within the Mizuho group with strengths in **real estate, aircraft, and large-scale project finance**, and coexisted with [[JapanFG/fuyo-lease|芙蓉総合リース]], a member of the Fuyo Group, with some overlap in business. Inheriting the DNA of the former IBJ's **Large Corporate/Project Finance** system, we demonstrate our strengths in large-scale projects such as real estate, ships, aircraft, and overseas project projects.

Main former business areas:

- General equipment lease/industrial equipment lease
- Real estate lease/finance (large properties)
- Aircraft/ship operating lease/JOL
- Overseas (mainly in Asia) bases
- Environment/Renewable Energy PJ Finance

### 2019 - 04  Background of Mizuho Lease Integration

- The leasing functions within Mizuho FG have an overlapping structure with IBJ Lease + Fuyo General Lease, etc., and there is pressure to reorganize within the group.
- The company name change from IBJ Lease to Mizuho Lease (2019-04-01) is part of Mizuho FG's restructuring of non-bank functions.
- In 2024 年, Marubeni's strategic shareholder participation in [[JapanFG/mizuho-lease|みずほリース]] itself was announced, evolving into a hybrid platform of Mizuho-affiliated + trading company-affiliated companies.

## 3. Strategic significance of this transition

The merger of the former IBJ Lease was the result of Mizuho FG's decision that there was no merit in maintaining multiple leasing subsidiaries. On the other hand, [[JapanFG/fuyo-lease|芙蓉総合リース]], which is affiliated with the Fuyo Group, continues to exist as an independent listed company, and the lease integration within the Mizuho Group has not been completed.

この経緯は [[JapanFG/mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]] の non-bank 戦略 (旧 IBJ・旧 富士・旧 第一勧業 の 3 つの遺伝子の融合プロセス) を理解する重要な事例。

In the context of [[finance/japan-acquisition-finance|日本アクイジションファイナンス]], the large-scale project know-how of the former IBJ Lease has been inherited by Mizuho Lease, and is currently being utilized in Mizuho-affiliated LBO/M&A financing.
