# Higashi-Nippon Bank

## TL;DR

Higashi-Nippon Bank is a Tokyo-headquartered second regional bank listed in the FSA bank-license roster. It is an operating bank under [[concordia-fg|Yokohama Financial Group]], formerly Concordia Financial Group. Even after the 2016 business integration with Bank of Yokohama, it remains the separate legal bank-license surface for Tokyo and Greater Tokyo SME banking.

## 2. Operating model

Higashi-Nippon Bank focuses on SME lending, relationship banking, deposits, and local-business support in Tokyo and the broader metropolitan area. Bank of Yokohama is the Kanagawa retail and public-sector core; Higashi-Nippon Bank carries the group's urban SME specialization. For the wider Kanto consolidation pattern, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern]].
