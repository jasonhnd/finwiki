# Saxo Bank Securities


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/gmo-click-securities|GMOクリック証券]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Saxo Bank Securities is a Japan FIEA operator whose parent company is Denmark's Saxo Bank A/S. In the FSA financial instruments business operators list, it appears as Saxo Bank Securities Ltd., Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第239号. It is the Japan access point for foreign stocks, FX, CFDs, and derivatives-related products.

## 2. Business role

- Provides multi-market and multi-product access as the Japan entity of a global online trading platform.
- Compared with domestic online brokers, it has strong cross-product coverage of foreign equities, options, CFD / FX. The main domestic retail FX competitors are [[JapanFG/gmo-click-securities|GMOクリック証券]] and [[JapanFG/dmm-com-securities|DMM.com 証券]], with [[JapanFG/sbi-fx-trade|SBI FXトレード]] as a standard side-by-side benchmark.
- As a foreign-affiliated FIEA operator, it is treated separately from foreign bank branches.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

Saxo Bank Securities is less a standard domestic online securities company than the Japan-regulated gateway of a global trading platform. It is made a standalone page to review product range, trading infrastructure, and foreign-affiliated broker regulation.
