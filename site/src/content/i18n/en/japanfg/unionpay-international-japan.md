# UnionPay International Japan

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. ## TL;DR

UnionPay International Japan is the Japan base of UnionPay International (UPI), the international expansion subsidiary of China's China UnionPay. It develops the UnionPay brand in Japan, mainly as an acceptance-side merchant network for inbound Chinese visitors. Its domestic issuer function, meaning issuing UnionPay cards for Japanese residents, is limited; the main axis is the acquirer side, namely merchant acquisition and acceptance expansion. Its history with [[JapanFG/jcb|JCB]] is a 2006 年 mutual acceptance partnership: JCB cards became usable at UnionPay merchants in mainland China, while UnionPay cards became usable at JCB merchants in Japan.

## 2. Business lines in Japan

- **Inbound merchant acceptance expansion**: expands merchant acceptance for UnionPay cardholders issued in mainland China, especially Chinese tourists visiting Japan. Priority areas include department stores, electronics retailers, tourist destinations, hotels, and drugstores.
- **Domestic issuer support**: provides UnionPay brand-card licensing to some domestic issuers, though issuance scale is limited.
- **QR payments (UnionPay QR / UnionPay QuickPass)**: develops domestic acceptance of QR / NFC payment methods for inbound Chinese demand. This is a separate channel from the widely used mainland China Alipay / WeChat Pay rails.
- **ATM cash withdrawal support**: connects UnionPay cards to domestic ATM withdrawal services, including major ATM networks such as Seven Bank ATMs.
- **Cross-border corporate settlement**: payment and remittance-adjacent services for China-Japan business settlement.

Domestic issuance of UnionPay cards for Japanese residents is limited, so the brand's main function is concentrated in the one-way role of accepting Chinese cardholders in Japan.
