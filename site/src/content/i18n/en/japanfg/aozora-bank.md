## TL;DR

A medium-sized independent bank in the pedigree of the former **Nippon Credit Bank (NCB)**. **12 -2000A representative example of the history of the recovery of Japanese banks that experienced bankruptcy → special public management (temporary nationalization)**. 09 -2001 Softbank + Orix + Sold to Tokio Marine Union → 01-2003  Renamed “Aozora Bank” → 2006 U.S. PE **Cerberus (Cerberus Capital Management)** Became a major shareholder → 11 -2023 Relisted on the First Section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange → Completed phased sale of Cerberus. The biggest issue recently is **2024 -2024 Huge losses on US CRE (commercial real estate) loans** → 05 -^[extracted] full-year deficit announcement → Downward revision of business results and management strategy restructuring phase. QZX014QZX is a "medium-sized independent bank" model that does not belong to either the mega or regional bank series.
