## TL;DR

Mizuho Bank is the core commercial-bank operating company of [[JapanFG/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]]. On the FSA bank-license list it appears as "Mizuho Bank, Ltd.", a licensed bank with a bank-license boundary separate from the parent company, the trust bank, the securities company, and the asset-management company.

## 2. Business role

- The main deposit, lending, settlement, and corporate-finance engine of the Mizuho group, with lineage from Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Fuji Bank, and the Industrial Bank of Japan.
- The Mizuho FG page covers consolidated holding-company strategy; this page covers the standalone bank license, branch / overseas network, deposits and loans, and settlement functions.
- Read it together with [[JapanFG/mizuho-trust-bank|みずほ信託銀行]], [[JapanFG/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]], and [[JapanFG/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]] to understand the role split among banking, trust banking, securities, and asset management.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

[[JapanFG/mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]] is the right unit for listed-company, G-SIB, and group-strategy analysis, but business-improvement orders, branch networks, deposits and loans, FX, and corporate-payment functions are analyzed at the bank operating-company level.
