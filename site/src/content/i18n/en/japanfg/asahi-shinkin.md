## TL;DR

Asahi Shinkin Bank is a major urban credit union with its head office located in Taito Ward, Tokyo, and its main operations are in central Tokyo (Taito, Chuo, Chiyoda, Sumida, etc.) and parts of Saitama and Chiba prefectures. A cooperative financial institution that primarily provides loans to small and medium-sized businesses, wholesalers, shops, and individual business owners.

## 2. Operating model

Asahi Shinkin Bank's core business is providing loans to small and medium-sized wholesalers, retailers, and sole proprietorships in central Tokyo's wholesale districts (Asakusabashi, Kuramae, Nihonbashi, etc.), as well as small and medium-sized manufacturing and service businesses in Taito, Chuo, Chiyoda, and Sumida wards. In Tokyo's shinkin landscape[[JapanFG/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]]It is one of the major urban Shinkin Banks, and ranks among the top Shinkin Banks nationwide in terms of deposit size.
