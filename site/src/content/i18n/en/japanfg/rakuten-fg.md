# Rakuten Group Financial Business (Rakuten FG)

## TL;DR

This is the financial-technology segment of Rakuten Group, Inc. (TSE PRIME 4755). Its **EC-centered independent model** uses Rakuten Ichiba + Rakuten Points as the core and bundles banking / securities / cards / insurance / payments into the "Rakuten ecosystem" through a cross-sell strategy. Unlike megabank groups ([[mufg]] / [[smfg]] / [[mizuho-fg]]) or telecom financial groups ([[ndfg]] / au-FH / [[paypay-fg]]), its business structure is distinctive: **commerce origin -> finance**. 2023-04  Rakuten Bank IPO; 2024  Rakuten Securities accepted 49% investment from Mizuho Securities. **2024  fiscal-year FinTech segment Non-GAAP operating profit was 1,534 億円 (+37.9%), and group IFRS operating profit turned positive (530 億円)**. Rakuten Mobile losses (Non-GAAP operating loss 2,163 億円) still exceed FinTech profit, but are improving. Founder Hiroshi Mikitani (1965-) has been chairman and president since the 1997  founding.

### "Rakuten ecosystem" model

- **Rakuten Points = OS**: points earned / used across EC purchases, telecom, cards, banking, securities, insurance, and travel
- **SPU (Super Point Up Program)**: raises the point multiplier for Rakuten Ichiba shopping in stages through "Rakuten Mobile contract / Rakuten Card use / salary receipt at Rakuten Bank / Rakuten Securities investment-trust accumulation" and similar triggers -> powerful driver of financial-product cross-sell
- **Unified member ID**: all services under 1 Rakuten ID -> advantage in CRM data integration

### Mobile losses x financial profit support structure

- From 2019-10, Rakuten Mobile entered the MNO market -> base-station investment made **large multi-year losses** normal
- FY 2024 : Mobile Non-GAAP operating loss 2,163 億円 vs FinTech Non-GAAP operating profit 1,534 億円 -> **FinTech still cannot directly offset Mobile losses**, but the improvement amounts (Mobile +850 億, FinTech +37.9%) helped FY 2024  IFRS operating profit turn positive (530 億円)
- Stable earnings in the FinTech segment, especially Rakuten Bank / Rakuten Card, support consolidated group EBITDA
- 2022-2024  period: Rakuten Securities IPO plan -> accepted 49% investment from Mizuho Securities, reorganizing into **fundraising + IPO withdrawal**
- 2023-04  Rakuten Bank IPO executed -> group liquidity + realization of listed book value

### Capital and business alliance with Mizuho FG

- 2023-10  Mizuho Securities 19.99% stake -> raised in stages in 2024  to **49%**
- Rakuten Securities IPO was effectively withdrawn
- For Mizuho, this means access to a retail / online customer base; for Rakuten, it means group financing and deeper linkage with a megabank-affiliated securities firm

## 4. Regulation / policy

- **Primary supervisor**: Financial Services Agency (FSA)
- **Business-line regulations**:
  - Rakuten Bank: Banking Act (internet bank)
  - Rakuten Securities: Financial Instruments and Exchange Act (Type I financial instruments business)
  - Rakuten Card: Installment Sales Act + Money Lending Business Act
  - Rakuten Life / Rakuten General Insurance: Insurance Business Act (life and non-life respectively)
  - Rakuten Wallet: Payment Services Act (crypto-asset exchange service provider registration = JVCEA member)
  - Rakuten Pay / Rakuten Edy: Payment Services Act (prepaid / funds transfer business)
- **Holding-company regulation**: Rakuten Group itself is an operating holding company, not a pure holding company. Each business-format subsidiary is individually regulated under its industry law.
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - Rakuten Mobile frequency allocation / capital expenditure -> Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications jurisdiction, with indirect effects on the financial segment
  - From 2024, new NISA system -> growth in Rakuten Securities accounts
  - Stablecoin / crypto-asset regulatory revisions -> spillover to Rakuten Wallet
