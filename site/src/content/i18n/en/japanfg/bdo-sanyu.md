## 1. Institutional Profile

- **Legal form**: audit corporation with unlimited-liability form; public registrations identify the entity as Sanyu Audit Corporation, and the name does not include the statutory “limited liability” designation required for limited-liability audit corporations.^[extracted]
- **Network membership**: BDO International, headquartered in Brussels.^[extracted]
- **Group entities**: BDO Sanyu Tax Corporation and BDO Advisory Co., Ltd.^[extracted]

### Scale

Publicly confirmable attributes from the firm profile, JICPA audit-firm materials, and J-LiC auditor registration include:

- **Established**: 1986-10-21.^[extracted]
- **CPAAOB category**: mid-tier / semi-major audit corporation.^[extracted]
- **Main client segments**: mid-sized listed issuers, smaller listed issuers, IPO candidates, foreign-affiliated Japanese subsidiaries, Web3 startups, and VASPs, based on public IR and industry reporting.^[extracted]
- **Offices**: Tokyo headquarters in Shinjuku, plus Osaka, Nagoya, Fukuoka, and Sapporo.^[extracted]
- **Personnel**: 382 total, including 49 partners and 158 full-time CPAs as of 2025-10-01.^[extracted]
- **Listed clients**: 82 Financial Instruments and Exchange Act audit clients out of 227 audited companies as of 2025-06-30.^[extracted]
