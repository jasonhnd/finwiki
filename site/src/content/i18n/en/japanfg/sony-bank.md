## TL;DR

Sony Bank is an internet-bank operating company under [[JapanFG/sony-fg|Sony FG]]. On the FSA bank license list it appears as "Sony Bank Incorporated" and operates as a retail asset-formation bank handling yen deposits, foreign-currency deposits, investment trusts, housing loans, Sony Bank WALLET, and related services.

## 2. Business Role

- Combines foreign currency, investment trusts, housing loans, and debit cards as an internet-based retail asset-formation bank.
- Has customer-base adjacency with the life and non-life insurance businesses of [[JapanFG/sony-fg|Sony FG]].
- Unlike Rakuten Bank, PayPay Bank, au Jibun Bank, and SBI Sumishin Net Bank, it is backed by the financial brand power of Sony Group / Sony FG.
