# Mercari HD (Mercari, Inc.)

## Wiki Route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/monex-group|マネックスグループ (Monex Group)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

One of Japan's largest **C2C flea-market app + payments + crypto-asset** vertically integrated ecosystems. Founded by Shintaro Yamada in 2013-02-01, TSE Mothers IPO in 2018-06-19 (moved to 2022  PRIME, TSE PRIME **4385**). Its 4  axes are **Mercari (flea-market marketplace) + Merpay (payments / deferred payment) + Mercoin (Bitcoin) + Mercard (JCB partnership)**. Domestic MAU **2,304 万** (FY2025.6 4Q), Marketplace GMV **1.12 兆円** (FY2025.6  full year), more than **300 万 Mercard cards issued**, and more than **200 万 Mercoin accounts**. In FY2025.6  full year, revenue was **1,926 億円** (YoY +3%), net income was **261 億円** (YoY +94%, 3  consecutive record-profit periods), and the **U.S. business achieved its first full-year profitability** (US GMV of 728 百万 USD ≒ 1,091 億円). It does not hold a banking license (funds-transfer service provider). Unlike megabank-line [[mufg]] / [[smfg]], its distinctive feature is becoming a financial platform starting from a marketplace.

### Characteristics of Service Linkage

- **Mercari sales proceeds -> Merpay balance -> Mercoin purchases / Mercard settlement**: vertical integration in which EC, payments, crypto assets, and credit are completed in a single wallet
- **Merpay Smart Payments**: credit model using Mercari sales proceeds as a "quasi-income proof"
- **Mercard**: JCB brand, but screening / credit are based on Merpay's own standards
- **Fintech receivables balance**: about 2,000 億円 scale (as of FY2025.6 4Q presentation materials) -- accumulated credit from deferred payments + Mercard

### Vertical Integration Strategy

- **Marketplace + payments + credit + crypto assets**: becoming a financial platform completed in a single app
- **Mercoin**: unlike Rakuten Wallet / SBI VC Trade / bitFlyer, its strength is the "flea-market sales proceeds -> crypto assets" pathway
- **Mercard**: through the JCB partnership, supports both **physical / virtual** cards and can immediately apply Mercari sales proceeds

### Overseas Strategy

- **U.S. business (Mercari US) achieved first full-year profitability in FY2025.6 **: GMV 728 百万 USD, segment profit 737 百万円
- Shifted to a stage aiming to entrench profitability alongside restructuring and cost-reduction measures
- Avoids direct competition with global EC players (Amazon / eBay), shifting toward domestic deepening + sustaining U.S. profitability

### Banking License (Current Status)

- Current status: **no in-house bank**, with Merpay operating as a funds-transfer service provider (Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General 第 00058 号) + issuer of prepaid payment instruments
- Strategic speculation on future banking-license acquisition scenarios, BaaS partnerships vs in-house acquisition, is separated into `.opinions/JapanFG/mercari-hd.md` (not confirmed in public materials)

## 4. Regulation / Policy

- **Supervisors**:
  - Mercari parent: Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (Specified Commercial Transactions Act)
  - Merpay: **funds-transfer service provider** (Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General 第 00058 号, Payment Services Act) + issuer of prepaid payment instruments
  - Mercoin: **crypto-asset exchange operator** (Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General 第 00029 号, Payment Services Act)
- **JVCEA (Japan Virtual and Cryptoassets Exchange Association) membership**: Mercoin is a regular member
- **JADA (Japan Payment Service Association) membership**: Merpay
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - 2023 onward crypto-asset staking regulation development -> monitor impact on Mercoin
  - 2024 onward BNPL (deferred payment) regulatory debate -> impact on Merpay Smart Payments
  - Personal Information Protection Act / Secondhand Articles Dealer Act -> Mercari parent listing management
