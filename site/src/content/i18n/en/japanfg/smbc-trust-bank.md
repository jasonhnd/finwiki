## TL;DR

SMBC Trust Bank is a 100% subsidiary of [[JapanFG/sumitomo-mitsui-banking-corp|三井住友銀行]] and the trust-bank operating company of [[JapanFG/smfg|SMBC グループ]]. It handles the PRESTIA brand and functions close to foreign-currency banking, real estate, trusts, and private banking.

## 2. Business role

- The bank descends from a former foreign-affiliated trust bank and became a subsidiary of Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation in 2013 年, then changed its name to SMBC Trust Bank.
- In 2015 年 it integrated Citibank Japan's retail banking business and, through the PRESTIA brand, has strength in foreign-currency services, affluent retail, and cross-border personal finance.
- It combines banking, trust services, real-estate related ancillary business, and registered financial institution business.
