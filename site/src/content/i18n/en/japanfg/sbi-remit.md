# SBI Remit

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/wise-payments-japan|Wise Payments Japan]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

SBI Remit is the international remittance operator of [[JapanFG/sbi-hd|SBI group]]. Official company information shows its Type II funds-transfer service provider registration number, and it is important through connections with foreign workers, regional financial institutions, and bank agency business.

## 2. Business Role

- Provides Japan-origin international remittance with migrant-worker and resident-foreigner use cases.
- Expands through bank and regional-financial-institution partnerships.
- Important for tracking bank-fintech remittance cooperation after ISO 20022 and AML/CFT pressure.

## 3. Why This Standalone Page Matters

SBI's bank, securities, crypto, and regional-bank pages do not explain the remittance operator. SBI Remit is its own licensed funds-transfer entity.
