# UCS Card (UCS)


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/life-card|ライフカード (Life Card)]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

UCS is a retail finance operator handling credit cards, majica electronic money, insurance agency services, and related businesses. The official company profile explicitly lists credit-card business, electronic-money business, and insurance-agency business, making UCS an entry point for reading the PPIH / UNY / Don Quijote payment and loyalty boundary.
