# JA Mutual Insurance Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives (National Mutual Insurance Federation of Agricultural Cooperatives)

## Wiki route

## TL;DR

**JA グループ 4 大全国連の共済事業中核 = 生保 + 損保 両域の協同組合「共済」事業者**。1951 設立、農業協同組合法を根拠とする **連合会**。**保有契約高は生命系で約 200 兆円規模、自動車・建物等の損害系も大規模に展開**、組合員 (農家・地域住民) を対象とした生命・年金・自動車・建物・傷害等の共済を一括で提供する**国内屈指の保険事業組織**。**保険業法上の株式会社「保険会社」ではなく、農協法に基づく「共済」**として規制 (異なる法域)。**[[insurance/japan-life-insurance-big-four|生命保険ビッグ 4]] (日本生命 / 第一生命 / 明治安田 / 住友生命) に比肩する規模**を持ちながら、株式公開もしない協同組織型の独自セグメント。[[JapanFG/kyoritsu-fire-marine|共栄火災海上保険]] の **74.20% 株主**として損保会社の親会社でもある (協同組合外の損保事業を共栄火災経由で展開)。^[extracted]

### Mutual aid vs insurance channel

- **Sales channel**: National JA 497  union + prefectural union + Zenkyoren direct management
- Mainly **face-to-face/door-to-door sales** to member households—similar to the “sales lady” type in the life insurance industry
- **Policy amount in force (total of comprehensive life insurance and building rehabilitation mutual aid) is 216 兆 9,652 億円** (as of the end of 令和 5 年) — A scale comparable to the big 4  in the life insurance industry

### Parent company functions for non-life insurance supplements

- **[[JapanFG/kyoritsu-fire-marine|共栄火災海上保険]] の 74.20% 株主** ^[extracted]
- Kyoei Fire is a non-life insurance company compliant with the Insurance Business Act** = Non-life insurance sales to non-association members + Transactions with general corporations possible
- Kyoei Fire handles areas that cannot be handled by JA Kyosairen's own mutual aid (high-value cases such as marine insurance and liability)
- **Hybrid model between cooperative and non-life insurance company**

### One of the largest insurance company positions in Japan

- **Total asset size**: 58 兆 4,751 億円 (令和 5 年 end of fiscal year, year-on-year change 101.4%) — Scale comparable to [[JapanFG/nippon-life|日本生命]] (more than 90 兆円)
- However, because mutual aid is based on membership under the Agricultural Cooperative Act, it is not normally included in the size ranking based on the Insurance Business Act (regulatory reports are different).

### product competition

- **Building Rehabilitation Mutual Aid (Construction Rehabilitation)**: **Integrated product** of fire + earthquake + death protection = Unique strength of mutual aid, unprecedented in the private insurance industry
- **Agricultural cooperative brand/community-based approach** is directly linked to premium (insurance premium) competitiveness

### digital strategy

- **JA Mutual Aid App** to check contract, insurance claims, and estimated interest rate ^[extracted]

### competitive structure

- **Life insurance big 4**: [[JapanFG/nippon-life|日本生命]] / [[JapanFG/dai-ichi-life|第一生命]] / [[JapanFG/meiji-yasuda|明治安田生命]] / [[JapanFG/sumitomo-life|住友生命]] — Competing with the same type of life products
- **3  Large Sompo Insurance**: [[JapanFG/tokio-marine|東京海上]] / [[JapanFG/msad|MS&AD]] / [[JapanFG/sompo|SOMPO]] — Competitors in automobiles, buildings, etc.
- **Other cooperatives**: [[JapanFG/zenrosai|全労済 / こくみん共済 coop]], prefectural mutual aid — partial competition with mutual aid based on membership
- **Inclusion of customers within JA** is the fundamental differentiation from other financial institutions (close contact with members + JA Bank account linkage)

## 4. Supervision/Regulation

- **Supervisor**: **Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries** (supervision of cooperatives) + **Financial Services Agency** (cooperative supervision of financial soundness and consumer protection)
- **Legal basis**: Agricultural Cooperative Act (mutual aid business provisions) + mutual aid business guidelines
- **Current policy issues**:
  - **Mutual aid = insurance regulatory gap** Discussion (competitive fairness of insurance business law vs. mutual aid law)
  - Application theory of similar regulations to mutual aid of **economic value-based solvency (ESR)**
  - **Consumer protection in digital sales channels**
  - Member protection functions in **Food Security Plan (2026~)**
