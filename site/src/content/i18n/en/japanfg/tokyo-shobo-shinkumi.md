# Tokyo Fire Department Credit Cooperative

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]] as the **Tokyo Fire Department employee occupation-based shinkumi anchor**, completing the Tokyo emergency/civil-service shinkumi trio with [[JapanFG/keishicho-shinkumi|警視庁職員信用組合]], the police peer, and [[JapanFG/tokyo-to-shokuin-shinkumi|東京都職員信用組合]], the metropolitan civil-servant peer, within the 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]].

## TL;DR

Tokyo Fire Department Credit Cooperative is an **occupation-based credit cooperative for Tokyo Fire Department employees, including firefighters, office staff, and retirees**, with its head office in Otemachi, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, inside the Tokyo Fire Department headquarters. It is FSA registry No. 49  under the Kanto Local Finance Bureau and corporate number 2010005002121. It is a small occupation-based shinkumi whose membership is limited to **Tokyo Fire Department employees plus their spouses and relatives**, and its main businesses are **living-expense loans, housing loans, and retirement-benefit investment services** for members. It is a member of [[JapanFG/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] and covered by [[JapanFG/dic|預金保険機構]]. It is a niche representative of occupation-based shinkumi, operating on a relatively small member base of about 1.8 万 Tokyo Fire Department personnel, including firefighters and administrative staff. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

Because it is an occupation-based shinkumi, membership eligibility is strictly **limited to Tokyo Fire Department personnel**:

1. **Basic requirement**: employees belonging to the Tokyo Fire Department, including firefighters, ambulance personnel, office staff, and technical staff
2. **Retirees**: OB / OG who retired from the Tokyo Fire Department, with continued membership generally possible within certain conditions
3. **Spouses / relatives**: spouses and same-household family members of members, subject to the articles of association
4. **Nonmember use**: strictly limited
