## TL;DR

**興銀リース (IBJ Leasing)** was established in 1969 年 as a leasing company in the old Industrial Bank of Japan (IBJ) lineage, and for many years operated as a representative leasing company of the Mizuho / former IBJ lineage. **In 2019-04-01 it was renamed "Mizuho Leasing" → effectively becoming [[leasing-firms/mizuho-lease|Mizuho Leasing / みずほリース]], and "興銀リース" as an independent corporate entity came to an end.** This page is therefore positioned as a **historical entity + naming-consolidation note**. For current business / financial status, refer to [[leasing-firms/mizuho-lease|みずほリース]].

## 2. Merger history & business lines (former)

The old IBJ Leasing coexisted within the Mizuho lineage as a quasi-major leasing company with strengths in **real estate, aircraft, and large-scale PJ finance**, partially overlapping in business scope with the Fuyo Group's [[leasing-firms/fuyo-lease|芙蓉総合リース]]. Inheriting the **large-corporate / project-finance** DNA of the old IBJ, it demonstrated strengths in large deals such as real estate, ships, aircraft, and overseas PJ.

Main former business areas:

- General equipment leasing / industrial equipment leasing
- Real estate leasing / finance (large properties)
- Operating leases / JOL of aircraft and ships
- Overseas (mainly Asia) bases
- Environmental / renewable-energy PJ finance

### 2019-04 background of the Mizuho Leasing consolidation

- Leasing functions within Mizuho FG had an overlapping structure across IBJ Leasing + Fuyo General Lease, etc., and intra-group reorganization pressure existed
- The IBJ Leasing → Mizuho Leasing name change (2019-04-01) was part of Mizuho FG's reorganization of non-bank functions
- In 2024 年, Marubeni's strategic shareholder participation in [[leasing-firms/mizuho-lease|みずほリース]] itself was announced, evolving it into a Mizuho + trading-company hybrid platform

## 3. Strategic significance of this transition

The consolidation of the old IBJ Leasing was the result of Mizuho FG concluding that "there is no merit in holding multiple leasing subsidiaries." On the other hand, the Fuyo Group's [[leasing-firms/fuyo-lease|芙蓉総合リース]] continues to exist in parallel as an independent listed company, and leasing consolidation within the Mizuho lineage has not been carried out completely.

This sequence is an important case for understanding the non-bank strategy of [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]] (the fusion process of the 3  genes of the old IBJ, old Fuji, and old Dai-Ichi Kangyo).

In the context of [[finance/japan-acquisition-finance|日本アクイジションファイナンス]], the large-scale PJ know-how of the old IBJ Leasing has been inherited by Mizuho Leasing and is still used today in Mizuho's LBO / M&A finance.
