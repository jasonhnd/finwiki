# Tokyo Century Corporation

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[leasing-firms/INDEX|leasing-firms INDEX]]. Read it against [[leasing-firms/orix-corp|オリックス株式会社 (ORIX Corporation)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Part of Japan's 3  general leasing and finance company ([[leasing-firms/orix-corp]] / [[megabanks/mufg]] Mitsubishi HC Capital / Tokyo Century). Unusual capital structure: **Mizuho FG ([[megabanks/mizuho-fg]]) + Itochu Corporation's 2  axis major shareholder**. Diversified from automobile, IT, and industrial equipment leasing to real estate, **aircraft finance**, overseas (Americas and Asia), environment/renewable energy, concessions, and rental cars (formerly Nippon Rent-A-Car Service). TSE PRIME **8439**. 2019 年 Entered **Aircraft Leasing World Top 10** after becoming a wholly owned subsidiary of US Aviation Capital Group (ACG).

## 1. Company Profile

**Official name**: Tokyo Century Co., Ltd.
**English name**: Tokyo Century Corporation
**Stock code**: TSE PRIME **8439**
**Business type**: Comprehensive lease/finance (3  Large Lease)
**Major shareholders (total approximately 50% class)**:
- **Mizuho FG ([[megabanks/mizuho-fg]])** (former Dai-ichi Kangyo Bank lineage)
- **ITOCHU Corporation**

2  axis shareholder structure is unique among Japan's major general leasing companies. Mizuho is providing financial support, while Itochu is providing its trading company network (automobiles, industrial equipment, and overseas projects), supporting both lease funding and project supply.

### 2  two predecessors

Tokyo Century was born from the merger of two leasing companies **2 **.

| Old company | Establishment | Genealogy |
|---|---|---|
| **Tokyo Lease** | 1969 | Former Daiichi Bank / Japan Long-Term Credit Bank |
| **Century Leasing System** | 1969 | Former Dai-ichi Kangyo Bank / Industrial Bank of Japan (IBJ) |

Both companies were founded as 1969 年, and each started up as a banking company during the boom of the leasing industry during Japan's period of high economic growth.

### Chronology (excerpt)

| Year | Event |
|---|---|
| 1969  | Tokyo Lease established (Daiichi Bank/Long Bank Bank) |
| 1969  | Century Leasing System established (Daiichi Kangin Bank / IBJ affiliate) |
| 2009 | **Tokyo Lease + Century Leasing → Tokyo Century Lease Merger** (In the process of Mizuho FG management integration, duplicate lease 2 社 within Mizuho FG was integrated) |
| 2016  | Trade name change: Tokyo Century Lease → **Tokyo Century** (Declaration of intention to become a financial complex that goes beyond the leasing business) |
| 2019 | ** Became a wholly owned subsidiary of US Aviation Capital Group (ACG)** → Aircraft leasing world Top 10 |
| 2024 | Medium-term management plan update |

The integration of 2009 年 was carried out in the context of **reorganization** of multiple affiliated leasing companies within Mizuho FG (formerly DKB Daiichi Kangin + IBJ Kogin + Fuji Bank). By merging Century Leasing System, which was affiliated with the former Daiichi Kangin Bank, with Tokyo Lease, which was affiliated with the former Daiichi Bank/Long Bank Bank, the leasing functions within the Mizuho Group were consolidated. The removal of "lease" from 2016 年 is a move that follows Orix's efforts to become a "comprehensive financial company."

## 4. Regulation/Policy

- **Supervising**: Financial Services Agency/Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (leasing industry)
- **Accounting**: Due to compliance with IFRS 16 (new lease accounting standard), lease accounting methods have changed in Japan and overseas, and although the impact is minor on the lender side, it does affect the decision-making of lessee companies.
- **Holding company regulations**: Not subject to the Banking Act (leasing companies are not banking businesses)
- **Overseas regulations**: ACG complies with US law and international aviation law (Cape Town Convention, etc.) for aircraft leasing.

### Policy issues

- 2024 ~ Bank of Japan policy rate normalization → upward pressure on borrowing costs vs. risk of delay in passing on lease fees
- 2024 ~ EV/decarbonization → Fleet EV/renewable energy PPA Demand growth is a tailwind
- 2025 ~ US interest rate environment → ACG Impact on aircraft lease yields, dollar-denominated financing cost fluctuations
