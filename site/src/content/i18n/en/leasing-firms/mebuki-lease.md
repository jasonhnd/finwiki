## TL;DR

Mebuki Lease Co., Ltd. is a regional-bank leasing company and 100% subsidiary of [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg|Mebuki FG]] (TSE Prime 7167). It originated from Joyo Lease, established by [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|Joyo Bank]] in 1974-09, and was launched when Mebuki FG acquired all shares in 2017-04-03 and renamed it Mebuki Lease (confirmed by official history and corporate registration). On the same date, the leasing business operated by [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|Ashikaga Bank]] subsidiary Ashikaga Credit Guarantee was consolidated into Mebuki Lease through business succession, and the Utsunomiya and Ashikaga sales departments were opened to expand on the Tochigi side (confirmed by Nikkei 2017-03-07 and official history). **As a legal entity, this was a standalone renaming of Joyo Lease, not an equal merger of "Joyo Lease + Ashikaga Lease" ("Ashikaga Lease" did not exist as an independent company, and the Ashikaga-side leasing business was operated by Ashikaga Credit Guarantee).** The company provides equipment leasing, finance leases, operating leases, vendor leases, and related services to mid-sized main-bank customers in Ibaraki, Tochigi, and the Tokyo metropolitan area. It is one of the stronger regional-bank leasing companies. ^[extracted]

### Former Joyo Lease Lineage (Base Entity)

- **1974-09**: [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|Joyo Bank]] established Joyo Lease Co., Ltd. as a complementary business for its lease and finance operations, confirmed by official history
- As a [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|Joyo Bank]] affiliated leasing company, its main business was equipment leasing for mid-sized companies in Ibaraki Prefecture
- Typical postwar regional-bank leasing model: supporting SME capital investment through equipment leases rather than bank loans
- **2017-04-03**: Mebuki FG acquired all shares and renamed the company "Mebuki Lease Co., Ltd." (confirmed by corporate registration)

### Relationship with Ashikaga Bank Leasing Functions - Confirmed (2026-05-30 Verification)

- **There was no independent company named "Ashikaga Lease."** Historically, the [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|足利銀行]] affiliated leasing company was "Kitakanto Lease Co., Ltd." (FSA 2003-03-11 business reconstruction plan certification, capital 145 百万円), and later **Ashikaga Credit Guarantee Co., Ltd. operated the leasing business through seconded bank employees** (Nikkei 2017-03-07). The former framing of an "equal merger of Joyo Lease + Ashikaga Lease" was wrong on both the company name and the integration form.
- **Succession of the Ashikaga-side leasing business did occur.** Effective 2017-04-03, **the leasing business of Ashikaga Credit Guarantee was consolidated into Joyo Lease, which was renamed Mebuki Lease on the same date** (confirmed by Nikkei 2017-03-07 and Mebuki Lease official history). Mebuki Lease opened Utsunomiya and Ashikaga sales departments and expanded into Tochigi, with Ashikaga Bank continuing to second staff. In other words, "succession to Ashikaga Credit Guarantee's leasing business" is confirmed, but the successor and form were business consolidation into Joyo Lease / Mebuki Lease, not an equal merger with Ashikaga Lease.
- Ashikaga Credit Guarantee was later wholly acquired by Mebuki FG in 2020-10-01 and renamed Mebuki Credit Guarantee Co., Ltd. It is now a credit-guarantee specialist, because the leasing business had already been transferred to Mebuki Lease in 2017-04  (Nikkei 2020-07 and group company information).
- [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|Ashikaga Bank]] went through the 2003-11 temporary nationalization phase and was integrated into Mebuki FG in 2016-10. The 2017-04 leasing consolidation above was part of the post-integration reorganization of group functions.

### Mebuki FG Establishment and Group Integration

- **2016-10-01**: [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg|Mebuki FG]] established through a joint share transfer by [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|Joyo Bank]] + [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|Ashikaga Bank]]
- **2017-04-03**: Mebuki FG acquired all shares of Joyo Lease and renamed it Mebuki Lease, reorganizing it as a common group leasing function for customer bases at bank subsidiaries 2 行

### Position Inside the FG

- **Core of bank-leasing cooperation**: Receives leasing needs from mid-sized main-bank customers of the two [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg|Mebuki FG]] banks
- **Capital-investment support**: Supplements bank lending and responds to off-balance-sheet treatment needs
- **Municipal and public-sector cooperation inside the prefectures**: Lease deals for municipalities in Ibaraki and Tochigi

### Competitive Structure

- **Major lessors**: [[leasing-firms/mitsubishi-hc-capital|Mitsubishi HC Capital]] / [[leasing-firms/smfl|SMFL]] / [[leasing-firms/mizuho-lease|Mizuho Leasing]] / [[leasing-firms/fuyo-lease|Fuyo Lease]] / [[leasing-firms/tokyo-century|Tokyo Century]] / [[leasing-firms/ricoh-leasing|Ricoh Leasing]] - advantage in large, national, and complex transactions
- **Regional-bank lessors**: [[leasing-firms/hokkaido-lease|Hokkaido Lease]] / Chiba Kogyo Lease / Gunma Lease and similar companies - same model
- **Manufacturer-affiliated lessors**: [[card-issuers/toyota-finance|Toyota Finance]] / [[leasing-firms/nec-leasing|NEC Capital Solutions]] - product-linked
- **Local competitors**: Lessors affiliated with [[regional-banks/tsukuba-bank|Tsukuba Bank]] and [[regional-banks/tochigi-bank|Tochigi Bank]]

### Strengths and Weaknesses

- Strengths: [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|Joyo Bank]] / [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|Ashikaga Bank]] customer base, namely main-bank relationships with mid-sized companies in Ibaraki and Tochigi, plus local on-site response capability

## 7. Regulation and Policy

- **Primary authority**: Financial Services Agency (FSA); leasing as a standalone business does not require a banking license, while money-lending registration depends on the business area
- **Regulatory issues**:
  - Support for lease-accounting standard changes, including IFRS16  and Japanese GAAP
  - Use of SME lease tax incentives, including the SME investment promotion tax system
  - Environmental leases and decarbonization tax treatment for renewable-energy equipment leasing
  - Anti-social-force screening and AML controls carried out jointly by the [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg]] group
