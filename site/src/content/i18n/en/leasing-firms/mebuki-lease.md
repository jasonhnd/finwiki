## TL;DR

Mebuki Lease Co., Ltd. is a regional-bank-affiliated leasing company that is a 100% subsidiary of [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg|めぶき FG]] (TSE PRIME 7167). It **was established with "Joyo Lease" — set up by [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|常陽銀行]] in 1974-09  — as its parent body, and was launched when, in 2017-04-03, Mebuki FG acquired all its shares and renamed it "Mebuki Lease"** (confirmed by the official corporate history / corporate registration). On the same date, the leasing business that had been handled by "Ashikaga Credit Guarantee," a subsidiary of [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|足利銀行]], was consolidated into Mebuki Lease (business succession), and the Utsunomiya / Ashikaga sales departments were opened to expand to the Tochigi side (confirmed by Nikkei 2017-03-07 and the official corporate history). **As a legal entity this is a sole renaming of Joyo Lease, not an "equal merger of Joyo Lease + Ashikaga Lease" (there is no independent company called "Ashikaga Lease"; the leasing business on the Ashikaga side was operated by Ashikaga Credit Guarantee).** It provides equipment leasing, finance leasing, operating leasing, vendor leasing, etc., to mid-sized main-bank customers in Ibaraki, Tochigi, and the metropolitan area. One of the top tier of regional-bank-affiliated lessors. ^[extracted]

### Former Joyo Lease lineage (parent body)

- **1974-09**: [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|常陽銀行]] established "Joyo Lease Co., Ltd." as a complementary business to its leasing / finance division (confirmed by the official corporate history)
- As a [[regional-banks/joyo-bank|常陽銀行]]-affiliated leasing company, its main business was equipment leasing for mid-sized companies within Ibaraki Prefecture
- A typical postwar regional-bank-affiliated lessor: supporting SME capital investment in the form of equipment leasing rather than bank lending
- **2017-04-03**: Mebuki FG acquired all its shares and renamed it "Mebuki Lease Co., Ltd." (confirmed by corporate registration)

### Relationship with the Ashikaga Bank-affiliated leasing function — confirmed (2026-05-30 verification)

- **There is no independent company called "Ashikaga Lease."** The [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|足利銀行]]-affiliated leasing company was historically "Kita-Kanto Lease Co., Ltd." (FSA 2003-03-11 business-restructuring plan certification, capital of 145 百万円), and in later years **"Ashikaga Credit Guarantee Co., Ltd." operated the leasing business in the form of seconded bank staff** (Nikkei 2017-03-07). The old version's framing of "an equal merger of Joyo Lease + Ashikaga Lease" is wrong on both the company name and the form of integration.
- **The succession of the leasing business on the Ashikaga side did actually take place.** On 2017-04-03, **the leasing business of Ashikaga Credit Guarantee was consolidated (business succession) into Joyo Lease (renamed "Mebuki Lease" on the same date)** (confirmed by Nikkei 2017-03-07 and the Mebuki Lease official corporate history). Accordingly, Mebuki Lease opened the Utsunomiya / Ashikaga sales departments and expanded to the Tochigi side, with Ashikaga Bank continuing to second staff. That is, the statement that it "succeeded the leasing business of Ashikaga Credit Guarantee" **can be confirmed** (but the successor / form is not an "equal merger with Ashikaga Lease," rather a business consolidation into Joyo Lease = Mebuki Lease).
- Ashikaga Credit Guarantee subsequently had all its shares acquired by Mebuki FG in 2020-10-01 and was renamed "Mebuki Credit Guarantee Co., Ltd."; it is now a credit-guarantee specialist (the leasing business had already been transferred to Mebuki Lease in 2017-04 ) (Nikkei 2020-07 / group-company information).
- [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank|足利銀行]] went through a temporary nationalization phase in 2003-11  and was then integrated into Mebuki FG in 2016-10 ; the above 2017-04  lease consolidation is part of the group-function reorganization after that integration.

### Mebuki FG establishment and group integration

- **2016-10-01**: [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg|めぶき FG]] established (joint share transfer of [[regional-banks/joyo-bank]] + [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank]])
- **2017-04-03**: Mebuki FG acquired all shares of Joyo Lease and renamed it "Mebuki Lease" — reorganized as a group-common leasing function for the customer base of bank subsidiaries 2 行

### Position within the FG

- **Core of bank-lease coordination**: undertakes the leasing demand of mid-sized main-bank customers of [[regional-banks/mebuki-fg]] bank 2 行
- **Capital-investment support**: complements bank lending + responds to off-balance-sheet processing needs
- **Coordination with in-prefecture local governments / public sector**: leasing deals for municipalities in Ibaraki / Tochigi prefectures

### Competitive landscape

- **Major lessors**: [[leasing-firms/mitsubishi-hc-capital|三菱 HC キャピタル]] / [[leasing-firms/smfl|SMFL]] / [[leasing-firms/mizuho-lease|みずほリース]] / [[leasing-firms/fuyo-lease|芙蓉リース]] / [[leasing-firms/tokyo-century|東京センチュリー]] / [[leasing-firms/ricoh-leasing|リコーリース]] - advantage in large / nationwide / complex deals
- **Regional-bank-affiliated lessors**: [[leasing-firms/hokkaido-lease|北海道リース]] / Chiba Kogyo Lease / Gunma Lease, etc. - same-type model
- **Manufacturer-affiliated lessors**: [[card-issuers/toyota-finance|トヨタファイナンス]] / [[leasing-firms/nec-leasing|NEC キャピタルソリューション]] - product-linked
- **In-prefecture competitors**: [[regional-banks/tsukuba-bank|筑波銀行]]-affiliated / [[regional-banks/tochigi-bank|栃木銀行]]-affiliated leasing

### Strengths and weaknesses

- Strength: [[regional-banks/joyo-bank]] / [[regional-banks/ashikaga-bank]] customer base (mid-sized main-bank customers in Ibaraki / Tochigi) + region-rooted on-site responsiveness
