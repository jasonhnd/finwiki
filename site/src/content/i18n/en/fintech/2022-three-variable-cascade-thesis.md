> [!info] TL;DR
> The 2025-2026  Stablecoin explosion / the three-tier split of Tokenized Money / geopolitical multipolarization / the AI Agent economy — these "independent currents" are all **a cascade reaction set off by 3  variables triggered simultaneously in 2022 年**. Once you understand this structure, you can predict the next event with the same structure.

## The three variables

| Variable | Trigger month | Energy released |
|---|---|---|
| **Normalization of US Treasury rates** (0 → 4-5%) | 2022 H2  | Provided an economic model for the privatization of the Stablecoin settlement medium |
| **Weaponization of the US dollar** (freezing of $300B+ of Russian reserves) | 2022 Q1  | Conferred political legitimacy on the multipolarization of central-banking functions |
| **The generative-AI breakthrough** (public release of ChatGPT) | 2022 Q4  | Pushed "the machine economy / Agent protocols" into a credible theme |

**The 3  variables were triggered in the same year and mutually amplified one another.**

## The causal chain

```
2022 rate normalization →  the economics of Stablecoins such as Circle / [[fintech/tether-business-model-short-treasury-yield|Tether]] become viable for the first time
                → 2025-06 the listing window for the Circle IPO opens
                → 2025-2026 the three-tier split of SC/TD/MMF accelerates

2022 sanctions on Russia → the global de-dollarization debate becomes politically normalized
              → 2022-2026 [[fintech/mbridge-bis-multi-cbdc-overview|mBridge]] / BIS Agorá / e-CNY / Project Nexus all accelerate
              → the 5 -pole structure of geopolitical multipolarization takes shape

2022 ChatGPT  → the Agent economy moves from sci-fi to a credible theme

              → AWS Bedrock AgentCore + Visa TAP + FIDO migration become standardized
```

**3  chains intersect**:
- AI Agents change the decision-making subject → Programmable Money (the 3 layer) executes → geopolitical multipolarization reallocates the rules
- rates + sanctions + AI = the 3 axes of currency / regulation / decision-making are rebuilt in sync

## Why 2022 年

| Historical comparison | Event of comparable scale |
|---|---|
| 1971 US-dollar suspension of gold convertibility | A single event, with impact confined to the #1 unit of account |
| 1989 fall of the Berlin Wall | A political event; it affected geopolitics but did not directly spill over into currency |
| 2008 global financial crisis | A monetary event; central banks reinforced rather than dismantled #2  with QE |
| **2022 three variables simultaneously** | The rare case of a multidimensional synchronous event |

**Structural factors**:
1. **The end of the zero-interest-rate era** (lasting 14  years) — released the suppressed room for financial innovation
2. **The end of the post–Cold War order** (lasting 30  years) — unlocked political multipolarization
3. **The emergence of generative AI** (~5  years of training + sudden emergence) — expanded the decision-making subject

## Application / transfer

**Predicting the next event with the same structure**:

The next wave of "multidimensional synchronous triggers" may arrive from:
- **The practical realization of quantum computing** (affecting the cryptographic foundations of the #5 execution layer)
- **A structural shift in climate policy** (carbon tariffs + carbon pricing → affecting the #1 unit of value)
- **A breakthrough in AI Agents' autonomous trading capability** (affecting the #4 identity layer + the #2 settlement layer)
- **A structural sovereign-debt crisis** (affecting the credit of #1 sovereign currencies)

**By understanding the 2022 年cascade**, you become able to identify the next event of the same paradigm.
