> [!info] TL;DR
> The stablecoin explosion of 2025-2026 , the three-tier divergence of tokenised money, geopolitical multipolarity, and the AI Agent economy — these apparently "independent trends" are all **cascade reactions triggered simultaneously by 3  variables in 2022 年**. Understanding this structure makes it possible to predict the next event with the same structure.

## Three Variables

| Variable | Trigger month | Energy released |
|---|---|---|
| **US Treasury rate normalisation** (0  → 4-5%) | 2022 H2  | Provided an economic model for the privatisation of stablecoin settlement media |
| **Weaponisation of the USD** (Russian reserves $300B+ frozen) | 2022 Q1  | Granted political legitimacy to the multipolarity of central bank functions |
| **Generative AI breakthrough** (ChatGPT public release) | 2022 Q4  | Elevated "machine economy / Agent protocols" to a credible theme |

**3  variables triggered in the same year, mutually amplifying each other.**

**3  chains intersect**:
- AI Agents change the decision-making subject → Programmable money (3  layer) executes → Geopolitical multipolarity redistributes the rules
- Rates + sanctions + AI = 3  axes of currency / regulation / decision-making synchronously restructured

## Why 2022 年 Is Significant

| Historical comparison | Event of equivalent scale |
|---|---|
| 1971  USD departure from gold convertibility | Single event; impact confined to #1  unit of account |
| 1989  Fall of the Berlin Wall | Political event; affected geopolitics but no direct monetary transmission |
| 2008  Global Financial Crisis | Monetary event; central banks reinforced rather than dismantled #2  via QE |
| **2022  three variables simultaneously** | Rare case of a multi-dimensional synchronous event |

**Structural factors**:
1. **End of the zero-interest-rate era** (lasting 14  years) — released financial-innovation headroom that had been suppressed
2. **End of the post-Cold-War order** (lasting 30  years) — unlocked political multipolarity
3. **Emergence of generative AI** (~5  years of learning + sudden emergence) — expanded the pool of decision-making agents

## Applications / Extrapolation

**Predicting the next event with the same structure**:

The next wave of "multi-dimensional synchronous triggers" may come from:
- **Practical quantum computing** (impacts cryptographic foundations of #5  execution layer)
- **Structural shift in climate policy** (carbon tariffs + carbon pricing → impacts #1  unit of value)
- **Breakthrough in AI Agent autonomous trading capability** (impacts #4  identity layer + #2  payment layer)
- **Structural sovereign debt crisis** (impacts #1  sovereign currency credibility)

**Understanding the 2022 年 cascade** enables identification of the next event within the same paradigm.
