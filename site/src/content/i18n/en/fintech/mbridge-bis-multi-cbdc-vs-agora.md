# mBridge vs Agora · BIS 2025  Two-track comparison of switching in the same year

> [!info] TL;DR
> 2025 年 was an important year when BIS made a “trajectory switch” in its CBDC cross-border payment strategy: 10  withdrawal of mBridge (BRICS+ multi-CBDC) + accelerated promotion of Agora (7  central bank + private bank tokenized correspondent banking) throughout the year. The 2  project has changed from a two-track structure of the ``Neutral Bridge'' and ``U.S. Dollar Friendship Bridge'' to a dual structure of ``China-led'' vs. ``U.S.-Europe-led''.

## Key facts

- BIS 2025-10 mBridge withdrawal · Same year Agora propulsion acceleration ^[extracted]
- mBridge Central Bank Members 5(PBoC / HKMA / BoT / CBUAE / SAMA) · All non-G7 ^[extracted]
- Agora Central Bank 7 行:Fed / BoE / BoJ / SNB / Banque de France / Bank of Korea / Banco de México ^[extracted]
- Agora is a “tokenized correspondent banking” model · Jointly verified by central bank + commercial bank ^[extracted]

## Mechanism / How it works

Although mBridge and Agora both adopt permissioned DLT + BFT consensus at the technical layer, their governance and participation structures are fundamentally different. **mBridge** has a two-layer structure of central bank nodes + commercial bank sub-participants, bypassing SWIFT + Fedwire and making payments directly at each country's CBDC. **Agora** is "tokenized correspondent banking" - central bank issues tokenized reserve - commercial bank issues tokenized deposit - joint settlement on unified ledger, in effect "on-chain of existing SWIFT/Fedwire correspondent banking", and does not overturn the US dollar system (see [[fintech/institutional-stablecoin-deposit-token-thesis|機関 SC vs deposit token 論題]] for the deposit token topic). The BIS 2025  switch means that BIS has chosen the route of an “on-chain patch” rather than an “alternative system” — forcing the USD side to embrace tokenization and leaving mBridge to non-USD autonomy.

## Origin & evolution

2021 年 mBridge startup · At the same time Agora is only an ideation inside BIS. 2023-2024 年 mBridge is in MVP and Agora is still in preparation stage. 2024-04 Agora official announcement · 7 Central bank + 41 Commercial bank participation (JPM / Citi / HSBC / [[megabanks/mufg|三菱 UFJ]] etc.). 2025-Q1-Q3 Agora first atomic settlement PoC completed · mBridge was surrounded by G7  political pressure at the same time. 2025-10 BIS announces withdrawal from mBridge operations — while continuing to promote Agora's progress. 2026-Q2 mBridge and Brazilian DREX interoperability test · Agora enters central bank expansion recruitment stage. Both projects have changed from "BIS two-track" to "technology stacks of competing 2  currency camps".
