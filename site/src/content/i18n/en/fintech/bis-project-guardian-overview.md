# MAS Project Guardian Overview

> [!info] TL;DR
> Project Guardian is a tokenised-asset industry proof-of-concept project launched by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) in 2022.05  — 25+ global financial institutions (DBS / Citi / JPM / Standard Chartered / HSBC / UBS / Franklin Templeton, etc.) are running tokenised bonds, funds, foreign exchange, and carbon credits in pilot form within an MAS-supervised sandbox. It is the Asia-led tokenisation coordination hub, forming the "Asia three-pole" tokenisation infrastructure division of labour together with Agorá and Ensemble.

## Key facts

- Launched 2022.05 ; first cohort was DBS / JPM / Marketnode ^[extracted]
- 2022.11  Pilot 1: JPM tokenised JPY / SGD foreign exchange on the Aave Arc permissioned pool ^[extracted]
- 2024.03  expanded to 25+ institutions, adding fund / FX / carbon credit domains ^[extracted]
- 2024.11  Guardian Wholesale Network activated (inter-institutional settlement layer) ^[extracted]
- 5  major pilot directions: bonds / tokenised funds / FX / trade finance / carbon credits ^[extracted]
- MAS simultaneously participates in BIS Agorá ^[extracted]
- DBS / JPM / Standard Chartered most deeply involved (covering 3+ pilots) ^[extracted]
- All pilots within MAS-supervised sandbox; no retail exposure ^[extracted]

## Mechanism / How it works

Guardian is a "tokenised-asset laboratory," led by MAS as a single regulator, so the coordination costs of a central-bank alliance do not arise. 25+ institutions run **asset-class-by-asset-class pilots** within the supervised sandbox:

1. **Bonds**: UBS / Citi tokenised bond secondary trading
2. **Tokenised funds**: Franklin Templeton / Schroders / Fidelity International
3. **Foreign exchange**: JPM / DBS / SBI cross-currency atomic settlement
4. **Trade finance**: Standard Chartered / Ant International
5. **Carbon credits**: In conjunction with Singapore's Climate Impact X (CIX)

The **Guardian Wholesale Network** (activated 2024.11 ) is an MAS-led institutional settlement layer that does not depend on sovereign CBDC, effectively providing "tokenised asset vs tokenised asset" institutional settlement infrastructure. **Single MAS regulator = faster decision-making**: compared to the 7 -central-bank coordination cost in [[fintech/bis-project-agora-overview|Agorá]], Guardian advances at a pace of 1  milestones per year. For MAS retail / institutional SC licence development, see [[fintech/singapore-mas-payment-services-act-overview|MAS PS Act 概要]] and [[exchanges/sg-mas-dpt-licensing-overview|SG MAS DPT ライセンス概要]].

## Origin & evolution

2020  MAS published its fintech sandbox framework, and in 2022  advanced 2  tracks: Project Orchid (retail CBDC research) + Project Ubin (early wholesale CBDC experiments). 2022.05  Project Guardian was launched, with a differentiated positioning focused on "tokenised assets" (division of labour vs mBridge / Ensemble's "tokenised money"). 2022-2024  pilot count grew from 3  to 5  domains, and institution count from 3 行 to 25+. 2024.11  Guardian Wholesale Network activated → upgraded from "experiment" to "de facto settlement layer." 2025  cross-connection experiment with BIS Agorá (MAS participates simultaneously); Guardian's tokenised-asset standards become settleable on Agorá's monetary infrastructure.
