## 2.Plate/registration status

- **Electronic payment agency**: Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Electric bill)92号(Registration date2021年11月）
- **Crypto asset exchange business**: **Unregistered** (DCJPY does not fall under the category of crypto assets. It is a tokenization of "deposits" based on the Banking Act, and is not an electronic payment method under the Payment Services Act)
- **JVCEA member**: **non-member**
- **Legal basis**: DCJPY is positioned as a “Tokenized Deposit” issued by banks and is operated within the framework of the Banking Act. It is a structure that maintains the characteristics of bank deposits while tokenizing them on the blockchain, and is a separate system from regulations on crypto assets and transactions such as electronic payment methods.
- **Positioning of the three types of SC classification**: Among the three types of SC (trust type / bank-issued type / funds transfer service provider type) stipulated by the revised Payment Services Act, DCJPY is classified as **bank-issued type (第 1 号It is similar to EPI (with some discussion)**, but it is characterized by being mainly operated as a deposit system under existing banking laws. For details[[fintech/jp-trust-type-sc-architecture|日本信託型 SC 架構]]reference.

## 3. How the DCJPY platform works

- **DCJPY**: Deposit token issued by participating banks. Yen-denominated,1:1 fixed.
- **Amic Sign**: Platform foundation of the DCJPY network. A two-tiered structure consisting of the “Financial Zone” (bank infrastructure) and the “Business Zone” (for companies and individuals).2022年7月Patent registration).
- **4Core elements (AMIC)**: Asset (digital asset) / Money (deposit token DCJPY) / Identity (identifier) ​​/ Contract (smart contract)
- **Participating banks (published)**: GMO Aozora Net Bank, Japan Post Bank (2026年degree issuance plan) / SBI Shinsei Bank (currently considering implementation,2025年9月Report), MUFG Bank of Mitsubishi UFJ (dispatch of directors/participation in forum), Bank of Yokohama, etc. will participate in the demonstration.
- **Digital Currency Forum**: Domestic100A venue for demonstration and discussion in which the above companies, public organizations, and related ministries and agencies participate. DeKalet DCP will be in charge of the secretariat. Use cases are being considered by each subcommittee (2026年4月progress report第5号Publication).

  2**Integration with digital securities (ST)**: ST issuance/DVP (simultaneous delivery) settlement (2026年4月Completed the first actual issuance verification in Japan by SBI Securities, IIJ, etc.)
  3**NFT/Corporate Initiatives**: Turning transaction data into NFT/SME credit information platform (demonstration stage)
  4**Commerce DX**: Intercompany bill payment automation (2026年3月With First Accounting, GMO Aozora Net Bank, and IIJ4社PoC）
