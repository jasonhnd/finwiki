## TL;DR

The Japan Ship Owners' Mutual Protection & Indemnity Association (Japan P&I Club, JPI) is **Japan's only P&I club** (Protection & Indemnity mutual marine insurance association). It is a mutual association established under the Ship Owners' Mutual Insurance Association Act (enacted in 1950 年), and covers **shipowner liability** such as personal injury, oil pollution, and wreck removal for ocean-going and coastal shipping companies. It is **one of the 13  full member clubs of the International Group of P&I Clubs (IG)** and shares large-casualty risk through the global reinsurance pooling mechanism.

## 2. Business lines

- **Shipowner liability (P&I cover)** — personal injury (crew, passengers, third parties), oil-pollution damage, cargo-damage liability, wreck removal, excess collision liability, etc.
- **FD&D (Freight, Demurrage and Defence)** — legal costs and defence related to charter parties and carriage contracts
- **Claims response services** — incident response and compensation negotiations through a correspondents network at major ports worldwide
