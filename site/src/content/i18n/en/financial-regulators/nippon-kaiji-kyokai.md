# 日本海事協会 (ClassNK)

## TL;DR

The general incorporated foundation Nippon Kaiji Kyokai (commonly ClassNK) is one of the **world's largest classification societies, conducting ship survey and registration (classification registration)**. It is a long-established body founded in 1899 年 (Meiji 32 ) and is one of the 1  full members out of the 11 社 members of IACS (International Association of Classification Societies). It is **not an insurance company but a survey / certification body**, yet because ship insurance, P&I, and financing cannot be arranged without the class certificate that ClassNK issues, it is placed in JapanFG as **adjacent infrastructure** to marine insurance and ship finance.

## 2. Business lines

- **Classification registration** — rule-conformity surveys in the design, construction, and operation of merchant ships, tankers, LNG carriers, container ships, bulk carriers, etc.
- **Statutory-survey delegation** — IMO-convention surveys such as SOLAS and MARPOL, delegated by flag-state governments (Japan + other countries that have entrusted it)
- **ISM / ISPS / MLC certification** — management-system certification for ship safety management, security, and the maritime labour convention
- **Licensing / industry certification** — Marine industry standards, software certification
