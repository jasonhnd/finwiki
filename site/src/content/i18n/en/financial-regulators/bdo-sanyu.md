## 1. Corporate overview

- **Official name**: BDO Sanyu Audit Corporation ^[extracted]
- **English name**: BDO Sanyu LLC ^[extracted]
- **Legal form**: Audit corporation (**unlimited liability** — an ordinary audit corporation that does not bear "limited liability" in its name. Both Wikipedia and the J-LiC auditor registration list the corporation's name as "Sanyu Audit Corporation." Because under the Certified Public Accountants Act a limited-liability audit corporation is obligated to include "limited liability" in its name, this corporation is of the unlimited-liability form) ^[extracted]
- **Headquarters**: Nishi-Shinjuku, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo ^[extracted]
- **Member network**: **BDO International** (headquartered in Brussels) ^[extracted]
- **Group entities**:
  - BDO Tax Corporation
  - BDO Advisory Co., Ltd. (FAS / consulting) ^[extracted]

### Scale (only items verifiable from public sources)

Objective attributes verifiable in the official corporate information (https://www.bdo.or.jp/firm/) and the JICPA audit-corporation search:

- **Established**: 1986-10-21 ^[extracted]
- **CPAAOB classification**: Quasi-large audit corporation (the classification category of the FSA Certified Public Accountants and Auditing Oversight Board) ^[extracted]
- **Main target segments** (based on official IR / industry reporting): mid-tier / small listed companies, IPO-preparation companies, Japanese subsidiaries of foreign firms, Web3  startups, VASPs ^[extracted]
- **Locations**: 5  offices (Tokyo headquarters [Shinjuku] / Osaka / Nagoya / Fukuoka / Sapporo) ^[extracted]
- **Personnel**: 382 名 (of which partners [社員] 49 名 / full-time accountants 158 名, as of 2025-10-01) ^[extracted]
- **Listed clients**: FIEA audits 82 社 (of 227 社 audited companies, as of 2025-06-30) ^[extracted]

The above figures are confirmed via Wikipedia "Sanyu Audit Corporation" (descriptions based on the JICPA-published "Overview of Audit Corporations," etc.) and the J-LiC auditor registration. The latest values should be confirmed each time in JICPA-published materials or the official BDO Sanyu transparency report. Estimated figures without third-party source confirmation are not recorded on this page.
