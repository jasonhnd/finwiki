## 2. History

| Year/Month | Event |
|---|---|
| **1968** | **Tohmatsu Awoki & Co. established** (the direct predecessor of Tohmatsu; "Tohmatsu" is short for Tomatsu-Aoki) ^[extracted] |
| 1975 | Touche Ross international affiliation ^[extracted] |
| **1989** | **Touche Ross + Deloitte Haskins & Sells global merger → formation of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu** (a Japanese-affiliated presence in that "Tohmatsu" was incorporated into the firm name) ^[extracted] |
| **2008-07** | **Conversion into a limited-liability audit corporation under the amendment of the Certified Public Accountants Act** ^[extracted] |
| 2003 | DTT's consulting division (the former Deloitte Tohmatsu Consulting) dissolved its capital relationship with Tohmatsu and left DTT → Braxton (2003-01) → became independent as **ABeam Consulting** (2003-11). The current "Deloitte Tohmatsu Consulting" is a separate lineage (the former Tohmatsu Consulting renamed in 2008-07 ) and has no direct relationship with ABeam |
| Around 2020 〜 | **Early entry into digital-asset audit / Travel Rule consulting** ^[extracted] |

### Banking / insurance / securities ^[extracted]

- Tohmatsu is involved in audits of major securities firms, asset managers, life and non-life insurers, and regional banks, but the latest auditor of any individual company should be confirmed in EDINET primary sources, and this entry does not enumerate specific client names

### Crypto-assets / digital assets ^[extracted]

- **Blockchain / crypto-asset advisory** group
- As referenced in **[[exchanges/japan-crypto-audit-firm-landscape|国内クリプト監査ランドスケープ]]**, a front-runner in the **financial-statement audit + segregated-management audit** of FSA-registered VASPs
- Involved in the formulation of the **JICPA Industry Committee Practical Guideline 第 61 号 (audit of crypto-asset-related businesses)** ^[extracted]
- Major domestic crypto-asset exchanges (public, related cases including the [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]] group)

### M&A / FAS (separated from audit) ^[extracted]

- Deloitte Tohmatsu Financial Advisory (DTFA) handles **M&A deal advisory**
