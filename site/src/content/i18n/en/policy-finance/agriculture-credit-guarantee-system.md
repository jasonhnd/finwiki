# Agriculture credit guarantee system in Japan

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance index]]. Read it against [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system|Japan credit guarantee system]] for SME-side contrast, [[policy-finance/fisheries-credit-guarantee-system|fisheries credit guarantee system]] for the sister sector system, and [[policy-finance/japan-policy-finance-system|Japan policy finance system]] for the broader public-credit perimeter. The deposit-and-lending counterpart for farmers is [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan|JA Bank system]] and the cooperative apex is [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin|Norinchukin]].

## TL;DR

Japan's agriculture credit guarantee system (農業信用保証保険制度) supplements farmers' and agriculture-related borrowers' credit through a two-tier layered structure: (1) prefectural-level **農業信用基金協会** (Agriculture Credit Fund Associations, 47 prefectural bodies) that guarantee farmer / agriculture-corporation debt to lending institutions, reinsured by (2) the national-level **独立行政法人農林漁業信用基金** (Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Credit Foundations, JAFFIC) agriculture-credit-insurance business. It is adjacent to [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan|JA Bank system]] but it is a guarantee-insurance route, not a deposit / lending route. Compared with [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system|the general SME credit guarantee system]], the agriculture system is sector-specific, MAFF-supervised, and integrated with the agricultural-cooperative system (系統金融) rather than with general regional banks.

## 1. 系統金融との位置関係

Japanese agricultural finance flows through the **系統金融** (cooperative system finance) stack:

| Layer | Function | FinWiki route |
|---|---|---|
| 農家 / 農業法人 | Borrower (farmer, agriculture corporation, agricultural worker, agri-business). | System route here |
| 単位農協 (JA / 単協) | Primary cooperative; takes farmer deposits; extends agricultural and household credit. | [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan]] |
| 信連 (JA信連 / 都道府県信用農業協同組合連合会) | Prefectural credit federation of agricultural cooperatives; intermediates JA deposits / lending. | [[banking/ja-bank-system-japan]] / prefectural-信連 layer |
| 農林中央金庫 (Norinchukin) | National apex bank for JA / 信連 / fisheries / forestry cooperatives; manages excess cooperative deposits as a global investor. | [[cooperative-banks/norinchukin]] |
| 農業信用基金協会 | Prefectural guarantee body for agricultural loans; performs subrogation on default. | This page |
| 独立行政法人農林漁業信用基金 (JAFFIC) | National-level credit-insurance / reinsurance body for prefectural agriculture and fisheries guarantee associations; also direct lending support. | This page |
| MAFF (農林水産省) | Policy supervisor for cooperative system + credit guarantee insurance system. | [[policy-finance/INDEX]] |

The guarantee system is therefore **a parallel public-credit infrastructure layered on the cooperative-banking stack** — JA / 信連 / Norinchukin handle deposits and lending, while 農業信用基金協会 + JAFFIC handle the guarantee-and-reinsurance layer that backs that lending.

## 4. 主要保証商品

- **農業近代化資金保証** — modernization-loan guarantee for farm-equipment investment, facility modernization (greenhouses, dairy parlors, storage).
- **農業改良資金保証** — improvement-loan guarantee for innovation, new-crop adoption, organic conversion.
- **農業経営基盤強化資金 (スーパーL資金) 保証** — guarantee for large-scale farm-management consolidation loans channeled through JFC's agriculture-forestry-fisheries arm.
- **就農支援資金保証** — new-entrant farmer support guarantee.
- **担い手・農業法人向け保証** — large-scale farmer / corporate farm guarantee.
- **災害復旧保証** — disaster-recovery agriculture guarantee (typhoon damage, flood damage, snow damage, livestock disease).
- **6次産業化 / 加工流通関連保証** — guarantee for farmer-led food processing and direct distribution (the "6th-industrialization" policy lane).
- **借換 / 経営改善資金保証** — refinancing and management-improvement guarantee.

## 5. Operating logic — guarantee, subrogation, reinsurance

1. **Application** — A farmer or agriculture corporation borrows from a lending institution (typically JA / 信連 / Norinchukin / regional bank / JFC's agriculture arm). The lender requires guarantee credit and applies to the prefectural 農業信用基金協会.
2. **Guarantee** — The prefectural association evaluates the loan, accepts the guarantee, and charges a guarantee fee (保証料 — typically a low single-digit annual rate on outstanding principal, varying by product).
3. **Lending** — The lender disburses the loan with the guarantee in place.
4. **Default** — If the borrower defaults, the lender claims under the guarantee; the prefectural association pays subrogation (代位弁済) to the lender, then pursues recovery (求償) against the borrower / collateral.
5. **Reinsurance** — JAFFIC's agriculture-credit-insurance business reinsures the prefectural association's guarantee exposure; insurance payouts flow from JAFFIC to the prefectural association under the applicable insurance contract.
6. **Residual loss** — After recovery and JAFFIC insurance, residual loss is absorbed by the prefectural association's reserves, supplemented by national / prefectural / cooperative contributions.

The system thus has **three loss-absorption layers**: (a) borrower recovery, (b) prefectural association reserves + national JAFFIC reinsurance, (c) public / cooperative capital contributions backstopping the system.

## 6. 保証料 economics

Guarantee fee schedule is set by each prefectural 農業信用基金協会 within MAFF guidance, typically structured as:

- Annual rate on outstanding principal, in the low single-digit basis-point range for senior policy loans (e.g., スーパーL資金) and higher for higher-risk products.
- Tiered by product (近代化資金 / 改良資金 / 就農支援 etc.) and borrower category (担い手 priority discounts).
- Subsidized for priority policy categories (new-entrant farmers, disaster-recovery, 6th-industrialization).
- The fee economics are deliberately below market-equivalent commercial guarantee pricing — the spread is absorbed by public / cooperative subsidies.

## 8. Comparison vs general SME credit guarantee system

| Dimension | Agriculture credit guarantee system | [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system\|General SME credit guarantee system]] |
|---|---|---|
| Borrower target | 農家 / 農業法人 / 農業関連事業者 | SME / small business (broad sectors) |
| Local guarantor | 農業信用基金協会 (47 prefectural) | 信用保証協会 (51 prefecture / city) |
| National federation | 全国農業信用基金協会連合会 | [[policy-finance/national-federation-credit-guarantee-corporations\|全国信用保証協会連合会 (JFG)]] |
| National reinsurer | 農林漁業信用基金 (JAFFIC) | [[financial-regulators/jfc\|JFC]] credit-insurance |
| Supervisor | MAFF (農林水産省) | METI / SME Agency (中小企業庁) |
| Lender partners | JA / 信連 / Norinchukin / banks / JFC ag-arm | Regional banks / shinkin / credit cooperatives / JFC |
| Crisis-era flagship | Disaster / livestock-disease / 食料安全保障 | セーフティネット / 危機関連 (ZeroZero 2020–2022) |
| Funding economics | 公的 / 系統金融 subsidies | 公的補助 + 信用保険 reinsurance |

The two systems are **parallel public-credit guarantee infrastructures** with different sector targets, different supervisors, and different cooperative-banking interfaces. They do not compete for borrowers — a loan to a farmer routes through the agriculture system, a loan to a small manufacturer routes through the SME system.

## 9. Boundary cases

- **Not JA Bank** — JA Bank is a lender route; this page is the guarantee-insurance mechanism layered on top.
- **Not the SME guarantee system** — SME guarantees route to [[policy-finance/japan-credit-guarantee-system]].
- **Not fisheries** — fisheries guarantee-insurance belongs to [[policy-finance/fisheries-credit-guarantee-system]] (sister sector under the same JAFFIC parent).
- **Not 林業 (forestry)** — forestry credit support is also a JAFFIC business but routed separately under MAFF forestry policy.
- **Not JFC's agriculture lending** — JFC's [[financial-regulators/jfc|agriculture-forestry-fisheries arm]] lends directly to farmers; this page is about guarantee insurance for farmer borrowing, which may overlap when guaranteed loans are originated by JFC.
