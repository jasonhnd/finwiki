# T&D Asset Management (T&D AM)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[asset-managers/INDEX|asset-managers INDEX]]. Read it against [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|ニッセイアセットマネジメント]] for peer / contrast context, [[financial-licenses/securities-license-stack|securities license stack]] for the FIEA license boundary, and [[securities/financial-instruments-business-operators-japan-index|FSA 金商業者 index]] for the registry layer.

## TL;DR

T&D Asset Management (T&D AM) is the asset-management operating company of [[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D ホールディングス (8795)]]. In the FSA Financial Instruments Business operators list it appears as "T&Dアセットマネジメント株式会社", Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第363号. It carries the asset-management function of [[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D HD]] (Taiyo Life + Daido Life + T&D Financial Life), a **mid-tier life-insurance FG ranking behind Dai-ichi Life / Nippon Life / Meiji Yasuda Life**, handling group life-insurer policy-reserve management + publicly-offered investment trusts + pension management. As part of the **mid-tier life-insurer asset-manager category**, it occupies a complementary position to the largest player [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|ニッセイ AM]] and to [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセマネOne]] (Dai-ichi Life + Mizuho JV).

## 2. Business role

- **Life-insurer policy-reserve management** (for [[life-insurers/taiyo-life|太陽生命]] / [[life-insurers/daido-life|大同生命]] / T&D Financial Life)
- **Publicly-offered investment-trust management** (for retail investors)
- **Pension management** (DB / DC / iDeCo): discretionary mandates for institutional investors and corporate pensions
- **Discretionary investment management and advisory** (for institutional investors)

## 4. Why a standalone page matters

[[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D HD]] is a mid-tier life-insurance FG ranking behind the 4  major life insurers (Nippon Life / Dai-ichi Life / Sumitomo Life / Meiji Yasuda Life), but **T&D AM**, which carries its asset-management function, is important as an independent analytical unit:

1. **Mid-tier life-insurer asset-manager category**: positioned to bridge [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|ニッセイ AM]] (the largest) and [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセマネOne]] (Mizuho + Dai-ichi Life JV)
2. Integrates the asset-management needs of the 3  heterogeneous life-insurance group of **Taiyo Life (focused on seniors) + Daido Life (for SME owners) + T&D Financial Life (focused on distribution channels)**
3. A mid-tier life-insurer player in the **NISA expansion + publicly-offered-investment-trust competition** ([[securities/nisa-2024-flow|nisa-2024-flow]] context)
4. Because it has a **listed parent (T&D HD)**, it has investment-performance transparency under capital-market disclosure obligations (different from the mutual-company-affiliated [[life-insurers/nippon-life|日本生命]] / [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|ニッセイ AM]])
