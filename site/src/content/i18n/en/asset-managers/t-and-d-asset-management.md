# T&D Asset Management (T&D AM)

## Wiki Route

Read it against [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|Nissay Asset Management]] for peer / contrast context, [[financial-licenses/securities-license-stack|securities license stack]] for the FIEA license boundary, and [[securities/financial-instruments-business-operators-japan-index|FSA Financial Instruments Business Operators index]] for the registry layer.

## TL;DR

T&D Asset Management (T&D AM) is the asset-management operating company of [[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D Holdings (8795)]]. In the FSA financial instruments business operator list, it appears as "T&D Asset Management Co., Ltd." with Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General (FIBO) No. 363. It provides the investment-management function for [[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D HD]] (Taiyo Life + Daido Life + T&D Financial Life), a **mid-sized life-insurance financial group after Dai-ichi Life / Nippon Life / Meiji Yasuda Life**, and handles group life-insurer policy-reserve management, public investment trusts, and pension management. As a **mid-sized life-insurer-affiliated asset manager**, it complements the largest players such as [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|Nissay AM]] and [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|Asset Management One]] (Dai-ichi Life + Mizuho JV).

## 2. Business Role

- **Life-insurer policy-reserve management** for [[life-insurers/taiyo-life|Taiyo Life]] / [[life-insurers/daido-life|Daido Life]] / T&D Financial Life
- **Public investment trust management** for individual investors
- **Pension management** (DB / DC / iDeCo): discretionary investment management for institutional investors and corporate pension plans
- **Discretionary investment management and investment advisory** for institutional investors

## 4. Why a Standalone Page Matters

[[life-insurers/td-hd|T&D HD]] is a mid-sized life-insurance financial group after the 4  major life insurers (Nippon Life / Dai-ichi Life / Sumitomo Life / Meiji Yasuda Life), but its investment-management unit **T&D AM** is important as a separate analytical unit:

1. **Mid-sized life-insurer-affiliated asset-manager category**: positioned between [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|Nissay AM]] (largest) and [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|Asset Management One]] (Mizuho + Dai-ichi Life JV)
2. Integrates the investment needs of the 3  heterogeneous life-insurance group: **Taiyo Life (senior-focused) + Daido Life (SME owner-focused) + T&D Financial Life (sales-channel-focused)**
3. A mid-sized life-insurer-affiliated player in the context of **NISA expansion + public investment trust competition** ([[securities/nisa-2024-flow|nisa-2024-flow]] context)
4. Because the parent is a **listed company (T&D HD)**, investment-performance transparency sits under capital-market disclosure obligations, unlike mutual-company groups such as [[life-insurers/nippon-life|Nippon Life]] / [[asset-managers/nissay-asset-management|Nissay AM]]
