## 1. Company overview

> [!warning] Important capital change (2022)
> **Mitsubishi Corp.-UBS Realty Inc. (MC-UBSR)**, formerly the core of Mitsubishi Corporation-affiliated real estate AM, was fully acquired by KKR (indirect subsidiary 76  Co., Ltd.) after **Mitsubishi Corporation (8058) and UBS Asset Management AG transferred all their shares to KKR in 2022-04-28 (approximately 2,300 hundred million yen)**, and the company name was changed to KJR Management Co., Ltd. In other words, that former core company is **now under KKR and no longer affiliated with Mitsubishi Corporation**. The old premise of this page as a "Mitsubishi Corporation-affiliated real estate AM collection node" no longer applies to MC-UBSR. The table below is retained for historical organization.

The real estate AM industry is grouped by **where the source of the real assets is drawn from**. Trading-company lines use a vertically integrated model that fund-structures real assets discovered through the parent company's overseas plant / infrastructure / logistics projects plus domestic development.

### Division of labor with group-treasury functions

Mitsubishi Corporation-affiliated financial functions have a **multi-layer structure**: the AM group on this page + [[trading-company-finance/mitsubishi-corp-finance|Mitsubishi Corporation FS]] (internal CMS / group finance) + [[leasing-firms/mitsubishi-hc-capital|Mitsubishi HC Capital]] (listed leasing / asset finance) + [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]] group banks, trusts, and AM. The AM on this page is a specialized function responsible for "fund businesses derived from real assets."
