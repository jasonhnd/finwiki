# Mitsubishi UFJ Asset Management

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Mitsubishi UFJ Asset Management is the asset-management operating company of [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]]. In the FSA list of financial instruments business operators, it appears as "Mitsubishi UFJ Asset Management Co., Ltd." with Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General (Kinsho) registration 第404号. It is MUFG's investment-management company for investment trusts, discretionary investment management, and investment advisory services.

- MUFG's operating company for investment trusts, discretionary investment management, and investment advisory services.
