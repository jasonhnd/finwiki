# Sumitomo Mitsui DS Asset Management

## TL;DR

Sumitomo Mitsui DS Asset Management is an asset-management operating company whose lead shareholder is [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG]]. In the FSA financial instruments business operator list, it appears as Sumitomo Mitsui DS Asset Management Company, Limited, registered as Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第399号.

| Shareholder mix | SMFG 50.1%, [[securities-firms/daiwa-sg|Daiwa Securities Group]] 23.5%, MS&AD / Sumitomo Life / SMTB |

## 2. Business role

- Serves as the core asset-management arm of the SMBC Group, covering investment trusts, investment advisory mandates, and institutional asset management.
- It is not a wholly owned single-megabank subsidiary, because shareholders also include the Daiwa Securities Group and others.
- Connects the wealth / asset-formation strategy of [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG]] with the management and distribution coordination of [[securities-firms/daiwa-sg|Daiwa]].

## 3. Why standalone page matters

The SMFG page tends to center on banking, cards, and securities, leaving the asset-management arm easy to miss. Splitting out SMDAM makes it easier to track SMFG's fee income, investment trusts, pensions, and institutional asset-management business.
