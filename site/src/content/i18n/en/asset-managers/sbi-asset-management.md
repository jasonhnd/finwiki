## TL;DR

SBI Asset Management is the asset-management operating company of [[megabanks/sbi-hd|SBI group]]. In the FSA financial instruments business operators list, it appears as "SBI Asset Management Co., Ltd." under Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第311号. It handles SBI's low-cost investment trusts, ETF / index fund competition, and group sales channels.

## 2. Business role

- SBI's asset-management layer, including investment trusts, ETF / index products, investment advisory, and alternative-management subsidiaries.
- Connects to the sales funnel of [[securities-firms/sbi-securities|SBI証券]] and the financial ecosystem of [[megabanks/sbi-hd|SBI HD]].
- Should be read as a beneficiary of low-cost fund competition and NISA inflows.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

SBI has a strong customer funnel across securities accounts, banking, insurance, and crypto assets, but product manufacturing as an asset manager is a separate analytical unit. Separating SBI Asset Management clarifies the roles of distributor and manufacturer.
