## TL;DR

Asset Management One Co., Ltd. is an asset-management operating company holding an **Investment Management Business** registration under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act. In the FSA Financial Instruments Business operators list it appears as "アセットマネジメントOne株式会社", Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第324号. It is a **JV (joint venture)** funded by [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]] and [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life|第一生命グループ]], and unlike other affiliated asset managers it is not simply a wholly-owned subsidiary of a megabank. Its business is built around publicly-offered investment trusts (publicly-offered toushin) plus discretionary investment management.

## 2. Operating model

As an investment-management-business operator, it handles (1) **investment-trust management** of publicly-offered toushin (formation and management of investment trusts), (2) **discretionary mandates** for pensions and institutional investors, and (3) investment advisory. Distribution spans the bank / trust channels of [[megabanks/mizuho-bank|みずほ銀行]] and [[trust-banks/mizuho-trust-bank|みずほ信託銀行]] and Dai-ichi Life's insurance channel, forming a **bank-trust-insurance joint platform**. Supervision is a two-tier structure of FSA + self-regulatory organisations (Investment Trusts Association / Japan Investment Advisers Association). It contrasts in ownership structure with [[asset-managers/mufg-asset-management|三菱UFJ AM]], a wholly-owned megabank subsidiary.
