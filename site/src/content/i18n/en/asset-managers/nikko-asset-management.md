## TL;DR

Amova Asset Management Co., Ltd. is the former "Nikko Asset Management." It changed its trade name on 2025-09-01, and in the FSA financial-instruments-business-operators list it is registered under its current name "Amova Asset Management Co., Ltd.," as Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments) 第368号. It is an asset-management company holding an **investment-management-business (投資運用業)** registration under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act, and a **wholly-owned subsidiary** of [[trust-banks/sumitomo-mitsui-trust|三井住友トラストグループ (SuMi TRUST)]] (per public announcements, the rename leaves capital and governance structure unchanged). As a global asset manager headquartered in Japan, it handles public investment trusts, ETFs, and management for institutional investors.

## 2. Operating model

As an investment-management operator, it is a Japan-originating global asset manager that handles **investment-trust management** (構成・運用) of public investment trusts, ETFs, and **discretionary investment management (投資一任)** (discretionary mandates for institutional investors). Distribution runs through the trust / bank channels of [[trust-banks/smtb|三井住友信託銀行]] / [[trust-banks/sumitomo-mitsui-trust|SuMi TRUST]] and the sales networks of securities firms. Because materials and fund names under the former Nikko AM brand persist, the wiki **bundles the former name (Nikko) and the current name (Amova) on the same page**. Supervision is a two-tier structure of FSA + self-regulatory organizations. It is differentiated by role from [[asset-managers/smt-am|SMT-AM]], also in the SuMi TRUST line (Amova = global management / SMT-AM = pensions and public investment trusts).
