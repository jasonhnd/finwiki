# Amova Asset Management (formerly Nikko Asset Management)


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Amova Asset Management is the former Nikko Asset Management. It changed its trade name in 2025-09-01, and the FSA financial instruments business operator list shows it as "Amova Asset Management Co., Ltd.", registered as Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第368号. It is a [[trust-banks/sumitomo-mitsui-trust|三井住友トラストグループ]]-group global asset manager.

## 2. Business role

- As a Japan-headquartered global asset manager, it handles investment trusts, ETFs, and institutional-investor asset management.
- Because the former Nikko AM brand has transitioned to Amova, both the former-name link and current-name link are needed.
- It connects with the trust / asset-management strategies of [[trust-banks/smtb|三井住友信託銀行]] / [[trust-banks/sumitomo-mitsui-trust|SMTG]].

## 3. Why standalone page matters

Materials, fund names, and distributor documents still remain under the former Nikko Asset name, while the current registered name is Amova. In the wiki, the former and current names are tied to the same page so the FSA registration and brand transition can be tracked together.
