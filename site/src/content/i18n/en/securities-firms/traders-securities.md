# Traders Securities

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/sbi-fx-trade|SBI FXトレード]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Traders Securities is the FX / securities operator of the Traders HD group. In the FSA financial instruments business operator list, Traders Securities Co., Ltd. is listed as Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第123号. Its core brand includes Minna no FX.

## 2. Business role

- Mainly provides FX / derivatives services for individual investors.
- In the retail FX market map, it is a comparator against [[securities-firms/gmo-click-securities|GMOクリック証券]], [[securities-firms/dmm-com-securities|DMM.com 証券]], [[securities-firms/sbi-fx-trade|SBI FXトレード]], and [[securities-firms/saxo-bank-securities|サクソバンク証券]].
- As a listed-parent revenue source, its FX / financial derivatives cyclicality needs to be tracked separately.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

Traders Securities is neither a megabank-affiliated securities firm nor the largest online securities operator, but it is an important operator in Japan's retail FX / derivatives market. It is worth tracking the FSA registration, product structure, and its position in group earnings separately.
