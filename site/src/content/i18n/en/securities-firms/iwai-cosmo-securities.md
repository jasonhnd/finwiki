# IwaiCosmo Securities (岩井コスモ証券)

## TL;DR

IwaiCosmo Securities Co., Ltd. is a **mid-tier independent securities firm headquartered in Osaka**, the core 100% subsidiary of TSE PRIME-listed [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-hd|岩井コスモホールディングス (8707)]]. Founded in 1917 年 (Taisho 6 ) in Osaka as Iwai Securities, it adopted its current name when **the former Iwai Securities and the former Cosmo Securities merged in 2010-04 **. **Financial instruments business operator: Director-General of the Kinki Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第15号** (FIEA Type 1  Type I financial instruments business). Head office in Imabashi, Chuo Ward, Osaka City; capital of 135 億円; operating 28  sales branches nationwide + 2  call centers + 2  net-support centers. As a **mid-tier independent securities firm centered on Kansai-based face-to-face retail**, it conducts overseas brokerage of US stocks / Greater China stocks, IPO underwriting, and consulting-style sales. Its competitors are mid-tier independent securities firms such as [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|藍澤証券グループ]], [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ]], [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京FH]], [[securities-firms/matsui-sec|松井証券]], and [[securities-firms/marusan-securities|丸三証券]].

## 1. Corporation / shareholders

- **Trade name**: IwaiCosmo Securities Co., Ltd. (English: IwaiCosmo Securities Co., Ltd.)
- **Founded**: 1917 年 (Taisho 6 ) 12  — "Iwai Securities" established in Osaka (Ujiro Iwai)
- **Launch of current structure**: 2010 年 4 月 1 日 — merger of the former Iwai Securities + the former Cosmo Securities (Kyodo Securities lineage)
- **Head office location**: 〒541-0042  Imabashi 1 -chome 8 -ban, Chuo Ward, Osaka City 12 号
- **Capital**: 135 億円 (contributed by [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-hd|岩井コスモホールディングス]] 100%)
- **Parent company**: IwaiCosmo Holdings Co., Ltd. (TSE PRIME 8707, head office in Osaka)
- **Representative**: Representative Director and President
- **Number of employees**: approx. 700 名 (consolidated basis, HD disclosure)

## 2. Licenses / registrations

- **Type I financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Kinki Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第15号
- **Industry association memberships**:
  - Japan Securities Dealers Association (JSDA) full member
  - Financial Futures Association of Japan
  - Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association
  - Japan Investment Advisers Association
- **PTS / proprietary trading system**: none operated in-house (connects to PTS such as [[securities/japannext-securities|Japannext]])
- **Trading participation rights**: [[securities/tokyo-stock-exchange|TSE]] / [[securities/osaka-exchange|OSE]] trading participant

## 3. Sales base

- **Face-to-face sales**: 28  sales branches nationwide — centered on the Kansai base (Osaka, Kyoto, Kobe, Nara), also deployed in Kanto, Tokai, Kyushu, and Hokkaido
- **Call centers**: 2  locations (Tokyo, Osaka)
- **Net-support centers**: 2  locations
- **Online trading service**: "Cosmo Netore" (in-house online trading)
- **Shareholder composition**: centered on former Iwai Securities-lineage shareholders (the Iwai family / Kansai business community), broadly dispersed following the HD listing

## 4. Products handled

- **Domestic stocks**: cash and margin, Tokyo and Osaka markets, including via PTS
- **Foreign stocks**: US stocks (NYSE, NASDAQ), Greater China stocks (HKEX-listed, US-listed Chinese ADRs), ASEAN stocks
- **Investment trusts**: in-house recommended funds + funds managed by various companies
- **Bonds**: government bonds, municipal bonds, corporate bonds, foreign bonds
- **NISA**: supports both the growth-investment quota and the accumulation-investment quota
- **iDeCo**: provides individual-type defined-contribution pension plans
- **Derivatives**: futures, options, FX
- **IPO underwriting / lead underwriting**: handles mid-tier underwriting (especially for companies originating in Kansai)
- **Consulting-style sales**: advisory for elderly high-net-worth clients on inheritance, business succession, retirement-allowance management, etc.

## 5. Industry position

- As a **mid-tier independent securities firm based in Osaka / Kansai**, it takes a position different from megabank FG affiliates ([[securities-firms/nomura-holdings|野村]], [[securities-firms/daiwa-securities-group|大和]], [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興]], [[securities-firms/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]], [[securities-firms/mufg-msms|三菱UFJモルガン・スタンレー]])
- Competitors: [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|藍澤証券]], [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券]], [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京]], [[securities-firms/matsui-sec|松井証券]], [[securities-firms/marusan-securities|丸三証券]]
- **Strengths**: thick personal networks with the Kansai business community, a track record of IPO underwriting / lead underwriting for Kansai-originating companies, face-to-face consulting services
- **Weaknesses**: compared with online brokers ([[securities-firms/sbi-securities|SBI証券]], [[securities-firms/rakuten-securities|楽天証券]], [[securities-firms/matsui-sec|松井]], etc.) its retail commissions are relatively high, and branch operating costs are high
- **Strategy**: continued deepening of the "face-to-face × elderly high-net-worth × foreign stocks × consulting" model
