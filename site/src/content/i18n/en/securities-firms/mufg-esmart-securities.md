# Mitsubishi UFJ eSmart Securities


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[securities-firms/INDEX|securities-firms INDEX]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/sbi-securities|SBI証券 (SBI Securities)]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Mitsubishi UFJ eSmart Securities is the online securities operator of the [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]] group. It descends from the former au Kabucom Securities, and appears in the FSA financial instruments business operators list as "Mitsubishi UFJ eSmart Securities Co., Ltd.", registered with the Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第61号.

## 2. Business role

- Handles equities, investment trusts, margin trading, futures, FX, and related products as MUFG's online brokerage channel.
- The name change from au Kabucom Securities in 2025 年 moved the brand away from KDDI coloring and closer to the MUFG brand.
- It is a retail online broker layer distinct from [[megabanks/mufg-bank|三菱UFJ銀行]] and [[securities-firms/mufg-mums|三菱UFJモルガン・スタンレー証券]].
