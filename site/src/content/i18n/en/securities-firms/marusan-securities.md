## TL;DR

Marusan Securities is a **long-established mid-tier independent securities firm founded in 1910 年** (TSE PRIME **8613**). With more than 110  years of history, it specializes in **face-to-face retail and consulting sales**. In the FSA financial instruments business operator list, it appears as Marusan Securities Co., Ltd., registered as Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director (FIBO) 第167号. It is a **regular member of JSDA**. It is not affiliated with a megabank or telecom group, and maintains a traditional mid-tier securities model centered on face-to-face sales of equities, bonds, and investment trusts. Its peer set is the mid-tier independent securities group including [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|アイザワ証券]] / [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ]] / [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-hd|岩井コスモ HD]] / [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京 FH]].

## 2. Company Structure

**Legal name**: Marusan Securities Co., Ltd.  
**Founded**: 1910 年 as a private shop in Tokyo during the Meiji era ^[extracted]  
**Listing**: listed on the TSE after postwar stock-market reorganization; currently TSE PRIME (8613) ^[extracted]  
**Headquarters**: Kojimachi, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo  
**Business type**: financial instruments business operator (Type I) — mid-tier independent securities firm

### Business Characteristics

Marusan Securities is a long-established mid-tier independent securities firm that has not converted into a holding-company structure. It has no banking, insurance, or crypto-asset business inside the group, and has a simple structure focused on securities business, especially face-to-face retail sales of equities, bonds, and investment trusts. This differs from [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|アイザワ証券グループ]] and [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ]], which have already adopted holding-company structures.

### Position as a Long-Established Mid-Tier Independent Securities Firm

Marusan Securities was founded in 1910 年 and has more than 110  years of history. It occupies a different position from megabank-affiliated securities firms ([[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC 日興]] / [[securities-firms/mufg-mums|MUMSS]] / [[securities-firms/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]]), major face-to-face firms ([[securities-firms/nomura-hd|野村]] / [[securities-firms/daiwa-sg|大和]]), and online-only firms ([[securities-firms/sbi-securities|SBI 証券]] / [[securities-firms/rakuten-securities|楽天証券]] / [[securities-firms/monex-group|マネックス]]), maintaining a **face-to-face retail x mid-tier independent** niche. Compared with [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三]] / [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-hd|岩井コスモ]] / [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京]] in Chukyo and Kansai, its regional base is different: Tokyo versus Chukyo / Kansai.

### Competitive Environment ([[securities/japan-online-brokerage-competition|japan-online-brokerage-competition]] Context)

- Differentiates through face-to-face consulting sales against the online-securities zero-commission trend.
- Maintains a niche in education-oriented sales and in-person consultation for wealthy and senior customers.
- Faces a scale disadvantage against major face-to-face securities firms, but maintains an independent brand within the mid-tier independent securities group.
