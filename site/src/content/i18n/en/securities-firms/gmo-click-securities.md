## TL;DR

GMO Click Securities is [[payment-firms/gmo-financial-hd|GMOフィナンシャルHD]]'s online brokerage / FX / CFD core company. In the list of FSA financial instruments business operators, it is listed as "GMO Click Securities Co., Ltd.", Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第77号.

## 2.Business role

- GMO Financial Group's securities / FX / CFD platform.
- Unlike [[payment-firms/gmo-payment-gateway|GMO PG]] and [[payment-firms/gmo-coin|GMOコイン]], it is treated as a FIEA broker / derivatives operator.
- It also has banking adjacencies such as a Securities Connect account with GMO Aozora Net Bank.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

Since GMO crosses payment, banking, crypto, and FX/CFD, the regulatory boundaries of each business company tend to mix. By making GMO Click Securities independent, we can properly follow the FIEA / FX / CFD / broker competition.
