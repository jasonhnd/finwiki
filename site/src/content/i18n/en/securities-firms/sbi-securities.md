# SBI Securities


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/paypay-securities|PayPay証券]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

SBI Securities is the core online securities company of [[megabanks/sbi-hd|SBI HD]]. In the FSA financial instruments business operators list, it appears as SBI Securities Co., Ltd., Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General (Financial Instruments Business Operator) 第44号. It should be treated separately from the SBI Group parent when reading Japan's retail brokerage / NISA / iDeCo / online trading funnel.

## 2. Business role

- Online full-line securities company handling domestic stocks, investment trusts, foreign stocks, bonds, FX, futures / options, NISA / iDeCo.
- The entry point to [[megabanks/sbi-hd|SBI HD]]'s financial ecosystem of securities, banking, insurance, and crypto assets.
- It is adjacent to [[securities-firms/sbi-fx-trade|SBI FXトレード]] and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-sbi-vc-trade|SBI VC Trade]], but the subject as a FIEA broker-dealer is SBI Securities.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

SBI HD is a financial holding and investment group, while SBI Securities is the retail brokerage operating company. To track NISA fund inflows, zero-commission brokerage, bank linkage, point linkage, and online broker competition, SBI Securities needs a standalone page.
