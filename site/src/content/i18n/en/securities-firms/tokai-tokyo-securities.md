## TL;DR

Tokai Tokyo Securities Co., Ltd. is a **mid-tier independent securities firm headquartered in Nagoya**, the core 100% subsidiary of the TSE PRIME-listed [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京フィナンシャル・ホールディングス (8616)]]. The current structure was established by the **spin-off of the former Tokai Tokyo Securities (the Tokai Bank-affiliated firm) into a separate company in 2008–10 **, with its origins in Maruman Securities, founded by 1929 年（昭和 4 年）. **Financial instruments business operator, Director-General of the Tokai Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第140号** (FIEA Type 1  Type I financial instruments business). Head office at Midland Square, Meieki, Nakamura-ku, Nagoya; capital 60 億円; employees 1,869 名. As a **mid-tier independent securities firm centered on face-to-face retail based in the Tokai region (Aichi, Gifu, Mie, Shizuoka)**, it develops a regional-bank partnership model (jointly operated branches with Tokai-region regional banks), IPO underwriting, and consulting-style sales. The joint ventures / co-branch brand "Tokai Tokyo XX Securities" with regional banks is a distinctive feature. Competitors are [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-securities|岩井コスモ証券]]・[[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ]]・[[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|藍澤証券]]・[[securities-firms/matsui-sec|松井証券]].

- **Trade name**: Tokai Tokyo Securities Co., Ltd.（English: Tokai Tokyo Securities Co., Ltd.）
- **Established**: 2008 年 10 月 8 日（spun off at the transition of [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京FH]] to a holding company）
- **Founding origin**: founded as Maruman Securities by 1929 年（昭和 4 年）
- **Head office location**: 〒450-6212 名, Midland Square, 4-7-1  Meieki, Nakamura-ku, Nagoya
- **Capital**: 60 億円
- **Parent company**: [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|東海東京フィナンシャル・ホールディングス]]（TSE PRIME 8616, headquartered in Nagoya）100% investment
- **Representative**: Representative Director and President
- **Number of employees**: 1,869 名（HD consolidated basis, as of 2024 年）

## 2. ライセンス・登録

- **Type I financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Tokai Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第140号
- **Industry association membership**:
  - Japan Securities Dealers Association (JSDA) regular member
  - Financial Futures Association of Japan (general incorporated association)
  - Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association (general incorporated association)
- **Trading participation rights**: [[securities/tokyo-stock-exchange|TSE]]・[[securities/osaka-exchange|OSE]] trading participant・[[securities/japan-securities-clearing-corp|JSCC]] clearing participant

## 3. 営業基盤

- **Face-to-face sales**: head office + more than 130  branches / sales offices nationwide
- **Regional-bank partnership model**: holds regional securities companies established jointly with the following regional banks
  - "**Hokuhoku TT Securities**"（joint venture with [[regional-banks/hokuhoku-fg|ほくほくFG]] of the [[regional-banks/hokuriku-bank|北陸銀行]] / [[regional-banks/hokkaido-bank|北海道銀行]] group）
  - "**Tokai Tokyo & City Securities**"（joint venture with the [[regional-banks/oita-bank|大分銀行]] / FFG group）
  - "**Hamagin TT Securities**"（joint venture with the [[regional-banks/yokohama-bank|横浜銀行]] / [[regional-banks/concordia-fg|コンコルディアFG]] group）
  - "**Nishi-Nippon City TT Securities**"（joint venture with [[regional-banks/nishi-nippon-city-bank|西日本シティ銀行]]）
  - cooperation with multiple other regional banks
- **Online trading services**: "Anshin Comprehensive Service", "Tokai Tokyo Net Fish"
- **Call center**: multiple locations

## 4. 取扱商品

- **Domestic equities**: cash and margin; as a trading participant of TSE / OSE / Nagoya Stock Exchange (NSE), the 3 -major-market line
- **Foreign equities**: US stocks, European stocks, Asian stocks
- **Investment trusts**: in-house management + funds from various firms
- **Bonds**: JGBs, municipal bonds, corporate bonds, foreign bonds
- **NISA**: growth investment quota, accumulation investment quota
- **iDeCo**: individual-type defined contribution pension plans
- **Derivatives**: futures, options, FX
- **IPO underwriting**: lead manager / underwriter for IPOs of mid-tier companies centered on the Tokai region
- **M&A advisory**: business succession / sale / acquisition consultation for mid-tier companies

## 5. 業界ポジション

- A unique position as a **mid-tier independent securities firm based in Nagoya and the Tokai region**, developing a "joint-venture securities" model between mega-FG-affiliated securities firms and regional banks
- A hybrid strategy of face-to-face + regional-bank cooperation, also called **"the Nomura of the Tokai region"**
- Competitors: [[securities-firms/iwai-cosmo-securities|岩井コスモ証券]]（Kansai）, [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券]]（Nagoya peer）, [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg|藍澤証券]], [[securities-firms/matsui-sec|松井証券]], [[securities-firms/marusan-securities|丸三証券]]
- **Strengths**: deep personal networks with mid-tier / major companies in the Tokai region, expansion of retail customers via regional-bank channels, pioneer of the regional-bank securities joint-venture model
- **Special business**: differentiates itself with a face-to-face + regional-bank cooperation model distinct from [[securities/japan-online-brokerage-competition|オンライン証券競争]]
- **Strategy**: deepening regional-bank partnerships; expansion of overseas securities business in Southeast Asia (Vietnam / Indonesia)
