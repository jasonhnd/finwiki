# Aizawa Securities Group

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[securities-firms/INDEX|securities-firms INDEX]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ (Okasan Securities Group)]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

An independent securities holding company (TSE PRIME **8708**). The official history states that Yahachi Aizawa began securities operations as a regular trading member of the Tokyo Stock Exchange on 1918-07-07, established Aizawa Shoten Co., Ltd. on 1933-10-03, and changed the company name to Aizawa Securities Co., Ltd. in 1948-10. Today the group is built around Aizawa Securities and related investment, asset-management and life-design companies. Its differentiation axis is providing individual investors with access to overseas equities as an **"Asia-stock pioneer"**, including China, Taiwan, South Korea, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines and Vietnam. Competitors are [[securities-firms/okasan-sg]] / [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh]] in the same independent-mid-tier bracket and the online players [[securities-firms/matsui-sec]] / [[securities-firms/monex-group]].

## 3. #26 deepening: retail brokerage, Asia stocks and license boundary

Aizawa Securities Group should be read as a two-layer structure: the listed holding company sits above the operating securities firm, Aizawa Securities Co., Ltd. The group-company page lists Aizawa Securities as the core company carrying Type I financial instruments business, Type II financial instruments business, investment advisory and agency business, and investment-management business. Aizawa Securities' own company profile also states Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) No. 3283 and membership in the Japan Securities Dealers Association, the Asset Management Association and the Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association.

The business model is deeper than "regional broker." It combines face-to-face retail brokerage, access to Asian equities, and long-term household asset-building advice. The official product page describes the company as an Asia-stock pioneer, while the medium-term management plan frames the three fiscal years from FY2025 through FY2027 as a transition toward a model that accompanies customers and their families in asset management and asset formation over the long term. It should therefore be treated as an independent securities group combining advice, overseas-equity trading routes and a shift toward stock-type revenue.

In peer comparison, Aizawa is not an online-only economics story like [[securities-firms/matsui-sec|Matsui Securities]]. It is closer to regionally rooted, face-to-face, product-advice independent securities groups such as [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|Okasan Securities Group]] and [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh|Tokai Tokyo Financial Holdings]]. At the same time, its Asia-stock access and overseas touchpoints such as Japan Securities Co., Ltd. give it a stronger cross-border retail brokerage angle than an ordinary domestic retail broker.
