# Iwai Cosmo Holdings

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[securities-firms/INDEX|securities-firms INDEX]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/okasan-sg|岡三証券グループ (Okasan Securities Group)]] for peer / contrast context and [[securities/INDEX|securities index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

**A mid-tier independent securities holding company rooted in Osaka** (TSE PRIME 8707). With its roots in Iwai Securities, founded in Osaka by Ujiro Iwai in 1918 年, it was formed in 2010-04  through the management integration of the former Iwai Securities and the former Cosmo Securities to launch **Iwai Cosmo Securities**, with Iwai Cosmo HD established the same month through holding-company conversion. As a mid-tier independent brokerage centered on face-to-face retail, it develops overseas brokerage of US stocks・emerging-market stocks, IPO underwriting, and consulting-style sales on a Kansai base. Competitors are mid-tier independent securities groups such as [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg]] / [[securities-firms/okasan-sg]] / [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh]] / [[securities-firms/matsui-sec]]. ^[extracted]

#### Former Iwai Securities (Osaka lineage・1918〜2010)

- **1918**: Ujiro Iwai founded **Iwai Securities** (the predecessor stockbroking shop) in Osaka ^[extracted]
- Prewar: grew as a local broker in the Osaka securities industry
- Postwar: incorporated, made a fresh start as a mid-tier brokerage rooted in Kansai
- Heisei era: continued as a mid-tier independent brokerage centered on face-to-face retail, based in Kansai

#### Former Cosmo Securities (1923〜2010)

- **1923**: predecessor (Kyoei Shoji lineage) established ^[extracted]
- Postwar: name changed to Cosmo Securities, grew as one corner of the major・semi-major firms
- Daiwa Bank affiliate → later moved toward independent reorganization through management restructuring
- 2000 年s: came up as a sale candidate due to business difficulties

#### Integration・holding-company conversion (2010)

- **2010-04**: management integration of former Iwai Securities + former Cosmo Securities → **Iwai Cosmo Securities** launched ^[extracted]
- **Same 2010-04**: adopted the holding-company method → **Iwai Cosmo Holdings** established, listed on the TSE and elsewhere ^[extracted]

### Basic strategy

- **Kansai base × face-to-face retail**: headquartered in Osaka, with face-to-face sales to Kansai's affluent individuals・SME owners at its core
- **Agility of an independent**: as an independent not belonging to a megabank affiliate・major-securities affiliate, it touts the independence of product selection・management policy
- **Strengthening overseas-stock brokerage**: building out a product lineup of US stocks・emerging-market stocks to counter online brokers
- **Consulting-style sales**: a shift from simple trade brokerage to an asset-management-proposal type

### Competitive position

The cluster of mid-tier independent brokerages is as follows (each with its own history and base):

- [[securities-firms/aizawa-sg]] Aizawa Securities G — Tokyo base, strong in Asian stocks・emerging-market stocks
- [[securities-firms/okasan-sg]] Okasan G — founded in Nagoya, Tokai base
- [[securities-firms/tokai-tokyo-fh]] Tokai Tokyo FH — Tokai base, Chubu business-community affiliate
- [[securities-firms/matsui-sec]] Matsui Securities — independent online brokerage・pioneer
- Iwai Cosmo HD — **Kansai base, centered on face-to-face retail**

Compared with megabank affiliates (securities firms under [[megabanks/mufg]] / [[megabanks/smfg]] / [[megabanks/mizuho-fg]]) and major online firms ([[megabanks/sbi-hd]] / Rakuten Securities, etc.), while inferior in scale, it uses regional closeness・face-to-face proposal capability・the flexibility of an independent as its axes of differentiation.
