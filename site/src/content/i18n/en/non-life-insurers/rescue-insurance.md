## Overview

Rescue Insurance is a licensed non-life insurer connected to Japan Best Rescue System and home-service / daily-trouble assistance routes. In FinWiki it is a platform / service-adjacent non-life insurer beside [[non-life-insurers/zenkankyo-reiwa-insurance]] and [[non-life-insurers/sakura-insurance]].

## Entity Boundary

This page covers Rescue Insurance Co., Ltd. It should not be merged into JBR's service business or small-amount short-term insurance subsidiaries unless the insurance-license relationship is explicit.
