## TL;DR

アニコム ホールディングス株式会社 is a pet-insurance group holding company that appears on the FSA's **list of approved insurance holding companies**. The core of the group is [[non-life-insurers/anicom-insurance|アニコム損害保険]], which holds a non-life insurance business license, and the HD is its parent company・group strategy anchor. The HD itself does not underwrite insurance; it handles the capital/strategy oversight of the subsidiary non-life insurance business, animal-health data, and adjacent services. Read the **HD (listed parent) and the operating insurer (license holder) as separate entities**.

| Wiki role | Pet-insurance holding-company / group strategy page |

The HD is an **insurance holding company** under the Insurance Business Act, not a non-life insurance company itself. The actual underwriting (a proper non-life insurance business license, not small-amount short-term) is done by the subsidiary アニコム損保.

## 2. Operating model

The group operates a pet **non-life insurance** franchise: the subsidiary アニコム損保 underwrites pet insurance, and the HD layers animal-health data, advanced-medicine research, and adjacent services on top of it. Holding-company analysis should focus on how underwriting data (請求・診療データ) and animal-health services reinforce each other — this data loop becomes the differentiation axis against the monoline non-life insurers ([[non-life-insurers/dai-ichi-ipet-insurance|アイペット損保]], [[non-life-insurers/pet-and-family-insurance|ペット&ファミリー損保]]).
