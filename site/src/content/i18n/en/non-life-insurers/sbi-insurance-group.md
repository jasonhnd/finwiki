# SBI Insurance Group (SBI インシュアランスグループ)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[non-life-insurers/INDEX|non-life-insurers INDEX]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/sbi-securities|SBI証券 (SBI Securities)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

[[megabanks/sbi-hd]]'s **insurance-business supervisory intermediate holding company**. **Established 2017-04 **, it is the platform for the online-direct-sales insurance business. Under it are **SBI Insurance** (mainly automobile insurance) / **SBI Life Insurance** (formerly PCA Life, term life + medical) / **SBI Ikiiki Small-Amount Short-Term Insurance** (for the elderly) / **SBI Resta Small-Amount Short-Term Insurance** (earthquake-expense insurance). The group strategy is "**a Japanese version of the U.S. Geico / Progressive model**" = low-price, direct type + **cross-selling** with SBI Securities, [[regional-banks/sbi-shinsei-bank]], and SBI Sumishin Net Bank. 2017-09  Listed on the TSE Mothers → re-privatized 2023-09  by SBI HD making it a wholly owned subsidiary (TOB squeeze-out). ^[extracted]

### Core concept

- **Online-direct-sales insurance** (direct type) = eliminating agency costs → low-price pitch
- **U.S. Geico / Progressive model**: multi-product cross-selling starting from automobile insurance
- **SBI economic-zone cross-selling**: SBI Securities 1300 万 accounts + SBI Sumishin Net Bank + the customer-base sharing of [[regional-banks/sbi-shinsei-bank]]
- 4  product group (non-life + life + elderly small-amount short-term + earthquake small-amount short-term) covering the entire life-cycle range

### Partnership / acquisition strategy

- **Conversion to a wholly owned subsidiary** (2023-09): reducing listing-maintenance costs + improving management-decision speed + integrated operation of the [[megabanks/sbi-hd]] economic zone
- **Linkage within the SBI economic zone**: synergy design such as proposing insurance at the time of securities-account opening, and bundling fire insurance with Shinsei Bank loan contracts
