## TL;DR

The Japan branch of Swiss Reinsurance Company Ltd. (Schweizerische Rückversicherungs-Gesellschaft AG, listed on SIX). Alongside Munich Re, it is **one of the world's 2  largest reinsurance companies**. It appears on the FSA's non-life insurance company license list (foreign non-life insurance companies, etc.), and operates both the Property & Casualty Reinsurance and Life & Health Reinsurance segments in the Japanese market.

## 2. Business lines in Japan

- **P&C Reinsurance** — non-life reinsurance for earthquake/typhoon catastrophe, property, liability, etc.
- **Life & Health Reinsurance** — life reinsurance for mortality, medical, longevity, disability, and long-term-care risks
- **Corporate Solutions** — specialty insurance written directly for large corporates (D&O, cyber, marine, aviation, etc.; this is a direct/large-corporate line, not reinsurance)
- **Public Sector Solutions** — parametric insurance for governments and local authorities (earthquake/weather-trigger type)
- **Reinsurance solutions** — Insurance-Linked Securities (CAT bond) arrangement and risk-transfer solutions
