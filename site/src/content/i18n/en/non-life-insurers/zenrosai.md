# Zenrosai / Kokumin Kyosai coop (National Federation of Workers and Consumers Insurance Cooperatives)

## Wiki route

Read with [[non-life-insurers/ja-kyosairen|JA 共済連]] for the agricultural cooperative kyosai peer, [[non-life-insurers/kyoritsu-fire-marine|共栄火災海上保険]] for cooperative-system non-life adjacency, [[insurance/INDEX|insurance domain]] for the kyosai-vs-insurance regulatory boundary, and [[cooperative-banks/rokin-renraku|労働金庫連合会]] for the workers' cooperative finance peer in banking.

## TL;DR

**A national federation responsible for consumer cooperative-related "mutual aid" business for workers and consumers**. 1957  established as a **Co-op Federation** (Co-op Act, not Agricultural Co-operative Act) based on the Consumer Co-operative Association Act (Co-op Act). Under the **Kokumin Kyosai coop (formerly Kokumin Kyosai)** brand, we provide life mutual aid + damage mutual aid (fire, automobile, injury, etc.) to labor unions, workers, and local residents. **Members = National local worker co-ops + occupational co-ops + prefectural worker co-ops**. **As a cooperative insurance company with more than 1000 万 members in terms of policies in force and members**, it is a large-scale mutual-aid organization in Japan, along with JA Kyosairen. **The Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare** is in charge of this (under the Consumer Cooperative Act; consumer cooperatives are under the Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare), and the regulatory system is different from that of insurance companies, which are subject to the Insurance Business Act. ^[extracted]

## 4. Supervision/Regulation

- **Supervising**: **Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare** (Jurisdiction of the Co-op Law) + Financial Services Agency (Cooperative supervision of financial soundness and consumer protection)
- **Legal basis**: Consumer cooperative law (昭和 23 年 law 第 200 号) + Consumer cooperative mutual aid regulations
- **Current policy issues**:
  - **Mutual aid = insurance regulatory gap** (Competitive fairness of insurance business law vs. consumer cooperative law)
  - Direction for strengthening **Co-op mutual aid regulations** (Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare/Financial Services Agency cooperative supervision)
  - Digitization of tax deductions, etc. with **My Number linkage**
  - Voluntary disclosure of **ESR similar indicators**
