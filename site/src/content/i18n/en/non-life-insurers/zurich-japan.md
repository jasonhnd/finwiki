# Zurich Insurance Company (Zurich Japan)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[non-life-insurers/INDEX|non-life-insurers INDEX]]. Read it against [[life-insurers/axa-japan|アクサ・ジャパン (AXA Japan)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

The Japanese base of the Swiss Zurich Insurance Group. **1986 年 Opening of Japanese branch** One of the pioneer foreign non-life insurance companies that entered Japan. In Japan, it gained recognition with its 2  major products, ``**Overseas Travel Insurance**'' and ``**Online Auto Insurance (Direct Type)**''. In 2010 年, it established **Zurich Life** as a separate corporation and expanded its business area to direct online sales of medical insurance and cancer insurance. It has a consistent feature of a **direct sales model** with call centers and the web at its core, without operating branches, and is a major player in domestic direct non-life insurance along with Sony Assurance in [[megabanks/sony-fg]], AXA Sompo Insurance in [[life-insurers/axa-japan]], Secom Sompo Insurance, and SBI Sompo Insurance in [[megabanks/sbi-hd]].

## 1. Group structure

**Ultimate parent company**: Zurich Insurance Group AG (Switzerland Zurich, SIX Swiss Exchange: ZURN, SMI constituents)
**Major corporations in Japan**:
- **Zurich Insurance Company Japan Branch** (non-life insurance, Japanese branch of foreign insurance company)
- **Zurich Life Insurance Co., Ltd.** (Life insurance, Japanese corporation)

### positioning

- **"Not just direct sales"**: Unlike [[life-insurers/lifenet]] (Lifenet Life), which is completely online-only, overseas travel insurance is a hybrid type that also utilizes travel agency channels.
- **Foreign capital that also manages life and non-life insurance**: [[life-insurers/axa-japan]] has a 3 -company structure of "life and non-life insurance + online direct sales life insurance" and also has a sales staff channel, whereas Zurich **does not have a face-to-face sales staff channel and is consistent with a direct sales model**

### global leverage

- **Zurich Insurance Group's reinsurance platform**: Absorb large contract and natural disaster risks through intra-group reinsurance
- **Global overseas travel insurance network**: Provide local relief and medical transportation network for Japanese people traveling overseas through Zurich Assistance (group function)

## 4. Regulation and policy

- **Administrator**: Financial Services Agency (FSA).
  - **Zurich Insurance Company Japan Branch**: Based on Insurance Business Law Article 185  (Japanese branch license for foreign insurance companies)
  - **Zurich Life Insurance Co., Ltd.**: Regular license as a life insurance company (Japanese corporation) under the Insurance Business Act
- **Solvency**: Japan branch and Nippon Life Insurance Company are subject to Japan's solvency margin ratio regulations. **2025~ The same issue as other non-life insurance in responding to the introduction of economic value-based solvency regulations (ESR)**
- **Current policy issues**:
  - **Automobile insurance compatible with autonomous driving and advanced safety equipment**: Redesign of risk rate system, degree of deployment of telematics insurance
  - **Accountability of online direct sales life insurance**: Compliance with notification obligations and recruitment regulations during the online application flow
