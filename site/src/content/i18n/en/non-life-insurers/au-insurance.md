## 2. Operating model

au Insurance is an insurer with a mobile carrier ecosystem, app / web distribution, and travel / bicycle / pet / auto-adjacent insurance use cases. Unlike traditional agency-led non-life insurers, mobile touchpoints and embedded / short-duration product experiences are important.
