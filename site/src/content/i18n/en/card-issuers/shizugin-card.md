## TL;DR

Shizugin Card Co., Ltd. is the credit-card subsidiary of the [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg|Shizuoka FG]] group, TSE Prime 5831. Backed by the individual and corporate customer base of core subsidiary [[regional-banks/shizuoka-bank|Shizuoka Bank]], the leading bank in Shizuoka Prefecture and a main bank for the local manufacturing cluster, it provides bank-originated credit-card services under the JCB / Visa / Mastercard brands, together with loans, cash advances, collection agency services, electronic money, and QR-payment-related services. It is a typical regional-bank credit-card-company model, supporting bank-account-linked payments, local merchant networks, and payment functions inside the regional economic area. After the 2022-10-03 holding-company transition, it was repositioned as one of the group's parallel subsidiaries. ^[extracted]

### Regulatory Framework

- **Primary regulators**: Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry for the Installment Sales Act; Financial Services Agency for the Money Lending Business Act and Payment Services Act.
- **Legal basis**:
  - **Installment Sales Act**: core regulation for credit-card issuance and shopping-credit business, including comprehensive credit-purchase intermediary registration.
  - **Money Lending Business Act**: cash-advance and card-loan operations, including money-lender registration.
  - **Payment Services Act**: electronic money / prepaid cards / QR-payment-related operations.
  - **Act on the Protection of Personal Information / My Number Act**: cardholder information management.
- **Parent-FG governance**: As a consolidated subsidiary of [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]], it sits under holding-company governance and is positioned within non-bank subsidiary regulation, including rules on main businesses, incidental businesses, and business-scope limits.

### Regulatory Issues

- **Installment Sales Act amendment (2018-)**: stronger obligations for acquirers and mandatory security measures, including PCI DSS compliance.
- **Revised Installment Sales Act (2020-)**: stronger user protection, including credit screening, usage-limit management, and complaint-handling systems.
- **Act on the Protection of Personal Information (2022 amendment)**: duties around cross-border transfer and proper acquisition of credit-card member information.
- **Cashless-payment promotion**: merchant-fee-level debate under METI's Cashless Vision 2025 and the target of a "cashless-payment ratio of 40% by 2025 年."
- **AML and anti-social-forces checks**: implemented jointly within the [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]] group.
- **EDINET / annual securities reports**: stand-alone disclosure by Shizugin Card is limited because it is unlisted; indirect disclosure appears through [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]] consolidated segment information.
