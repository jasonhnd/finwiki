## TL;DR

Shizugin Card Co., Ltd. (Shizuoka Bank Card) is the credit-card subsidiary of the [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg|しずおか FG (東証 PRIME 5831)]] group. Against the retail / corporate customer base of its core subsidiary [[regional-banks/shizuoka-bank|静岡銀行]] (Shizuoka Prefecture share No. 1  / main bank of a manufacturing cluster), it provides **a bank-originated credit card based in Shizuoka Prefecture** (JCB / VISA / Master brands), loans, cashing, collection agency, electronic money, and QR-payment-related services. As a typical regional-bank-affiliated card-company model, it carries the functions of bank-account linkage, a regional merchant network, and settlement within the regional economic zone. [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]] 2022-10-03 After the move to a holding-company structure, it was repositioned as one of the parallel subsidiaries within the group. ^[extracted]

### Regulatory framework

- **Lead authorities**: Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (jurisdiction over the Installment Sales Act) and the Financial Services Agency (FSA, jurisdiction over the Money Lending Business Act and the Payment Services Act)
- **Legal basis**:
  - **Installment Sales Act**: the core regulation for credit-card issuance and shopping-credit business (registration as a comprehensive credit-purchase intermediary)
  - **Money Lending Business Act**: cashing / card-loan business (money-lending registration)
  - **Payment Services Act**: electronic money / prepaid card / QR-payment-related business
  - **Act on the Protection of Personal Information / My Number Act**: management of cardholder information
- **Parent FG governance**: as a consolidated subsidiary of [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]], positioned under holding-company governance and subject to non-banking-subsidiary regulation (core business / ancillary business / scope-of-business restrictions)

### Regulatory issues

- **Installment Sales Act amendment (2018-)**: strengthened obligations for merchant-contracting parties (acquirers), security-measure obligations (PCI DSS compliance)
- **Amended Installment Sales Act (2020-)**: strengthened user protection (credit screening / usage-limit management / complaint-handling structure)
- **Act on the Protection of Personal Information (2022- amendment)**: cross-border transfer / proper-acquisition obligations for cardholder information
- **Cashless promotion**: under METI's Cashless Vision 2025 and the target of a "2025 年 cashless ratio of 40%," there is debate over the level of merchant fees
- **AML measures / anti-social-forces checks**: jointly implemented with the [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]] group
- **EDINET / securities reports**: Shizugin Card on a standalone basis is unlisted with limited disclosure; indirectly disclosed via [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg]] consolidated segment information
