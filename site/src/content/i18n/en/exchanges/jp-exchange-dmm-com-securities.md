# DMM.com Securities Co., Ltd. — Japanese financial-instruments business operator (crypto-asset derivatives) overview

## 1. Corporate entity / shareholders

- **Trade name**: DMM.com Securities Co., Ltd. (English: DMM.com Securities Co.,Ltd.)
- **Established**: 2006年12月6日
- **Head-office location**: 〒103-6010  Tokyo Nihonbashi Tower, 2-7-1  Nihonbashi, Chuo-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: 98億円 (capital reserve 73億9千万 yen)
- **Representative Director**: Ryuji Tanikawa
- **Parent company**: DMM.com LLC (DMM.com Group) — unlisted
- **Corporate number**: not disclosed (not posted on the official site)

## 2. Licensing / registration status

- **Type 1 financial-instruments business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第1629号 (registration date 2007年9月30日)
- **Type 2 financial-instruments business**: included in the same 第1629号 (2017年6月27日 registration)
- **Commodity-futures business**: included in the same 第1629号 (2011年1月1日 registration)
- **JVCEA Type 1 member #1043**: crypto-asset derivatives business only (no spot / crypto-asset exchange business registration)
- Electronic-payment-instruments business / funds-transfer business: not registered (not disclosed)
- **Affiliated associations**:
  - Japan Securities Dealers Association (association member number 1105)
  - Financial Futures Association of Japan (association member number 1145)
  - Japan Commodity Futures Association
  - Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association (association member number 480)
  - Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA association member number 1043)
- **Capital-adequacy ratio**: 419.6% (end of 令和8年3月 = 2026-03)

## 3. Handled currencies (crypto assets)

- **No crypto-asset spot trading**. JVCEA registration is for "crypto-asset derivatives business" only.
- The 22 instruments handled by the official CFD service (DMM CFD) consist of **8 stock-index instruments + 14 commodity CFD instruments**. As of 2026-05-18, the listing of crypto-asset CFD instruments cannot be confirmed on the official site.
- The company-overview page explicitly states "crypto-asset-related OTC derivatives trading" in its business description, consistent with the JVCEA registration. The actual provision status of crypto-asset CFDs is judged to be "registered / non-public service (or suspended)" (not disclosed).

## 4. Scope of business

The company's mainstay business consists of the following 4 pillars:

| Service | Content |
|---------|------|
| DMM FX | OTC foreign-exchange margin trading |
| DMM CFD | 8 stock-index instruments + 14 commodity CFD instruments = 22 instruments in total (OTC derivatives) |
| Equity trading (DMM Kabu) | Listed-securities trading covering domestic equities / US equities / IPOs |
| DMM Banusy | Racehorse fund (commodity-investment-related business) |

- Crypto-asset spot: **none** (handled by the separate entity DMM Bitcoin, but it closed in 2025-03 )
- Leveraged trading (crypto assets): JVCEA registration exists, but crypto-asset CFD instruments are not posted on the official service page (as of 2026-05-18)
- Fiat-currency deposit channels: bank transfer / quick deposit (supporting approx. 340 financial institutions nationwide)
- Staking / lending / NFT: not supported
- Corporate services: no public information

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- Number of accounts / monthly trading volume: not disclosed (disclosure materials not public)
- Capital-adequacy ratio 419.6% (end of 2026-03) — financial soundness maintained at a high level
- Position among competitors: in the FX / CFD field, a major player alongside GMO Click Securities and Gaitame.com. As a crypto-asset derivatives business operator, among the 33 社 JVCEA Type 1 members, it belongs to the operator category with no spot / derivatives-only (1社 of 13社). The actual handled volume of crypto-asset CFDs is not disclosed.

**Relationship with the closure of DMM Bitcoin (a separate entity)**:

DMM.com Securities and DMM Bitcoin are separate entities (their address is the same Tokyo Nihonbashi Tower, but their corporate personality / registration numbers differ). No official announcement can be confirmed that DMM Bitcoin's closure (announced 2024-12  / completed 2025-03 ) directly affected DMM.com Securities' JVCEA crypto-asset derivatives registration or business continuity (as of 2026-05-18). However, since the crypto-asset spot trading business as part of the DMM Group was extinguished, the group's overall crypto-asset business shrank substantially.

## 7. Strategic developments

- The most recent public announcements center on functional enhancements of FX / CFD / equity trading. No announcement of new crypto-asset-field services can be confirmed (as of 2026-05-18).
- DMM CFD was expanded from 14 instruments before 2024年 to 22 instruments (adding 8 commodity CFD instruments). No plan to add crypto-asset CFDs is noted on the official site.
- DMM Banusy (racehorse fund) continues as a distinctive service unmatched by other firms.
