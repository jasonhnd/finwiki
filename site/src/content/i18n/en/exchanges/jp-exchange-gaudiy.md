# Gaudiy Financial Labs, Inc. — JVCEA Type-II Member (Web3  IP Platform Operator)

> FSA Registration Number **Not disclosed (Type-II Member = application in preparation or planned)** · JVCEA Member #2033 (Type-II Member) · Parent Group: Gaudiy Group, Inc. (formerly Gaudiy, Inc.)

Gaudiy Financial Labs, Inc. was established as the financial infrastructure subsidiary of Gaudiy Group, Inc. — a startup in the Web3  × IP fan-economy space that is **not itself a crypto asset exchange operator**. Its membership in the JVCEA as a Type-II member is positioned as preparatory licensing work for the group to build infrastructure for fan tokens and crypto asset-related services connecting IP holders with fans. The business model differs substantially from existing crypto asset exchanges (Type-I members); the primary purpose is understood to be the expansion of financial capabilities within a B2B IP community platform.

## 3. Handled Currencies

These differ from "handled instruments" in the crypto asset exchange business context but are expected to require regulatory compliance once their status as crypto assets is formalised
- Number of handled instruments / major instruments: not disclosed

## 4. Business Scope

Gaudiy Financial Labs is positioned as the financial infrastructure component of the parent group's Web3  × IP business model. The principal businesses operated by parent Gaudiy Group are as follows:

**Gaudiy Fanlink (flagship SaaS)**
- Provides B2B fan co-creation community platforms to IP holders (publishers, anime and game companies, etc.)
- Delivers one-stop implementation of token issuance, fan-contribution evaluation, point design, and NFT issuance using blockchain technology
- Builds an economic ecosystem for each IP by combining proprietary blockchain with generative AI
- IP deployments (public examples): Shueisha's "Shōnen Jump+," Sanrio, Bandai Namco Entertainment ("World of Denon-bu"), Aniplex, Sony Music Entertainment, KONAMI ("Qto"), and others

**Gaudiy Financial Labs (JVCEA-registered entity)**
- Mission stated as creating new financial infrastructure for the Web3  era (official website)
- Building the tokenomics foundation to convert fan passion and contributions into "economic value"
- Oriented toward future support for IP-specific token listings and provision of trading infrastructure (stated explicitly in past Series B announcements)

**MyAnimeList**
- Owns a subsidiary operating one of the world's largest anime/manga community sites, with big data on 240  countries and 12 億 titles (acquisition timing and amount not disclosed)

**Gaudiy US Inc.** (established 2023 年, New York State)
- Established as a North American base; part of global expansion

Fiat deposit channels / margin trading / derivatives: not disclosed (prior to formal launch of crypto asset exchange operations)

## 7. Strategic Developments

- **Use of Pre-Series C proceeds** (announced 2025 年 5 月): Large-scale investment in existing services and M&A; global expansion through additional overseas bases; Web3/AI-related R&D and engineer hiring; launch of social and educational ventures. A second close was also planned within the year
- **Capital partnerships with major publishers and entertainment companies**: With Shueisha, Kodansha, Shogakukan, Bandai Namco HD, Sony Group, and others as shareholders, the structure substantially lowers the barrier to collaboration with content IP holders
- **Dual pillars of AI × blockchain**: In addition to the proprietary blockchain, the company is commercialising analysis of fan behaviour data and optimisation of reward design using large language models. Internal labs — "Gaudiy AI Lab" and "Gaudiy Web3 Lab" — drive R&D
- **Global expansion as a priority**: Explicitly signalled entry into global IP markets, including the New York base (Gaudiy US) and Southeast Asia (from 2023 年 onward)
- **Leveraging MyAnimeList**: Oriented toward expanding the IP fan economy through synergies with the global platform holding 12 億 titles of anime/manga viewing data
- **Support for IP-specific token listings**: From the Series B announcement stage (2022 年), the company has publicly stated that it aims "in the medium to long term to support listing of tokens unique to each IP"; Gaudiy Financial Labs' JVCEA registration is consistent with this direction
