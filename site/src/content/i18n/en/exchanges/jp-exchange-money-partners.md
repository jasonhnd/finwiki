# Money Partners Co., Ltd. — Japan Crypto Asset Exchange Operator Overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number: Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00001号 (Crypto Asset Exchange Business) · JVCEA member #1001  · Registration date 2017-09-29

## 1. Legal entity and shareholders

- **Trade name**: Money Partners Co., Ltd. (English: MONEY PARTNERS CO.,LTD.)
- **Date of incorporation**: 2008年5月9日 (established through holding company demerger)
- **Corporate number**: 6010401075907
- **Registered office**: 〒106-6216  Roppongi 3-2-1 , Minato-ku, Tokyo, Sumitomo Fudosan Roppongi Grand Tower 16F (relocated 2025年12月)
- **Paid-in capital**: 36億円 (as of 2026年3月31日)
- **Number of employees**: 23名 (as of 2026年3月31日)
- **Parent company and listing status**: The former parent company "Money Partners Group Co., Ltd." was listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange (Standard Market); a public tender offer (TOB) by Gaitame.com Co., Ltd. was completed on 2024年12月, and the company was delisted on 2025年2月. It is currently an unlisted subsidiary under the Gaitame.com Group.
- **Statutory auditor**: Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu LLC

## 2. Licences and registration status

- **Crypto asset exchange business**: Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director 第00001号 (registration date 2017-09-29) — the first number issued domestically
- **Type I financial instruments business**: Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director (FIEA) 第2028号 (registered 2008年9月)
- **JVCEA member**: Type I member #1001  — registered for both crypto asset exchange business and crypto asset derivatives trading (OTC)
- **Membership associations**: Japan Securities Dealers Association; Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA); Japan Cryptoasset Business Association

## 3. Handled currencies

- **Trading format**: Spot trading is not offered; only **crypto asset CFD (OTC derivatives trading)** is provided.
- **Listed pairs**: BTC/JPY, ETH/JPY, LTC/JPY, BCH/JPY — 4  pairs total (confirmed on 2026-05-18 service detail page).
- **Features**: Simple line-up centred on BTC. No pure spot trading account; trading is structured as leveraged CFD contracts.

## 4. Business scope

- **Crypto asset CFD (OTC derivatives)**: Launched as the "Mainichi Crypto Asset" service on 2021 年 12 月. Provides leveraged trading and smartphone/PC tools.
- **FX business (former)**: "Partners FX" and "Partners FX nano" were the main products, but the entire FX business was transferred to Gaitame.com Co., Ltd. by absorption-type demerger on 2025年6月.
- **Securities and CFD-Metals (former)**: Termination notice issued on 2025年3月; services discontinued.
- **Deposit channels**: Bank transfer and quick deposit (temporarily suspended due to system failure on 2026-02-16, later resumed).
- **New account opening**: Acceptance suspended as of 2025年2月. Only existing accounts continue to operate.

## 5. Market share and trading volume

- No public account-number or trading-volume data has been disclosed (after delisting, IR information is being consolidated under the Gaitame.com Group).
- Given CFD-only crypto trading, 4  pairs, and suspended new-account registration, the company's position in the retail market is judged to be a niche (primarily serving existing customers).
- During its peak FX era, the company held a recognised brand in the domestic retail FX market as part of a TSE First Section-listed group, but expansion of its crypto asset business was limited.

## 7. Strategic developments

- Since 2025年, the FX, securities, and CFD-Metals businesses have been successively integrated into Gaitame.com or discontinued. The remaining business is concentrated solely in crypto asset CFD.
- As of 2026年2月, the "Mainichi Crypto Asset" service continues to be offered (explicitly stated in official announcements).
- New account registration has been suspended; the company is in a phase of continued operation for existing account holders. The possibility of future business restructuring within the Gaitame.com Group has not been confirmed in public information.
- A content and information service called "Mane-Para-Bo" (2025年11月) has been launched, but detailed specifications are limited in public disclosure.
