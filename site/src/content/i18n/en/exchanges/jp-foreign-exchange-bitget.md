# Bitget Limited — Overview of an Unregistered Foreign Crypto-Asset Exchange Operator Targeting Japan

> **No Japan FSA Registration** · Not a JVCEA Member · FSA Warning Letters: 2  in total (2023-03-31 / 2024-11-28)

## 1. Corporate Entity and Shareholders

- **Corporate name**: Bitget Limited
- **English name**: Bitget Limited
- **Founded**: 2018年
- **Registered address**: Republic of Singapore
- **Listing status**: Unlisted (global private company)
- **Parent / major shareholders**: Not disclosed

## 2. Licenses and Registration Status

- **Japan crypto-asset exchange business**: **Unregistered** (no FSA registration)
- **JVCEA membership**: **Non-member** (neither Type 1 nor Type 2)
- **Principal regulators outside Japan**: According to public information, regulatory compliance is underway in multiple overseas jurisdictions, but a detailed registration list has not been disclosed
- **Japan FSA warning history**:
  - 2023-03-31 — 1 st warning letter issued. Listed as representative unknown. Warning issued under the FSA administrative guidelines (Crypto-Asset Exchange Business Ⅲ-1-6(2)②) for operating an unregistered crypto-asset exchange business targeting Japan-resident users.
  - 2024-11-28 — 2 nd warning letter issued. Listed under the name of representative Gracy Chen (CEO). Issued by the FSA on the same day as warning letters to KuCoin, bitcastle LLC, Bybit Fintech Limited, and MEXC Global — a total of 5 社 issued simultaneously.

## 3. Supported Currencies

- **Number of listed assets**: Over 1,300  (as of early 2026年, per company announcement)
- **Major assets**: BTC, ETH, USDT, BNB, SOL, XRP, BGB (proprietary token), etc.
- **Characteristics**:
  - Policy of broadly listing altcoins and meme coins
  - Issues and operates the proprietary native token BGB (Bitget Token)
  - Operates the listing launchpad "LaunchX" for token sales of new projects

## 4. Business Scope

- **Spot trading**: Supported (Maker 0.01% / Taker 0.01%, among the lowest in the industry. Up to 80% discount for BGB holders)
- **Futures / perpetual contracts (derivatives)**: Supported (maximum leverage 125 x)
- **Copy Trading**: The company's primary differentiating feature. A mechanism that allows automatic mirroring of positions held by top-performing traders; the company claims it is the largest in the industry
- **Crypto-asset lending**: Supported
- **Web3  wallet**: Companion product "Bitget Wallet" (formerly BitKeep) operated under a separate brand
- **Fiat deposit**:
  - Japanese yen (JPY) not supported
  - Domestic bank transfers from Japan are unavailable. Credit-card-based deposits for some currencies are offered, but direct JPY deposits are not

## 5. Market Share and Trading Volume

- **Derivatives trading volume**: Within the global top 4–5  (company statement; multiple sources including CoinGecko)
- **User count**: As of 2025 年 1 月, the company announced "ranked 2 位rd largest ecosystem globally by total user count" (company press release; specific figures not disclosed). Multiple secondary reports also reference "over 800 万 users in 100 + countries"
- **BGB token**: By end of 2024 年, the price had risen approximately 8 ドル, with a reported annual gain of 1,000%+ (company press release 2025-01-23)
- **Client base**: Retail (especially copy trading for beginner-to-intermediate users); some institutional investor and VIP services also available
- **Competitive position**: Ranks in the tier2  group for derivatives volume, behind Binance and OKX. Claims the largest copy-trading operation in the industry. Other overseas unregistered exchanges that have received FSA warnings include [[exchanges/jp-foreign-exchange-bybit|Bybit]], [[exchanges/jp-foreign-exchange-mexc|MEXC]], and [[exchanges/jp-foreign-exchange-kucoin|KuCoin]]; for native token strategy comparison see [[exchanges/cex-native-token-strategy-comparison|CEX ネイティブトークン戦略比較]].

## 7. Strategic Developments

- **2025 年 stated policy**: The CEO explicitly outlined three pillars: strengthening institutional and VIP services, expanding the BGB token ecosystem (including PayFi), and enhancing security and compliance (2025-01-07 press release)
- **Bitget Wallet (formerly BitKeep)**: Operated as a self-custody Web3  wallet under a separate brand. Integrated with the exchange platform to provide DEX and DeFi bridging functions
- **Copy trading expansion**: Ongoing expansion of covered asset classes and strengthening of copy trader certification and performance disclosure systems
- **Japan market policy**: The company states it does not actively solicit Japan residents, but Japanese-language content (wiki pages, blogs, exchange review articles) exists in large quantities on the company's domain. No registration application has been filed since the FSA warnings

## 8. Key Persons

- **CEO**: Gracy Chen — Over 10  years of experience in business management, marketing, and investment. Known as an early investor in BitKeep (now Bitget Wallet). Committed to advancing diversity in the Web3  industry and reported to have participated in activities related to the UN Commission on the Status of Women. Listed as representative in the FSA 2024–11  warning letters
- **Founders**: Within the scope of public information, no specific founder names are widely disclosed (not disclosed)
