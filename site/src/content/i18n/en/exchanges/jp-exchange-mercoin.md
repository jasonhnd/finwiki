# Mercoin, Inc. — Japanese crypto-asset exchange operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00030号 · JVCEA member #1039 · registration date 2022-06-17

## 1. Corporate entity / shareholders

- **Trade name**: Mercoin, Inc. (株式会社メルコイン)
- **Established**: 2021年4月28日
- **Head office location**: Roppongi Hills Mori Tower, 6-chome 10-1号, Roppongi, Minato-ku, Tokyo
- **Parent company**: A wholly owned subsidiary of Mercari, Inc. (株式会社メルカリ, TSE Prime 4385)
- **Affiliated company**: Merpay, Inc. (株式会社メルペイ, fintech sister company within the group)
- Capital and shareholding ratio not disclosed

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00030号 (registration date 2022-06-17)
- **Financial instruments business**: Not obtained (derivatives trading not supported)
- **Electronic payment instruments business**: Not obtained
- **JVCEA member**: Type 1, crypto-asset exchange business only (membership number 1039)

## 3. Supported currencies

- **Number of listed coins**: 3  (confirmed as of 2025-04-24)
  - Bitcoin (BTC) — listing started 2023-03 
  - Ethereum (ETH) — added 2024-05-21
  - XRP — added 2025-04-24
- Order-book trading (exchange format) is not offered. All coins are **brokerage (sales-outlet) format only** (with spread, no commission)
- Leveraged trading / derivatives: not supported

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot brokerage outlet**: buying and selling of BTC / ETH / XRP
- **Crypto-asset accumulation (recurring-buy) feature**: BTC (started 2024-08-01), ETH (started 2024-10-22). Supports automatic debit from Merpay balance (2024-12); daily-accumulation feature added (2025-07-31)
- **Bitcoin payments**: bitcoin payment for shopping within Mercari (started 2024-02-15)
- **Points-grant feature**: Mercari points awarded monthly simply by holding eligible crypto-assets (started 2024-12-18)
- **Fiat deposit channels**: Merpay balance top-up (bank / ATM transfer); the biggest distinguishing feature is direct purchase using Mercari sales proceeds and Mercari points
- Corporate services / staking: not offered (within disclosed scope)

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- **2024-04 (approx. 1 year(s) after service launch)**: number of users surpassed 200万 (company press release)
- **2023-10 (7 months after service launch)**: number of users surpassed 100万
- **2024-05**: the company announced that its crypto-asset account openings over the most recent 1 year(s) ranked No.1  in the industry (MMD Labo survey)
- Detailed figures such as monthly trading volume and assets under custody: not disclosed
- **Customer base**: individual retail centered on Mercari's existing users (smartphone users). Designed for small-lot purchases starting from 1 円
- **Position among competitors**: it claims an industry-top-class number of account openings. However, trader-oriented features are limited, and as a full-fledged trading platform it operates in a different segment from the major dedicated exchanges ([[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]] / [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitbank|bitbank]], etc.). Its retail-economy-zone integration, alongside [[exchanges/jp-exchange-rakuten-wallet|楽天ウォレット]], forms the [[exchanges/jp-cex-points-economy-integration|JP CEX ポイント経済統合]] pattern.

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Business alliance with Coincheck** (concluded 2025-08-05): detailed terms not disclosed
- **Pace of coin expansion**: BTC (2023) → ETH (2024-05) → XRP (2025-04), increasing at a pace of 1  coin(s) per year
- **Expansion of the accumulation feature**: accumulation targets expanded from BTC to ETH, with a daily-accumulation option added (2025-07)
- **XJPY / XUSD industry-wide stablecoins**: at 2023-11  announced participation in joint study with Ginco, Mitsubishi UFJ Trust, and Progmat (commercialization schedule, etc. undisclosed)
- Its positioning within the Mercari Group's three fintech pillars (Merpay: payments/remittance/credit / Mercard: credit card / Mercoin: crypto-assets) is clear. Mercoin is positioned at the core of the blockchain / Web 3  strategy
