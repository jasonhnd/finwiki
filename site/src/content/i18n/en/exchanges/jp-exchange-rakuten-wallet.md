# Rakuten Wallet, Inc. — Japan crypto-asset exchange overview

## 1. Corporation / shareholders

- **Trade name**: Rakuten Wallet, Inc. (English: Rakuten Wallet, Inc.)
- **Date of establishment**: 2016年12月15日 (former trade name: Minna no Bitcoin, Inc.)
- **Head office location**: 〒107-0062  Rakuten Crimson House Aoyama, 6-21号 Minami-Aoyama 2-chome, Minato-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: 1,850百万円 (18.5億円)
- **Parent company**: Rakuten Securities Holdings, Inc. (100% stake). Rakuten Securities Holdings is under Rakuten Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 4755).
- **Corporate number**: 7010401128837
- **Representative Director and President**: Tatsuya Yamada

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00015号 (registration completed 2019年3月25日)
- **Electronic-payment-instrument business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Dendai) 第48号
- **Financial instruments business (Type 1)**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第3190号 (registration completed 2020年5月)
- **JVCEA membership type**: Type 1, member number 1018. Holds both crypto-asset exchange business and crypto-asset derivatives trading qualifications.
- **ISMS certification**: ISO/IEC 27001:2013 (certification registration number IS 510581)

## 3. Handled currencies

**Number of handled currencies**: 16 currencies (as of 2026-05-18, from the official-site handled-crypto-asset list)

| Category | Currencies |
|---------|------|
| With spot trading | BTC, ETH, BCH, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, POL, SOL, TRX, AVAX, DOGE, SHIB, TON |
| Margin trading only | LTC, XTZ |

Margin trading (crypto-asset derivatives) supports the 9 currencies of BTC, ETH, BCH, LTC, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, and XTZ.

**Characteristics**: With its strength in linkage with the Rakuten ecosystem, a balanced currency lineup centered on major coins. Relatively new meme / utility coins such as SHIB and TON are also added sequentially (marked "NEW" as of 2026年).

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot trading (spot)**: yes (smartphone app and web)
- **Crypto-asset margin trading (derivative)**: yes. Service started 2020年3月. Provides a dedicated website, the "Margin Trading Exchange."
- **Rakuten Point exchange**: Rakuten Points (regular points) can be exchanged for spot-trading currencies at 1 points = the equivalent of 1円 (minimum 100 points). Started 2021年3月 as "Point Bitcoin by Rakuten PointClub."
- **Rakuten Cash charge**: held crypto-assets can be converted and charged to Rakuten Cash and used at Rakuten Pay member stores (started 2021年2月).
- **web3 wallet**: separately provides an NFT/web3 wallet app usable with a Rakuten ID (a separate service from the crypto-asset trading app).
- **Corporate accounts**: yes (provides a corporate account-opening service).
- **API**: provides a trading API for external developers (including "GPT-Trade" linkage).
- **Deposit channels**: supports instant deposit linked to a Rakuten Bank account, bank transfer, and deposits/withdrawals supported on weekends and holidays.

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- The number of accounts / monthly trading volume is not published (as of 2026-05-18).
- A customer base leveraging the ecosystem of the Rakuten Group (over 1億 members) is a differentiating factor. Through linkage with Rakuten Points / Rakuten Cash, it aims to onboard Rakuten users with no crypto-asset experience.
- Position among competitors: mid-tier. Amid a configuration where [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|Coincheck]], and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-gmo-coin|GMOコイン]] form the top, it has established a unique position in [[payment-firms/rakuten-fg|楽天経済圏]] (the [[exchanges/jp-cex-points-economy-integration|JP CEX ポイント経済統合]] pattern). It has a stronger retail-specialized character than institutional-investor services.

## 7. Strategic trends

- **Deepening Rakuten-economic-zone integration**: continuing a strategy of treating the over-2,000万 Rakuten Point members as a potential customer base through linkage with Rakuten Points / Rakuten Cash / Rakuten Pay.
- **web3 wallet expansion**: since 2025年, providing NFT / web3-compatible apps as a standalone service. A design that lowers the entry barrier for existing users via single sign-on with a Rakuten ID.
- **GPT-Trade linkage**: providing an AI-leveraging trading interface as an official service (as of 2026年).
- **SPU (Super Point Up Program)**: incorporating the use of Rakuten Wallet into the conditions for boosting the point-reward rate on Rakuten Ichiba purchases, seeking mutual customer referral with other Rakuten services.
- **Handled-currency expansion**: added meme / high-liquidity currencies such as SHIB, TON, and DOGE around 2026年, strengthening the lineup.

## 8. Related figures

- **Tatsuya Yamada**: Representative Director and President (disclosed information)
- **The original starting point**: Traders Holdings (an FX-affiliated corporate group) established the original corporation, but it fully transitioned to the Rakuten Group in 2018年10月. Details of the founder / officers at the time of establishment are not recorded in disclosed materials.
