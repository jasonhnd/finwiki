# Rakuten Wallet, Inc. — Japanese Crypto-Asset Exchange Operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number, Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00015号 · JVCEA member #1018 · Registration date 2019-03-25

## 1. Corporate Entity / Shareholders

- **Trade name**: Rakuten Wallet, Inc. (Japanese: 楽天ウォレット株式会社)
- **Date of establishment**: 2016年12月15日 (former trade name: Minna no Bitcoin Inc.)
- **Head office location**: Rakuten Crimson House Aoyama, 6-21号 Minamiaoyama 2-chome, Minato-ku, Tokyo 〒107-0062 
- **Capital**: 1,850百万円 (18.5億円)
- **Parent company**: Rakuten Securities Holdings, Inc. (holding 100%). Rakuten Securities Holdings is under Rakuten Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 4755).
- **Corporate number**: 7010401128837
- **President and Representative Director**: Tatsuya Yamada

## 2. Licenses / Registration Status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00015号 (registration completed 2019年3月25日)
- **Electronic payment instrument trading business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Dendai) 第48号
- **Financial instruments business (Type I)**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第3190号 (registration completed 2020年5月)
- **JVCEA membership type**: Class I, member number 1018. Holds both crypto-asset exchange business and crypto-asset derivatives trading qualifications.
- **ISMS certification**: ISO/IEC 27001:2013 (certification registration number IS 510581)

## 3. Supported Currencies

**Number of supported assets**: 16 assets (as of 2026-05-18, per the official site's list of supported crypto-assets)

| Category | Assets |
|---------|------|
| Spot trading available | BTC, ETH, BCH, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, POL, SOL, TRX, AVAX, DOGE, SHIB, TON |
| Margin trading only | LTC, XTZ |

Margin trading (crypto-asset derivatives) supports the 9 assets BTC, ETH, BCH, LTC, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, and XTZ.

**Characteristics**: Leverages integration with the Rakuten ecosystem as a strength, with a balanced asset lineup centered on major coins. Relatively new meme / utility coins such as SHIB and TON are also added in sequence (shown as "NEW" as of 2026年).

## 4. Scope of Business

- **Spot trading**: Available (smartphone app and web)
- **Crypto-asset margin trading (derivative)**: Available. Service launched 2020年3月. Provides a dedicated website, "Margin Exchange."
- **Rakuten Point exchange**: Rakuten Points (regular points) can be exchanged for spot-trading assets at 1 points = 1円 equivalent (minimum 100 points). Launched 2021年3月 as "Point Bitcoin by Rakuten PointClub."
- **Rakuten Cash charge**: Held crypto-assets can be converted and charged to Rakuten Cash and used at Rakuten Pay merchants (launched 2021年2月).
- **web3 wallet**: A separate NFT / web3 wallet app usable with a Rakuten ID is provided (a different service from the crypto-asset trading app).
- **Corporate accounts**: Available (corporate account-opening service provided).
- **API**: Provides a trading API for external developers (including "GPT-Trade" integration).
- **Deposit channels**: Supports real-time deposits linked to a Rakuten Bank account, bank transfers, and deposits/withdrawals available on weekends and holidays.

## 5. Market Share / Trading Volume

- Account count and monthly trading volume are not disclosed (as of 2026-05-18).
- A customer base leveraging the ecosystem of the Rakuten Group (over 1億 members) is a differentiating factor. Through integration with Rakuten Points and Rakuten Cash, it seeks to onboard Rakuten users with no crypto-asset experience.
- Position among competitors: Mid tier. While [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|Coincheck]], and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-gmo-coin|GMOコイン]] form the upper ranks, it has established a distinctive position in [[JapanFG/rakuten-fg|楽天経済圏]] (the [[exchanges/jp-cex-points-economy-integration|JP CEX ポイント経済統合]] pattern). It leans more toward a retail-specialized character than institutional-investor services.

## 7. Strategic Developments

- **Deepening integration of the Rakuten economic sphere**: Through integration with Rakuten Points, Rakuten Cash, and Rakuten Pay, continues a strategy of treating the over-2,000万 Rakuten Point members as a potential customer base.
- **web3 wallet rollout**: Since 2025年, provides an NFT- and web3-compatible app as an independent service. A design using single sign-on via Rakuten ID lowers the entry barrier for existing users.
- **GPT-Trade integration**: Provides an AI-driven trading interface as an official service (as of 2026年).
- **SPU (Super Point Up Program)**: Incorporates Rakuten Wallet usage into the conditions for raising the point-reward rate on Rakuten Ichiba purchases, seeking mutual customer referral with other Rakuten services.
- **Expansion of supported assets**: Around 2026年, added meme-type / high-liquidity assets such as SHIB, TON, and DOGE, strengthening the lineup.

## 8. Related People

- **Tatsuya Yamada**: President and Representative Director (publicly disclosed information)
- **Original starting point**: Traders Holdings (an FX-related corporate group) established the original entity, but it fully transitioned to the Rakuten Group in 2018年10月. Details of the founder and officers at the time of establishment are not stated in public materials.
