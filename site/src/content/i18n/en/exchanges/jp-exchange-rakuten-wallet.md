# Rakuten Wallet, Inc. — Japanese Crypto-Asset Exchange Operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA crypto-asset exchange registration system: numbering, Local Finance Bureau jurisdiction, and registration requirements]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|Japanese financial regulation for tokens, crypto-assets, and payments]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number, Kanto Local Finance Bureau No. 00015 · JVCEA member #1018 · Registration date 2019-03-25

## 1. Corporate Entity / Shareholders

- **Trade name**: Rakuten Wallet, Inc. (Japanese: 楽天ウォレット株式会社)
- **Date of establishment**: 2016-12-15 (former trade name: Minna no Bitcoin Inc.)
- **Head office location**: Rakuten Crimson House Aoyama, 6-21 Minamiaoyama 2-chome, Minato-ku, Tokyo 〒107-0062
- **Capital**: JPY 1,850 million (JPY 1.85 billion)
- **Parent company**: Rakuten Securities Holdings, Inc. (holding 100%). Rakuten Securities Holdings is under Rakuten Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 4755).
- **Corporate number**: 7010401128837
- **President and Representative Director**: Tatsuya Yamada

## 2. Licenses / Registration Status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau No. 00015 (registration completed 2019-03-25)
- **Electronic payment instrument trading business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Dendai) No. 48
- **Financial instruments business (Type I)**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) No. 3190 (registration completed 2020-05)
- **JVCEA membership type**: Class I, member number 1018. Holds both crypto-asset exchange business and crypto-asset derivatives trading qualifications.
- **ISMS certification**: ISO/IEC 27001:2013 (certification registration number IS 510581)

## 3. Supported Currencies

**Number of supported assets**: 16 assets (as of 2026-05-18, per the official site's list of supported crypto-assets)

| Category | Assets |
|---------|------|
| Spot trading available | BTC, ETH, BCH, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, POL, SOL, TRX, AVAX, DOGE, SHIB, TON |
| Margin trading only | LTC, XTZ |

Margin trading (crypto-asset derivatives) supports the 9 assets BTC, ETH, BCH, LTC, XRP, ADA, DOT, XLM, and XTZ.

**Characteristics**: Leverages integration with the Rakuten ecosystem as a strength, with a balanced asset lineup centered on major coins. Relatively new meme / utility coins such as SHIB and TON are also added in sequence (shown as "NEW" as of 2026).

## 4. Scope of Business

- **Spot trading**: Available (smartphone app and web)
- **Crypto-asset margin trading (derivative)**: Available. Service launched 2020-03. Provides a dedicated website, "Margin Exchange."
- **Rakuten Point exchange**: Rakuten Points (regular points) can be exchanged for spot-trading assets at 1 point = JPY 1 equivalent (minimum 100 points). Launched 2021-03 as "Point Bitcoin by Rakuten PointClub."
- **Rakuten Cash charge**: Held crypto-assets can be converted and charged to Rakuten Cash and used at Rakuten Pay merchants (launched 2021-02).
- **web3 wallet**: A separate NFT / web3 wallet app usable with a Rakuten ID is provided (a different service from the crypto-asset trading app).
- **Corporate accounts**: Available (corporate account-opening service provided).
- **API**: Provides a trading API for external developers (including "GPT-Trade" integration).
- **Deposit channels**: Supports real-time deposits linked to a Rakuten Bank account, bank transfers, and deposits/withdrawals available on weekends and holidays.

## 5. Market Share / Trading Volume

- Account count and monthly trading volume are not disclosed (as of 2026-05-18).
- A customer base leveraging the ecosystem of the Rakuten Group (over 100 million members) is a differentiating factor. Through integration with Rakuten Points and Rakuten Cash, it seeks to onboard Rakuten users with no crypto-asset experience.
- Position among competitors: Mid tier. While [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|Coincheck]], and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-gmo-coin|GMO Coin]] form the upper ranks, it has established a distinctive position in [[payment-firms/rakuten-fg|Rakuten economic sphere]] (the [[exchanges/jp-cex-points-economy-integration|JP CEX point-economy integration]] pattern). It leans more toward a retail-specialized character than institutional-investor services.

## 7. Strategic Developments

- **Deepening integration of the Rakuten economic sphere**: Through integration with Rakuten Points, Rakuten Cash, and Rakuten Pay, continues a strategy of treating the over-20 million Rakuten Point members as a potential customer base.
- **web3 wallet rollout**: Since 2025, provides an NFT- and web3-compatible app as an independent service. A design using single sign-on via Rakuten ID lowers the entry barrier for existing users.
- **GPT-Trade integration**: Provides an AI-driven trading interface as an official service (as of 2026).
- **SPU (Super Point Up Program)**: Incorporates Rakuten Wallet usage into the conditions for raising the point-reward rate on Rakuten Ichiba purchases, seeking mutual customer referral with other Rakuten services.
- **Expansion of supported assets**: Around 2026, added meme-type / high-liquidity assets such as SHIB, TON, and DOGE, strengthening the lineup.

## 8. Related People

- **Tatsuya Yamada**: President and Representative Director (publicly disclosed information)
- **Original starting point**: Traders Holdings (an FX-related corporate group) established the original entity, but it fully transitioned to the Rakuten Group in 2018-10. Details of the founder and officers at the time of establishment are not stated in public materials.
