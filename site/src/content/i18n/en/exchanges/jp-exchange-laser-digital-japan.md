## 1. Corporate / Shareholders

- **Trade name**: Laser Digital Japan (same in English)
- **Established**: 2023 年 (year details not disclosed)
- **Head office**: Toyosu Bayside Cross Tower, 2-2-1  Toyosu, Koto-ku, Tokyo

- **Ultimate parent**: Nomura Holdings, Inc. (TSE Prime: 8604). Nomura HD established Laser Digital Holdings AG in 2022-09  and brought the group under its umbrella.
- **Capital ratio**: Nomura HD holds 100% of Laser Digital Holdings AG (disclosed). The specific shareholding ratio of the Japanese entity is not disclosed.

## 2. License / Registration status

- **JVCEA Type II member**: Member number #2037 (does not hold exchange-format crypto-asset exchange registration)
- **Crypto-asset exchange business (FSA registration)**: As of 2025-10 , at the pre-consultation stage with the FSA (Bloomberg report). As of 2026-05-18, the JVCEA member list does not record an exchange-business registration number. Confirming the FSA registration status requires direct verification of the original XLSX table.
- **Financial instruments business**: Cannot be confirmed from public materials ("not disclosed").
- **Group other-location licenses**: Laser Digital Middle East FZE (Dubai VARA VL/23/06/001), Laser Digital UK Ltd (FCA FRN 1000108), ADGM Financial Services Permission (obtained 2026 ).

## 3. Supported currencies

- Because the business model centers on institutional-investor OTC and market-making, no list of supported assets for general retail accounts is disclosed.
- At the group level, it offers trading and fund products targeting major assets such as BTC / ETH.
- The Japanese entity currently primarily plays the role of "providing operational services to the group" and does not conduct direct retail trading.

## 4. Scope of business

- **Trading (OTC / market-making)**: As the Laser Digital group, provides OTC trading, token market-making, and liquidity provision. Provides two-way price streams to institutions via API and GUI.
- **Asset management**: Renewed the Bitcoin Adoption Fund (launched 2023 年) as the Bitcoin Diversified Yield Fund SP (BDYF, upgraded 2026-01 ). Cayman-domiciled, limited to institutional and qualified investors.
- **Solutions (cross-asset / treasury management)**: TradFi × crypto-asset fusion finance solutions.
- **Early-stage investment**: Direct investment in and incubation of digital-asset ecosystem companies (KAIO tokenization platform, etc.).
- **Positioning of the Japanese entity (LDJ)**: Operational service provision to the group. Plans to develop broker-dealer business for domestic financial institutions and crypto-asset trading operators (Bloomberg 2025-10  report).

## 5. Market share / Trading volume

- Both trading volume and account count are undisclosed (because it specializes in institutional business).
- Customer base: Specialized in institutional investors and qualified accredited investors. Trading services for general individuals in Japan are not currently offered.
- Position among competitors: Institutional digital-asset specialist (a Nomura HD crypto-asset entity alongside Crypto Garage). Outside the scope of retail-trading-volume rankings.

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Core of the [[securities-firms/nomura-hd|野村グループ]] digital asset strategy**: Unlike [[exchanges/jp-exchange-crypto-garage|Crypto Garage]] (Nomura × MUFG × SBI JV line), Laser Digital is a global digital-asset specialist subsidiary directly established and held by Nomura HD in 100%. As a whole group, it promotes the construction of an institutional ecosystem for trading, AM, and custody.
- **Division of roles with [[exchanges/jp-custody-komainu|Komainu]]**: Komainu (established 2018 年, a JV of Nomura × CoinShares × Ledger) handles custody and infrastructure. Laser Digital handles trading and asset management. Laser Digital's Steve Ashley and Jez Mohideen (CEO) sit on the Komainu Board, indicating a close cooperative structure between the two companies.
- **BDYF (natively tokenized BTC yield fund)**: The flagship product of 2025-2026 年. Positioned as the first natively tokenized BTC yield fund domiciled in Cayman.
- **US national trust bank application (2026-01)**: Through the de novo application to the OCC, lays the groundwork for custody and spot trading in the US.
- **Functional evolution of the Japanese entity**: Appears to be transitioning from "in pre-consultation aiming for FSA registration" as of 2025-10 , through JVCEA Type 2  membership, into a full-scale domestic-business expansion phase. Continued attention to the disclosure status of the domestic FSA registration number is needed.
- **AML × regulatory cooperation**: Participation in the FSA FinTech PoC Hub in 2026-02  is a move that clarifies a coordinated stance with regulatory authorities.
