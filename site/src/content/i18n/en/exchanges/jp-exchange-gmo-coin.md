# GMO Coin, Inc. — Japan crypto-asset exchange operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA crypto-asset exchange registration system]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|Japan financial regulation for tokens, crypto assets, and payments]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00006号 · JVCEA member #1006 · registration date 2017-09-29

## 1. Corporate entity / shareholders

- **Trade name**: GMO Coin, Inc.
- **Established**: 2016-10
- **Head office**: 〒150-0043  Shibuya FUKURAS, 1-2-3  Dogenzaka, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: JPY 3.758 billion, including capital reserve; value published on the official site in 2026-05
- **Parent company**: GMO Financial Holdings, Inc. (a 100% subsidiary of that company)
- GMO Financial Holdings is an intermediate holding company positioned under GMO Internet Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 9449). It is the financial sub-group consolidating the crypto-asset, FX, and foreign-exchange businesses.
- Listing status: unlisted (the parent GMO Internet Group is listed)
- **Corporate number**: 7011001113188

## 2. Licenses / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00006号 (registration date 2017-09-29)
- **Financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments) 第3188号
  - Financial-instruments registration to conduct crypto-asset derivatives trading (crypto-asset FX / exchange leverage)
- **JVCEA member**: Type I member #1006 (both crypto-asset exchange business + crypto-asset derivatives trading qualifications)
- **Member associations**: Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA), Financial Futures Association of Japan (FFAJ)
  - FFAJ membership is for the concurrent foreign-exchange FX business (financial futures business)

## 3. Listed currencies

- **Sales outlet**: 22 listings as of 2026-05: BTC/ETH/BCH/LTC/XRP/XLM/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/DAI/LINK/DOGE/SOL/FIL/SAND/CHZ/AVAX/SUI/ZPG/ZPGAG/ZPGPT
  - First in Japan to handle the Zipangcoin (ZPG) series, linked to gold / silver / platinum, in 2026-04
  - First in Japan to handle Wilder World (WILD) in 2026-03
- **Exchange (spot)**: 19 listings (BTC/ETH/BCH/LTC/XRP/XLM/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/DAI/LINK/FCR/DOGE/SOL/ASTR/NAC/SUI/WILD)
- **Staking supported**: the 7 listings of ETH/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/SOL/ASTR (auto-distribution / segregated management)
- Distinctive features: claims to have the "largest number in Japan" of exchange-leverage listings (per 2024年4月12日 research). Also handles proprietary IEO listings such as NOT A HOTEL COIN (NAC) and FCR Coin.

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot trading**: sales outlet (2Way price) + exchange (order-book trading / Maker negative fee -0.01%)
- **Derivatives**: exchange leverage (max 2x / individuals) + crypto-asset FX (same)
- **Foreign-exchange FX**: handled as a financial instruments business. 21 currency pairs (as of 2026年5月), max 25x leverage.
- **Crypto-asset lending**: Basic (fixed-term / max 10% annual rate) + Premium, the 2 types
- **Staking**: 7 listings / monthly automatic distribution on a holdings basis
- **Crypto-asset accumulation**: accumulation service from JPY 500
- **IEO**: previously conducted with FCR, NAC, etc.
- **Corporate services**: corporate accounts supported, API trading (Public/Private), WebTrader (high-function trading tool)
- **Deposit channels**: bank transfer (GMO Aozora Net Bank recommended), instant deposit (Pay-easy, internet banking, etc.), and instant withdrawal, started 2026-02-09

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- Published monthly trading volume / account count: not disclosed (no standalone IR disclosure)
- Figures are included as "crypto-asset business" in GMO Internet Group's consolidated accounts, but GMO Coin's standalone trading volume is not regularly disclosed
- Industry position: with FSA registration number 00006, the sixth registered operator, it ranks in the upper tier by operating history and regulatory continuity. The combined model of spot trading, derivatives, and foreign-exchange FX places it alongside [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|Coincheck]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]], and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-sbi-vc-trade|SBI VC Trade]], although public numerical support remains limited. For the regulatory boundary of staking / lending business, see [[exchanges/jp-cex-staking-lending-regulation|JP CEX staking and lending regulation]].
- Customer base: mainly individual retail investors. Also addresses trader / institutional demand through corporate accounts / API provision

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Aggressive addition of new listings**: in 2026 alone, sequentially listed SUI, WILD, ZPG series, and related assets, strategically pushing first-in-Japan listings.
- **Expansion of foreign-exchange FX**: in 2026-05 expanded currency pairs to 21, adding high-interest and exotic pairs such as HUF/JPY and NOK/SEK.
- **AI integration**: in 2026-02 introduced the AI price alert "SignalWise" and provides external integration with the automated-trading service "GPT-Trade".
- **Instant deposit/withdrawal infrastructure**: deposit via integration with GMO Aozora Net Bank, with instant withdrawal supported from 2026-02.
- **Zero staking-fee campaign**: continuously deployed as a user-acquisition measure
- **Intra-group DLT / SC matters**: GMO Internet Group issues a USD stablecoin (formerly GYEN/ZUSD) through a separate entity, GMO Trust (USA), but this company is not directly involved in the issuance / operation of these coins. As for entry into the domestic stablecoin / electronic-payment-instruments trading business, there is currently no public information

## 8. Related persons

- **Directors who are Audit and Supervisory Committee members**: Yoshiro Nakanou, Fumimaro Kumagai, Daigo Hanaki
- No standalone description of founding circumstances / founder is published; established in 2016-10 as a strategic business of the GMO group.
