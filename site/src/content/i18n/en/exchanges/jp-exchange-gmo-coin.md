# GMO Coin, Inc. — Japan crypto-asset exchange operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00006号 · JVCEA member #1006 · registration date 2017-09-29

## 1. Corporation / shareholders

- **Trade name**: GMOコイン株式会社 / GMO Coin, Inc.
- **Date of establishment**: 2016年10月
- **Head-office location**: 〒150-0043  Shibuya Fukuras (SHIBUYA FUKURAS), 1-2-3  Dogenzaka, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: 37.58億円 (including capital reserves; figure published on the 2026年5月 official site)
- **Parent company**: GMO Financial Holdings, Inc. (a 100% subsidiary of that company)
- GMO Financial Holdings is an intermediate holding company positioned under GMO Internet Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 9449). A financial sub-group that brings together the crypto-asset, FX, and foreign-exchange businesses.
- Listing status: unlisted (the parent GMO Internet Group is listed)
- **Corporate number**: 7011001113188

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00006号 (registration date 2017-09-29)
- **Financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第3188号
  - The Kinsho registration for conducting crypto-asset derivative transactions (crypto-asset FX / exchange leverage)
- **JVCEA member**: Type-1 member #1006 (both crypto-asset exchange business + crypto-asset derivative transaction qualifications)
- **Member associations**: Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA), Financial Futures Association of Japan (FFAJ)
  - FFAJ membership is because it also conducts the foreign-exchange FX business (financial futures business)

## 3. Handled currencies

- **Sales outlet (販売所)**: 22 issues (as of 2026年5月 — BTC/ETH/BCH/LTC/XRP/XLM/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/DAI/LINK/DOGE/SOL/FIL/SAND/CHZ/AVAX/SUI/ZPG/ZPGAG/ZPGPT)
  - First in Japan to handle the Zipangcoin (ZPG) series (linked to gold / silver / platinum) in 2026年4月
  - First in Japan to handle Wilder World (WILD) in 2026年3月
- **Exchange (spot)**: 19 issues (BTC/ETH/BCH/LTC/XRP/XLM/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/DAI/LINK/FCR/DOGE/SOL/ASTR/NAC/SUI/WILD)
- **Staking support**: the 7 issues ETH/XTZ/DOT/ATOM/ADA/SOL/ASTR (automatic distribution / segregated management)
- Distinctive feature: claims to have the "most in Japan" for the number of exchange-leverage issues (per its own research 2024年4月12日). Also handles its own IEO issues such as NOT A HOTEL COIN (NAC) and FCR Coin.

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot trading**: sales outlet (2 Way price) + exchange (order-book trading · Maker negative fee -0.01%)
- **Derivatives**: exchange leverage (up to 2x · individuals) + crypto-asset FX (same)
- **Foreign-exchange FX**: handled as a financial instruments business. 21 currency pairs (as of 2026年5月), up to 25x leverage.
- **Crypto-asset lending**: 2 types — Basic (fixed-term type, up to 10% annual rate) + Premium
- **Staking**: 7 issues · automatically distributed monthly on a holdings basis
- **Crypto-asset accumulation (つみたて)**: accumulation service (from 500円~)
- **IEO**: previously conducted with FCR, NAC, and others
- **Corporate services**: corporate-account support, API trading (Public/Private), WebTrader (high-functionality trading tool)
- **Deposit channels**: bank transfer (GMO Aozora Net Bank recommended), instant deposit (Pay-easy, internet banking, etc.), instant withdrawal (started 2026年2月9日)

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- Published monthly trading volume / number of accounts: not disclosed (no standalone IR disclosure)
- Figures are included as the "crypto-asset business" in GMO Internet Group's consolidated financial results, but GMO Coin's standalone trading volume is not regularly disclosed
- Industry position: with FSA registration number 第00006号 (the 6社 registration), it is in the upper tier in terms of business history and regulatory compliance. The combined structure of spot trading + derivatives + foreign-exchange FX is, together with [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|コインチェック]] · [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitflyer|bitFlyer]] · [[exchanges/jp-exchange-sbi-vc-trade|SBI VC トレード]], thought to be positioned in the upper tier (no numerical basis; awaiting published data). For the regulatory boundary of staking / lending businesses, see [[exchanges/jp-cex-staking-lending-regulation|JP CEX ステーキング・レンディング規制]].
- Customer base: mainly individual retail investors. Also accommodates trader / institutional demand through corporate accounts and API provision

## 7. Strategic trends

- **Aggressive addition of new issues**: in 2026年 alone, listed SUI, WILD, the ZPG series, etc. in sequence. Strategically promotes first-in-Japan listings
- **Expansion of foreign-exchange FX**: in 2026年5月 expanded currency pairs to 21 pairs (adding high-interest / exotic ones such as HUF/JPY · NOK/SEK)
- **AI integration**: in 2026年2月 introduced the AI price alert "SignalWise" and provides external linkage with the automated-trading service "GPT-Trade"
- **Instant deposit/withdrawal infrastructure**: deposits via linkage with GMO Aozora Net Bank, instant-withdrawal support from 2026年2月
- **Zero staking-fee campaign**: continuously rolled out as a user-acquisition measure
- **Group DLT / SC-related**: GMO Internet Group issues a USD stablecoin (formerly GYEN/ZUSD) through a separate entity, GMO Trust (US), but this company is not directly involved in their issuance or operation. As for entry into the domestic stablecoin / electronic-payment-instrument trading business, there is no public information at present
