# SBI SECURITIES Co., Ltd. — Japan crypto-asset derivatives trading operator overview

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Director-General of Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第44号 · JVCEA member #1041 · Holds only the crypto-asset derivatives trading business

## 1. Corporate / Shareholders

- **Trade name**: SBI SECURITIES Co., Ltd. (株式会社SBI証券)
- **English notation**: SBI SECURITIES Co., Ltd.
- **Established**: 1999年4月26日 (date of trade-name change). The corporate entity traces its origin to the former Osawa Securities, evolving through E*TRADE Securities → SBI E*TRADE Securities → the current trade name
- **Head office**: Izumi Garden Tower, 1-chome 6-ban 1号, Roppongi, Minato-ku, Tokyo 〒106-6001  (same building as SBI VC Trade)
- **Capital**: 54,323百万円 (as of 2025年3月31日)
- **Employees**: 997名 (as of 2025年3月31日)
- **Shareholder**: SBI Holdings, Inc. 100% (TSE Prime, securities code 8473)

**Business division among the 3 crypto-asset-related entities within the SBI group** (see §4 ):

| Entity | JVCEA # | Main business | Crypto-asset services |
|------|---------|------|--------------|
| SBI VC Trade Co., Ltd. | 1011 | Crypto-asset specialist | Spot + derivatives + electronic payment instruments (USDC) |
| SBI FX Trade Co., Ltd. | 1026 | FX specialist | Crypto-asset CFD "SBI Bitcoin FX" (BTC/XRP/ETH × 2  currency denominations = 6  instruments) |
| **SBI SECURITIES Co., Ltd.** (this entry) | **1041** | Comprehensive securities / FX | Only crypto-asset CFD trading via OTC CFD "SBI CFD" (started 2025年8月) |

SBI Securities centers on securities, FX, and commodity futures, and does not handle spot crypto-assets themselves. Crypto-assets are handled as OTC CFDs (financial instruments), positioned as an ancillary business.

## 2. License / Registration status

| Business type | Registration number | Notes |
|------|---------|------|
| Type I Financial Instruments Business | Director-General of Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第44号 | Equities, FX, derivatives, etc. |
| Commodity futures business | — | Conducted in a form included within 第44号 |
| Crypto-asset derivatives trading business | Same as Kinsho 第44号 (additional-authorization format) | JVCEA Type I #1041 |

- **JVCEA membership type**: Type I (member number 1041)
- **Held business categories (JVCEA classification)**: Crypto-asset derivatives trading business **only** (does not hold crypto-asset exchange business or electronic-payment-instrument trading business)
- **Member associations**: Japan Securities Dealers Association, Financial Futures Association of Japan, Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association, Japan Investment Advisers Association, Japan STO Association, Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association

## 3. Supported currencies

**Spot trading of crypto-assets is not offered**. Crypto-asset CFDs are offered as OTC CFDs (SBI CFD):

- **Number of supported instruments** (as of 2026年5月): 20 instruments across the entire OTC CFD account (combined total of stock-index CFDs, commodity CFDs, and crypto-asset CFDs). For the crypto-asset-only instruments, the main instruments confirmed from the official site are **Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL)**. The exact number of crypto-asset-only instruments should be verified on the company's service page
- **Feature**: The "one-stop CFD" structure allowing stock indices, commodities, and crypto-asset CFDs to be traded together in 1  account is a first among major online securities firms (per the 2025年8月 press release)

## 4. Scope of business

The company's core business is securities, FX, and commodity futures. Crypto-assets are an ancillary service added in 2025年8月.

| Main service | Overview |
|-----------|------|
| Stock trading (domestic / foreign) | Domestic No.1  online securities firm (by account count) |
| Investment trusts | Over 2,700 funds handled |
| FX (foreign-exchange margin) | 24-hour trading |
| Exchange CFD (Click Kabu 365) | Nikkei 225, etc. |
| OTC CFD "SBI CFD" | Provides stock indices, commodities, and **crypto-asset CFDs** at 1口座. **2025年8月22日** service start |
| NISA account | Reached 500万 accounts in 2024年6月 |
| iDeCo | Individual-type defined-contribution pension |

**Institutional positioning of crypto-asset CFDs (SBI CFD)**:
- "OTC CFD" is a derivatives transaction under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act. Not spot crypto-assets but a contract-for-difference settlement product
- Margin rate for crypto-asset CFDs: 50% of the consideration (the highest level compared with commodities 5% and stock indices 10%)
- Trading hours: Crypto-asset CFDs can be traded nearly 24  hours including weekends

**Fiat deposit channels**: Bank transfer, quick deposit via SBI Sumishin Net Bank, Pay-easy, etc. (common with ordinary securities-account deposits)

## 5. Market share / Trading volume

**Scale in the securities business** (as of 2025年11月):
- **Over 1,500万 securities comprehensive accounts** (reached 2025年11月) — a domestic first and the most in Japan. Greatly exceeds Nomura Securities (approx. 539万 accounts) and Rakuten Securities, ranking industry No.1
- Exchange CFD "Click Kabu 365" account-count share **87.6%** and trading-volume share **51.0%** (the company's 2025年7月 press release, market-share No.1)
- **NISA account count 500万 accounts** (reached 2024年6月)

**Crypto-asset CFD standalone trading volume / account count**: Not disclosed (a service just begun in 2025年8月, with no regular disclosure)

**Customer base**: Centered on individual retail. Leveraging the securities firm's customer base, the structure anticipates cross-selling from securities accounts to crypto-asset CFDs. Corporate services are also provided.

**Position among competitors**: From the perspective of crypto-asset derivatives specialists, it is one of the Type I members 13 社 alongside [[exchanges/jp-exchange-monex|マネックス証券]] (JVCEA #1024), [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bi-fxtrade|SBI FXトレード]] (JVCEA #1026), and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-gmo-coin|GMO コイン]] (JVCEA #1006) (see [[exchanges/jp-vasp-derivative-license-system|JP VASP デリバティブライセンス制度]] for background). However, SBI Securities' core business is securities, and crypto-asset derivatives are positioned as a niche ancillary business.

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Crypto-asset CFD entry (2025年8月)**: Positions crypto-assets as a "one-stop CFD at 1 口座" integrated with stock indices and commodities. Cross-selling to the securities firm's 1,500万 customer accounts is a structural advantage. By avoiding spot handling, it sidesteps crypto-asset exchange registration (separate FSA registration, AML/KYC costs, etc.), entering within the scope of its existing 第44号 licenses
- **SBI HD's crypto-asset strategy (general information)**: SBI Holdings approaches the crypto-asset market through a multi-layered structure: spot (SBI VC Trade), FX/CFD (SBI FX Trade, SBI Securities), stablecoins (SBI VC Trade's electronic-payment-instrument trading business), and XRP-related investment (Ripple Labs investment). SBI HD absorbed BITPoint Japan into SBI VC Trade (2026年4月), consolidating the group's spot services into SBI VC Trade, while distributing derivatives across multiple securities and FX subsidiaries
- **Interest in promoting crypto-asset ETFs**: The SBI HD group has published a "Proposal toward the formation of crypto-asset ETFs etc. in Japan" (2024年10月), actively advancing industry proposals anticipating ETF deregulation after the shift to crypto-asset regulation under the FIEA (the press release in SBI VC Trade's name footnotes SBI Securities' registration number)
