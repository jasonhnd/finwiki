## 2. Licenses / registration status

The company is currently the only operator in Japan that "holds 3 types of licenses simultaneously," singly holding crypto-asset exchange business, financial instruments business (crypto-asset derivatives), and electronic payment instruments business.

| Business type | Registration no. | Notes |
|------|---------|------|
| Crypto-asset exchange business | Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00011号 | Registered 2017-12-01 |
| Type 1 financial instruments business (crypto-asset derivatives) | Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (FIB) 第3247号 | OTC derivatives support |
| Electronic payment instruments business | Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00001号 | Registration completed 2025-03-04, for USDC handling |

- **JVCEA membership type**: Type 1 (member number 1011)
- **Associations joined**: Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA), Japan Crypto Asset Business Association (JCBA), Japan Digital Economy Federation, Japan Digital Decentralized Finance Association, JPCrypto-ISAC

### Electronic payment instruments / USDC cross-link

In the FSA's registration list of electronic payment instruments business operators, SBI VC Trade is listed as a registered operator under Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00001号, with its handled electronic payment instrument given as USDC. The company is therefore not merely a crypto-asset exchange operator but also a primary route for reading Japan's electronic payment instruments / stablecoin implementation.

## 3. Supported currencies

As of 2026年5月, spot-trading targets number roughly 40 tokens. Major coins: BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC, BCH, SOL, ADA, DOGE, DOT, LINK, XLM, AVAX, MATIC, SHIB, TON, SUI, etc. In addition, as electronic payment instruments (EP instruments), it handles **USDC** and JPY stability assets (ZPG, DAI).

Distinctive features:
- **USDC** (USD Coin) became tradable for the general domestic public in 2025年3月25日. Leveraging its electronic payment instruments business registration (第00001号), it offers JPY⇔USDC spot exchange and a **USDC lending service** (launched 2026年3月18日, a domestic first).
- **Staking-supported tokens** (ETH, SOL, etc.) are available.
- A somewhat broader listing lineup than other domestic firms, including **NIDT** (Nippon Idol Token), **FLR** (Flare), and **XDC**.

| Service | Overview |
|---------|------|

| Leveraged trading (OTC derivatives) | Up to 2 x (individuals), JVCEA-prescribed multiples (corporates) |

| Staking | PoS tokens such as ETH |
| SBI Web3  Wallet | Self-custody linkage |

**Fiat-currency deposit channels**: bank transfer, quick deposit (Pay-easy, etc.). Account-maintenance fees, annual fees, JPY deposit/withdrawal fees, and crypto-asset deposit/withdrawal fees are all free.

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Strategic significance of the 3 -license structure**: consolidating crypto-asset exchange business (spot), financial instruments business (derivatives), and electronic payment instruments business (stablecoins) into a single legal entity is unique domestically. In particular, the electronic payment instruments business (USDC handling) was registered in 2025-03 , and JPY-denominated spot exchange and lending of Circle's USDC are being rolled out.
- **Intra-SBI-Group synergy**: a structure that enables linkage with same-group financial instruments business operators such as SBI SECURITIES (FIB 第44号, JVCEA #1041) and BI FXTRADE (FIB 第2635号, JVCEA #1026). SBI Holdings has an equity / partnership relationship with Ripple Labs (XRP-related).
- **The BITPoint Japan merger** (effective 2026-04 ) aims to concentrate intra-group management resources in anticipation of strengthened crypto-asset regulation under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act (per official announcement). After the merger, both the VCTRADE and BITPOINT 2  brands are maintained for the time being.
- It has established a **response desk for MTGOX creditors** and is involved in asset returns connected to the Mt.Gox liquidation (details within the scope of public information).
