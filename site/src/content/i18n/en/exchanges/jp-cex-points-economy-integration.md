## Overview

A business model unique to Japan — integrating the points/currency of existing major platforms into a crypto-asset purchasing channel, simultaneously achieving **new-customer acquisition + reduced KYC cost + loyalty lock-in**. Whereas overseas CEXs (Binance / Coinbase) take a pure-crypto single-brand strategy, in Japan the "existing economic sphere × VASP" hybrid is the dominant structure. The US Cash App / PayPal Crypto Hub is a similar structure, but direct integration with a **points economy** is unique to Japan.

## 4  main partnership models

- **Mercari → Mercoin (BTC/ETH/XRP)** — BTC can be purchased directly with Mercari sales proceeds. Service started 2023-03 . The UX of flea-market sales proceeds → BTC is unique even globally. [[exchanges/jp-exchange-mercoin]]
- **Rakuten → Rakuten Wallet** — 16  crypto-asset tokens can be purchased with Rakuten Points. 2018-10  brought "Minna no Bitcoin" under the Rakuten umbrella. A lock-in tool for the Rakuten economic sphere (securities / banking / cards). [[exchanges/jp-exchange-rakuten-wallet]]
- **PayPay → Binance Japan** — 2025-10  PayPay announced a 40% investment (per reporting). The SoftBank-affiliated payments × crypto-asset partnership is at an exploratory stage; the detailed integration scheme is undisclosed. [[exchanges/jp-exchange-binance-japan]]
- **LINE → LINE Xenesis (BITMAX)** — started with LINK → Finschia → KAIA token linkage, but the service is scheduled for full termination in 2026-06 . A symbol of LY Corp's strategic withdrawal. [[exchanges/jp-exchange-line-xenesis]]

## Business-model analysis

- **CAC (customer-acquisition cost) reduction** — reusing existing platform IDs + KYC to minimize account-opening friction
- **AUM (assets under custody) increase** — lowering the psychological and operational friction of points → crypto-asset conversion
- **Regulatory risk** — the boundary between points (prepaid payment instruments) and crypto assets (Payment Services Act) is an ongoing point of debate in FSA supervisory guidelines

## Overseas comparison

The US Cash App (Bitcoin), PayPal (Crypto Hub), and Revolut Crypto are payment-app-integrated types, but **the direct-exchange model with airline miles / e-commerce points is unique to Japan**. China and South Korea likewise do not permit similar structures.

## Strategic implications

The "finance × platform" hybrid is the competitive axis of domestic CEXs. **LINE's withdrawal vs PayPay's entry** = a sign of SoftBank-affiliated restructuring. Going forward, d Points and PayPay Points are the next integration candidates.
