# bitFlyer Inc. — Japanese crypto-asset exchange operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00003号 · JVCEA member #1002 · registration date 2017-09-29

## 1. Corporate / shareholders

- **Name**: bitFlyer Inc. (株式会社 bitFlyer)
- **Established**: 2014年1月9日
- **Head-office location**: Midtown Tower, 9-7-1  Akasaka, Minato-ku, Tokyo 〒107-6230 
- **Capital**: 41億238万円 (including capital reserve) (value posted on the official site, point in time unspecified)
- **Parent company**: bitFlyer Holdings, Inc. (100%-owned, unlisted, established 2018年10月1日, capital 50億円)
- **Shareholder composition (at the Holdings level)**: Yuzo Kano approx. 40%, Takashi Komiyama approx. 13%, Sekisui House just under 13% (as of 2022年4月). This ratio is consistent with the 2022年4月 Nikkei coverage (a coalition of minority shareholders + the agreement of Komiyama and Sekisui House, under which ACA was expected to secure over 50%) and with the figures posted on Wikipedia. Subsequently, the acquisition by the ACA group fell through at the end of 2022年9月 (Kano's opposition + withdrawal of some shareholders' sale, with the valuation rising to approximately 900億円 as Rakuten and Monex entered the contest), and Kano was reappointed Representative Director in 2023-03-30. Because of these developments, the 2022年4月 ratio has likely changed, but the latest post-reappointment ownership ratio (2023–2026年) is unconfirmed in public information (unlisted, IPO in preparation).
- **Accounting auditor**: Ernst & Young ShinNihon LLC
- **Main bank**: Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation
- **Corporate number**: 2011101068824

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00003号 (registration date 2017-09-29)
- **Financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (FIBO) 第3294号
- **JVCEA member**: Type 1 #1002  — holds both crypto-asset exchange business and crypto-asset derivatives business
- Registration for electronic-payment-instruments business / funds-transfer business unconfirmed

## 3. Handled currencies

Because the handled-asset list page on the official corporate site could not be obtained, refer to the asset list posted on Wikipedia (a secondary source) (date obtained 2026-05-18): BTC, ETH, ETC, BCH, LTC, MONA, LSK, XRP, BAT, XLM, XEM, XTZ, DOT, LINK, XYM, MATIC, MKR, ZPG, FLR, SHIB, PLT — 21 assets in total (subject to change). Official confirmation needed.

**Characteristics**: BTC trading volume has continued to rank among the top domestically (since the first half of 2016年). There is a dedicated Lightning (high-speed trading) order book for BTC/JPY (Lightning Spot). In addition to yen-denominated spot, leveraged trading (Lightning FX) is also offered.

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot trading (sales counter / exchange)**: yes (Lightning Spot)
- **Derivatives (Lightning FX)**: yes (as financial instruments business)
- **Crypto-asset lending**: unknown (cannot be officially confirmed)
- **Credit card**: "bitFlyer Credit Card" (issued by Aplus, started 2021年12月. A type that accrues bitcoin.)
- **Fiat deposit channels**: bank transfer (Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation and others) / quick-deposit support (disclosed)
- **Corporate-facing services**: "Bitcoin Payment Service" (multiple track records including Marui Group and IDOM)
- **Points linkage**: history of linkage with T-Points, Moppy, PeX, etc.

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- No. 1位 in domestic bitcoin trading volume in the first half of 2016年 (bitFlyer official announcement, posted on 2016-08-15 GoodWay)
- 2025年12月 fiscal year revenue: 135億6700万円, operating profit: 42億5700万円 (bitFlyer Inc. 12-term settlement announcement / Official Gazette dated 2025-05-23)
- As of 2020年3月, cumulative Japan–US–Europe customers surpassed 250万 (official announcement, Crypto Watch)
- Public data on account numbers / recent trading volume unconfirmed (treated as not disclosed)
- **Competitive positioning**: within the domestic top 3 , with main competitors being [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck|Coincheck]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-gmo-coin|GMOコイン]], [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitbank|bitbank]], etc. (continuously ranking high in both trading volume and account numbers since 2016年, with the history of an early FSA registration number 第00003号). For JVCEA self-regulation and the membership system, see [[exchanges/jvcea-self-regulatory-overview|JVCEA 自主規制体系]].

## 7. Strategic developments

- **2024年6月 FTX Japan acquisition**: announced the acquisition of the Japanese entity of bankruptcy-filed FTX (see [[exchanges/ftx-japan-100pct-return-case-study|FTX Japan 100% 返還ケーススタディ]]). The aim is believed to be the acquisition of a customer base and license assets in Japan (official press release 2024-06-20).
- **Recent financial-performance improvement**: 2025年12月 fiscal year operating profit 42億5700万円 (a trend of improvement versus the prior period — per the Holdings 12-term business report).
- **Status of overseas entities**: the overseas expansion of bitFlyer USA, Inc. (a Delaware entity) and bitFlyer EUROPE S.A. (Luxembourg) at the time of 2018年 is disclosed, but the operating status as of 2026年5月 cannot be officially confirmed.
- **Tsubasa Honda used in commercials**: from 2024年9月, advertising campaigns using Tsubasa Honda (official release 2024-08-28).

## 8. Related people

- **Yuzo Kano** (かのう ゆうぞう) — co-founder / Representative Director. Served as representative from the founding in 2014年, and after stepping down once was reappointed in 2023年3月. A face of the industry who led the establishment of the Japan Blockchain Association (JBA).
- **Takashi Komiyama** (こみやま たかし) — co-founder. A shareholder at the Holdings level with approx. 13% (as of 2022年4月, at the time of Nikkei coverage). In the 2022年 ACA acquisition he had agreed to sell, but that acquisition fell through. His current ownership ratio after reappointment is unconfirmed.
- **Keisei Hirako** (ひらこ けいせい) — Representative Director 2019年1月–2020年3月.
- **Tomohiro Mine** (みね ともひろ) — Representative Director 2020年3月–2021年3月.
- **Kuniyoshi Hayashi** (はやし くによし) — Representative Director 2021年3月–2023年3月.
- **Ryota Sasaki** (ささき りょうた) — Holdings director (as of 2024年).
- **Joel Edgerton** — Holdings director (as of 2024年).
- **Sekisui House** — as a Holdings shareholder, held just under 13% (as of 2022年4月, at the time of Nikkei coverage). In the 2022年 ACA acquisition it had indicated an intention to sell, but that acquisition fell through. Its subsequent holding / sale status is unconfirmed in public information.
