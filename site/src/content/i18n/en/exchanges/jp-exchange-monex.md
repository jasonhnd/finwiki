# マネックス株式会社 — Japan crypto-asset-derivatives trading operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第165号 · JVCEA member #1024  · crypto-asset derivatives trading only (spot trading is handled by the subsidiary Coincheck)

## 1. Corporate / shareholders

- **Trade name**: マネックス株式会社 (English: MONEX, Inc.)
- **Common name**: Monex Securities (widely used as a common name / service name rather than the trade name)
- **Established**: 1999年 (originally established as Monex-Beans Securities)
- **Head office location**: 1-12-32
 Akasaka, Minato-ku, Tokyo
- **Parent company**: Monex Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 8698)
- **Listing status**: Unlisted (a wholly-owned subsidiary of Monex Group)

**Division of roles within the group** (important):
- マネックス株式会社 (this entry): **crypto-asset derivatives (CFD)** trading business + online securities business (main business)
- [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck]] (subsidiary): **spot crypto-asset exchange business** (FSA registration number 00014, JVCEA #1017)
- Monex, Inc. (US subsidiary): US securities business

マネックス株式会社 is not a crypto-asset exchange service provider (spot trading); it provides crypto-asset CFDs (contracts for difference) as a financial instruments business. Its own site also explicitly states, "We are not a crypto-asset exchange service provider, and we do not provide crypto-asset trading."

## 2. License / registration status

- **Financial instruments business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第165号
- **JVCEA membership type**: Type I member #1024 (**crypto-asset derivatives trading only**)
- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: none (no spot-exchange-business registration)

**Associations joined**:
- Japan Securities Dealers Association
- Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association
- Financial Futures Association of Japan
- Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA)
- Japan Investment Advisers Association

## 3. Handled currencies

As Monex Bitcoin (crypto-asset CFD), it handles the following against-the-yen pairs:

- BTC/JPY (Bitcoin)
- XRP/JPY (XRP)
- ETH/JPY (Ethereum)
- Other major instruments (the number of published instruments is not disclosed)

**Characteristics**: No spot holding of crypto assets whatsoever. Everything is contracts for difference (CFD) only. Leverage up to 2 x. Entry from the sell side supported (short possible).

## 4. Scope of operations

**Core business (principal business)**: internet securities business
- Brokerage and dealer operations for domestic equities / US equities / Chinese equities / investment trusts / FX / futures and options / gold and platinum / bonds, etc.
- NISA / iDeCo support

**Crypto-asset-related (ancillary business)**:
- Crypto-asset CFD (Monex Bitcoin) only
- No spot trading / physical custody / wallet / staking, etc. provided
- Trading fees: free (revenue from spreads and leverage fees)
- Trading tool: dedicated smartphone app "MONEX TRADER CRYPTO" (iOS / Android)
- Margin ratio: 50% or above (i.e., leverage up to 2 x)
- Trading hours: in principle 24  hours, 365  days including weekends (excluding weekly maintenance)

**Fiat-currency deposit channels**: via the comprehensive securities trading account (bank transfer / quick deposit, etc.). No dedicated direct deposit channel for the crypto-asset CFD is published.

## 6. History / major incidents

- **1999年**: Established as Monex-Beans Securities (one of Japan's first internet-specialist securities firms)
- **2000年 era**: there was a period with support from Sony Financial (group transitions)
- **2019年**: Acquired Coincheck, Inc. (after the 2018 年 NEM outflow incident). Incorporated it under the Monex Group umbrella, with a structure where the crypto-asset spot business is handled by Coincheck
- **From around 2023年 onward**: Monex Bitcoin (crypto-asset CFD) service launched (as a JVCEA derivatives member)
- Recent administrative actions / large-scale system failures: none published (as of the access date 2026-05-18)

**Note**: The 2018 年 Coincheck NEM (XEM) outflow incident (about 580 億円 in scale) occurred at Coincheck, not at マネックス株式会社 itself. Monex was the party that subsequently acquired Coincheck.
