# Monex, Inc. — Japanese crypto-asset derivatives trading operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Director-General of Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第165号 · JVCEA member #1024 · Crypto-asset derivatives trading only (spot trading is handled by subsidiary Coincheck)

## 1. Corporate entity / shareholders

- **Trade name**: Monex, Inc. (Japanese: マネックス株式会社)
- **Common name**: Monex Securities (widely used as a common name / service name rather than the trade name)
- **Established**: 1999年 (originally established as Monex Beans Securities)
- **Head office location**: 1-12-32
 Akasaka, Minato-ku, Tokyo- **Parent company**: Monex Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 8698)
- **Listing status**: Unlisted (wholly-owned subsidiary of Monex Group)

**Intra-group division of roles** (important):
- Monex, Inc. (this entry): **Crypto-asset derivatives (CFD)** trading business + online securities business (main business)
- [[exchanges/jp-exchange-coincheck]] (subsidiary): **Spot crypto-asset exchange business** (FSA registration number 00014, JVCEA #1017)
- Monex, Inc. (U.S. subsidiary): U.S. securities business

Monex, Inc. is not a crypto-asset exchange operator (spot trading); rather, it offers crypto-asset CFDs (contracts for difference) as a financial instruments business. Its own website also explicitly states, "This company is not a crypto-asset exchange operator and does not provide crypto-asset trading."

## 2. Licensing / registration status

- **Financial instruments business**: Director-General of Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Kinsho) 第165号
- **JVCEA membership type**: Type 1 member #1024 (**crypto-asset derivatives trading only**)
- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: None (no spot exchange business registration)

**Affiliated associations**:
- Japan Securities Dealers Association
- Type II Financial Instruments Firms Association (general incorporated association)
- Financial Futures Association of Japan (general incorporated association)
- Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA) (general incorporated association)
- Japan Investment Advisers Association (general incorporated association)

## 3. Handled currencies

As Monex Bitcoin (crypto-asset CFD), the following yen pairs are handled:

- BTC/JPY (Bitcoin)
- XRP/JPY (XRP)
- ETH/JPY (Ethereum)
- Other major issues (number of disclosed issues not published)

**Characteristics**: No physical holding of crypto-assets whatsoever. All are contracts for difference (CFD) only. Maximum leverage 2 x. Supports entry from the sell side (shorting possible).

## 4. Scope of business

**Main business (principal business)**: Internet securities business
- Brokerage and intermediary business for buying and selling domestic stocks, U.S. stocks, Chinese stocks, investment trusts, FX, futures and options, gold and platinum, bonds, etc.
- NISA / iDeCo supported

**Crypto-asset related (ancillary business)**:
- Crypto-asset CFD (Monex Bitcoin) only
- Spot trading, physical custody, wallets, staking, etc. are not provided
- Trading fees: Free (revenue from spread and leverage fees)
- Trading tool: Dedicated smartphone app "MONEX TRADER CRYPTO" (iOS / Android)
- Margin ratio: 50% or higher (i.e., maximum leverage 2 x)
- Trading hours: In principle 24  hours, 365  days including Saturdays and Sundays (excluding weekly maintenance)

**Fiat-currency deposit channel**: Via the comprehensive securities trading account (bank transfer, quick deposit, etc.). A dedicated direct deposit channel for crypto-asset CFDs is not published.

## 6. History / major incidents

- **1999年**: Established as Monex Beans Securities (one of Japan's earliest internet-specialized securities firms)
- **2000年s**: There was a period of support from Sony Financial (group transition)
- **2019年**: Acquired Coincheck, Inc. (after the 2018 年 NEM outflow incident). Incorporated under the Monex Group, with a structure in which Coincheck handles the crypto-asset spot business
- **From around 2023年 onward**: Launched the Monex Bitcoin (crypto-asset CFD) service (as a JVCEA derivatives member)
- Recent administrative dispositions / large-scale system failures: Not published (as of retrieval date 2026-05-18)

**Note**: The 2018 年 NEM (XEM) outflow incident at Coincheck (on the order of about 580 億円) occurred at Coincheck, not at Monex, Inc. itself. Monex was the party that subsequently acquired Coincheck.
