FINX JCrypto is a registered Japanese crypto asset exchange operator. The current FSA list shows registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau No.00012 and a broad asset lineup including BTC, ETH, XRP, XLM, BCH, DAI, DOT, LTC, SHIB, OAS, ADA, AVAX, BNB, DOGE, PEPE, POL, SOL, and TRX.

The public company-facing surface is narrower than many retail exchanges. The official company page emphasizes crypto-asset exchange registration and real-estate / crypto-asset transaction adjacency. Treat this as a licensed operator page, not as evidence of high retail-exchange market share.
