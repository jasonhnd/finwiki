## 1. Entity / shareholders

- **Trade name**: 株式会社 Crypto Garage (English: Crypto Garage, Inc.)
- **Established**: 2018年9月
- **Head office location**: Frances Building, 2-19-9  Ebisu-nishi, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo
- **Corporate number**: 6011001124112
- **Capital structure** (as disclosed):
  - Digital Garage, Inc. (TSE Prime 4819) is the lead investor. The joint-venture partner at founding was Tokyo Tanshi Co., Ltd. In 2021年10月 a third-party allotment of new shares was carried out with Nomura Holdings, Inc. as the subscriber, and Nomura HD joined as a new investor.
  - Blockstream is also listed in the "Backed By" section of the corporate site, publicly indicating a capital / technology partnership.
  - The specific shareholding ratios of each company are not disclosed.

Digital Garage positions the company as a strategic subsidiary in the fintech / blockchain field and has dispatched 2 名 directors (Kaoru Hayashi, Keizo Odori). Tokyo Tanshi has also dispatched 2 名 directors (Toshiaki Terada, Misazo Kato).

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director 第00029号 (registration date: 2021年6月29日, under the Payment Services Act)
- **Financial instruments business**: Not registered (JVCEA Class-I member #1035  is for crypto-asset exchange business only, with no derivatives registration)
- **Electronic payment instruments trading business / funds transfer business**: Not registered (as disclosed)
- **JVCEA**: Class-I member #1035 (crypto-asset exchange business only)
- **Global Digital Finance**: Member (industry self-regulatory body)

## 3. Currencies handled

The number of tradable assets and a specific list are not individually enumerated on the official site or in disclosed documents; not disclosed.

Asset characteristics inferred from the service design:
- **Bitcoin (BTC)** is the core asset (R&D themes are Bitcoin / Lightning / Liquid Network, and BTC-denominated OTC / settlement are the core)
- SETTLENET PRO offers "JPY-denominated tokens (JPY-based) × on-chain DvP settlement," using JPY-denominated tokens (internal settlement currency)
- The company's R&D includes Discreet Log Contracts (DLC) and Liquid Network sidechain assets
- As it is an institution-focused type rather than a retail-facing multi-asset exchange, the number of tradable assets is inferred to be a small, select set

## 4. Scope of business

The company is **not a retail-facing spot exchange**, but an operator specialized in digital-asset infrastructure for institutions / corporates (self-described: "regulated institutional digital assets infrastructure company"). The main service lines are as follows:

| Service | Content |
|----------|------|
| **SETTLENET PRO** | Intermediation / OTC settlement platform in the crypto-asset inter-dealer market. Provides non-custodial DvP settlement, JPY-denominated tokens (issued by the company), and point-in-time net settlement functions |
| **SETTLENET CUSTODY** | Domestically-regulation-compliant digital-asset custody service for institutional investors / corporates (launched 2023年4月). Insured segregated management, staking (added 2024年3月), and OES (Off Exchange Settlement) functions |
| **SETTLENET OTC** | Crypto-asset OTC sales desk for corporates / operating companies (launched 2023年4月). Leverages a global LP network; premium service with email/phone support |
| **R&D** | Research and development on Bitcoin / Lightning / Liquid Network, OSS contributions to the Discreet Log Contracts (DLC) protocol, and research on P2P derivatives trading |
| **Blockchain Dev** | Blockchain development outsourcing for corporate clients |
| **mahola** | Blockchain backend service (API), officially launched 2024年10月 |

**Fiat deposit channels**: For corporates only (bank transfer). No retail-facing channels such as convenience-store deposit or quick deposit.

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- Both trading volume and number of accounts are **not disclosed**
- **Customer base**: Specialized in institutional investors, operating companies, crypto-asset exchanges, liquidity providers, high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and asset managers. Does not provide services for general retail investors
- Market position: A **niche (institutional infrastructure)** player that does not compete on retail trading volume. Inferred to target on the order of several dozen corporate clients, but quantitative data is not disclosed

## 7. Strategic developments

- Consistently maintains an **institution-focused × Bitcoin-native** line. SETTLENET PRO has established a niche position as a domestically one-of-a-kind class within the crypto-asset inter-dealer market
- **Collaboration with Blockstream / Liquid Network**: Blockstream is listed in the official site's "Backed By" section. With R&D themes specialized in Bitcoin / Lightning / Liquid Network, this suggests a role as a key adoption partner for Liquid Network sidechain technology in the Japanese market (the specific investment amount / contract terms with Blockstream are not disclosed)
- **Nomura group × Komainu collaboration** (2023年11月): Collaboration in the institutional-investor custody-service domain. Through coordination with [[exchanges/jp-custody-komainu|Komainu]] (a joint-venture custodian of [[JapanFG/nomura-hd|Nomura HD]] × Ledger × CoinShares), it is advancing global-level institutional infrastructure development (the company's position is detailed in [[exchanges/jp-institutional-custody-three-pillars|JP institutional custody three pillars]] and [[exchanges/jp-crypto-market-maker-otc-layer|JP crypto market maker / OTC layer]])
- **Expansion of web3  development outsourcing**: Since 2024年, blockchain-development support projects in non-crypto domains — such as Nissan Motor, the EIKEN Foundation of Japan, SKY Perfect JSAT, and Shiwa Town in Iwate Prefecture — have increased. Diversifying the developer-facing business line via the mahola API
- **Most recent 12  months (as of 2025年5月)**: Jointly offered crypto-asset-holding consulting with Pacific Meta (2025年8月), and a paper accepted at IEEE Blockchain 2025  (2025年12月). No public announcements regarding IPO / overseas expansion
