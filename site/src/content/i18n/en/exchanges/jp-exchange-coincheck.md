# Coincheck, Inc. — Japanese crypto-asset exchange operator overview

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00014号 · JVCEA member #1017 · registration date 2019-01-11

## 1. Entity · shareholders

- **Trade name**: Coincheck Inc. / Coincheck, Inc. (Japanese entity)
- **Established**: 2012年 (established as Wonder Inc. Changed its trade name to Coincheck Inc. in 2014年)
- **Head office**: Shibuya Sakura Stage SHIBUYA side 27F, 1-4号 Sakuragaoka-cho, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo
- **Corporate number**: 1010001148860

- **Parent company**: Monex Group, Inc. (TSE Prime 8698)
  - 2018年4月 Monex Group made it a wholly owned subsidiary through share acquisition (acquisition price approx. 36億円)
- **Listed holding company**: Coincheck Group N.V. (a Dutch public limited liability company, headquartered in Amsterdam)
  - Listed on Nasdaq (tickers CNCK / CNCKW). The direct parent of Coincheck Inc.
  - Monex Group is the largest shareholder of Coincheck Group N.V. (retaining a majority even after the merger completed)

## 2. License · registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00014号 (registration date 2019-01-11)
- **JVCEA member**: Type I member #1017 (crypto-asset exchange business only; derivatives-trading business not authorized)
- Registration for electronic-payment-instruments-related business · Financial Instruments Business · funds-transfer business is not confirmed within the scope of JVCEA public information

## 3. Handled currencies

- **Number of handled tokens**: 18 tokens as of 2022年9月 (+1  QoQ). Continuing to expand as needed thereafter
- **Major tokens**: BTC, ETH, ETC, XRP, NEM (XEM), LTC, XLM, MONA, BAT, QTUM, LSK, XEM, ENJ, SAND, AXS, etc.
- **Distinctive features**: an early mover domestically in multi-token support. The first to introduce IEO (Initial Exchange Offering) functionality in Japan. Operates an NFT marketplace (Coincheck NFT). Spot-centric; does not offer margin / derivatives trading

## 4. Scope of business

- **Spot trading**: provides both the exchange (order-book trading) + the sales outlet (OTC)
- **Crypto-asset lending** (Coincheck Lending): borrows crypto-assets from customers and uses them for cover transactions
- **NFT marketplace**: Coincheck NFT (β). 1 primary sales with IP holders (BtoC) + 2 secondary trading between users (CtoC)
- **IEO**: rolls out the first IEO service in Japan as "Coincheck IEO"
- **Fiat deposits**: bank transfer · quick deposit (convenience store · Pay-easy, etc.)
- **Web3 / metaverse**: Coincheck Labs (startup support · investment), NFT LAND sales for the metaverse (The Sandbox, Otherside, Decentraland, etc.)
- Also provides corporate-oriented services (OTC large-lot trading, etc.)

## 5. Market share · trading volume

- **App downloads**: No.1 crypto-asset trading app in Japan (5 consecutive years 2019–2023年, per AppTweak)
- **Verified user count**: 175万 (end of 2022年9月) → 183万 (end of 2023年6月)
- **Assets in custody (cold wallet)**:
  - End of 2022年9月: approx. 2,796億円
  - End of 2023年6月: approx. 3,465億円
  - End of 2024年12月: approx. 1兆305億円 (Coincheck Group N.V. financial disclosure 2025-02)
- **Trading volume**: spot trading volume 2Q FYE Mar.2022 (3 months) 7,695百万円 (at the time). It contracted sharply during the market downturn (around 2023年). It expanded sharply in FY 2024年 after the market recovered. For individual monthly data, see JVCEA statistics
- **Competitive position**: the largest scale domestically by verified-account count. Together with bitFlyer · GMO Coin, etc., it is among the top 3社

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Connection to global capital markets**: after the Nasdaq listing, strengthened IR activities aimed at US institutional investors. As Coincheck Group N.V., implements continuous SEC disclosure such as F-1 / F-3 / 6-K (CIK: 0001913847)
- **Web3 / metaverse expansion**: aims to maintain its No.1  position as a domestic NFT platform and continues partnerships with IP holders
- **IEO expansion**: leveraging the first IEO service in Japan, continues to attract projects
- **Coincheck Labs**: continues investment in · incubation of blockchain-related startups
- **Handled-token expansion**: adding spot · new tokens. Account openings · assets in custody surged during the market-recovery period of 2024-2025年

## 8. Related people

- **Oki Matsumoto**: Monex Group Representative Executive Officer, President & CEO; Coincheck Group N.V. Executive Chairman (confirmed in SEC disclosure. Involved in Coincheck governance since the 2018年4月 acquisition following the NEM incident)
- **Satoshi Hasuo**: Coincheck Inc. Representative Director and President (confirmed in SEC disclosure as of 2022-2023年 as "President: Satoshi Hasuo")
- **Gary Simanson**: former President & CEO of Thunder Bridge IV → Coincheck CEO after the merger (confirmed in SEC disclosure)
- **Founders**: Koichiro Wada · Yusuke Otsuka co-founded it (public knowledge). With the management-structure renewal following the NEM incident, it transitioned to a Monex-led management team
