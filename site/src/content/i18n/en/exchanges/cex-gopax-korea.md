# GOPAX (고팍스) — Korea CEX #5  / Binance 2023-02  majority acquisition → partial retreat amid FIU objection overview

> 2017-11  launch · formerly Streami (스트리미) · Korea spot market #5 (estimated 1%) · **2023-02  Binance acquired ~72% of GOPAX shares** → FIU change-notification processing delay → **compromise plan to dilute Binance voting rights** under continued negotiation due to Korea FIU concerns · GoFi (GOPAX Earn) Genesis Global bankruptcy exposure incident

## 1. Corporation / Shareholders

- Trade name: 주식회사 스트리미 (Streami Inc.) — holding / operating company
- Established: 2015-04 (Streami founded) / 2017-11 (GOPAX exchange launched)
- Head office location: 서울특별시 강남구 (Gangnam-gu, Seoul)
- Founder: 이준행 (Lee Joon-haeng) — Streami founder, started from a blockchain remittance business
- **Shareholder structure (as of 2026 年)**:
  - **Binance**: announced in **2023-02-08** the acquisition of ~**72%** of GOPAX shares from existing Streami shareholders (via **Binance Asia Services Pte. Ltd.**)
  - However, Korea FIU's processing of the **변경신고** (VASP change notification) is **under prolonged continued negotiation** → official approval of the controlling-shareholder change has been delayed / partial
  - 2024–2025  period: compromise plans such as diluting Binance voting rights / strengthening the independence of the Korean entity remain under discussion
  - Existing minority shareholders (early investors) also continue to hold
- IPO consideration: no concrete announcement amid Binance-related regulatory uncertainty

## 2. Registration / License status (Korean system)

- **가상자산사업자 (VASP)** registration: registered with FIU (2021-09  Korea registration class No. 1 )
- **ISMS / ISMS-P certification**: holds KISA ISMS-P
- **실명확인 입출금 계정** (real-name account): contract with **전북은행 (Jeonbuk Bank, JB금융그룹 / JB Financial Group)**
  - **전북은행 is a 지방은행 (regional bank) outside Korea's 5  major banks** (KB / Shinhan / NH / Hana / Woori). GOPAX is the only VASP offering KRW spot through a regional-bank affiliation
  - A structure dependent on the creditworthiness of JB금융그룹 (JB Financial Group, KOSDAQ-listed)
- After the **2023-02  Binance share-acquisition** announcement:
  - Korea FIU took a stance of **변경신고 처리지연** (de-facto suspension of change-notification processing)
  - After **global Binance**'s settlement with the US SEC / DOJ (2023-11, $4.3B fine, CZ resignation), Korea FIU has taken an even more cautious position
- For the detailed regulatory system, see [[exchanges/korea-cex-five-pillars-comparison|韓国 5 大 CEX 制度比較]]

## 3. Listed currencies

- KRW spot market (legal as one of Korea's 5  major CEXs, 1 )
- Number of listed assets: 80+ (the most conservative among Korea's 5  major CEXs)
- Centered on BTC / ETH / major altcoins
- Listing review: DAXA common standards + GOPAX's own standards

## 4. Scope of business

- Spot trading (centered on KRW pairs)
- Derivatives / margin trading: **not offered under Korean VASP regulation**
- **GoFi (GOPAX Earn)**: a lending product; at the end of 2022  a **Genesis Global bankruptcy-exposure incident** occurred (the GoFi withdrawal-freeze situation arose due to the impact of the bankruptcy of global lending operators such as Genesis Capital)
- staking: limited service
- Fiat deposits: only via 전북은행 real-name account
- B2B / institutional: API trading, OTC

## 5. Market share / Trading volume

- **Korea spot market share**: ~1% (lowest among Korea's 5  major CEXs, #5)
- 24h volume: $1–20M (correlated with market volatility)
- Customer base: centered on Korean residents (full KYC implemented)
- User trust weakened after the GoFi incident; recovery in progress

## 7. Strategic developments

- **Post-acquisition governance negotiation with Binance**: due to Korea FIU's cautious stance, there is a possibility of compromise toward a model of **diluting Binance voting rights + strengthening the Korean entity's independence**. After global Binance's settlement with the US SEC / DOJ, Korean authorities' concerns have risen further
- **GoFi repayment processing**: user repayment / restructuring after the 2022-09  GoFi withdrawal freeze is key to GOPAX restoring trust
- **전북은행 relationship**: the real-name account contract with a regional bank (전북은행) that is not one of Korea's 5  major banks shows GOPAX's unique position. The issue of the bank's AML/CFT responsibility-sharing directly affects GOPAX's business sustainability
- **DAXA participation**: standardizing listing criteria at DAXA together with [[exchanges/cex-upbit-korea|Upbit]] / [[exchanges/cex-bithumb-korea|Bithumb]] / [[exchanges/cex-coinone-korea|Coinone]] / [[exchanges/cex-korbit-korea|Korbit]]
- **2024  User Protection Act response**: contributing to the user-protection fund, strengthening unfair-trading surveillance, rebuilding internal controls
- **Expectations of Binance global synergy**: there is potential to leverage Binance global liquidity / asset lineup / technical infrastructure, but realization is limited due to Korea FIU's cautious stance

## 8. Positioning in the industry

Although GOPAX is the **lowest among Korea's 5  major CEXs**, as the **core case of global Binance's attempt to enter Korea as a branch**, it is a representative case of the triangular governance problem among regulators / global CEXs / Korean domestic VASPs.

In the context of [[exchanges/korea-cex-five-pillars-comparison|韓国 5 大 CEX 制度比較]], it is **the only case among the 5  major KRW spot CEXs in which a global CEX (Binance) attempted to become a controlling shareholder**, and it shows how cautiously the Korean regulators handle a global CEX's domestic entry. The negotiation stalemate after the 2023-11  global Binance US settlement reflects the Korean regulators' credibility concerns.

The GoFi incident is also important in the context of [[exchanges/global-crypto-exchange-bankruptcy-comparison|글로벌 CEX 파산 비교]] as a case of the **pattern in which the insolvency of a global lending operator spreads contagiously to a Korean CEX's subsidiary lending product**.
