# SBI FX Trade Co., Ltd. (BI FXTRADE) — Japan crypto-asset derivatives operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director (Kinsho) 第2635号 · JVCEA Class-I member #1026 (**crypto-asset derivatives trading only**) · Established 2011年11月

## 1. Entity / shareholders

- **Trade name (JP)**: SBI FXトレード株式会社 (JVCEA registered name: BI FXTRADE Co., Ltd.)
- **Trade name (EN)**: SBI FXTRADE Co., Ltd.
- **Established**: 2011年11月
- **Head office**: 〒106-6019  Izumi Garden Tower, 1-6-1  Roppongi, Minato-ku, Tokyo (same address as SBI VC Trade / SBI Securities)
- **Capital etc.**: 9億6,000万円 (including capital reserves)
- **Shareholders**: SBI Liquidity Market Co., Ltd. 100% (SBI HD consolidated subsidiary)
- **Listing status**: Non-listed (wholly-owned subsidiary of the SBI HD group)

**Note**: The JVCEA member list shows "BI FXTRADE Co., Ltd.", but its corporate site, FIEA registration (第2635号) and location (Izumi Garden Tower) all match, confirming it to be the same legal entity as SBI FX Trade Co., Ltd.

## 2. License / registration status

- **Financial instruments business**: Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director (Kinsho) 第2635号 (Type-I Financial Instruments Business)
- **Crypto-asset exchange business (FSA)**: **Not registered** (does not hold a spot-trading / exchange-business license)
- **JVCEA Class-I member #1026**: Crypto-asset derivatives trading only (Cryptocurrency derivatives trading)
- **Associations joined**: Financial Futures Association of Japan (membership no. 1588), Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (membership no. 1026)

## 3. Currencies handled

- **Number of tradable assets**: 6 assets (derivatives only; no spot trading)
- **Assets**: BTC/JPY, XRP/JPY, ETH/JPY, BTC/USD, XRP/USD, ETH/USD
- **Distinctive feature**: Crypto-asset CFDs (contracts for difference) offered against both JPY and USD. No spot holding, no wallet. Positioned as a crypto-asset derivatives add-on service by an FX-primary operator.

## 4. Scope of business

The company is an FX (foreign-exchange margin trading) specialist, offering crypto-assets as a derivatives service (**SBI Bitcoin FX**) added onto the same account base as FX.

| Service | Availability |
|---------|---------|
| FX (over-the-counter foreign-exchange margin trading) | ✓ Core business · 34 currency pairs |
| Accumulation forex (Tsumitate FX) | ✓ Foreign-currency accumulation service |
| SBI Bitcoin FX (crypto-asset CFD) | ✓ 6 assets · leverage up to 2x (individuals) |
| Crypto-asset spot trading | ✗ None |
| NEOBANK | ✓ Bank agency business (SBI Sumishin Net Bank) |
| Crypto-asset lending · NFT · staking | ✗ None |
| Electronic payment instruments trading business | ✗ None |

**Deposit channels (crypto-asset CFD)**: Quick deposit (SBI Sumishin Net Bank, Rakuten Bank, MUFG Bank, Sumitomo Mitsui Banking, Japan Post Bank, Mizuho Bank, PayPay Bank), instant deposit from SBI FX Trade NEOBANK. **No convenience-store payment, no crypto deposit.**

**Key specs of the crypto-asset CFD**: trade type = OTC crypto-asset margin trading; leverage = 2x for individual accounts (separate for corporates); loss-cut level = margin maintenance ratio at or below 50%; trading fee, loss-cut fee and account maintenance fee all free; floating funding-rate scheme.

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- **Trading volume / number of accounts**: Not disclosed (no regular disclosure for the crypto-asset CFD alone)
- **Customer base**: Mainly existing FX customers (retail-centric). No mention of a dedicated program for institutional investors.
- **Position**: No published trading-volume ranking for the crypto-asset CFD. In the FX segment it touts "industry's narrowest spread levels" and "Oricon Customer Satisfaction FX Beginner Category No. 1位". Against the backdrop of being an FX specialist and the SBI group's brand recognition, its customer base is mainly the retail FX trader segment.

## 7. Strategic developments

- **2026年4月**: "Foreign-currency home-delivery service" launched (delivery of physical foreign-currency cash to FX-account customers)
- **2026年4月**: 15th-anniversary special page published; anniversary campaign rolled out
- **2026年5月**: Narrowed the reference spread of a new currency pair (Turkish lira / yen) (1.28 sen)
- Since the 2020年8月 launch of the crypto-asset CFD, asset expansion (in the order BTC→XRP→ETH) is complete, and USD-denominated assets have also been added. No new expansion announcements in the crypto-asset segment were confirmed over the most recent 12 months.
- The SBI group's overall crypto-asset strategy is led by the group's controlling company ([[JapanFG/sbi-hd|SBI HD]]) and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-sbi-vc-trade|SBI VCトレード]], with this company positioned as complementary on the retail FX base. For the JP VASP derivatives / spot dual-business structure, see [[exchanges/jp-vasp-derivative-license-system|JP VASP デリバティブライセンス制度]].
