# Korea 5  Major CEX Regulatory Comparison — Upbit / Bithumb / Coinone / Korbit / GOPAX

## Korea Regulatory Overview

South Korea established a **Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP / VA-OP, 가상자산사업자)** registration regime under the **Act on Reporting and Using Specified Financial Transaction Information (특정금융정보법, effective 2021-03-25)**. The 2  mandatory registration conditions are: **(a) ISMS certification from KISA (Korea Internet & Security Agency)** + **(b) real-name account contract with a domestic bank (실명확인 입출금 계정)**. Only exchanges that satisfy both conditions and register with the **Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU, the Korean equivalent of FinCEN)** may offer **KRW trading pairs (KRW spot market)**. As of 2026 年, only the **5 社 exchanges below** are authorised to trade KRW. Coin-only exchanges (e.g. GDAC) that have registered cannot offer KRW pairs.

## Real-name account bank contract (실명확인 입출금 계정)

Korea's 5  major bank groups and 1  exchanges at a 1  bank-per-exchange principle:
- **KB Kookmin / Shinhan / NH Nonghyup / Hana / Woori** are the domestic 5  major banks.
- Exchanges that cannot connect **cannot offer KRW spot trading** → a driver of exit (many small to mid-sized exchanges in the past have been eliminated by this requirement).
- Banks are reluctant to sign contracts because they bear AML/CFT liability → high barrier to new entry.

## ISMS-CASP Certification

Information Security Management System certification audited by **KISA**.
- **Basic ISMS**: Required for all VASPs.
- **Enhanced ISMS-P** (personal information): Required for operators with trading volume **exceeding 100 億 won**.
- Annual renewal audit; deficiencies may result in registration cancellation.

## International Comparison

| Item | Korea | Japan | USA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Main regulator | **FSC/FIU single** | FSA + [[exchanges/jvcea-self-regulatory-overview|JVCEA]] dual oversight | State + federal multiple (NYDFS / SEC / CFTC / FinCEN) |
| Registration regime | [[exchanges/jp-vasp-regulatory-timeline|VASP 登録]] (2021-) | [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA VASP 登録]] (2017-) | MSB + state BitLicense |
| Certification | KISA ISMS | FSA inspection + JVCEA self-regulation | State-by-state |
| Real-name account requirement | **Mandatory 1:1  bank contract** | Bank contract recommended but not mandatory | Bank contract at discretion |

Korea is structurally distinct from Japan's [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|二重監督モデル]] and from the US multi-licence model in that it has institutionally created a 5 社 oligopoly through a **single regulator + mandatory real-name account** requirement.
