# BitTrade Inc. — Japan crypto-asset exchange operator overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA registration number Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00007号 · JVCEA member #1007 · registration date 2017-09-29

## 1. Corporate / shareholders

- **Trade name**: BitTrade Inc. (Japanese: ビットトレード株式会社)
  - Former trade names: BitTrade Inc. → Huobi Japan Inc. (renamed 2018-12-17) → BitTrade Inc. (renamed 2023-04-21)
- **Established**: 2016年9月
- **Head office location**: 〒105-0003  Nishi-Shimbashi 1-1-1
, Minato-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: 1億円 (as of end-2023年2月)
- **Listing status**: Unlisted
- **Major shareholders (disclosed)**:
  - SINOHOPE SG PTE. LTD. (presumed top shareholder)
  - Sinohope Technology Holdings Limited
  - Tokai Tokyo Financial Holdings, Inc.
  - FPG Co., Ltd.

## 2. License / registration status

- **Crypto-asset exchange business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第00007号
- **Type I Financial Instruments Business**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments) 第3295号
- **JVCEA member**: Type I member #1007 (covering both crypto-asset exchange business and crypto-asset derivatives transactions)
- **JCBA member**: Regular member of the Japan Crypto-asset Business Association
- **Fintech Association**: Venture member
- **Japan STO Association / Japan Security Token Association**: Supporting member

## 3. Listed currencies

- **Number of listed assets**: 49 assets in total (as of 2026年5月)
  - ※ Notice of planned delisting of BSV (Bitcoin SV) given on 2026年5月8日
- **Major assets (examples)**: BTC / ETH / XRP / SOL / ADA / DOGE / BNB / DOT / LTC / BCH / LINK / TON / APT / SUI / MATIC / ARB / OP, etc.
- **Features**:
  - Altcoin lineup at one of the largest levels domestically (at launch claimed "No.1 in Japan by number of listed assets")
  - Also lists several proprietary / project-based assets (JOC, UPC, CRTS, ZPG, etc.)
  - Operates an IEO (Initial Exchange Offering) platform

## 4. Scope of business

| Service | Availability |
|---------|---------|
| Sales counter (spot) | ✓ |
| Exchange (order-book trading) | ✓ |
| Leveraged trading (CFD) | ✓ (basis for Type I Financial Instruments Business) |
| Accumulation (regular savings) | ✓ |
| Crypto-asset lending | ✓ |
| IEO | ✓ (dedicated listing page available) |
| Corporate services | ✓ (dedicated page available) |
| API | WebSocket specification (millisecond support) |

- **Fiat-currency deposit channels**: Quick deposit (free except Pay-easy) / bank-transfer deposit / Pay-easy deposit / convenience-store deposit
- **Banking partners**: PayPay Bank, Rakuten Bank, SBI Sumishin Net Bank, Resona Bank, Japan Post Bank, GMO Aozora Net Bank
- **Security**: Zero hacking incidents since service launch (official statement), crypto-asset 100% cold-wallet management, 24-hour 365-day system monitoring

## 5. Market share / trading volume

- **Account count / trading volume**: Not disclosed (no figures stated in disclosure materials)
- **Position within the market**: A mid-tier exchange whose main axis of differentiation is the breadth of its listed assets. Of the 26社 JVCEA Type 1  members, only 13社 hold both crypto-asset exchange business and derivatives transactions, and this company is one of them (for details see [[exchanges/jvcea-self-regulatory-overview|JVCEA 自主規制体系]] / [[exchanges/jp-vasp-derivative-license-system|JP VASP デリバティブライセンス制度]]). For altcoin-axis competitors see [[exchanges/jp-exchange-bitbank|bitbank]]; for a full-range industry comparison see [[exchanges/jp-exchange-binance-japan|Binance Japan]].
- **Customer base**: Mainly individual retail. Also offers corporate-facing services.

**Hacks / major incidents**: According to official statements, no hacking since establishment (not disclosed).

## 7. Strategic developments (recent 12  months)

- **2025年7月**: Began joint research with JTB and NTT TechnoCross on regional revitalization through "oshi-katsu" (fan activities) (press release)
- **2025年8月**: Concluded a basic agreement with Hokubo Co., Ltd. on crypto-asset / Web3.0-related business
- **2025年11月**: Agreed with the ENI Foundation to advance collaboration in the Web3.0  domain (press release)
- **2026年1月**: Concluded a strategic alliance with Australian real-estate developer Avantaus Development (ADG) leveraging RWA (real-world assets)
- **IEO rollout**: Continues regular information disclosure for domestic proprietary assets (such as JOC); the new-listing application window is also open
- **Use of Type I Financial Instruments Business**: Provides a CFD (crypto-asset derivatives) platform. Discloses its capital-adequacy ratio quarterly (disclosed through end-2026年3月)
