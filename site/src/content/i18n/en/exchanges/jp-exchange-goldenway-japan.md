# Goldenway Japan Co., Ltd. — Japan crypto-asset exchange operator (derivatives only) overview


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[exchanges/INDEX|exchanges index]]. Read it with [[exchanges/fsa-vasp-registration-system|FSA 暗号資産交換業登録制度 — 番号体系・財務局管轄・登録要件]] for adjacent context and [[fintech/japan-financial-regulation|日本金融規制 — トークン・暗号資産・決済に関する法体系]] for the broader system boundary.

> FSA Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments) 第 258 号 · JVCEA member #1040 (Type I · crypto-asset derivatives trading only) · Brand name: FXTF

## 1. Corporate entity & shareholders

- **Trade name**: Goldenway Japan Co., Ltd. (Japanese: ゴールデンウェイ・ジャパン株式会社)
- **Established**: 2006年6月14日 (former trade name: FX4 Japan Co., Ltd. Renamed to FX Trade Financial Co., Ltd. in 2008年8月, and to the current trade name in 2019年4月)
- **Head office**: 〒108-0073  Mita Kawasaki Building 4F, 2-11-15  Mita, Minato-ku, Tokyo
- **Capital**: 1億円 (as of 2025年3月)
- **Major shareholder**: FXTF HOLDINGS Pte. Ltd. (Singapore), shareholding ratio 100%
- **Listing status**: Unlisted (the parent is an unlisted Singaporean holding company)

The name "Goldenway" derives from Goldenway Investment, the Hong Kong-based financial group at which founder Wu Yifan was previously employed. The same name was adopted through the 2019年 trade-name change.

## 2. Licensing & registration status

- **Type I Financial Instruments Business Operator**: Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau (Financial Instruments) 第258号 (registered 2007年9月)
- **Commodity Futures Business Operator**: 2022年10月 permit
- **Investment advisory & agency business**: registered 2013年9月
- **JVCEA member**: Type I #1040 (crypto-asset derivatives trading)
  - **Important**: The JVCEA registration category is "crypto-asset derivatives trading" only. It has not obtained "crypto-asset exchange business (spot)" registration.
- **Financial Futures Association of Japan** member number 第1570号
- **Japan Commodity Futures Association** affiliated
- **Japan Investment Advisers Association** member number 012-02639

## 3. Handled currencies

- **Crypto-asset CFD symbols** (as of 2026年5月 · from the company's rate-list page):
  - BTC/JPY, BTC/USD
  - ETH/JPY, ETH/USD
- 4 pairs total (2 symbols × JPY-denominated・USD-denominated)
- Characteristics: **No spot trading**. All are leveraged CFDs (margin trading). Spreads are zero (fixed in principle, with exceptions); instead, a position-linked commission is charged under the mechanism.

## 4. Scope of business

- **Core business**: Foreign-exchange margin trading (FX) — trading brands "FXTF GX" and "FXTF MT4"
- **Crypto-asset CFD trading (FXTF GX-Crypto CFD)**: service launched 2023年8月. Limited to BTC/ETH only.
- **Crypto-asset knock-out options (FXTF GX-Crypto KO)**: service launched 2025年4月
- **Commodity CFD/Commodity KO**: launched sequentially 2022年12月–2023年6月 (gold, crude oil, etc.)
- **Currency knock-out options (FX KO)**: launched 2023年9月
- **Corporate services**: corporate-oriented FX, commodity CFD, and crypto-asset CFD services launched from 2024年11月
- **Deposits**: quick deposit (24 hours)・bank transfer. Major partner banks: Mitsubishi UFJ, Sumitomo Mitsui, Mizuho, Rakuten, PayPay, Sumishin SBI, Japan Post, AEON, Seven, GMO Aozora
- **Spot crypto-asset trading**: none

## 5. Market share & trading volume

- Neither account numbers nor trading volume disclosed
- Position within the industry: **niche**. The main business is FX (a mid-tier domestic operator). Crypto-assets are a limited entry via BTC/ETH CFDs only. Both the number of handled symbols and account numbers are substantially fewer than those of major crypto-asset exchange operators.
- Customer base: primarily retail traders (FX-shared accounts); corporate clients also supported from 2024年11月

## 7. Strategic developments

- **2025年4月**: Newly established crypto-asset knock-out option (KO) trading. Expanded option products to crypto-assets in line with FX and commodity KO.
- **2025年8月**: Obtained the "Silver" certification as a health-excellent enterprise (appealing to operational stability).
- **2026年5月**: Launched an integration service between TradingView and FXTF GX. Strengthened convenience on the chart and tooling front.
- The most recent expansion direction is an integrated-platform strategy of "providing the three fields of FX, commodities, and crypto-assets on 1口座". There is no public information regarding expansion of the crypto-asset symbol lineup.

## 8. Related people

- **Representative Director and President**: Wu Yifan (Goh Yee-Fan)
  - After graduating from City University of Hong Kong, he worked at the Hong Kong-based financial company Goldenway Investment. He subsequently came to Japan and became involved with the company, taking the representative post in 2019年. He is said to be the person who led the 2019年 trade-name change (from company overview and official information).
