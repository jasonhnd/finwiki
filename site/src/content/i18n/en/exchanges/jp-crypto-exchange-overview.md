# Japan crypto exchange overview — FSA / JVCEA regulated VASP market

## TL;DR

Japan's crypto-asset exchange business is built on two layers: the registration system of the FSA / Local Finance Bureaus, and [[exchanges/jvcea-self-regulatory-overview|JVCEA]] self-regulation. The crypto-asset exchange registration system began in 2017-04-01, and in 2023-06-01 the Electronic Payment Instrument exchange business was also institutionalized. As of 2026-02-28, the FSA's English-language registration list shows 27  crypto-asset exchange operators. The domestic market needs to be viewed all the way through "registered VASP + JVCEA member + FSA supervision + tax reporting/CARF".

The Bank of Japan explains crypto assets as property value that can be electronically recorded and transferred, and that is not legal tender. To conduct exchange business domestically requires FSA / Local Finance Bureau registration.

## 3. Strategic reading

- **Registration as a moat**: services for Japan residents are premised on registration. Overseas majors face a binary choice: be warned in an unregistered state, or acquire / establish a Japan entity to obtain registration.
- **The trade-off between number of assets and safety**: JVCEA's asset screening restricts domestically listed assets and curbs the product count relative to overseas CEXs, but prioritizes investor protection and market soundness.
- **Sales-counter economics**: in the domestic retail market, the sales-counter spread readily becomes a revenue source, and must be viewed separately from order-book depth · fee competition on the exchange.
- **Financial-group connectivity**: SBI, GMO, Rakuten, Monex, Mercari, etc., have cross-sell with securities · FX · banking · points · e-commerce as a strength.
- **stablecoin / EPI adjacency**: since 2023-06-01, the Electronic Payment Instrument framework stands as a separate layer, and the boundary between crypto-asset exchange business and stablecoin intermediation becomes important.

## 4. Regulation and policy

- FSA registration is the entrance to the crypto-asset exchange business; one cannot conduct exchange business for the domestic market without registration.
- As a certified self-regulatory organization, JVCEA handles member rules · asset screening · trading management · advertising regulation, etc.
- The incident history (Mt.Gox, Coincheck, Zaif, DMM Bitcoin, etc.) connected directly to the strengthening of segregated management · cold storage · AML/CFT · internal-management rules.
- On the tax front, the NTA's CARF response is advancing, with crypto-asset transaction information exchange — domestic and overseas — moving toward institutionalization.
