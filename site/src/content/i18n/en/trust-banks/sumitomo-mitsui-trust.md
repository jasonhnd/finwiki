# Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Group (SuMi TRUST)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[trust-banks/INDEX|trust-banks INDEX]]. Read it against [[trust-banks/master-trust-bank|日本マスタートラスト信託銀行]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

The only trust-specialized holding company independent of the megabanks, **one of the 3  major trusts** (Mitsubishi UFJ Trust / Sumitomo Mitsui Trust / Mizuho Trust). 2002-02  establishment of Chuo Mitsui Trust HD → 2011-04  integration with Sumitomo Trust & Banking → formation of Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Holdings → **renamed to Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Group in 2024-10-01**. Specializes in pensions, real estate, stock-transfer agency, and testamentary trusts. FY2024  net income **2,576 億円** (3.3 × year-on-year, a record high), total assets **78.2 兆円**, **trust-asset balance 263.3 兆円 (No. 1 in the industry)**. **Note: a separate legal entity from [[megabanks/smfg]] (Sumitomo Mitsui FG)** — although they share the "Sumitomo Mitsui" name, SuMi TRUST has **trust** roots in the former Mitsui-family and former Sumitomo-family lineages, while SMFG has **banking** roots in the former Taiyo Kobe + former Sumitomo Bank lineage, with no capital relationship. It runs [[regional-banks/ssnb]] (SBI Sumishin Net Bank) as a JV with SBI HD.

### Merger history / predecessors

- **Former Mitsui Trust & Banking**: established 1924  (former Mitsui-zaibatsu trust, one of Japan's earliest trust-company lineages)
- **Former Sumitomo Trust & Banking**: established 1925  (former Sumitomo-zaibatsu trust)
- **Former Chuo Trust & Banking**: established postwar, later merged with Mitsui Trust
- **Chuo Mitsui Trust & Banking**: 2000  merger of Mitsui Trust & Banking + Chuo Trust & Banking
- **Chuo Mitsui Trust HD**: established 2002-02  (holding company of Chuo Mitsui Trust + Chuo Mitsui Asset Trust)
- **Sumitomo Mitsui Trust HD**: 2011-04  integration of Chuo Mitsui Trust HD + Sumitomo Trust & Banking

> **Integration background**: Sumitomo Trust & Banking had been oriented toward a standalone path, but from the post-Lehman-shock industry-reorganization pressure and the strategic alignment of maintaining trust-specialized independence, it announced in 2009 年 a policy of management integration with Chuo Mitsui Trust HD. The integration was carried out via a 2011-04  share-exchange method, establishing the largest scale as a trust-specialized FG. ^[likely]

### Trust-specialized independent path

- **Independent from megabanks**: Of the 3  major trusts, Mitsubishi UFJ Trust is under [[megabanks/mufg]] and Mizuho Trust is under [[megabanks/mizuho-fg]]. **Only SuMi TRUST is a holding company independent of the megabanks, trust-specialized.** This is both its identity and its differentiation axis.
- **Confusion risk of the "Sumitomo Mitsui" name**: It shares its name with [[megabanks/smfg]] (Sumitomo Mitsui FG = SMBC lineage) but is a separate legal entity with separate capital. SMFG is a banking lineage of the former Taiyo Kobe Bank + former Sumitomo Bank, while SuMi TRUST is a trust lineage of the former Mitsui Trust + former Sumitomo Trust.

### Shift toward HNW / corporate management

- **Individual HNW**: leveraging the customer base since the former Mitsui family / former Sumitomo family through testamentary trusts, asset succession, and inheritance measures
- **Corporate**: a large-corporate customer base through pension-asset custody (DB / DC), real-estate trusts, and stock-transfer agency
- **Institutional investors**: investment-trust management under a 2  brand structure with Nikko Asset + Sumitomo Mitsui Trust AM

### Alliance / acquisition strategy

- **JV with SBI HD** ([[regional-banks/ssnb]] / SBI Sumishin Net Bank): a 50% stake, a JV with SBI HD, the main channel for entering the BaaS / net-bank field
- **JV with Panasonic** (Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Panasonic Finance): leasing / finance
- **Acquisition of Nikko Asset Management** (in stages from Citi, 2009 ): strengthening the investment-trust-management field

### Medium-term management plan "Trust 2030" (2024)

- Centered on trust functions, expanding pension, real-estate, asset-succession, and sustainability-related custody
- The BaaS field is developed indirectly via SBI Sumishin Net Bank ^[likely]

## 4. Regulation / policy

- **Supervisor**: FSA
- **Holding-company regulation**: Banking Act Article 52 -17 (also applied mutatis mutandis to trust-bank holding companies)
- **Trust Business Act**: trust-bank business overall
- **G-SIB**: unlike MUFG, not designated as a G-SIB (relatively smaller scale / global interconnectedness) ^[likely]
- **Recent policy points**:
  - 2024~ BOJ policy-rate normalization → changes in the trust-fee / investment-yield environment
  - 2025~ the movements of Sumishin SBI Net Bank ([[regional-banks/ssnb]]) in the BaaS / net-bank field
  - 2025~2026  changes in the regulatory environment for asset-succession / inheritance-related services
