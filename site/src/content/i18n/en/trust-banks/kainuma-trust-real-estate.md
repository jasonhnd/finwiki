# Kainuma Trust Real Estate / Kainuma Trust Real Estate

Wiki route: [[JapanFG/INDEX]] -> [[trust-banks/trust-companies-japan-index]] -> [[trust-banks/kainuma-trust-real-estate]]

## Overview

Kainuma Trust Real Estate is a Tokai-region management-type trust company linked to rental-property owner asset management and succession. In FinWiki it is a Nagoya / local rental-housing trust route beside [[trust-banks/daito-mirai-trust]] and [[trust-banks/daiwa-living-trust]].

## Entity Boundary

This page covers Kainuma Trust Real Estate Co., Ltd. as a trust-company operator. It does not cover the full Kainuma Construction group or general property-management services outside the trust-company context.

Kainuma matters because local property groups can use management-type trust-company registration to offer owner succession and rental-asset continuity services. It is a useful contrast to national housing-group trust companies.
