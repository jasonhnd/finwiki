# Mitsubishi UFJ Trust and Banking (MUTB)

## TL;DR

Mitsubishi UFJ Trust and Banking Corporation (三菱UFJ信託銀行, MUTB) is the core trust-bank operating company of [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]]. It is a "trust-business-concurrent financial institution" that, **in addition to a bank license under the Banking Act, holds a license to concurrently conduct trust business under the Concurrent Operation Act (the Act on Concurrent Operation of Trust Business by Financial Institutions)**, conducting banking business such as deposits and lending together with trust assets, pensions, real estate, transfer agency, and entrusted-asset administration as an integrated whole. It is a separate banking legal entity from the commercial bank [[megabanks/mufg-bank|三菱UFJ銀行]], and is distinguished from the megabank entity itself by the combination of a bank license + concurrent trust operation.

## 2. Operating model

As a trust-business-concurrent financial institution, MUTB carries out the following functions:

- **Asset administration / entrusted assets**: It handles asset administration and securities administration for institutional investors and pension funds, connecting with the group's asset-administration platform [[trust-banks/master-trust-bank|日本マスタートラスト信託銀行]].
- **Pension trusts, real estate, transfer agency**: Defined-benefit / defined-contribution pension trusts, real-estate trusts, and transfer-agency business as a shareholder-registry administrator.
- **Securitization / liquidation**: Acting as trustee for securitization schemes using trusts of monetary claims and real estate.

Whereas [[megabanks/mufg-bank|三菱UFJ銀行]] handles deposits / lending, foreign exchange, and the commercial-banking network, MUTB handles trusts, entrusted assets, real estate, pensions, and transfer agency — a division of functions. Supervision is conducted by [[financial-regulators/fsa|金融庁]] on both the banking and trust sides.
