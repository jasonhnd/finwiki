# Mizuho Trust & Banking (みずほ信託銀行)

## TL;DR

Mizuho Trust & Banking Co., Ltd. (みずほ信託銀行) is the trust-bank operating company of [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]]. It is a trust-concurrent financial institution that holds, **in addition to a bank license under the Banking Act, concurrent authorization for trust business under the Concurrent Operation Act**, and conducts banking business together with trust, pension, real-estate, transfer-agency, and entrusted-property services as an integrated whole. The commercial-banking core is borne by [[megabanks/mizuho-bank|みずほ銀行]], while Mizuho Trust & Banking bears the trust / entrusted-property side — a division of functions.

## 2. Operating model

As a trust-concurrent financial institution, Mizuho Trust & Banking bears the following:

- **Personal and corporate trust solutions**: trust products for individuals, such as inheritance, asset succession, and testamentary trusts, and for corporations.
- **Pension trusts, transfer agency, real estate**: pension trusts for defined-benefit / defined-contribution pensions, transfer-agency services as shareholder-registry administrator, real-estate trusts, and real-estate brokerage.
- **Entrusted-property management and liquidation**: entrusted-property management including monetary claims and electronically recorded monetary claims, and trusteeship of securitization schemes.

Together with [[megabanks/mizuho-bank|みずほ銀行]] (commercial bank), [[securities-firms/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]], and [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]], the division of functions across banking, securities, asset management, and trust within Mizuho FG becomes visible. Supervision is borne by [[financial-regulators/fsa|金融庁]].
