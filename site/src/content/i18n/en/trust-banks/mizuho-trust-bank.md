## TL;DR

Mizuho Trust & Banking is the trust-bank operating company of [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]]. [[megabanks/mizuho-bank|みずほ銀行]] handles the core commercial banking role, while Mizuho Trust & Banking handles trust, pension, real estate, securities agency, and entrusted-asset functions.

## 2. Business role

- It handles the Mizuho group's trust functions and provides trust solutions to individuals and corporations.
- It complements functions that are difficult for a standalone commercial bank to cover, including pensions, securities agency, real estate, entrusted assets, and electronically recorded monetary claims.
- Analyze it across the group together with [[megabanks/mizuho-bank|みずほ銀行]], [[securities-firms/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]], and [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]].

## 3. Why standalone page matters

Mizuho FG's group strategy alone cannot track trust-bank-specific entrusted assets, pensions, real estate, securities agency, and electronically recorded monetary-claim services. Separating the trust bank page clarifies the boundary between trust business and banking business.
