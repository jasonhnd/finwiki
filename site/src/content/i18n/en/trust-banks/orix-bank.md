# ORIX Bank

## TL;DR

ORIX Bank is a bank corporation listed in the FSA bank-license list as "ORIX Bank Corporation" in the trust-bank category. It has traditionally been a bank within the [[leasing-firms/orix-corp|ORIX]] group, but in 2026-04-27 ORIX and Daiwa Securities Group / [[regional-banks/daiwa-next-bank|大和ネクスト銀行]] announced a full share transfer and subsidiary conversion, so ownership transition watch is required.

## 2. Business role

- It has a specialty bank model distinct from ordinary megabanks and regional banks, including investment-property loans, retail deposits, and trust products.
- If the transition to Daiwa Securities Group is completed, it will become an important piece for strengthening the securities group's deposit, lending, and trust functions.
- The issue shifts from [[leasing-firms/orix-corp|ORIX]]'s integrated financial portfolio to [[securities-firms/daiwa-sg|大和証券グループ]]'s bank / wealth strategy.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

It is not enough to view ORIX Bank only as "ORIX's bank subsidiary." With the announced subsidiary conversion by Daiwa Next Bank in 2026 年, it became a target of reorganization across a securities group, bank license, real-estate loans, and trust functions. This page tracks future integration, name changes, and regulatory approvals.
