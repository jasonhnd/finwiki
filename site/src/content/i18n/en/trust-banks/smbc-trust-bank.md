# SMBC Trust Bank (SMBC信託銀行)

## TL;DR

SMBC信託銀行 (SMBC Trust Bank Ltd.) is a 100% subsidiary of [[megabanks/sumitomo-mitsui-banking-corp|三井住友銀行]] and the trust-bank operating company of [[megabanks/smfg|SMBC / SMFG グループ]]. It is a trust-coterminous financial institution that holds, **in addition to a bank license under the Banking Act, the concurrent operation of trust business under the Concurrent Operation Act**, and, centered on the PRESTIA brand, handles foreign-currency, high-net-worth, and cross-border personal finance together with trust and real-estate functions. The key point is that it is a separate group and separate legal entity from the similarly-named [[trust-banks/smtb|三井住友信託銀行 (SMTB)]].

## 2. Operating model

As a trust-coterminous financial institution, SMBC Trust Bank handles the following:

- **PRESTIA retail**: It integrated Citibank, N.A.'s retail bank business in 2015 年 and, under the PRESTIA brand, is strong in foreign-currency deposits, high-net-worth clients, and cross-border personal finance.
- **Trust and real estate**: Trust business and real-estate-related concurrent operations.
- **Registered financial institution business**: Investment trusts and financial-instruments intermediation.

It has a lineage as a former foreign-affiliated trust bank, and has the background of becoming a subsidiary of Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation in 2013 年 and changing its trade name to SMBC Trust Bank. Whereas [[megabanks/sumitomo-mitsui-banking-corp|三井住友銀行]] handles the commercial-banking core and [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興証券]] handles securities, this bank handles functions leaning toward affluent / private banking. Supervision is by [[financial-regulators/fsa|金融庁]].
