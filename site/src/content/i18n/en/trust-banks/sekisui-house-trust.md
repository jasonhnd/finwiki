## Overview

Sekisui House Trust is a management trust company connected to the Sekisui House group. Its company page describes registration as a management trust business and states that the company was opened to help solve real-estate owner challenges around rental-housing management and succession.

The page matters because it is a housing-company trust route. It shows how a construction / housing group can use trust-company functions to support long-term property management and family succession.
