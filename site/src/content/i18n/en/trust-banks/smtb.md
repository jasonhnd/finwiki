# 三井住友信託銀行 (SMTB)

## Wiki route

## TL;DR

Sumitomo Mitsui Trust Bank, Limited (三井住友信託銀行, SMTB) is the core trust-bank operating company of [[trust-banks/sumitomo-mitsui-trust|三井住友トラストグループ (三井住友トラスト・ホールディングス)]]. It is a trust-business-concurrent financial institution that holds, **in addition to a bank license under the Banking Act, a concurrent trust business under the Concurrent Operation Act**, and is the center of one of Japan's largest, strongly specialist-oriented trust-bank groups. Despite the similar name, it is a **separate group** from [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG / SMBC グループ]], and this boundary is decisively important in trust-bank analysis.

## 2. Operating model

As a trust-business-concurrent financial institution, SMTB conducts the following on an integrated basis:

- **A combination of banking + trust + real estate**: a combined commercial bank + trust bank + real estate service solution for individuals, corporations, financial institutions, and institutional investors.
- **Pensions / asset administration / asset management**: pension trusts, asset administration, and the provision of asset-management products.
- **Securities agency / entrusted assets**: securities agency as a shareholder-registry administrator, and management of entrusted assets.

Whereas the megabank-affiliated trust banks ([[trust-banks/mitsubishi-ufj-trust-bank|MUTB]] and [[trust-banks/mizuho-trust-bank|みずほ信託]]) are one function of a commercial-banking group, SMTB differs in that it is the core of an independent holding-company group with a strongly trust-specialist character. On the asset-administration side it connects to [[trust-banks/custody-bank|資産管理サービス系 custody 機関]] and [[trust-banks/master-trust-bank|日本マスタートラスト信託銀行]]. Supervision is by [[financial-regulators/fsa|金融庁]].

## 3. Why this page matters

- A disambiguation anchor that establishes that SMTB — easily confused with SMFG due to the "Sumitomo Mitsui" name — is the center of an independent trust-bank group.
- From the standpoint of asset administration, pensions, real estate, trust entrustment, and investment-trust / financial-instruments intermediation, it must be viewed as a specialized trust bank rather than a mega commercial bank.
- A contrast with the group boundary against [[trust-banks/smbc-trust-bank|SMBC信託銀行]] under SMFG.
