# Kookmin Bank Tokyo Branch

## Wiki route

## TL;DR

Kookmin Bank Tokyo Branch is the Japan branch of Republic of Korea / KB Kookmin Bank, listed on the FSA foreign bank branch list. As the Japan branch of a Korean retail / commercial banking giant, it serves the remittance, deposit, and corporate-banking corridor between Korea and Japan.

## 2. Operating model

KB Kookmin Bank's overseas-office page lists the Tokyo branch address and contact details. JBA also lists the bank as a Japan banking database entry. The Japan role is a foreign-bank branch connection to Korean banking flows and corporate / customer support, not a Japan-incorporated bank.
