## TL;DR

E.SUN Bank Tokyo Branch is the Japan branch of Taiwan's E.SUN Bank, listed on the FSA's list of foreign bank branches. E.SUN's Japan operations function as a Northeast Asia corridor linking the Tokyo, Fukuoka, and Kumamoto sub-branches, handling corporate deposits, financing, trade finance, remittance, and treasury services.

## 2. Operating model

E.SUN's Japan Branch page states that E.SUN has branches in Tokyo and Fukuoka, plus Kumamoto sub-branch, and describes Japan as a key Northeast Asia financial hub. The Tokyo branch, inaugurated in 2017, provides customer deposit services, financing services, trade finance, remittance, and treasury functions.
