## TL;DR

E.SUN Bank Tokyo Branch is the Japan branch of Taiwan-based E.SUN Bank and appears on the Financial Services Agency list of foreign bank branches. E.SUN's Japan footprint links Tokyo, Fukuoka, and a Kumamoto sub-branch as a Northeast Asia banking corridor for corporate deposits, financing, trade finance, remittance, and treasury services.

## 2. Operating model

E.SUN's Japan branch materials state that the bank operates in Tokyo and Fukuoka, with an additional Kumamoto sub-branch, and position Japan as an important Northeast Asia financial hub. The Tokyo branch, inaugurated in 2017, provides customer deposit services, financing services, trade finance, remittance, and treasury functions.
