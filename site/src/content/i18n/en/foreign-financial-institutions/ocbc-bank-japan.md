# Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation Tokyo Branch

## TL;DR

Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation Tokyo Branch is the Japan bank branch of Singapore / Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation Limited listed on the FSA foreign bank branch list. OCBC's Tokyo branch is treated as a wholesale branch serving corporate and institutional banking customers in Japan, providing corporate lending, trade finance, deposits, and remittance services.

## 2. Operating model

OCBC's business-banking international page describes its Tokyo branch as serving corporate and institutional banking customers in Japan, with products including corporate lending, trade finance, deposits, and remittance services. The same page lists the Sanno Park Tower Tokyo main-branch address.

This makes OCBC's Japan profile closer to ASEAN / Greater China cross-border wholesale banking than to Japanese consumer banking.
