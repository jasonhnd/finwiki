## TL;DR

Royal Bank of Canada, Tokyo Branch is the Japan banking branch of Royal Bank of Canada listed in the FSA foreign-bank-branch list. Alongside the RBC Capital Markets Japan securities branch, it is the banking side of the RBC Japan platform, covering transaction banking, treasury, deposits / structured deposits, derivatives, and institutional coverage.

## 2. Operating model

RBC's Japan company profile lists the Tokyo bank branch as a banking and registered-financial-institution business, with JBA quasi-member status and Kanto registration. It also presents a paired securities branch, which means the Japan setup should be read as a bank / securities platform rather than a retail bank.

The banking-side functions include transaction banking support with Japanese financial institutions, treasury management of JPY liquidity and rate risk, deposits and structured deposits for eligible institutional investors, regional-financial-institution coverage, product development, and derivatives activity.
