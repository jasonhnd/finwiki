## TL;DR

DBS銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Singapore / DBS Bank Limited の在日銀行支店。1977 年営業開始の Asia wholesale branch であり、DBS Japan では個人向け日本リテールではなく corporate / institutional banking を中心に扱う。

## 2. Operating model

DBS Japan's official Tokyo Branch page lists the business inauguration date as 1977-04-21, JBA membership, and the Otemachi office. It explicitly states that DBS Bank Tokyo Branch does not handle individual customer business in Japan, which makes the branch a wholesale / institutional presence rather than a retail bank.

The practical role is Japan-Singapore / Asia connectivity: corporate banking, institutional relationships, cross-border treasury, remittance / transaction services, and group client support through DBS's Asia network.
