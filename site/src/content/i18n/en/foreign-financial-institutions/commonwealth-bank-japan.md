## Overview

Commonwealth Bank of Australia's Tokyo branch is a wholesale foreign-bank branch. CBA's public Asia page states that the Tokyo branch was established in 1986 and does not offer retail banking services to individual customers.

The branch is worth promoting from registry-only because the public disclosure identifies activities including funding / investment, FX, interest-rate swaps and futures, structured asset finance, commodity-related intermediary activity, and deposit business. That makes it a peer to [[securities-firms/anz-bank-japan|ANZ]] and [[foreign-financial-institutions/national-australia-bank-japan|NAB]] in the Australia-Japan corridor.
