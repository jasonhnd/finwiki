## TL;DR

Industrial and Commercial Bank of China Tokyo Branch is a bank branch in Japan of mainland China / Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, which is listed on the Financial Services Agency's list of foreign bank branches. Centered around the Tokyo branch established by 1997 年, we handle domestic payments in Japan, overseas remittances, foreign exchange, trade payments, and service corridor for corporations and individuals.

## 2. Operating model

ICBC Tokyo's branch overview states that the Tokyo branch was established in 1997, later adding Ikebukuro sub-branch and Osaka branch coverage. It describes deposits, loans, guarantees, trade settlement finance, domestic settlement, overseas remittance, FX settlement / trading, bank cards, and internet banking.

The branch is one of the key Chinese bank routes for Japan-China payments, trade finance, and renminbi-related services.
