# UnionPay International Japan (銀聯インターナショナル日本支社)

## TL;DR

UnionPay International Japan is the Japan base of UnionPay International (UPI), the international-expansion subsidiary of China's **China UnionPay (中国銀聯)**. It expands the UnionPay brand — which holds an overwhelming share as China's domestic payment infrastructure — within Japan, mainly handling merchant-network development aimed at **acceptance of payments by inbound Chinese tourists**. Domestic issuance (the issuer function of issuing UnionPay cards to domestic consumers) is limited; the main axis is the **acquirer side (merchant contracting / expanding acceptance)**. It is a historical partner that entered a mutual-use partnership with [[card-issuers/jcb|JCB]] in 2006 年, realising interoperability whereby JCB cards can be used at UnionPay merchants in mainland China and UnionPay cards can be used at JCB merchants in Japan.

## 2. Business lines in Japan

- **Inbound merchant development**: expanding merchant acceptance for holders of mainland-China-issued UnionPay cards (Chinese tourists visiting Japan). It focuses on **department stores, consumer-electronics retailers, tourist sites, hotels, and drugstores**.
- **Domestic issuer partnerships**: granting UnionPay-brand card licenses to some domestic issuers (issuance scale is limited).
- **QR-code payments (UnionPay QR / UnionPay QuickPass)**: domestic merchant rollout of QR / NFC payments to serve demand from Chinese visitors. This is a separate channel from Alipay / WeChat Pay, which are widely used within China.
- **ATM cash-withdrawal support**: domestic ATM cash-withdrawal service via UnionPay cards (partnering with major ATM networks such as Seven Bank ATMs).
- **Cross-border corporate payments**: business-payment and remittance-related services between China and Japan.

In terms of being a pure 4-party scheme operator, it is the same type as [[payment-firms/mastercard-japan|Mastercard Japan]], but UnionPay is decisively different in that it is single-mindedly about inbound acceptance and does not aim at domestic issuance.

UnionPay-card issuance for domestic consumers is limited, and the brand's main function is concentrated on the **one-directional function of "accepting Chinese cardholders in Japan"**.
