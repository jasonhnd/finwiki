## TL;DR

iBank Marketing Co., Ltd. is a fintech subsidiary of[[fukuoka-fg|ふくおか FG (FFG)]]100% and is responsible for planning and operating the **Wallet+** (Wallet Plus) smartphone app. As a smartphone-first personal financial service from a regional bank, we provide[[fukuoka-bank|福岡銀行]]/[[18-shinwa-bank|十八親和銀行]]/[[kumamoto-bank|熊本銀行]]customers with **purpose-based savings (myCoin)**, **household budget management**, **prepaid card Wallet+ Card**, **regional cooperation coupons**, etc. It is a typical example of regional bank fintech, and is different from communication / EC wallets such as[[paypay-fg|PayPay]]/[[au-fh|au PAY]]/ [[rakuten-fg|楽天]], which has a "bank account linkage + purpose-specific savings" axis. ^[extracted]

### iBank Marketing established

- **2016**:[[fukuoka-fg|FFG]]established iBank Marketing (a pioneering example of a fintech subsidiary from a regional bank)
- Planning a smartphone-based financial service for young people based on the concept of “reinventing banking”
- [[fukuoka-bank|福岡銀行]] Based on account linkage, household budget management + purpose-specific savings function is the core

### Wallet+ app deployment

- **2016**: Wallet+ app release
- **myCoin function**: Savings by purpose (set a purpose such as travel, marriage, down payment on a house, etc. and save a fixed monthly amount)
- **Wallet+ Card**: JCB brand prepaid card (rechargeable)
- **Regional cooperation**: Coupons linked to stores and tourist spots in Fukuoka city and various parts of Kyushu

### Group expansion

- **2018-2020**:[[18-shinwa-bank|十八親和銀行]]/[[kumamoto-bank|熊本銀行]]added link → Kyushu3 prefecture cover

### FG position

- **Channels to acquire young people**:[[fukuoka-fg]]Bank 3 行's gateway to becoming the main bank for young people
- **Entrance to digital strategy**: Minna no Bank (Digital Bank)/FFG A digital contact point alongside the bank app
- **Regional economic revitalization**: Local consumption circulation using coupons linked to stores in Fukuoka city and other parts of Kyushu

### competitive structure

- **Communication wallet** ([[paypay-fg|PayPay]] /[[au-fh|au PAY]]/ d payment / [[rakuten-fg|楽天ペイ]]): Nationwide, payment-focused, Wallet+ differentiates itself through bank collaboration + purpose-specific savings
- **Bank app** (mega banks/other regional banks): Differentiation with UI/UX, unique functions such as myCoin
- **Household account book app** ([[money-forward|マネーフォワード]] / freee personal): Bank cooperation is wide, but regional cooperation / prepaid is unique to Wallet+
- **Other regional bank fintech**: Similar model to regional bank fintech such as Hokkoku Bank (CCI Group) and Sanin Godo Bank.

### strengths and weaknesses

- Strengths:[[fukuoka-fg]]Bank3 行account customer base + deep regional cooperation in Kyushu + uniqueness of myCoin purpose-specific savings

## 7 . Regulation/Policy

- **Supervising**: Financial Services Agency (FSA) (Banking license not required for app business company alone, via partner bank[[fukuoka-bank]]/ prepaid issuer)
- **Regulatory issues**:
  - Prepaid issuing business is regulated by prepaid payment instrument issuers under the Payment Services Act.
  - Bank API linkage is an electronic payment agency business under the Banking Act (registered by partner bank)
  - Personal Information Protection Act + My Number Act (handling of household financial data)
  - Anti-social checks/money laundering measures (jointly implemented with partner banks)
