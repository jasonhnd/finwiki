# BitTrade (ビットトレード)

## TL;DR

A mid-tier domestic Japanese crypto-asset exchange operator. **The former Huobi Japan was re-renamed to "BitTrade" in 2022-08 **, projecting an impression of separation from the global Huobi parent (associated with Justin Sun). Registered as **Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第 00007 号**, JVCEA Type-I member. Its number of tradable currencies is among the largest domestically (30+ currencies as of 2024 ), taking a long-tail strategy. A mid-tier position next after bitFlyer / Coincheck / GMO Coin / SBI VC Trade. ^[extracted]
