# GMO コイン (GMO Coin)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[payment-firms/INDEX|payment-firms INDEX]]. Read it against [[securities-firms/monex-group|マネックスグループ (Monex Group)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

A crypto-asset exchange under the **GMO Internet Group**. **Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第 00006 号** (one of the 1  earliest registrations in the industry), JVCEA Type 1 member. A major domestic player with a 3 -axis model of **sales outlet + exchange + leveraged trading** plus **20 + tradable currencies**. The **crypto-asset arm** of the GMO financial ecosystem, alongside parent [[payment-firms/gmo-payment-gateway|GMO ペイメントゲートウェイ]] / GMO Click Securities / [[payment-firms/gmo-aozora-net|GMO あおぞらネット銀行]]. Competitors are [[payment-firms/bitflyer]] / Coincheck (within [[securities-firms/monex-group]]) / SBI VC Trade (within [[megabanks/sbi-hd]]) / DMM Bitcoin / bitbank / [[payment-firms/bittrade]].

## 1. Company profile

**Legal name**: GMO Coin Co., Ltd.
**English name**: GMO Coin, Inc.
**Established**: 2016-10-21
**Headquarters**: Cerulean Tower 26-1 , Sakuragaoka-cho, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo (within the GMO Internet Group headquarters)
**Business type**: Crypto-asset exchange operator
**Registration number**: **Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第 00006 号** (crypto-asset exchange business)
**Industry body**: Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association (JVCEA) Type 1 member
**Parent company**: [[payment-firms/gmo-financial-hd|GMO フィナンシャルホールディングス]] (TSE PRIME 7177, voting rights 100%) → GMO Internet Group (TSE PRIME 9449)

## 2. Business segment map

| Segment | Content | Characteristics |
|---|---|---|
| Sales outlet | Principal trading of customer vs GMO Coin | Spread-based; for beginners |
| Exchange (spot) | Order-book trading (customer-to-customer matching) | Centered on BTC / ETH / XRP / LTC / BCH |
| **Leveraged trading** | Crypto-asset FX (up to 2 x, individuals) | Offered since the early days of the industry; a main revenue source |
| Corporate OTC | Large-lot principal trading | Supports crypto-asset handling by institutional investors and listed companies |
| Staking | Some PoS currencies (ETH, etc.) | Rewards granted on deposited crypto-assets |
| Crypto-asset lending | Lending | For individuals |

**Tradable currencies**: BTC / ETH / XRP / LTC / BCH / XEM / XLM / BAT / OMG / XTZ / QTUM / ENJ / DOT / ATOM / ADA / MKR / DAI / LINK / DOGE / SOL / FCR / others, approximately 20-30  currencies (varies by point in time). **The number of tradable currencies in the exchange format is among the largest domestically.**

**Overseas expansion**: Limited. While the GMO financial group as a whole has overseas FX bases (Thailand, Hong Kong, UK), GMO Coin itself is **based on domestic licensing**.

## 5. Regulation / policy

- **Jurisdiction**: FSA / Kanto Local Finance Bureau
- **Registration**: Crypto-asset exchange operator (Payment Services Act Article 63 -2) / Director-General of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau 第 00006 号
- **Self-regulation**: JVCEA (Japan Virtual and Crypto assets Exchange Association) Type 1 member ── tradable-currency screening / unified AML/KYC standards
- **Derivatives**: FIEA Type 1 Financial Instruments Business (crypto-asset derivatives trading; since the 2019-05  amendment)
- **Policy topics from 2024**:
  - Stablecoin regime (amended Payment Services Act, in force 2023-06 ) → GMO has advance know-how via its US subsidiary
  - **Tax system**: crypto-asset gains/losses taxed as miscellaneous income under progressive taxation → request for a shift to separate self-assessment taxation (common across the industry)
  - Taxation on year-end fair-value valuation of crypto-assets held by corporations → amendment in progress
  - Speeding up of JVCEA tradable-currency screening (a common industry issue)
