# JPYC Inc.


## Wiki Route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[payment-firms/wise-payments-japan|Wise Payments Japan]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

JPYC Inc. is a Japanese-yen-denominated stablecoin / electronic payment instrument funds-transfer-type issuer. JPYC EX is the issuance and redemption gateway between Japanese yen and JPYC, and the company is listed in the FSA funds-transfer service provider registry as Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director-General 第00099号.

## 2. Business Role

- JPYC EX lets users issue and redeem JPYC against Japanese yen by bank transfer.
- Official JPYC EX materials describe JPYC as redeemable to Japanese yen, distinct from JPYC Prepaid.
- The company is an important comparator against [[payment-firms/progmat|Progmat]] trust-type infrastructure and [[exchanges/jp-exchange-sbi-vc-trade|SBI VC Trade]] USDC distribution.
