# SmartBank

## Wiki Route

## TL;DR

SmartBank is a household-finance and prepaid wallet fintech that operates B/43 / ONE BANK. Its official company profile states both funds-transfer service provider registration and third-party prepaid payment instrument issuer registration, making it a case that combines household-budgeting UX with a payment account.

## 2. Business Role

- Builds payment and account-like UX around household budgeting problems.
- Uses prepaid / funds-transfer licensing to support shared spending and wallet flows.
- A useful comparator against [[payment-firms/kyash|Kyash]], [[payment-firms/merpay|Merpay]], and bank app experiences such as [[payment-firms/paypay|PayPay]] super-app coverage.

## 3. Why This Standalone Page Matters
