## Overview

Recruit MUFG Business Co., Ltd. is a joint venture of Recruit and [[megabanks/mufg-bank|MUFG Bank]] that provides the Air Wallet app and COIN+ payment brand. Its official company profile lists third-party prepaid issuer registration and funds-transfer service provider registration; the FSA list records it as Kanto Local Finance Bureau Director 第00081号 with registration date 2020-08-13.

The company is strategically important because it combines Recruit's merchant / consumer service surfaces with MUFG banking rails. It is not a classic card issuer; it is an account-linked wallet / payment infrastructure route.

## 2. Business Role

- The official company site describes the business as providing the Air Wallet remittance app and COIN+ payment brand.
- Recruit's public releases position RMB as the operator behind COIN+ account-linking, bank-account integrations, wage digital payment, and the Air Wallet Quick Loan route.
- The key lens is distribution: Recruit's service ecosystem can create wallet touchpoints without starting from a standalone bank app.

## 3. Why This Standalone Page Matters

RMB sits between [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]], consumer apps, merchants, payroll flows, and account-to-account payment.
