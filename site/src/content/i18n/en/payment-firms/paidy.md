## TL;DR

Japan's first full-scale **BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later)** provider. Established in 2008-10  as the former **Exchange Corporation**, and renamed Paidy in 2018-09 社. Founder: **Russell Cummer** (from the United States). **In 2021-09-07, PayPal Holdings announced the acquisition for approximately 27 億 dollars (about 3000 億円)**, completed in 2022 , making it a PayPal Japan subsidiary. It enables payment with only an email address + phone number, with later payment by convenience store, bank transfer, or account transfer. Approximately 100 万 merchants participate (Amazon Japan / SHEIN / Apple Store / Mercari, etc.). It occupies the Japan-version position of US Affirm / Klarna / Afterpay. ^[extracted]

| Service | Description |
|---|---|

### Credit Model

- **Phone number + email authentication**: No bank account or credit-card registration required; credit decision in as little as a few seconds
- **AI scoring**: Dynamically calculates credit limit from transaction history / device / behavioral data
- **Post-payment collection channels**: Convenience store / bank transfer / account transfer / Pay-easy
- **Fee model**: Merchant-side fees (higher than credit cards) + consumer-side fees for installments (lump-sum is free)

### Global Comparison

- **US Affirm**: Listed BNPL company, partnered with Amazon US
- **Swedish Klarna**: Europe's largest BNPL company, global expansion
- **Australian Afterpay**: Acquired by Block (formerly Square) in 2022  (about 290 億 dollars)

### Merchant Strategy

- **Amazon Japan official BNPL**: Largest customer-acquisition channel; permanently available as Amazon's "deferred payment" option
- **SHEIN / cross-border e-commerce**: Fashion e-commerce for Gen Z
- **Apple Store**: Spread among younger users as installment purchase option for iPhone / Mac
- **Mercari**: Flea-market + BNPL cross-over (note: Mercari itself also operates Merpay deferred payment inside [[mercari-hd]], making it both competitor and merchant)

## 4. Regulation / Policy

- **Supervisors**: Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (Installment Sales Act; registered as a small-amount comprehensive credit purchase intermediary) + FSA (Payment Services Act-related)
- **Installment Sales Act amendment response** (promulgated 2020-06 , effective 2021-04 ): New "small-amount comprehensive credit purchase intermediary" category -> BNPL companies including Paidy became subject to registration. AI credit models were formally recognized ^[extracted]
- **Personal Information Protection Act**: Handling of personal data for credit decisions based on phone-number + email authentication
