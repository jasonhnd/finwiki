# Kyash

## TL;DR

Kyash is an independent fintech operator that combines a wallet, prepaid Visa, and funds-transfer functions. It discloses its corporate profile, funds-transfer service-provider registration, third-party prepaid payment instrument issuer registration, PCI DSS compliance, and related items, making it a non-bank benchmark in the Japanese wallet ecosystem.

## 2. Business role

- Bridges app-based money movement and Visa card acceptance.
- Serves as a startup-style alternative to bank, telecom, and retail payment groups.
- The shareholder mix links venture capital and financial institutions, making it useful for fintech ecosystem mapping alongside peers like [[payment-firms/smartbank|SmartBank]], [[payment-firms/pring|pring]], and major wallets such as [[payment-firms/paypay|PayPay]].

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

Kyash is not a bank-owned payment arm. It is a regulated independent operator whose value is in user experience, card issuing, and wallet transfer design.
