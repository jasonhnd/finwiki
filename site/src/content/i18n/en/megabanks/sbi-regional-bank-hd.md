# SBI Regional Bank Holdings

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[megabanks/mufg|三菱 UFJ FG (MUFG)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

**A regional-bank integration platform holding company under [[sbi-hd]].** As a legal entity, it was **established in 2015-08-25 as "ER6  Inc."**, renamed "SBI Bank Holdings Co., Ltd." in 2019-07-05, and **renamed again in 2020-05-01 to the current trade name, SBI Regional Bank Holdings, Inc.**, repurposing it as the execution vehicle for regional-bank integration (authorization as a bank holding company came in 2022-10-07). It is the core corporation behind the "**No. 4  Megabank Concept**" proposed by Yoshitaka Kitao in 2020 , and oversees capital / business alliances, management support, and system sharing for SBI-affiliated regional banks (partner regional banks). It holds [[sbi-shinsei-bank]] (made a wholly owned subsidiary in 2023-09 ) as its commercial-bank core, and has capital alliances with more than 10 行 medium-sized regional banks and related institutions including Chikuho Bank, Shimizu Bank, Daito Bank, Fukushima Bank, [[shimane-bank|島根銀行]], and Dai-Ichi Kangyo Credit Cooperative. The intended model is a "**loose federation model**" that shares SBI Group resources such as SBI Securities, crypto assets, and asset-management products through the regional-bank network. ^[extracted]

### Strategic intent of becoming an 2020  integration platform

SBI HD had been making individual investments in regional banks since the late 2010 年s, but after the "No. 4  Megabank Concept" proposed in 2020 , it repurposed an existing dormant entity (the "ER6  Inc." established in 2015-08-25 and renamed "SBI Bank Holdings" in 2019-07 ) by **changing its trade name in 2020-05-01 to "SBI Regional Bank Holdings" and turning it into an integration platform**. The mechanism supplements system investment, asset-management product development, and digital finance for younger customers, which are difficult for standalone regional banks, with SBI-side group resources. ^[extracted]

### Prehistory of the concept (late 2010 年s)

- **Yoshitaka Kitao** (SBI HD Chairman and CEO) recognized regional-bank management difficulty, population decline, and low profitability as an opportunity
- 2019-09 : invested about 34% in **[[shimane-bank|島根銀行]]** -- the 第 1 号 of regional-bank capital alliances
- 2019-11 : invested in **Fukushima Bank**
- 2020-03 : invested in **Chikuho Bank**

### Significance of the former Shinsei Bank tender offer

- Government-held shares in former Shinsei Bank (held by the Deposit Insurance Corporation and Resolution and Collection Corporation at about 22%) had long been an issue
- SBI HD acquired former Shinsei shares from 2019 , conducted the 2021-09  tender offer, and through full ownership in 2023-09  acquired the **commercial-bank core under the regional-bank HD**

### "Loose federation" model

- **Maintaining management independence**: partner regional banks retain their own management (mergers / integrations are not forced)
- **Resource sharing**: SBI Securities, crypto assets, asset-management products, systems, and talent are made common infrastructure
- **No need for vertical integration**: not 3  megabank-style full subsidiary conversion, but a loose federation based on capital alliances + business cooperation
- **Economies of scale**: shared burden for DX investment that is difficult for standalone regional banks

### Core functions and aims

- **Digital finance for individuals**: SBI infrastructure such as SBI Securities, SBI Sumishin Net Bank, and SBI Insurance supplied to regional-bank customers
- **Corporate side**: former Shinsei Bank's corporate lending and specialty-finance functions supplied to regional-bank corporate customers
- **Asset management**: SBI asset products and crypto assets (SBI VC Trade) distributed through regional-bank counters
- **DX**: regional-bank cost reduction through core-banking system sharing and unified mobile banking

### Commercial-bank core = SBI Shinsei Bank

- Uses former Shinsei Bank as a kind of "**central bank of the regional-bank HD**" [[sbi-shinsei-bank]]
- Opens functions such as APLUS (credit cards), Showa Leasing, and Shinsei Financial (consumer finance) to the regional-bank network

## 5. Regulation / policy

- **Primary authority**: Financial Services Agency (FSA) -- linked to regional-financial-institution reorganization policy
- **FSA regional-bank reorganization policy**: aligned with policy to promote "regional-bank reorganization for regional economic revitalization"
- **Public-funds issue**: of the public funds injected into former Shinsei Bank (former Long-Term Credit Bank of Japan), the unrecovered portion of about 3500 億円 was a longstanding issue (a widely reported figure). Repayment after SBI HD's subsidiary conversion was an issue, but based on the confirmed repayment scheme agreed in 2025-06 , the remaining amount of about 2300 億円 was fully repaid in 2025-07-31 (based on FSA and SBI HD announcements)
