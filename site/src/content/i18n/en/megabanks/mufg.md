# Mitsubishi UFJ FG (MUFG)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[megabanks/smfg|三井住友 FG (SMFG / SMBC グループ)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Japan's largest financial group, with total assets of **431.7 兆円** and net income of **2 兆 4300 億円** in **2026-03  (FY2026), the highest profit in Japanese banking history**, announced on 2026-05-15. It was formed in 2005-10-01 through the merger of Mitsubishi Tokyo FG and UFJ HD. Its core axes are **commercial banking + trust banking + securities through the Morgan Stanley strategic alliance + overseas operations, especially Asia and Krungsri, 4 **. It is a G-SIB Bucket 1 institution. Group slogan: "Committed to empowering a brighter future." The acronym UFJ comes from United Financial of Japan, and the company name includes "Financial" 2  times. ^[extracted]

### Merger history and predecessors

- Former **Mitsubishi Bank**: founded 1880  by Mitsubishi zaibatsu founder Yataro Iwasaki.
- Former **Bank of Tokyo**: from 1946, based on Yokohama Specie Bank; foreign-exchange specialist bank.
- **Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi**: 1996-04-01 merger of Mitsubishi Bank and Bank of Tokyo.
- Former **Sanwa Bank**: 1933  merger of Konoike, Yamaguchi, and Thirty-Four Bank 3 行; predecessor money changer Konoike was founded in 1656 .
- Former **Tokai Bank**: 1941 名 merger of Nagoya, Aichi, and Ito Bank 3 行.
- **UFJ Holdings**: 2002-01  combination of Sanwa Bank, Tokai Bank, and Toyo Trust and Banking.
- **MUFG**: 2005-10-01 merger of Mitsubishi Tokyo FG and UFJ HD.

> Integration background: UFJ Bank's nonperforming loans expanded, leading first to a planned sale of UFJ Trust to Sumitomo Trust (2004-06), then a policy switch in 2004-07  toward management integration with Mitsubishi Tokyo FG. Sumitomo Trust filed for an injunction with the Tokyo District Court. On 2006-08-11, the Tokyo High Court rejected Sumitomo Trust's petition; on 2006-11-20, the parties settled with a settlement payment of 25 億円 to Sumitomo Trust. ^[extracted]

### Digital strategy

- **GO (Global Open) strategy**: API opening and partner collaboration.
- **Insight as a Service**: Data / AI analytics for corporate clients.
- **Japan Digital Design** (established 2017-10-02): fintech subsidiary.
- **Global Open Network Japan** (JV with Akamai in 2019-04  -> closure in 2022-02 ): failed blockchain-settlement initiative.
- **MUFG x Google strategic alliance** (2026-05-07) ★: personal financial-service AI agent, practical-use target during 2026 年 ^[extracted]
- **Progmat Coin**: stablecoin infrastructure led by MUFG, with [[smfg]] and others participating.

### Alliance / acquisition strategy

- **Morgan Stanley strategic alliance** (from 2008-09-22): initial investment of 90 億 dollars, about 9000 億円, acquiring 21% of issued shares and later maintaining around ~20%. It is the deepest global IB tie-up by a Japanese bank. 2010-05  integration of Japan securities operations.
- **Google strategic alliance** (from 2026-05-07): AI agent area.
- **Krungsri TOB** (2013-12): major Thai bank, about 5360 億円.
- **Bank Danamon consolidation** (2019-04): staged Indonesian acquisition of about 3970 億円.
- **Union Bank sale -> U.S. Bancorp share switch** (2022-12): exit from US retail and shift toward IB concentration.
- **KDDI / au Jibun Bank stake unwind** (2025-01-31): sold all au Jibun shares to KDDI and received 100% of Mitsubishi UFJ e-Smart Securities (former Kabu.com Securities) as consideration.
- **Mitsubishi HC Capital** (2021-04): merger of former Mitsubishi UFJ Lease and Hitachi Capital.
- **Mobit unwind** (2013-05 to 2014): dissolved MUFG + SMFG JV and sold the business to the SMBC side.

### Overseas strategy

- **Asia concentration**: Krungsri / Bank Danamon / VietinBank / Security Bank, ranging from equity-method stakes to consolidated subsidiaries.
- **Americas**: sale of Union Bank -> U.S. Bancorp shareholding, with focus on IB / corporate lending.
- **Europe**: MUFG Bank Europe in the Netherlands and others.
- **China**: Mitsubishi UFJ Bank (China), 100%.

### B2C branding

- **MUFG Stadium**: contract signed 2025-10-15; naming rights for the National Stadium for 2026-01-26 to 2030-01 , a 5 -year term ^[extracted]
- **Corporate colors**: MUFG Red for passion + MUFG Gray for trust. Branding was by Kazumasa Nagai of Nippon Design Center.
