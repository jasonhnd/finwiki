## TL;DR

Mizuho Bank, Ltd. is the commercial-banking core entity of [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]]. On the FSA's bank license list it is a licensed bank listed as "株式会社みずほ銀行" (city bank), with bank code 0001. It has a bank-license boundary separate from the parent company, the trust bank, the securities company, and the AM company.

## 2. Operating model

- The main engine of the Mizuho group for deposits, lending, domestic/foreign exchange, settlement, and corporate finance, carrying the merger lineage of the former Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Fuji Bank, and Industrial Bank of Japan.
- The bank-license layer (this page) handles the branch / overseas network and the deposit-lending / settlement functions, while the [[megabanks/mizuho-fg|みずほ FG]] page handles the consolidated / holding-company strategy.
- Combine with [[trust-banks/mizuho-trust-bank|みずほ信託銀行]] / [[securities-firms/mizuho-securities|みずほ証券]] / [[asset-managers/asset-management-one|アセットマネジメントOne]] to see the role division across "bank / trust / securities / asset management".

## 3. Why a standalone page matters

[[megabanks/mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]]'s listing, G-SIB status, and group strategy are a different unit of analysis from the bank's standalone business-improvement orders, branch network, and deposit-lending / FX / corporate-settlement functions. Separating the bank operating company makes it possible to directly track the FSA registration list, the license under the Banking Act, settlement-infrastructure connections, and domestic and overseas locations.
