## TL;DR

Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation (三井住友銀行) is the commercial-banking core entity of [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG / SMBC グループ]]. On the FSA bank license list it is listed as "株式会社三井住友銀行" (city bank), with bank code 0009. It holds a licensed bank layer separate from its parent SMFG, [[trust-banks/smbc-trust-bank|SMBC信託銀行]], and [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興証券]].

## 2. Operating model

- The banking entity responsible for the SMBC group's deposit-taking, lending, domestic/foreign exchange, settlement, and global corporate finance.
- The bank-license layer is functionally separated from [[trust-banks/smbc-trust-bank|SMBC信託銀行]] (trust / PRESTIA / foreign-currency, real-estate, and trust functions) and the brokerage / underwriting layer of [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興証券]].
- Through coordination with Sumitomo Mitsui Card and SMBC Consumer Finance, it builds a retail / wealth / payment funnel beyond the bank alone.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

[[megabanks/smfg|SMFG]] is the listed holding company, but the subject of deposit-taking, lending, and exchange transactions under the Banking Act is Sumitomo Mitsui Banking. To analyze Olive, corporate settlement, overseas branches, and the bank-agency / trust-agency structure with SMBC Trust, the SMFG page alone is too coarse.
