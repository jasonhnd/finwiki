## TL;DR

Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation is the core commercial-bank entity of [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG / SMBC グループ]]. In the FSA bank-license list, it appears as Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation, and it has a licensed-bank layer separate from parent company SMFG, [[trust-banks/smbc-trust-bank|SMBC信託銀行]], and [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興証券]].

## 2. Business role

- Banking entity responsible for the SMBC Group's deposits, lending, payments, and global corporate finance.
- [[trust-banks/smbc-trust-bank|SMBC信託銀行]] functions as an 100% subsidiary of SMBC, covering trust / PRESTIA / foreign-currency, real-estate, and trust functions.
- Through coordination with [[securities-firms/smbc-nikko|SMBC日興証券]], Sumitomo Mitsui Card, and SMBC Consumer Finance, it builds a retail / wealth / payment funnel beyond the bank alone.

## 3. Why standalone page matters

SMFG is the listed holding company, but Sumitomo Mitsui Banking Corporation is the legal subject for deposit-taking, lending, and exchange transactions under the Banking Act. For analysis of Olive, corporate payments, overseas branches, and bank-agency / trust-agency structures with SMBC Trust, the SMFG page alone is too coarse-grained.
