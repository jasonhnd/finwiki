# Mizuho FG

## Wiki Route

This entry sits under [[megabanks/INDEX|megabanks INDEX]]. Read it against [[megabanks/mufg|三菱 UFJ FG (MUFG)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

Mizuho FG is Japan's 3  largest financial group and one of the 3  megabanks, with total assets of **283.3 兆円** and net income of **8854 億円** (record high) for the 2025-03  period ^[extracted]. It is a holding company whose origin was the **3 行 integration of Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Fuji Bank, and Industrial Bank of Japan** in 2003-01-08. Structural complexity from the 3 行 merger and repeated major system failures in **2002  / 2011  / 2021 ** were long-term brand-damage factors. The **"One MIZUHO"** strategy moved the group toward integrated operations. It complements B2C digital weaknesses through partnerships, including the **capital and business alliance with Rakuten Securities** from 2022-11-, **strategic investment in PayPay Bank**, and **joint ventures with CyberAgent (CA Mobile / others)**. G-SIB Bucket 1. ^[extracted]

### Merger History and Predecessors

- Former **Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank (DKB)**: created through the 1971  merger of Dai-Ichi Bank and Nippon Kangyo Bank. The former Dai-Ichi Bank was Japan's oldest bank, founded by Eiichi Shibusawa in 1873 
- Former **Fuji Bank**: from 1948-, originally Yasuda Bank / Yasuda zaibatsu line, renamed after the postwar GHQ dissolution of zaibatsu
- Former **Industrial Bank of Japan (IBJ)**: established in 1902  as a special bank responsible for long-term credit finance; converted to an ordinary bank after the war and later specialized in corporate finance as a **long-term credit bank**
- **2000-09-29**: Mizuho Holdings established, with 3 行 becoming wholly owned subsidiaries through share transfer ^[extracted]
- **2002-04-01**: Mizuho Bank and Mizuho Corporate Bank launched, split by function into retail / SME and large-corporate / international businesses ^[extracted]
- **2003-01-08**: Mizuho FG launched as the upper pure holding company ^[extracted]
- **2013-07-01**: Mizuho Bank and Mizuho Corporate Bank recombined into the new **Mizuho Bank**, revising the function-split model after 11  years ^[extracted]

### Digital Strategy

- **"One MIZUHO" strategy**: Long-running theme of 3 行 culture integration and group-integrated operations ^[extracted]
- **e-Mizuho**: Digital banking for individuals ^[extracted]
- **MINORI** (Mizuho next-generation core banking system): Fully operational in 2019 年, a huge system renewal with investment over 4500 億円. It also became a source of repeated failures in 2021 年 ^[extracted]
- **AI / ChatGPT use in operations**: In 2023-06 , Mizuho began providing conversational AI to almost all domestic employees, about 3.5 万 people. Jointly developed with Microsoft Japan, it is used for document creation, summarization, translation, programming, and related work. It was the **first full-employee operational rollout among megabanks**, following the study announced in 2023-04 

### Alliance and Acquisition Strategy

- **Rakuten Securities capital and business alliance** (from 2022-10) ★: Mizuho Securities acquired about 49% of Rakuten Securities shares, later just under 50% after staged capital increases, at about 800 億円 scale. **First deep external online-securities investment by a megabank**, aimed at common retail customer channels and product platforms ^[extracted]
- **U.S. Greenhill acquisition** (completed 2023-09 , about 770 億円 / 5.5 億 dollars): Brought an independent global M&A advisory firm under Mizuho, the first acquisition of an independent investment bank by a Japanese bank ^[extracted]
- **JV with Dai-ichi Life**: Asset Management One (51:49) ^[extracted]
- **Investment in PayPay Securities** (former One Tap BUY): Mizuho Securities formed a JV with SoftBank in 2020-10  and invested about 24.8%. Even after PayPay made it a consolidated subsidiary in 2025-02 , Mizuho Securities continued to invest as a cooperation partner
- **Business alliance with CyberAgent and others**: Mizuho Bank, CyberAgent, and Flywheel concluded a strategic business alliance for a marketing platform business in 2024-01-26, and began advertising businesses such as digital signage ads in 2024-10 
- **JV with Marubeni**: Mizuho Leasing (equity-method 23.5%, Marubeni 23%) ^[extracted]
- **PE fund formation with Bain Capital** (announced 2025-09 ) ^[extracted]

### Overseas Strategy

- **Americas**: New York-based Mizuho Americas, from the former Mizuho Corporate Bank New York Branch, plus Greenhill as independent M&A advisor
- **Asia**: China (Mizuho Bank China), Thailand, Indonesia, Singapore, and others
- **Europe**: London IBD, derived from the former IBJ London line

### B2C Branding

## 4. Regulation and Policy

- **Primary authority**: Financial Services Agency (FSA)
- **Holding-company regulation**: Banking Act Article 52 -17

- **Overseas regulation**: U.S. Federal Reserve / OCC, ECB / BoE, China CBIRC, Thailand BoT, and others
- **Business-improvement orders**: Received multiple business-improvement orders due to major system failures in **2002  / 2011  / 2021  and 3 **. The 2021-11  order was the bank's 2 th such order historically ^[extracted]
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - From 2024 onward, Bank of Japan policy-rate normalization improves domestic interest margins, a common bonus for the 3 行 megabanks
  - Progress on the 2024-2026  medium-term plan and achievement of the ROE 8% target from 2025 onward
  - Strengthening sustainable finance and decarbonization lending policies
