# NTT DOCOMO Financial Group (NDFG)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[megabanks/INDEX|megabanks INDEX]]. Read it against [[non-life-insurers/rakuten-insurance-hd|楽天インシュアランスホールディングス]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

The **financial-business supervisory intermediate holding company "NTT DOCOMO Financial Group, Inc." (DOCOMO FG)** which NTT DOCOMO will **commence operations on 2026-07-01** (succeeding the businesses and related shares in the financial domain via absorption-type split). It reorganizes securities (Monex Group) + banking (SBI Sumishin Net Bank / docomo Neobank brand) + cards & payments (d CARD / d払い / d Smart Bank) under 1  roof. Its positioning is the **4 th pole of the "telco-affiliated FGs"** alongside [[megabanks/au-fh|au-fg]] / [[megabanks/paypay-fg]] / [[payment-firms/rakuten-fg]]. The parent company is NTT DOCOMO (NTT 100%). ^[extracted]

## 1. Company overview

**Parent company**: NTT DOCOMO, INC. (Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corporation 100%) ^[extracted]
**Headquarters**: 2-11-1  Nagatacho, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, Sanno Park Tower ^[extracted]

### Establishment scheme (on a public-document basis) ^[extracted]

NTT DOCOMO announced a spin-off / transfer scheme consolidating its existing financial-related subsidiaries and equity interests into the intermediate holding company NDFG. NDFG is positioned as a 100% subsidiary of NTT DOCOMO, and a listing plan has not been announced. The ultimate top-level parent of the entire group continues to be Nippon Telegraph and Telephone (NTT, TSE PRIME 9432).

### Positioning as the "4 th pole" of telco-affiliated FGs

- **The existing 3  poles**: [[megabanks/au-fh|au-fg]] (KDDI / a reorganization period after selling au Jibun Bank), [[megabanks/paypay-fg]] (SoftBank / Z Holdings keiretsu, PayPay Bank + PayPay Securities + PayPay Card), [[payment-firms/rakuten-fg]] (Rakuten Bank / Rakuten Securities / Rakuten Card / Rakuten Pay)

### Aim of the financial-services integration (the gist of the public announcement) ^[extracted]

1. Changing the **operating bodies of securities, banking, cards, and points from dispersed management to supervisory management**, clarifying group KPIs and ROE management
2. **Separating the management responsibility** of the NTT DOCOMO main body (telecom) from the financial business (regulatory response, governance)
3. Centralizing, at the intermediate-HD level, the **cross-sell design** of the d-point economic zone + financial products

### Partnership / acquisition strategy

- **Monex Group**: capital and business alliance agreement (2023-10-04) → consolidated-subsidiary conversion completed in 2024-01-04 (via docomo Monex HD, DOCOMO voting rights approx. 49%). The core of the securities segment ^[extracted]
- **SBI Sumishin Net Bank**: developing from providing the docomo Neobank brand, with a TOB launched in 2025-05-30 (established 2025-09 ), it becomes an **NTT DOCOMO consolidated subsidiary on 2025-10-01**. After capital reorganization, DOCOMO 55.37% : Sumitomo Mitsui Trust 44.63% (joint management at voting rights 50%／50%), and a 2026-08-03 name change to "docomo SMTB Net Bank" ^[extracted]
- **Coincheck Group**: succeeded as a crypto-asset business under Monex ^[extracted]

### B2C branding

- **d-brand integration**: continuing the commonization of the "d" prefix across d CARD, d Smart Bank, d POINT, and d払い ^[extracted]
