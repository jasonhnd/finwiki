# NTT DOCOMO Financial Group (NDFG)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[megabanks/INDEX|megabanks INDEX]]. Read it against [[non-life-insurers/rakuten-insurance-hd|楽天インシュアランスホールディングス]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

NTT DOCOMO created **NTT DOCOMO Financial Group** as a financial-business intermediate holding company, starting operations on 2026-07-01 through an absorption-type company split that transferred financial businesses and related shares. It reorganizes securities, banking, cards, payments, and related finance businesses under a 1  single roof: Monex Group, Sumishin SBI Net Bank / docomo Neobank, d Card, d Barai, and d Smart Bank. The entity is positioned as the **4 th pole of telco financial groups**, alongside [[megabanks/au-fh|au-fg]], [[paypay-fg]], and [[rakuten-fg]]. The parent is NTT DOCOMO, within the NTT 100% group. ^[extracted]

## 1. Company profile

**Formal name**: NTT DOCOMO Financial Group, Inc. ^[extracted]  
**English name**: NTT DOCOMO Financial Holdings, a working English label because the formal English name was not fully disclosed in the source note.  
**Business start**: 2026-07-01, the effective date of the absorption-type split, based on the official release. ^[extracted]  
**Parent**: NTT DOCOMO, Inc., under Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corporation 100%. ^[extracted]  
**Head office**: Sanno Park Tower, Nagatacho 2-11-1 , Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo. ^[extracted]

### Establishment scheme / public-release basis ^[extracted]

NTT DOCOMO announced a scheme to move existing financial subsidiaries and shareholdings into the intermediate holding company NDFG. NDFG is positioned as a 100% subsidiary of NTT DOCOMO, and no public listing plan was announced. The top parent of the overall group remains Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corporation, NTT, TSE Prime 9432.

### Telco financial-group "4 th pole" positioning

- **Existing 3  poles**: [[megabanks/au-fh|au-fg]] (KDDI / au, after au Jibun Bank sale and restructuring), [[paypay-fg]] (SoftBank / Z Holdings, PayPay Bank + PayPay Securities + PayPay Card), and [[rakuten-fg]] (Rakuten Bank / Rakuten Securities / Rakuten Card / Rakuten Pay).

### Aims of financial-service integration / public-release intent ^[extracted]

1. Move securities, banking, cards, and points from dispersed operating control to integrated control, clarifying group KPI and ROE management.  
2. Separate financial-business management responsibility from the NTT DOCOMO telecom parent for governance and regulatory response.  
3. Centralize cross-sell design for the d Point ecosystem and financial products at the intermediate-holding-company level.

### Partnership / acquisition strategy

- **Monex Group**: capital and business alliance signed 2023-10-04, with consolidation completed in 2024-01-04 through DOCOMO Monex HD and DOCOMO voting rights of about 49%; core of the securities segment. ^[extracted]
- **Sumishin SBI Net Bank**: developed from docomo Neobank brand provision into TOB starting 2025-05-30 and completed in 2025-09 , becoming an **NTT DOCOMO consolidated subsidiary** in 2025-10-01. After capital reorganization, DOCOMO holds 55.37%, Sumitomo Mitsui Trust holds 44.63%, and voting rights 50% / 50% create joint management. Scheduled name change to "DOCOMO SMTB Net Bank" in 2026-08-03. ^[extracted]
- **Coincheck Group**: inherited as Monex's crypto-asset business. ^[extracted]

### B2C branding

- **d-brand unification**: continues common "d" references across d Card, d Smart Bank, d Point, and d Barai. ^[extracted]
