# Saitama Resona Bank

## TL;DR

Saitama Resona Bank, Limited is a banking corporation under the [[megabanks/resona-hd|りそな HD]] umbrella, headquartered in Urawa-ku, Saitama City, Saitama Prefecture, that runs the retail / SME franchise within Saitama Prefecture. It is a licensed bank for the Saitama market holding a separate, independent banking license from Resona Bank; in the FSA's bank-license list it is listed as "Saitama Resona Bank, Limited," with bank code 0017.

## 2. Operating model

- An independently licensed commercial bank that, within the Resona group, handles regional finance (deposits, lending, settlement) in Saitama Prefecture.
- While using the same group app, brand, and management strategy as [[megabanks/resona-bank|りそな銀行]], it maintains touchpoints with Saitama Prefecture's regional corporate and individual customers.
- The parent [[megabanks/resona-hd|りそな HD]] handles consolidation and capital policy; this page covers the bank's standalone license, branch network, and regional franchise.

## 3. Why a standalone page matters

Saitama Resona Bank is not merely a branch network but an operating company holding an independent banking license for the Saitama market, and is a separate legal entity from [[megabanks/resona-bank|りそな銀行]]. To analyze community-based finance, the branch network within Saitama Prefecture, and the regional-bank portfolio within Resona HD, it is kept separate from the holding-company page.
