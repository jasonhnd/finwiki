##TL;DR

KDDI 100% subsidiary, holding company of KDDI Financial Economic Zone. **2019-04-01 Established**. Under its umbrella are **au Jibun Bank / au PAY Card / au PAY / au PAY General Insurance / au Asset Management** etc. 2025-01-31 Acquired all shares of au Jibun Bank from MUFG (as consideration, sold former au Kabucom Securities → Mitsubishi UFJ e-Smart Securities to MUFG) → **Completed making bank 100% a subsidiary**. **FY2026/3  Operating profit 432 hundred million yen** (Previous period 406 hundred million yen, +6.4% / CAGR FY2023+ **30.4%**), **Total assets approximately 9.3 trillion yen**. **2026-05-12 KDDI officially discloses in a timely manner the start of preparations for listing auFH on the Tokyo Stock Exchange** — First IPO candidate for telecommunications FG (timing undecided, KDDI plans to continue as major shareholder even after listing). au Ponta + 2024-07 Lawson 50/50 JV is building a "communications x convenience store x finance" ecosystem. A corner of the communication FG 4  pole ([[megabanks/ndfg]] / au-FH / [[megabanks/paypay-fg]] / [[payment-firms/rakuten-fg]]). ^[extracted]

### History Topic

> **Jibun Bank Replacement (2025-01-31)**: 2008-06 KDDI 50% + Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi UFJ 50% Established as a joint venture, 17  long-standing joint venture relationship has been dissolved. On the same day, MUFG sold 50% to KDDI and completed making KDDI 100% a subsidiary. MUFG acquired the former au Kabucom Securities (under au-FH) for consideration and changed it to Mitsubishi UFJ e-Smart Securities. In a cross-transaction due to the restructuring of both companies' financial strategies, KDDI chose to ``put banks at the core and part with securities.'' ^[extracted]

> **2026-05-12 IPO Preparation Announcement**: KDDI announces official timely disclosure ``About the start of preparations for listing au Financial Holdings, Inc.'s stock on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.'' Bloomberg / Nikkei / Mobile Watch also reported on the same day. First IPO candidate as a telecommunications FG. A strategy to independently disclose to the market a financial platform with total assets 9.3 trillion yen, FY26/3  operating income 432 hundred million yen (CAGR +30.4%). ^[extracted]
