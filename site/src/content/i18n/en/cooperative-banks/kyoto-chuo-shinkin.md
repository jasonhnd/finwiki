## TL;DR

Kyoto Chuo Shinkin Bank ("Chushin") is a major shinkin bank headquartered in Shimogyo-ku, Kyoto City, and is among the top class nationwide in the industry by deposit scale. Its operating base covers Kyoto City, Kyoto Prefecture, and parts of Shiga and Osaka Prefectures, and it mainly serves SMEs, traditional industries, and tourism-related businesses in Kyoto.

## 2. Operating model

Kyoto Chuo Shinkin Bank is a cooperative financial institution whose main customers are Kyoto SMEs, housing-loan borrowers, counterparties in traditional industries such as Nishijin weaving and Kyo-yaki ceramics, and tourism-related SMEs. It is consistently in the upper tier of the shinkin industry by deposit scale and has a strong presence in Kyoto City alongside [[regional-banks/kyoto-bank|京都銀行]]. It also offers regionally rooted services distinctive to Chushin, including local business succession, M&A consulting, and regional PE collaboration.
