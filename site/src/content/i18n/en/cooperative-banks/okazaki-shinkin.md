## TL;DR

Okazaki Shinkin Bank, also known as Okashin, is a major regional shinkin bank headquartered in Okazaki, Aichi Prefecture. Its core operating area is western Mikawa, including Okazaki, Kariya, Toyota, and Anjo. It is strong in SME finance for subcontractors and suppliers in the western Mikawa automotive manufacturing supply chain, including companies linked to Toyota Motor and Denso.

## 2. Operating model

Okazaki Shinkin Bank's core businesses are working-capital loans and equipment finance for automotive supply-chain SMEs in western Mikawa, housing loans, and retail deposits. Its base covers Toyota City, the home of Toyota Motor, and the supplier-heavy cities of Kariya, Anjo, and Okazaki. It competes directly with regional banks such as [[regional-banks/aichi-bank|あいち銀行]] and with Nagoya Bank / [[regional-banks/kyoto-bank|京都銀行]]-linked Nagoya branches in the automotive SME finance market. By deposit scale it ranks among the larger shinkin banks nationwide.
