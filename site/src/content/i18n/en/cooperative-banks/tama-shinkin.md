## TL;DR

Tama Shinkin Bank ("Tamashin") is a major regional shinkin bank headquartered in Tachikawa, Tokyo, with the Tama area of Tokyo (Tachikawa, Musashino, Hachioji, Mitaka, Chofu, etc.) as its main operating base. It is a cooperative financial institution centered on SME finance, housing loans, and retail deposits in the Tama area, and is also known for venture support, industry-academia collaboration, and regional CRE finance.

## 2. Operating model

Tama Shinkin Bank's core businesses are SME lending, housing loans, and retail deposits in the Tama area, while it also has strengths in venture support (such as the Tama Shinkin Bank Business Fair), industry-academia collaboration with local universities (Chuo University, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology, etc.), and regional CRE / real-estate finance. It is the Tama-district anchor shinkin that geographically divides roles with [[cooperative-banks/jonan-shinkin|城南信用金庫]] and [[cooperative-banks/asahi-shinkin|朝日信用金庫]], which are centered on Tokyo's 23  wards.
