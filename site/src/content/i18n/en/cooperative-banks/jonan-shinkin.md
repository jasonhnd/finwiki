## TL;DR

Jonan Shinkin Bank is a large urban shinkin bank headquartered in Shinagawa-ku, Tokyo, with its main operating base in southwestern Tokyo and eastern Kanagawa. It is a cooperative financial institution with a top-class national deposit scale among shinkin banks and is also known for its distinctive social messaging, including its declaration against nuclear power.

## 2. Operating model

Jonan Shinkin Bank is based in southwestern Tokyo (Shinagawa, Ota, Meguro, Setagaya, and nearby areas) and parts of Kawasaki and Yokohama. Its core businesses are SME lending, housing loans, individual deposits, inheritance / business succession consulting, and related services. It is consistently among the upper tier of the shinkin industry by deposit scale, and it is known for marketing that emphasizes the independence of the cooperative model while competing in urban markets with megabanks and regional banks. Its top-management-led social messaging strategy, including the declaration against nuclear power from 2011 年 onward, sets it apart from many other shinkin banks.
