# 愛知県医療信用組合 (Aichi Medical Credit Cooperative)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[cooperative-banks/INDEX|cooperative-banks INDEX]] as the **業域 (medical-occupation) shinkumi anchor** for the Chukyo region, peer to occupation-based [[cooperative-banks/keishicho-shinkumi|警視庁職員信用組合]] (police) and [[cooperative-banks/tokyo-to-shokuin-shinkumi|東京都職員信用組合]] (civil servant). Medical shinkumi form the **largest single-industry shinkumi category** in Japan's 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]].

## TL;DR

The Aichi Medical Credit Cooperative is a **occupation-based credit cooperative whose members are dentists** (its official site explicitly states "a cooperative of dentists, by dentists, for dentists"), headquartered at the Aichi Dental Association Hall (6 F), Marunouchi, Naka-ku, Nagoya, Aichi. FSA registry No. 79  (under the jurisdiction of the Tokai Local Finance Bureau), corporate number 6180005003438. Established in 1962 年 for members of the Aichi Dental Association, it expanded its area in 2015 年 to dental-association members in Gifu and Mie Prefectures. The [[cooperative-banks/aichi-ishi-shinkumi|愛知県医師信用組合]] (registry No. 78, , targeting physicians) coexists within the same Aichi Prefecture, and the two are demarcated by **dentists / physicians**. [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] members, [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] covered. **Housing loans, business-startup funds, and medical-equipment loans**, etc., for members (dentists) form the core of its business. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

As an occupation-based shinkumi, membership eligibility is **limited to the dentist occupation**:

1. **Basic requirement**: dental-association members (dentists) in Aichi, Gifu, and Mie Prefectures
2. **Medical institutions**: dental clinics, etc., established or staffed by the above dentists
3. **Retirees**: OB / OG who have retired from the above (continued membership within a certain period is common)
4. **Spouses / relatives**: members' spouses and same-household family (per the articles of association)
5. **Non-member use**: strictly restricted

### Positioning within Zenshinkumiren

- A Tokai-block member of **[[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全信組連]]**
- Among the Tokai 15  shinkumi, one wing of the Aichi 8  shinkumi
- One of the representative institutions of the **medical-occupation shinkumi group** (21 行 nationwide)
- System ATM linkage and surplus-fund management are routed through Zenshinkumiren

## 6. Supervision / regulation

- **Jurisdiction**: FSA / Tokai Local Finance Bureau
- **Legal basis**: Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act + Act on Financial Businesses by Cooperatives (Kyokin Act)
- **Deposit insurance**: [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] general-financial-institution category
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - **DX response** (member-facing smartphone apps, electronic contracts, web-completed loans)
  - Cost of responding to **AML/CFT strengthening**
  - Structural changes in loan demand accompanying the **surge in medical-corporation M&A**
  - The trend of **declining independent startups by physicians** (an increase in salaried physicians)
