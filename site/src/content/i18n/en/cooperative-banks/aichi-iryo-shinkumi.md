# Aichi Medical Credit Cooperative

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]] as the **occupation-based shinkumi anchor for the medical field** in the Chukyo region, a peer to occupation-based [[cooperative-banks/keishicho-shinkumi|警視庁職員信用組合]] (police) and [[cooperative-banks/tokyo-to-shokuin-shinkumi|東京都職員信用組合]] (civil servants). Medical shinkumi form the **largest single-industry shinkumi category** within Japan's 143 cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]].

## TL;DR

Aichi Medical Credit Cooperative is an **occupation-based credit cooperative for dentists** headquartered in Marunouchi, Naka-ku, Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture (on the 6  floor of the Aichi Dental Association Building). Its official site explicitly describes it as a "cooperative of dentists, by dentists, for dentists." FSA registry No. 79  (under the Tokai Local Finance Bureau), corporate number 6180005003438. It was established in 1962 年 for members of the Aichi Dental Association and expanded its district in 2015 年 to members of the dental associations in Gifu and Mie. Within Aichi it coexists with [[cooperative-banks/aichi-ishi-shinkumi|愛知県医師信用組合]] (registry No. 78, , for physicians), and the two divide the market between **dentists / physicians**. It has [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] members and is covered by [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. The core business is member-oriented lending such as **housing loans, practice-opening funds, and medical-equipment loans** for cooperative members (dentists). ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

Because it is an occupation-based shinkumi, membership is limited to the **dentistry occupational field**:

1. **Basic requirement**: members of the dental associations in Aichi, Gifu, or Mie (dentists)
2. **Medical institutions**: dental clinics and related institutions established or staffed by the above dentists
3. **Retirees**: OB / OG retirees from the above group (continued membership within a certain period is generally allowed)
4. **Spouses / relatives**: spouses and household family members of cooperative members (subject to the articles)
5. **Use by non-members**: strictly limited

## 6. Supervision and regulation

- **Primary supervisor**: FSA and Tokai Local Finance Bureau
- **Legal basis**: Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act + Act on Financial Business by Cooperatives
- **Deposit insurance**: [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] general-financial-institution framework
- **Current policy issues**:
  - **DX response** (member smartphone apps, electronic contracts, web-complete loans)
  - Cost of stronger **AML/CFT** compliance
  - Changing loan-demand structure due to the **surge in medical-corporation M&A**
  - Trend of **fewer doctors opening independent practices** (more employed physicians)
