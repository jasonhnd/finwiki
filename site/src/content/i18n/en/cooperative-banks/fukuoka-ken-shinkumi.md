## TL;DR

Fukuoka Prefectural Credit Union is a prefecture-based regional credit union with its main office located in Akasaka, Chuo-ku, Fukuoka City, Fukuoka Prefecture. FSA registry No.128 (Fukuoka Finance Branch Administration), Corporate Number4290005002505. Fukuoka prefecture3 As the core of the credit unions (Fukuoka Prefecture Doctors Credit Union, Fukuoka Prefecture Credit Union, Fukuoka Prefectural Office Credit Union), we are responsible for SME financing, housing loans, and regional finance within the prefecture.[[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]]member,[[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]subject. As it is a prefecture-based regional credit union, the membership qualification is a two-tier structure: **district (prefecturewide) + business area**:

## 6.Supervision/Regulation

- **Supervised by**: Financial Services Agency, Fukuoka Finance Branch
- **Legal basis**: Small and Medium Enterprise Cooperatives Act + Act on Financial Business by Cooperatives (Cooperative Act)
- **Deposit Insurance**:[[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]General financial institution limit
- **Current policy issues**:
  - **DX compatible** (smartphone app/electronic contract for union members)
  - **AML/CFT enhancement** Support costs
  - Conflicting trends with **continuation of population concentration in central Fukuoka** vs **depopulation in rural areas of the prefecture**
  - Positioning of **Kyushu Financial Group** (led by FFG and NNFH) in the regional financial restructuring framework
