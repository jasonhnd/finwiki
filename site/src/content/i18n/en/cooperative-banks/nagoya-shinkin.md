## TL;DR

Nagoya Shinkin Bank (Meishin) is a major Chukyo shinkin bank headquartered in Naka Ward, Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture, with Nagoya City and the Owari area as its main operating base. It is a cooperative financial institution whose core businesses are loans to SMEs, merchants, service companies, and sole proprietors in Nagoya, together with housing loans and retail deposits. Aichi's shinkin industry accounts for a large share of the 34 行 under the Tokai Local Finance Bureau, and Nagoya Shinkin Bank is a core player in urban commercial finance in Nagoya. It has a geographic and industry division of labor with Okazaki Shinkin Bank, the Nishimikawa automotive supply-chain anchor.

## 2. Operating Model

Nagoya Shinkin Bank's core business is SME lending, working capital, equipment finance, housing loans, and retail deposits for SMEs in Nagoya City, including wholesalers, retailers, service companies, construction firms, and sole proprietors. Nagoya is the core city of the Chukyo economic sphere, with population 230 万, and a concentration of commerce, services, and logistics; SME finance for these sectors is the bank's core.

The structure of Aichi's financial market can be summarized as follows:
- **First regional banks (mainstream regional banks)**: [[regional-banks/aichi-bank|Aichi Bank]] (former Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank business integration) and [[regional-banks/nagoya-bank|Nagoya Bank]]
- **Megabanks and other-prefecture regional banks in the Nagoya market**: [[mufg|MUFG]] Aichi base, plus [[smfg|SMBC]] and [[mizuho-fg|Mizuho FG]] Nagoya offices
- **Shinkin layer**: Nagoya Shinkin Bank (urban Nagoya) + [[cooperative-banks/okazaki-shinkin|Okazaki Shinkin Bank]] (Nishimikawa automotive supply chain) + [[cooperative-banks/hamamatsu-iwata-shinkin|Hamamatsu Iwata Shinkin Bank]] (western Tokai edge, western Shizuoka) + other shinkin banks in Aichi Prefecture

## 5. Related / Regional Issues

### Links with the Chukyo Economy

Nagoya is a world-class manufacturing cluster for automobiles, machinery, aerospace, and related industries, including Toyota's home base. Loans to second- and third-tier suppliers of these large companies and to related service-sector SMEs are an important portfolio for the shinkin industry overall. Nagoya Shinkin Bank covers the commerce and service side, while Okazaki Shinkin Bank covers the automotive supply-chain side.

### Industry Reorganization

The shinkin industry in Aichi Prefecture is also undergoing mergers and integration, and Nagoya Shinkin Bank is viewed in that context. On the regional-bank side, [[regional-banks/aichi-bank|Aichi Bank]] (former Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank business integration in 2025) is a symbolic move.

### Cashless Payments and Regional-Economy Digitization

Nagoya City is one of the ordinance-designated cities where cashless adoption has progressed, and for a shinkin bank, use of shared infrastructure through [[cooperative-banks/shinkin-network-services|Shinkin Network Services]] is important.
