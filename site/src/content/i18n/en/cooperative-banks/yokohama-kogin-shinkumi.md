# 横浜幸銀信用組合 (Yokohama Kogin Credit Cooperative)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[cooperative-banks/INDEX|cooperative-banks INDEX]] as a **Tokyo-metropolitan-area (Kanagawa) ethnic-business shinkumi anchor** within the 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]]. Read it against [[cooperative-banks/osaka-shogin-shinkumi|大阪商銀信用組合]] for the Kansai counterpart in the Korean-business shinkumi network and [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全信組連]] for the cooperative-system parent.

## TL;DR

Yokohama Kogin Credit Cooperative is an **ethnic credit cooperative whose base is business operators of the Zainichi Korean community**, headquartered in Onoe-cho, Naka-ku, Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture. FSA registry No. 58  (under the jurisdiction of the Kanto Local Finance Bureau), corporate number 3020005003571. It has a member base of **Zainichi merchant-and-industrialist SMEs** in Kanagawa Prefecture + the **Koreatowns (Tsurumi / Kawasaki / the area around Yokohama Chinatown)**, and is one wing of the **Kogin-affiliated / Shogin-affiliated** ethnic-shinkumi network in the Kanto area. A member of [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]], subject to [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. Operated under a three-layer membership eligibility of **region + trade area + ethnic community**, with cross-border remittance, community finance, and SME lending at the core of its business. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

As an ethnic shinkumi, membership eligibility has a three-layer structure of **district + trade area + ethnic community** (compliant with the Act on Cooperatives of Small and Medium Enterprises, etc.):

1. **District requirement**: persons who reside, work, or have a place of business in the articles-of-association-designated district centered on Kanagawa Prefecture
2. **Trade-area requirement**: merchants and industrialists (centered on Zainichi Korean business operators; strict on use by non-members)
3. **Ethnic community**: the de facto member base is Zainichi Koreans + their spouses, relatives, and related business operators

## 6. Supervision / regulation

- **Competent authority**: Financial Services Agency / Kanto Local Finance Bureau
- **Legal basis**: the Act on Cooperatives of Small and Medium Enterprises, etc. + the Act on Financial Businesses by Cooperatives (Kyokin-ho)
- **Deposit insurance**: [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] general-financial-institution category
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - **AML/CFT strengthening** (cross-border-remittance monitoring) — a regulatory-compliance cost inherent to ethnic shinkumi
  - **Generational change of members** (the risk of the 2 th- and 3 th-generation-onward Zainichi drifting away from the shinkumi)
  - **Competition with Korean banks** (Shinhan Bank Tokyo Branch, etc.)
