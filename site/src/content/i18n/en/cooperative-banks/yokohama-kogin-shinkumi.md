# Yokohama Kogin Credit Cooperative

## Wiki Route

This entry sits under [[cooperative-banks/INDEX|cooperative-banks INDEX]] as a **metropolitan-area (Kanagawa) ethnic-business shinkumi anchor** within the 143 cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]]. Read it against [[cooperative-banks/osaka-shogin-shinkumi|大阪商銀信用組合]] for the Kansai counterpart in the Korean-business shinkumi network and [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全信組連]] for the cooperative-system parent.

## TL;DR

Yokohama Kogin Credit Cooperative is an **ethnic credit cooperative rooted in businesses run by Korean residents in Japan** and headquartered in Onoecho, Naka-ku, Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture. It has FSA registry No. 58  (under the Kanto Local Finance Bureau) and corporate number 3020005003571. Its member base consists of **Korean-resident business SMEs** in Kanagawa plus communities around **Koreatown areas (Tsurumi / Kawasaki / around Yokohama Chinatown)**, and it forms one wing of the Kogin / Shogin ethnic shinkumi network in the Kanto area. It is a member of [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] and covered by [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. Operated with a three-layer member-eligibility structure of **region + business field + ethnic community**, its core businesses are cross-border remittances, community finance, and SME lending. ^[extracted]

### Member Eligibility ^[extracted]

As an ethnic shinkumi, its member eligibility consists of three layers: **district + business field + ethnic community** (under the Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act):

1. **District requirement**: Persons residing, working, or having a business office in the charter-designated district centered on Kanagawa Prefecture
2. **Business-field requirement**: Commercial and industrial businesses (centered on Korean-resident businesses; strict limits on non-member use)
3. **Ethnic community**: The de facto member base is Korean residents in Japan + their spouses, relatives, and related businesses

## 6. Supervision / Regulation

- **Primary supervisors**: Financial Services Agency / Kanto Local Finance Bureau
- **Legal basis**: Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act + Act on Financial Businesses by Cooperative
- **Deposit insurance**: [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] ordinary financial-institution category
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - **AML/CFT strengthening** (cross-border remittance monitoring) — regulatory-compliance cost specific to ethnic shinkumi
  - **Generational change in members** (risk of drift away from shinkumi among 2 -generation, 3 -generation and later Korean residents in Japan)
  - **Competition with Korean banks** (such as Shinhan Bank Tokyo Branch)
