# Metropolitan Police Employees Credit Cooperative

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[cooperative-banks/INDEX|cooperative-banks INDEX]] as the **Tokyo metropolitan occupation-based shinkumi anchor** for the police sector, complementing [[cooperative-banks/tokyo-shobo-shinkumi|東京消防信用組合]] (fire department) and [[cooperative-banks/tokyo-to-shokuin-shinkumi|東京都職員信用組合]] (metropolitan civil servants) within the 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]].

## TL;DR

Metropolitan Police Employees Credit Cooperative is an **occupation-based credit cooperative for Metropolitan Police Department personnel**, including active police officers, staff, and retirees, with its head office inside the MPD headquarters in Kasumigaseki, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo. Its FSA registry No. is 40  under the Kanto Local Finance Bureau, and its corporate number is 5010005002102. It is a typical occupation-based shinkumi that limits membership to MPD employees plus their spouses and relatives, and its main businesses are **living-expense loans, housing loans, ceremonial / family-event finance, and retirement-fund management** for members. It is a member of [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] and is covered by [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. As mutual-aid finance for the closed occupational community of the police organization, it is a special form that grew alongside the postwar development of local civil-service and police organizations. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

Because this is an occupation-based shinkumi, membership eligibility is strictly limited by **occupation**:

1. **Basic eligibility**: personnel affiliated with the Metropolitan Police Department, including active police officers, administrative staff, technical staff, and similar employees.
2. **Retirees**: former MPD personnel / retirees, commonly with continued membership within defined conditions.
3. **Spouses and relatives**: spouses and household family members of cooperative members, subject to the articles of incorporation.
4. **Non-member use**: strictly restricted under the credit-cooperative framework.

## 6. Supervision and regulation

- **Supervisory authority**: FSA / Kanto Local Finance Bureau.
- **Legal basis**: Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act + Act on Financial Businesses by Cooperative.
- **Deposit insurance**: [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]] general financial-institution framework.
- **Recent policy topics**:
  - **Digitalization** for member smartphone apps and electronic contracts.
  - **AML/CFT strengthening**, with occupation-based shinkumi generally treated as relatively lower risk.
  - **Function coordination with mutual-aid associations**, including pensions, lending, and welfare services.
