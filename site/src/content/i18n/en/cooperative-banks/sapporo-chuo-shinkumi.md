# Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative (札幌中央信用組合)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[cooperative-banks/INDEX|cooperative-banks INDEX]] as a **Hokkaido 地域型 (regional) shinkumi anchor**, peer to [[cooperative-banks/iwaki-shinkumi|いわき信用組合]] (Tohoku) within the 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]]. Contrast with [[cooperative-banks/sapporo-shinkin|札幌信用金庫]] for the shinkin-vs-shinkumi distinction in the Sapporo market.

## TL;DR

Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative is a **regional-type credit cooperative based on a membership of SMEs and residents of the Sapporo metropolitan area**, with its head office at Minami-2-jo Nishi, Chuo-ku, Sapporo, Hokkaido. FSA registry No. 3 (under the Hokkaido Local Finance Bureau), corporate number 5430005003132. Among Hokkaido's 7 credit cooperatives, it is the representative institution headquartered in central Sapporo, a member of [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]] and covered by [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. With **dual membership eligibility (geographic + occupational)**, its business core is SME lending, mortgages, and shopping-district finance within Sapporo. It is a niche financial institution coexisting alongside megabanks, [[regional-banks/hokuyo-bank|北洋銀行]], and [[regional-banks/hokkaido-bank|北海道銀行]] in Sapporo, the center of Hokkaido's economy. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

As a regional-type shinkumi, membership eligibility has a two-layer structure (geographic + occupational) under the Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act:

1. **Geographic requirement**: individuals and corporations who reside, work, or have a place of business in the district defined by Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative's articles of association (mainly Sapporo City)
2. **Size requirement**: SME operators — 300 or fewer employees or capital of 3 億円or less (varies by industry)
