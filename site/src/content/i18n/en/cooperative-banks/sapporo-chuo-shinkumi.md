# Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]] as a **Hokkaido regional shinkumi anchor**, peer to [[cooperative-banks/iwaki-shinkumi|いわき信用組合]] (Tohoku) within the 143-cooperative population catalogued in [[banking/credit-cooperative-registry-japan|Japan credit cooperative registry]]. Contrast with [[cooperative-banks/sapporo-shinkin|札幌信用金庫]] for the shinkin-vs-shinkumi distinction in the Sapporo market.

## TL;DR

Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative is a **regional credit cooperative whose member base is SMEs and residents in the Sapporo metropolitan area**, headquartered at Minami 2-jo Nishi, Chuo-ku, Sapporo, Hokkaido. FSA registry No. 3  (supervised by the Hokkaido Local Finance Bureau), corporate number 5430005003132. Among Hokkaido's 7  credit cooperatives, it is a representative institution based in central Sapporo, a member of [[cooperative-banks/zenshin-kumi|全国信用協同組合連合会 (全信組連)]], and covered by [[financial-regulators/dic|預金保険機構]]. Its core business is built around dual **regional + business-field** membership eligibility, SME lending in Sapporo, housing loans, and shopping-street finance. It is a niche financial institution that coexists with megabanks, [[regional-banks/hokuyo-bank|北洋銀行]], and [[regional-banks/hokkaido-bank|北海道銀行]] in Sapporo, the center of the Hokkaido economy. ^[extracted]

### Membership eligibility ^[extracted]

Because it is a regional shinkumi, membership eligibility has a two-layer **district + business-field** structure (under the Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Cooperatives Act):

1. **District requirement**: individuals and corporations that live, work, or have a place of business in the district stipulated by Sapporo Chuo Credit Cooperative's articles of incorporation (mainly Sapporo City)
2. **Scale requirement**: SMEs — 300  employees or fewer, or capital of 3 億円 or less (varies by industry)
