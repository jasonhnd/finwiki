## TL;DR

Hamamatsu Iwata Shinkin Bank (Hamashin) is a major local credit union whose main branch is located in Chuo Ward, Hamamatsu City, Shizuoka Prefecture, and whose main business base is the western part of Shizuoka Prefecture (Hamamatsu, Iwata, Kosai, Fukuroi, etc.). A cooperative financial institution with strengths in lending to small and medium-sized enterprises in the supply chain of transportation equipment, musical instruments, and optical technology manufacturing in the Hamamatsu area, including Suzuki, Yamaha, Yamaha Motor, and Roland.

## 2.Operating model

Hamamatsu Iwata Shinkin Bank's core business is SME financing, working capital, and equipment financing for small and medium-sized enterprises in the manufacturing supply chain, mainly in the Hamamatsu area, including transportation equipment (Suzuki and Yamaha Motor), musical instruments (Yamaha and Kawai Musical Instruments), and optical technology (Hamamatsu Photonics). Housing loans, personal deposits, and local business succession support are also pillars. The current base was formed through the merger of the former Hamamatsu Shinkin Bank and the former Iwata Shinkin Bank, and the Chubu shinkin landscape[[cooperative-banks/okazaki-shinkin|岡崎信用金庫]]Shinkin is a representative of the manufacturing industry cluster area along with (Nishi Mikawa Automotive Supply Chain).
