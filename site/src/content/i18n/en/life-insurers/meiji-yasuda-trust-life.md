# Meiji Yasuda Trust Life Insurance (明治安田トラスト生命保険)

## TL;DR

Meiji Yasuda Trust Life Insurance Co., Ltd. is a life-insurance operating company of the [[life-insurers/meiji-yasuda|明治安田生命]] group that holds a life-insurance company license under the Insurance Business Act and is listed in the FSA's life-insurance company license list. Its predecessors were Allianz Life → Aeon Allianz Life, and **in 2025 年 7 月 1 日 Meiji Yasuda Life acquired about 85% of its shares and made it a subsidiary, and in 2025 年 10 月 1 日 it changed its name to "Meiji Yasuda Trust Life Insurance."** Because the former Aeon Allianz Life had a history of specializing in group credit life insurance (団信), and it carries a name-change / group-transfer boundary, it is placed as a standalone page.

## 2. Operating model

Meiji Yasuda Trust Life is a subsidiary life insurer of the Meiji Yasuda group that runs the standard life-insurance model — premium underwriting, policy-reserve accumulation, long-term ALM, and claims payment. In its former Aeon Allianz Life era it underwrote specialized lines such as **group credit life insurance (団信)**, and after joining the Meiji Yasuda group it continues the in-force management and servicing of those policies. Its business form and channel are separate from its parent [[life-insurers/meiji-yasuda|明治安田生命]], a major mutual company centered on a face-to-face sales-representative channel. For the structural difference between mutual-company and stock-company types, see [[insurance/mutual-vs-stock-life-insurer|mutual vs stock life insurer]].
