# Fukoku Shinrai Life Insurance

## TL;DR

Fukoku Shinrai Life Insurance Co., Ltd. is the bancassurance life-insurance operating company of the Fukoku Life group, holding a life-insurance company license under the Insurance Business Act and listed in the FSA's life-insurance-company license list. It was established in 1996 年 as "Kyoei Fire Shinrai Life Insurance," a subsidiary of [[non-life-insurers/kyoritsu-fire-marine|共栄火災海上保険]]; in 2008 年, [[life-insurers/fukoku-life|富国生命]] acquired about 80% of its shares, made it a subsidiary, and changed its trade name to "Fukoku Shinrai Life." Because its primary channel is **over-the-counter sales at financial institutions (bancassurance)**, centered on Mizuho Bank and shinkin banks, its business model is differentiated from its parent Fukoku Life, which is centered on face-to-face sales staff. Its head office is in Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo.

## 2. Operating model

Fukoku Shinrai Life is a group-affiliated bancassurance life insurer that optimizes the standard life-insurance model — premium underwriting, policy-reserve accumulation, long-term ALM, and claims payment — for the **financial-institution agency channel**. Through the counters of Mizuho Bank and [[banking/shinkin-bank-registry-japan|信用金庫]] nationwide, it sells protection and savings-type products. In this way, within the Fukoku Life group it serves as the **entry point for regional finance / bank counter sales** that complements the face-to-face sales-staff channel. [[life-insurers/ms-primary-life|MS Primary 生命]] and [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-frontier-life|第一フロンティア生命]], also specialized in FI / bank counter sales, are direct comparison targets. For the revenue structure of bancassurance, see [[insurance/bancassurance-economics-japan|bancassurance economics in Japan]].
