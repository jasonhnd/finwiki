# ORIX Life Insurance

## TL;DR

ORIX Life Insurance is a mid-sized life insurer and a non-listed subsidiary of ORIX [[orix-corp]] 100%. It was established in 1991-04  as ORIX Omaha Life, a joint venture with the US mutual insurer Mutual of Omaha, and became an ORIX 100% subsidiary after the US partner withdrew in 2002 . Its distinguishing feature is a 3 -channel model combining agencies, bancassurance, and online direct sales. Medical-insurance products in the **CURE** series are the core domestic online-sales products, and the company is a central retail-finance business inside the ORIX group across medical, death, and cancer insurance.

### Position inside the ORIX group

ORIX is a diversified financial-services group spanning leasing, real estate, private equity, banking, insurance, energy, and other businesses. ORIX Life is the core company in the group's **retail life-insurance segment** and sits alongside the following retail finance companies.

```
ORIX Corporation (8591)
  ├── ORIX Bank (retail deposits and housing loans)
  ├── ORIX Life Insurance (life insurance; this page)

  └── ORIX Leasing / real estate / PE / automobile businesses, etc.
```

In parent-company segment disclosure, ORIX Life is included in the insurance segment and is positioned as a stable contributor to group earnings.

## 3. Main products

The lineup is centered on third-sector products such as medical and cancer insurance, plus death protection. Its design emphasizes simple, low-cost products and online completion.

| Category | Main products | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Medical insurance | **CURE** series | Representative online medical-insurance brand centered on hospitalization and surgery coverage |
| Death insurance | Term life and income-protection products | Comparatively simple designs, including online-completion and simplified-underwriting products |
| Cancer insurance | Cancer diagnosis and treatment-benefit products | Third-sector lineup |
| Education / whole-life, etc. | Whole-life insurance and related products | Supplementary lineup |

Sales channels:

- **Online direct**: official site and comparison sites.
- **Agencies**: insurance shops and multi-carrier agencies.
- **Bancassurance**: ORIX Bank and other bank channels.

### Parent-company synergies

- Core retail financial product inside the ORIX group.
- Bancassurance cooperation with ORIX Bank, including cross-sell around housing loans, group credit life, and medical insurance.
- Online direct sales using ORIX brand recognition.

### Channel strategy

ORIX Life is not a pure online-only insurer like Lifenet [[lifenet]]. It is a hybrid model combining online direct sales, agencies, and bancassurance, aiming to balance cost efficiency and sales-channel coverage. CURE products have high visibility on comparison sites and a top-tier presence in the online medical-insurance sales market.
