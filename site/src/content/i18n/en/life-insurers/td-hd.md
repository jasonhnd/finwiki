# T&D ホールディングス (T&D Holdings)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[life-insurers/INDEX|life-insurers INDEX]]. Read it against [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life|第一ライフグループ / 旧 第一生命 HD]] for peer / contrast context and [[insurance/INDEX|insurance index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

A mid-sized life insurance holding company that is **among the largest domestically in the independent (non-affiliated) life insurance space**. Its greatest feature is the **3 社 differentiation** of **Taiyo Life (founded 1893  / household market) + Daido Life (founded 1902  / SME-owner market) + T&D Financial Life (bancassurance)**. Established through the 2004-04  management integration, TSE PRIME 8795. It holds the No.5  position after the major 4  life insurers ([[life-insurers/nippon-life]] / [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life]] / Sumitomo Life / Meiji Yasuda Life). In 2020  it took an investment participation in the US Fortitude Group (reinsurance-platform conversion), and the 2024  mid-term plan envisions expanding overseas M&A. The corporate name "T&D" derives from **Taiyo + Daido**. ^[extracted]

### 3 -segment differentiation strategy (greatest feature)

- **Taiyo Life**: household-market-centered, female sales-staff channel, medical / cancer insurance, etc.
- **Daido Life**: specialized in the SME-owner market, business-succession / owner insurance, strong tax-accountant channels such as the tie-up with TKC National Federation ^[extracted]
- **T&D Financial Life**: bancassurance-specialized, savings-type / single-premium product-centered

### Largest-in-the-independent-space position

- The No.5  position after the major 4  life insurers ([[life-insurers/nippon-life]] / [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life]] / Sumitomo Life / Meiji Yasuda Life)
- Operates as a **fully independent** entity that is neither under a megabank nor of a trading-house affiliation

### Overseas strategy

- **Fortitude Group** (2020〜): investment participation in a US reinsurance platform, aiming to advance group-wide reinsurance / ALM sophistication
- **T&D United Capital**: expansion of alternative investments via a US PE investment vehicle
- The 2024  mid-term plan explicitly states **expanding overseas M&A** as a policy ^[extracted]

### DX / sales-staff channel

- Bancassurance is exclusively handled by T&D Financial Life, with functional division within the group
