## TL;DR

Credit Agricole Life Insurance Co., Ltd. is the Credit Agricole group’s Japan life-insurance operating company listed in the Financial Services Agency life-insurer roster. It should be treated as a bancassurance-oriented life insurer, distinct from [[foreign-financial-institutions/credit-agricole-bank-japan|Credit Agricole Bank Tokyo Branch]] and from asset-management contexts.
