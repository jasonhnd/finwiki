## TL;DR

クレディ・アグリコル生命保険株式会社 is the Japan life-insurance operating company of the French universal-banking group **Crédit Agricole**; it holds a life-insurance company license under the Insurance Business Act and appears in the FSA's life-insurance company license list and the [[financial-regulators/seiho-kyokai|生命保険協会]] member list. Rather than a large life insurer with a sales-representative channel, it is a foreign-affiliated life insurer whose primary channel is the counters of partner financial institutions (over-the-counter bank sales / bancassurance), handled separately from the group's banking and asset-management functions. Its head office is in Minato-ku, Tokyo. Crédit Agricole is a pioneer of bancassurance in France and, in Japan too, provides products such as individual annuities and group credit life insurance via partner financial institutions.

## 2. Operating model

As a foreign-affiliated bancassurance life insurer, the company's revenue structure optimises the standard life-insurer model — premium underwriting, accumulation of policy reserves, long-term ALM matching long-dated liabilities, and payment of insurance and annuity benefits — for the **financial-institution agency channel**. By selling savings-oriented and ancillary products such as single-premium individual annuities and group credit life insurance through the counters of partner banks, shinkin banks, and the like, its business model differs greatly from domestic majors such as [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life-insurance|第一生命]] that maintain face-to-face sales representatives. The direct comparators are [[life-insurers/cardif-life|カーディフ生命]], which shares the same foreign-affiliated / over-the-counter-bank-sales model, and [[life-insurers/ms-primary-life|MS Primary 生命]] and [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-frontier-life|第一フロンティア生命]], which specialise in the domestic FI channel. For the asset-liability-management framework see [[insurance/japan-life-insurance-alm-overview|Japan life-insurance ALM overview]]; for the Japan-market positioning of foreign-affiliated life insurers see [[insurance/foreign-life-affiliate-japan-positioning|foreign life-insurance affiliates positioning]].
