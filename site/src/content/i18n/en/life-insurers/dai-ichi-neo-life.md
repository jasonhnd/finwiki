# Dai-ichi Neo Life Insurance (第一ネオ生命保険)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[life-insurers/INDEX|life-insurers INDEX]] (its domain route). It is a specialty operating company under the listed parent [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life|第一ライフグループ]], alongside the core underwriter [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life-insurance|第一生命保険]] and the bancassurance arm [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-frontier-life|第一フロンティア生命]]. ## TL;DR

第一ネオ生命保険株式会社 is a specialty life-insurance operating company of [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life|第一ライフグループ]] that holds a life-insurance company license under the Insurance Business Act and is listed in the FSA's list of licensed life-insurance companies. 2026 年 4 月 1 日, in step with the parent Dai-ichi Life Holdings changing its trade name to "Dai-ichi Life Group Inc. (株式会社第一ライフグループ)" and unifying the group brand to "Daiichi Life," it **changed its name from the former "Neo First Life" to "Dai-ichi Neo Life,"** so this page places the exact-name anchor and the name-change boundary.

## 2. Operating model

Dai-ichi Neo Life is positioned within the group as a **specialty life insurer for agencies / specific channels**, providing protection-type products such as medical and cancer coverage through a distribution network (insurance shops, multi-company agencies, etc.) separate from Dai-ichi Life's own sales-representative channel. In this way, alongside [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-life-insurance|第一生命]] centered on face-to-face sales representatives and [[life-insurers/dai-ichi-frontier-life|第一フロンティア生命]] centered on bancassurance and single-premium savings-type products, it forms a three-tier division of channels and products across the group as a whole. For the life-insurance-common framework of underwriting, policy reserves, and ALM, see [[insurance/japan-life-insurance-alm-overview|Japan life-insurance ALM overview]].
