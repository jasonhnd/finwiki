## 4. Regulation / policy

- **Supervisor**: FSA (money-lending / FX-business related)
- **Regulatory points**:
  - **Transfer pricing** on intra-group financial transactions
  - The boundary between group factoring and the **Installment Sales Act / Money Lending Business Act**
  - **Elimination of intra-group transactions** in consolidated accounting
- **Recent policy points**:
  - 2024 to [[financial-regulators/boj-monetary-policy|日銀政策金利正常化]] → reassessment of intra-group interest charging
  - Strengthening of AML/CFT on overseas remittance / cross-border fund movement
