### Sojitz's business structure and finance function

Sojitz Finance supports supplier finance, overseas-currency hedging, cross-border remittances, and similar functions across these segments. Because Sojitz is a mid-tier trading company, its finance-function scale is smaller than that of the 5  major trading houses, but the function set is similar.

## 4. Regulation and policy

- **Main authority**: FSA (money-lending / FX-business related)
- **Regulatory points**:
  - **Transfer-pricing taxation** for intragroup financial transactions
  - Boundary between group factoring and the **Installment Sales Act / Money Lending Business Act**
  - **Elimination of internal transactions** in consolidated accounting
- **Recent policy topic**:
  - 2024 onward: [[financial-regulators/boj-monetary-policy|BOJ policy-rate normalization]] leading to a reassessment of intragroup interest attribution
