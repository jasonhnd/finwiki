### Mitsui & Co.'s business structure and financial functions

The **resources and energy** segment in particular centers on long-term contracts and large credit projects such as LNG, crude oil, and iron ore, and makes heavy use of intra-group financial functions.

## 4. Regulation and Policy

- **Supervisor**: Financial Services Agency (money lending / foreign-exchange business related)
- **Regulatory issues**:
  - **Transfer pricing taxation** for intra-group financial transactions, especially transaction pricing with overseas subsidiaries.
  - Boundary management between group factoring and the **Installment Sales Act / Money Lending Business Act**.
  - **Elimination of internal transactions** in consolidated accounting.
- **Recent policy issues**:
  - From 2024 onward, [[financial-regulators/boj-monetary-policy|日銀政策金利正常化]] -> reassessment of intra-group interest-rate charging.
  - From 2025 onward, stronger AML/CFT for overseas remittances and cross-border fund movement.
