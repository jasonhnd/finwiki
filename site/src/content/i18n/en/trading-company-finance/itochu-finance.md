## 2. Business segment map

|segment|Content|
|---|---|
|**CMS (Cash Management Service)**|Centralized payment/fund pooling for ITOCHU Group companies|
|**Bulk factoring**|Purchase of accounts receivable from group companies/payment agency|
|**Group Finance**|Short-term / mid-term loans to group companies|
|**Forex/interest rate risk hedging intermediary**|Derivative brokerage for group companies|
|**Overseas remittance service**|Overseas remittance and collection of group companies|
|**Trade finance assistance**|Credit subsidies linked to ITOCHU's transactions in textiles, machinery, food, housing, information, metals, energy, and chemicals|

### Relationship with ITOCHU Corporation's business structure

In particular, the **Food/Household/Information** segment has many transactions with small and medium-sized suppliers, and is a typical example of simultaneously achieving cash flow stabilization on the supplier side and optimization of payment costs on the head office side through CMS/bulk factoring.
