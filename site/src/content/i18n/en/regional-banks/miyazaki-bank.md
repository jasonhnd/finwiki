## TL;DR

The Miyazaki Bank, Ltd. (株式会社宮崎銀行, みやぎん) is a regional bank (first regional bank) headquartered in Miyazaki City, Miyazaki Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list. It is the Miyazaki prefecture first-regional-bank operating-company anchor, and is treated as a South Kyushu standalone regional bank. Its business consists of deposit-taking within the prefecture and lending to SMEs, individuals, and local public bodies.

## 2. Operating model

Miyazaki Bank is a first regional bank that, centered on Miyazaki Prefecture, handles retail deposits, SME finance, agriculture- and tourism-related support, and local relationship banking. In South Kyushu, compare it with [[regional-banks/higo-bank|肥後銀行]] / [[regional-banks/kagoshima-bank|鹿児島銀行]] (under [[regional-banks/kyushu-fg|九州FG]]) and the in-prefecture second-regional bank [[regional-banks/miyazaki-taiyo-bank|宮崎太陽銀行]].

## 3. Regulatory & system positioning

- **Supervision**: Under the Banking Act, supervised by the FSA ([[financial-regulators/fsa|FSA]]) and the Kyushu Local Finance Bureau. Soundness is disciplined by capital-adequacy regulation (domestic standard) and the like.
- **Industry body**: A first regional bank belonging to the Regional Banks Association of Japan (地銀協). It participates in the Zengin System (the nationwide bank funds-transfer network) and, as an industry body, falls under the framework of [[financial-regulators/zenginkyo|全国銀行協会（全銀協）]].
- **Depositor protection**: Deposits are covered by the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC).
- **Consolidation context**: For the general context of regional-bank reorganization / consolidation pressure in South Kyushu, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|regional-bank consolidation pattern]].
