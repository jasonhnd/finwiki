# The Minami-Nippon Bank (南日本銀行)

## TL;DR

The Minami-Nippon Bank, Ltd. (株式会社南日本銀行) is a second regional bank headquartered in Kagoshima City, Kagoshima Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list. It is the second-regional-bank operating-company anchor of Kagoshima Prefecture, treated as a local peer that is a separate legal entity from the first regional bank [[regional-banks/kagoshima-bank|鹿児島銀行]]. The substance of its business is the standard second-regional-bank model of deposit-taking within the prefecture and lending to SMEs and individuals.

## 2. Operating model

The Minami-Nippon Bank is a second regional bank that handles retail deposits, SME finance, consumer banking, and local relationship banking within Kagoshima Prefecture. The first-regional-bank [[regional-banks/kagoshima-bank|鹿児島銀行]] page handles the [[regional-banks/kyushu-fg|九州フィナンシャルグループ]] core-bank boundary, while this page handles the independent second-regional-bank license boundary. In southern Kyushu it is also compared with [[regional-banks/miyazaki-bank|宮崎銀行]].

## 3. Regulatory & system positioning

- **Supervision**: Supervised by the FSA ([[financial-regulators/fsa|FSA]]) and the Kyushu Local Finance Bureau under the Banking Act. Its soundness is disciplined by capital-adequacy-ratio regulation (domestic standard) and the like.
- **Industry body**: A second regional bank belonging to the Second Association of Regional Banks. It participates in the nationwide bank fund-settlement network (Zengin System), and as for an industry body it connects to the framework of [[financial-regulators/zenginkyo|全国銀行協会（全銀協）]].
- **Depositor protection**: Deposits are covered by the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC).
- **Consolidation context**: For the general context of Kyushu second-regional-bank realignment and consolidation pressure, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|regional-bank consolidation pattern]].
