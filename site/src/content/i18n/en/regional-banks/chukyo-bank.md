## TL;DR

Chukyo Bank, Ltd. (株式会社中京銀行) was a second regional bank headquartered in Nagoya City, Aichi Prefecture, that **merged with the former Aichi Bank in 2025-01-01 and was consolidated into [[regional-banks/aichi-bank|あいち銀行]]** (operating boundary migration complete). Established through the 1953 年 consolidation of "第三相互無尽 + 第十七相互無尽," it was a typical "mutual-bank-origin second regional bank" that went through the path of mutual savings bank → second regional bank ordinary-bank conversion. The former securities code **TSE 8530** was delisted at the time of [[regional-banks/aichi-fg|あいち FG]]'s establishment (2022-10), and this page is positioned not as the current legal entity but as a pre-merger historical anchor. ^[extracted]

### 戦前・戦時源流

- **1944**: establishment of 名古屋無尽 (one of the oldest origins of the former Chukyo Bank)
- After the war, it followed the ordinary-bank conversion route of 無尽会社 → 相互銀行 → 第二地方銀行

### 中京銀行 establishment (1953)

- **1953**: establishment of 中京相互銀行 (consolidation of former 第三相互無尽 + 第十七相互無尽)
- Nagoya intra-city / small and micro base, typical of a mutual-bank-origin second regional bank
- Member of the Second Association of Regional Banks ([[financial-regulators/zenshin-kyo]] / 第二地銀協会)

### 普通銀行転換と上場

- **1989**: ordinary-bank conversion → renamed 株式会社中京銀行
- 1990 年 era: established a main-bank position for small and micro enterprises within Nagoya City and Aichi Prefecture
- 2000-2010 年 era: restructuring pressure due to the over-supply of regional banks within Aichi Prefecture (Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank + [[regional-banks/nagoya-bank|名古屋銀行]])

### 経営統合と消滅

- **2022-04-21**: announcement of basic agreement on the management integration of Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank
- **2022-10-03**: establishment of [[regional-banks/aichi-fg|あいち FG]], both banks subsidiarized in 100%
- **2025-01-01**: Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank merger → trade name [[regional-banks/aichi-bank|あいち銀行]], Chukyo Bank dissolved and ceased to exist

### Integration synergies through [[regional-banks/aichi-fg]]

- Branch consolidation of overlaps (adjacent branches within Nagoya City and Aichi Prefecture)
- Core system integration (understood to be consolidated toward the former Aichi Bank system)
- Reorganization of the sales network and improvement of the cost structure
- Consolidation of mid-tier-enterprise main-bank transactions (single-window via [[regional-banks/aichi-bank]])

### 残存課題

- Residual restructuring pressure from the intra-prefecture regional-bank over-supply problem ([[regional-banks/nagoya-bank]] continuing independently)

## 7. 規制・政策

- **License status**: deregistered 2025-01-01 (merger into [[regional-banks/aichi-bank]])
- **業界団体 (historical)**: member of the Second Association of Regional Banks (lost eligibility upon merger; the current [[regional-banks/aichi-bank]] continues membership in the Second Association of Regional Banks)
- **主管 (historical)**: 金融庁 (FSA) / Banking Act Article 4  license
- **直近政策論点 (immediately before merger)**:
  - restructuring pressure from the intra-prefecture regional-bank over-supply problem
  - 2023〜 expectations of margin improvement from BOJ rate hikes were consolidated after the [[regional-banks/aichi-bank]] merger
