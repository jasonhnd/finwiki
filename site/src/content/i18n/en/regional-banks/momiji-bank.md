Momiji Bank, Ltd. is a second regional bank headquartered in Hiroshima Prefecture and listed on the FSA bank license list. Momiji Bank is a second regional bank responsible for retail deposits, SME finance, local relationship banking, and Setouchi-area business support, mainly in Hiroshima Prefecture. Within YMFG's 3-bank model, it keeps the Hiroshima brand / operating-company boundary visible.
