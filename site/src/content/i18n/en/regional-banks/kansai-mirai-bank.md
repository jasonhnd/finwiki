## TL;DR

Kansai Mirai Bank, Limited is a regional bank headquartered in Osaka Prefecture and listed in the FSA bank-license list. It is the Kansai operating bank under [[megabanks/resona-hd]] and should be treated as the post-integration bank-license entity formed from the former Kansai Urban Bank and Kinki Osaka Bank.

## 2. Operating Model

Kansai Mirai Bank is a regional bank centered on Osaka Prefecture, covering retail deposits, SME finance, mortgage / consumer banking, and Kansai-area business support. It works with the retail / trust-banking platform of [[megabanks/resona-hd]] while maintaining the branch network and local-finance functions of a Kansai regional bank.
