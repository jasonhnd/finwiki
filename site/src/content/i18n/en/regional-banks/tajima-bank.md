## TL;DR

Tajima Bank, Ltd. is a regional bank headquartered in Toyooka, Hyogo Prefecture and listed in the FSA bank license list. As a Hyogo / Tajima-area regional-bank operating company, it is given a standalone anchor separate from the major Kansai bank groups.

## 2. Operating Model

Tajima Bank is a regional bank centered on northern Hyogo Prefecture, handling retail deposits, SME finance, local tourism / agriculture finance, and regional relationship banking. Unlike [[regional-banks/kansai-mirai-bank|関西みらい銀行]] or [[regional-banks/minato-bank|みなと銀行]], it is organized as a local-community banking anchor that sits at some distance from the Kansai major-bank market, while reorganization trends can be followed through [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|地域銀行統合パターン]].
