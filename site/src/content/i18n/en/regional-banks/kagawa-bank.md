# The Kagawa Bank

## TL;DR

The Kagawa Bank, Ltd. (株式会社香川銀行) is a second regional bank headquartered in Takamatsu, Kagawa Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list. It is the Kagawa operating bank under [[regional-banks/tomony-hd|トモニホールディングス]], and is treated as a second-regional-bank peer that is a separate legal entity from [[regional-banks/hyakujushi-bank|百十四銀行]], the first regional bank of Kagawa Prefecture. The substance of its business is the standard second-regional-bank model of taking deposits within the prefecture and lending to SMEs and individuals.

## 2. Operating model

The Kagawa Bank is a second regional bank responsible for retail deposits, SME finance, personal loans, local relationship banking, and Tomony group cross-selling, centered on Kagawa Prefecture. The [[regional-banks/tomony-hd|トモニHD]] page covers group-level governance and the two-bank structure with [[regional-banks/tokushima-taisho-bank|徳島大正銀行]], while this page covers the bank-license entity. Across the wider Shikoku region it has a peer boundary with [[regional-banks/iyo-bank|伊予銀行]] and [[regional-banks/awa-bank|阿波銀行]].

## 3. Regulatory & system positioning

- **Supervision**: Under the Banking Act, supervised by the FSA ([[financial-regulators/fsa|FSA]]) and the Shikoku Local Finance Bureau. Its soundness is disciplined by capital adequacy regulation (domestic standard) and the like.
- **Industry body**: A second regional bank belonging to the Second Association of Regional Banks (第二地銀協). It participates in the nationwide bank funds-settlement network (Zengin System), and as an industry body it connects to the framework of [[financial-regulators/zenginkyo|全国銀行協会（全銀協）]].
- **Depositor protection**: Deposits are covered by the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC).
- **Consolidation context**: For the general context of second-regional-bank reorganization and consolidation pressure in Shikoku, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|regional-bank consolidation pattern]].
