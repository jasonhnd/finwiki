## TL;DR

The Kagawa Bank, Ltd. is a second regional bank headquartered in Kagawa Prefecture and listed in the FSA bank license list. It is the Kagawa operating bank under [[tomony-hd]] and should be treated as a second-regional-bank peer that is a separate corporation from [[hyakujushi-bank|百十四銀行]].

## 2. Operating model

Kagawa Bank is a second regional bank centered on Kagawa Prefecture, handling retail deposits, SME finance, local relationship banking, and Tomony group cross-selling. The [[tomony-hd]] page covers group-level governance and the two-bank structure with Tokushima Taisho Bank, while this page covers the bank-license entity.
