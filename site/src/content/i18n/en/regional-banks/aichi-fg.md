# Aichi FG (Aichi FG)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[regional-banks/INDEX|regional-banks INDEX]]. Read it against [[regional-banks/hachijuni-nagano-bank|Hachijuni Nagano Bank]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

##TL;DR

A regional-bank financial group based in Aichi Prefecture (TSE PRIME 7389). It was established through the **2022-10-03 management integration of Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank**, and its bank subsidiary has already been integrated into [[aichi-bank|Aichi Bank]]. It is a regional-bank restructuring model grounded in the Chukyo economic zone (Nagoya / Toyota supply chain). In-prefecture competitor: [[nagoya-bank]] (no holding-company structure, prefectural No. 2); nearby peers: [[juroku-fg]] (Gifu) / [[san-jusan-fg]] (Mie) / [[ogaki-kyoritsu-bank]] (Gifu). ^[extracted]

#### Aichi Bank Genealogy

- **1910** Establishment of the predecessor of Aichi Bank (merger with former Nagoya Bank, etc., origin of Nagoya-based regional banks) ^[extracted]
- **1943** “Aichi Bank” established due to wartime integration ^[extracted]
- As a post-war regional financial institution, we mainly provide loans to small and medium-sized enterprises in Aichi Prefecture.

#### Chukyo Bank Genealogy

- **1953** Establishment of Chukyo Bank (former Daisan Sogo Mujin + 17th Sogo Mujin integration → Sogo Bank → Second regional bank Fugin conversion) ^[extracted]
- Nagoya city, small and medium-sized enterprises, typical second-tier regional bank affiliated with Sogo Bank

#### Management integration process

- **2022-10-03 Establishment of Aichi FG** (Joint stock transfer, both banks 100% became a subsidiary, new FG listed on TSE PRIME) ^[extracted]
- **2025-01-01 Aichi Bank + Chukyo Bank merger**, current operating bank is [[aichi-bank|Aichi Bank]].

### Chukyo Economic Zone Concentration Strategy

- **Aichi prefecture base**: Nagoya economic area = Japan's No. 3  economic area (after Tokyo and Osaka), with dense population concentration and high manufacturing GDP density.
- **Regional bank reorganization model within the prefecture**: Integration of 2 行 within the same prefecture → Reducing duplicate branches, improving cost structure, and strengthening lending capacity through scale effects

### Merger synergy (2022-10  integration → 2025-01  bank merger)

- Integration of duplicate stores and headquarters functions → cost reduction
- System integration → IT investment efficiency
- Optimize personnel allocation → Strengthen sales capabilities

### Chukyo Economic Area Regional Bank Reorganization Model

Aichi FG is an 3  step integration model of **2 行 integration within the same prefecture → holding company → bank merger**.
- Stage 1 (2022-10): Business integration by becoming a holding company (maintaining both banks' brands)
- Stage 2 (2025-01  planned): Integration in name and reality through bank merger

- [[san-jusan-fg]] (2018  Mie Bank + Daisan Bank integration → 2021  Sanjusan Bank merger) = Aichi FG's predecessor model
