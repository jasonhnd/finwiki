## TL;DR

The Shonai Bank, Ltd. (株式会社荘内銀行) is a regional bank based in Yamagata Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list; it is an operating bank under [[regional-banks/fidea-hd|フィデアホールディングス]]. It has a lineage that includes the 67th National Bank and was established in 1941 年 through wartime consolidation. In 2025 年 5 月 it relocated its registered head-office location from Tsuruoka City to Yamagata City. Fidea HD has announced a plan under which, effective 2027 年 1 月 1 日, the Shonai Bank will absorb [[regional-banks/hokuto-bank|北都銀行]] on the Akita side and become "Fidea Bank."

## 2. Operating model

The Shonai Bank is a regional bank responsible for retail, SME, agriculture, tourism, inheritance / wealth support, and public finance in Yamagata Prefecture (centered on the Shonai region but also inland), and is the designated financial institution for Tsuruoka City, Sakata City, and others. Within the prefecture it competes with the first regional bank [[regional-banks/yamagata-bank|山形銀行]], while in the Yamagata–Akita wide-area model of [[regional-banks/fidea-hd|フィデアホールディングス]] it is organized in combination with [[regional-banks/hokuto-bank|北都銀行]] on the Akita side. For the curve of regional-bank consolidation pressure, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|地域銀行統合パターン]].

## 3. Registry / boundary note

Both the FSA bank-license list and the official company profile identify the legal entity, but the head-office-location notation differs by point in time (relocated from Tsuruoka City → Yamagata City in 2025-05 ). This page treats the official profile as the current company-profile source and the FSA workbook as the license-registry source; the 2027-01 Fidea Bank integration is treated as a forecast shape based on the holding company's disclosures.
