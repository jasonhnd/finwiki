## TL;DR

Tsukuba Bank, Ltd. is a regional bank headquartered in Tsuchiura, Ibaraki Prefecture and listed on the FSA bank license list. In Ibaraki Prefecture, [[joyo-bank]] is the larger [[mebuki-fg]] core bank, but Tsukuba Bank is treated here as a separate licensed regional-bank operating company.

## 2. Operating model

Tsukuba Bank is a regional bank centered on Ibaraki Prefecture, providing retail deposits, SME finance, local business support, and regional-consulting services. It shares the Ibaraki market with [[joyo-bank]], but Tsukuba Bank is organized as a standalone bank-license entity.
