## TL;DR

The Tohoku Bank, Ltd. (株式会社東北銀行, commonly "Tougin") is a regional bank (first-tier regional bank) headquartered in Morioka City, Iwate Prefecture, that holds a bank license under the Banking Act and is listed on the FSA's bank license list. According to its official company profile, it was established in 1950 年 10 月as a 1 行of postwar regional banks. Although it is based in Iwate, the same as [[regional-banks/iwate-bank|岩手銀行]], it is an independent licensed regional bank operating company; because the basic agreement on management integration with [[regional-banks/fidea-hd|フィデアホールディングス]] was terminated in 2022 年 2 月, it is not a Fidea-affiliated bank. Its business is the standard regional-bank model of deposit-taking within Iwate Prefecture and lending to SMEs / individuals.

## 2. Operating model

The Tohoku Bank is a regional bank that handles retail, SME, primary-industry finance, municipal finance, and local business succession support within Iwate Prefecture, and it also focuses on the 6 -ary-industrialization / agribusiness support of agriculture, forestry, and fisheries. Within Iwate Prefecture, [[regional-banks/iwate-bank|岩手銀行]] is strong as the prefecture's first-tier regional bank, but The Tohoku Bank handles a separate channel of SME finance and community-based finance, and it coexists with the second-tier regional bank [[regional-banks/kita-nippon-bank|北日本銀行]]. For the Tohoku regional-bank consolidation context, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|地域銀行統合パターン]].

## 3. Boundary note

In 2021 年a management-integration discussion with [[regional-banks/fidea-hd|フィデアホールディングス]] was announced, but in 2022 年 2 月the basic agreement was terminated. Therefore, in this wiki, The Tohoku Bank is classified not as a Fidea planned subsidiary but as an independent regional-bank operating company.
