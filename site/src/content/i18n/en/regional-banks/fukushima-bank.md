##TL;DR

Fukushima Bank, Ltd. is a second-tier regional bank with a head office in Fukushima Prefecture listed on the Financial Services Agency's list of bank licenses. While [[regional-banks/toho-bank]] is the first regional bank anchor of Fukushima Prefecture, Fukushima Bank is treated as a retail / SME / local reconstruction-finance anchor on the second-regional-bank side.

## 2. Operating model

Fukushima Bank is a second regional bank responsible for retail deposits, SME finance, mortgage / personal loans, and local business support within Fukushima Prefecture. In Fukushima Prefecture, the context of reconstruction finance after the earthquake and nuclear accident, business succession, and population decline is heavy, and it is necessary to look at the two-tier structure of regional finance in conjunction with [[regional-banks/toho-bank]].
