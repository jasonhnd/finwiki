## TL;DR

The Bank of Fukuoka, Ltd. (株式会社福岡銀行) is a regional bank (first-tier regional bank) headquartered in Fukuoka City, Fukuoka Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list. It is the core operating bank of [[regional-banks/fukuoka-fg|ふくおかフィナンシャルグループ]] and is treated as one of Kyushu's largest regional-bank operating-company anchors. Its business is the standard regional-bank business model of "deposit-taking across the prefecture and the wider Kyushu region + lending to SMEs, individuals, and local public bodies."

## 2. Operating model

The Bank of Fukuoka is a regional bank that, centered on Fukuoka Prefecture, handles retail deposits, SME finance, urban corporate banking, lending to local public bodies, and Kyushu-wide relationship banking. Because it is anchored in Fukuoka City, the largest metropolitan area in Kyushu, its weighting toward urban-style corporate / institutional business is high even among regional banks. The [[regional-banks/fukuoka-fg|ふくおかフィナンシャルグループ]] page covers group strategy, capital policy, and digital-bank (Minna Bank) strategy, while this page covers the bank-license entity and the operating boundary of branches and regional lending.

## 3. Regulatory & system positioning

- **Supervision**: Under the Banking Act, supervised by the FSA ([[financial-regulators/fsa|FSA]]) and the local Finance Bureau with jurisdiction over its location (the Kyushu Local Finance Bureau). Soundness is disciplined by capital adequacy regulation (domestic standard) and the like.
- **Industry body**: As a first-tier regional bank, it is positioned within the Regional Banks Association of Japan (Chiginkyo) lineage as a first-tier regional bank. On the payments-infrastructure side it participates in the nationwide bank funds settlement network (the Zengin System), and as an industry body it belongs to the framework of [[financial-regulators/zenginkyo|全国銀行協会（全銀協）]].
- **Depositor protection**: Deposits are covered by the Deposit Insurance Corporation (DIC), and insured deposits are protected within the prescribed range.
- **Consolidation context**: For the general context of regional-bank reorganization and consolidation pressure in Kyushu, see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|regional-bank consolidation pattern]].
