## TL;DR

Fukuoka Chuo Bank, Ltd. is the second regional bank with its main branch in Fukuoka Prefecture listed on the Financial Services Agency's list of bank licenses. It is a Fukuoka-area operating bank under [[fukuoka-fg]] and is treated as a licensed-bank anchor separate from [[fukuoka-bank]] and [[chikuho-bank]].
