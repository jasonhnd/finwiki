## TL;DR

The Shimizu Bank, Ltd. (株式会社清水銀行) is a regional bank (first regional bank) headquartered in Shimizu-ku, Shizuoka City, Shizuoka Prefecture, holding a bank license under the Banking Act and listed in the FSA's bank license list. Its predecessor was Sunshu Bank, established in 1928 年, which was renamed to its current trade name in 1948 年 as an independent operating company (not under [[regional-banks/shizuoka-fg|静岡FG]]). Its business is the standard regional-bank model of deposit-taking and lending to SMEs / individuals in central-eastern Shizuoka Prefecture, and since 2017 年 it has operated joint branches for financial-product intermediary with SBI Securities.

## 2. Operating model

Shimizu Bank is a regional bank that, centered on central-eastern Shizuoka Prefecture, handles retail deposits, SME finance, local business support, and regional relationship banking. Within the prefecture it is the next-largest after [[regional-banks/shizuoka-bank|静岡銀行]] and [[regional-banks/suruga-bank|スルガ銀行]], and although they coexist in the same in-prefecture market, the license and governance boundary is a separate legal entity. Because it has a financial-product-intermediary partnership (joint branches) with the SBI Group, read the SBI-linkage context together with [[megabanks/sbi-regional-bank-hd|SBI 地銀ホールディングス]], and for regional-bank consolidation pressure see [[banking/regional-bank-consolidation-pattern|地域銀行統合パターン]].
