## TL;DR

UI Bank is a digital-bank operating company invested in by [[regional-banks/kiraboshi-fg|東京きらぼし FG]] 100%. Opened in 2022 年, it is a smartphone bank launched by a regional financial group, emphasizing hybrid face-to-face / non-face-to-face service, lifestyle-partner finance, and API connectivity.
