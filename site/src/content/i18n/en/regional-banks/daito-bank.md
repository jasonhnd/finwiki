Daito Bank, Ltd. is a No. 2 regional bank with its main branch in Koriyama City, Fukushima Prefecture, and is listed on the Financial Services Agency's list of bank licenses. ## 2. Operating model

Daito Bank is a second regional bank that handles retail, SME lending, local deposits, cards / credit-adjacent group services, and community finance mainly in Nakadori, Fukushima Prefecture. The official company profile shows the head office in Nakamachi, Koriyama City, the establishment of 1942 年, and the history of conversion to a regular bank.
