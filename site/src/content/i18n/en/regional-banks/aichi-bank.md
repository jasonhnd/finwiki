## TL;DR

Aichi Bank, Ltd. is a second regional bank headquartered in Aichi Prefecture and listed on the FSA bank-license list. Under [[regional-banks/aichi-fg]], it is treated as the operating bank after the integration of the former Aichi Bank and the former Chukyo Bank.

## 2. Operating model

Aichi Bank is a second regional bank responsible for retail deposits, SME finance, Chukyo manufacturing supply-chain finance, business succession, and local consulting in Aichi Prefecture. The [[regional-banks/aichi-fg]] page covers holding-company integration and strategy; this page covers the current bank-license entity.
