### Merger history/predecessor^[extracted]

- Former **Shonai Bank**:1878 **The 67th National Bank** Founded (capitalized by the samurai clan of the Shonai clan, Tsuruoka) →1941 **Shonai Bank** Name changed. Expanded from the Shonai region of Yamagata Prefecture, expanded to the entire prefecture after the war
- Former **Hokuto Bank**:1895 Founded as **48th National Bank** → After war and post-war regional bank reorganization, became **Hokuto Bank**. Based in Akita Prefecture, with a tradition of financing rice cultivation and sake brewing.
- **Fidea HD launched** (2009-10-01): Launched through a joint stock transfer as **East Japan's first regional bank integration across prefectural borders** against the backdrop of population decline and decline in regional bank profits in both Yamagata and Akita prefectures.
- **Cancellation of basic agreement with Tohoku Bank** (2022-02):2021 年Business integration talks were announced in2022-02 The basic agreement was canceled. From then on, Tohoku Bank is no longer a Fidea operating bank.
- **Fidea Bank planned merger** (2027-01 Schedule): According to the official profile of Hokuto Bank, Hokuto Bank and Shonai Bank2027-01 There are plans to merge with Fidea Bank and launch it as Fidea Bank.
