The Bank of Saga, Ltd. is a regional bank headquartered in Saga Prefecture and listed in the FSA bank-license registry. It is a Saga prefecture first-regional-bank operating-company anchor and is treated as a standalone regional bank in northern Kyushu.

## 2. Operating model

Saga Bank is a regional bank centered on Saga Prefecture, responsible for retail deposits, SME finance, agriculture / local industry support, and northern Kyushu relationship banking. It is organized as a standalone peer adjacent to major bank groups in Fukuoka and Nagasaki prefectures.
