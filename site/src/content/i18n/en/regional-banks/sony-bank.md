## TL;DR

Sony Bank Incorporated (ソニー銀行) is an internet-only-bank operating company invested 100% by [[megabanks/sony-fg|ソニーフィナンシャルグループ]]; it holds a banking license under the Banking Act and is listed on the FSA's bank license list as "ソニー銀行株式会社". Per its official company outline, it was established 2001 年 4 月 2 日, with its head office in Uchisaiwaicho, Chiyoda, Tokyo. It is not a payment entity but a net bank whose pillars are retail asset management and asset formation for individuals, such as yen deposits, foreign-currency deposits, investment trusts, mortgages, and the Sony Bank WALLET.

## 2. Business role

- As an internet-based asset-management bank for individuals, it combines foreign currency, investment trusts, mortgages, and a debit card (Sony Bank WALLET). Placing asset formation rather than a payments business at its core is its distinctive feature in banking-model terms.
- It has customer base adjacency with the life and non-life insurance of [[megabanks/sony-fg|ソニーフィナンシャルグループ]], and is positioned within group-wide financial services.
- Unlike [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行]], [[banking/paypay-bank|PayPay銀行]], [[regional-banks/au-jibun-bank|auじぶん銀行]], and [[regional-banks/daiwa-next-bank|大和ネクスト銀行]], it is backed by the financial brand power of the Sony Group / Sony FG and an insurance-adjacent base.
