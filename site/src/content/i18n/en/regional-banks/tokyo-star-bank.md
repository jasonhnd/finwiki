## TL;DR

Tokyo Star Bank, Ltd. is a Tokyo-headquartered second regional bank listed in the FSA bank-license list. Its official company profile explains that Taiwan's CTBC Bank acquired all shares in 2014 年, so within the Japan regional-bank surface it is classified as a foreign-owned second regional bank.

## 2. Operating model

Tokyo Star Bank is a bank based in Tokyo that provides retail deposits, mortgages, real-estate-secured loans, wealth consultation, and selected SME services. Rather than reading it as a standard within-prefecture regional-bank model, it is more natural to read it as a foreign-owned banking platform and niche product model.
