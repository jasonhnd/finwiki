# Lawson Bank (ローソン銀行)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[regional-banks/INDEX|regional-banks INDEX]]. Read it against [[regional-banks/seven-bank|セブン銀行 (Seven Bank)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

An emerging retail bank specialized in convenience-store ATMs. **Established 2017-11-15 / opened 2018-10-15** ^[extracted]. Its parent, Lawson, Inc., shifted in 2024-09  to a **Mitsubishi Corporation + KDDI 50:50  jointly-held JV** structure and was delisted → Lawson Bank likewise took on the appearance of dual membership in the **Mitsubishi Corporation group + KDDI au economic sphere**. As a latecomer following the preceding [[regional-banks/seven-bank]] (Seven Bank model), its ATM count is **13,889  units** (end of 2025-03 , official "Lawson Bank by the numbers"). One corner of the convenience-store ATM big three ([[regional-banks/seven-bank]] / Lawson Bank / E-net). ^[extracted]
