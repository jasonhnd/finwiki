# Lawson Bank

## Wiki route

## TL;DR

Lawson Bank is a newer retail bank specialized in convenience-store ATMs. Lawson announced on 2018-06-26 that the FSA preliminary examination for a banking license at its subsidiary Lawson Bank Preparatory Company had been completed, and the FSA bank-license workbook also confirms Lawson Bank, Inc. as a bank.^[source:lawson-fsa-2018][source:fsa-ginkou-2026] Its ATM count was **13,889** at the end of 2025-03 according to Lawson Bank's 2025 disclosure report, and the JBA member list classifies Lawson Bank under "Other Banks."^[source:lawson-disclosure-2025][source:jba-members-2026] Seven Bank, the earlier peer, says it has over 28,000 ATMs at 7-Eleven and other locations, so Lawson Bank is best compared as a store-network-based ATM platform bank.^[source:seven-bank-atm-2026]
