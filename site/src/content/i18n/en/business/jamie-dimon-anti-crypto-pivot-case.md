# Jamie Dimon and JPMorgan's crypto-asset stance and business evolution · JPM Coin / Kinexys / JPMD on Base

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[business/INDEX|business INDEX]] as a public-company-strategic-case anchor. Read it against [[business/matt-huang-triple-role-coi-template|三身分COIテンプレート]] for peer / contrast context and [[fintech/INDEX|fintech index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## Key facts

- In 2017-09  Dimon stated "BTC is a fraud, and any trader who buys BTC will be fired immediately" ^[extracted]
- At the 2022-09  House hearing he testified that crypto assets are "decentralized Ponzi schemes," and at 2024-01  Davos said "Bitcoin is a pet rock" ^[extracted]
- Softened in 2024-Q2 : acknowledged that stablecoins + tokenisation have "real-demand use cases" ^[extracted]
- Renamed Onyx to Kinexys in 2024-11  ^[extracted]
- JPMD on Base launched in 2025-Q4  = the first tokenised deposit issued by a major bank on a public chain ^[extracted]
- In 2026-Q1 , JPMD circulation ~$1B / institutional customers 100+ ^[extracted]

## Origin & evolution

During the BTC price surge of 2017 年, Dimon publicly opposed it while JPM Coin had already been turned into an internal project within the company (for intra-corporate clearing purposes). JPM Coin was released in 2019-02 . The Onyx division was established in 2020-10  and renamed Kinexys in 2024-11 . In the same period: BlackRock × Coinbase partnership (2022-08), BUIDL launch (2024-03), IBIT approval (2024-01). In 2024-Q4  Dimon shifted to the supportive side. JPMD on Base launched in 2025-Q4 . In 2026-Q1 , JPMD circulation ~$1B / institutional customers 100+.
