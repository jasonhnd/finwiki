au Jibun Bank is the net bank of au Financial Holdings 100%. Established in 2008-06-17, with capital of 1,190 億円, it holds a smartphone-centric banking position connected to the KDDI / au financial sphere. As of 2026-04-01 its number of employees is 650 名, and its bank code is 0039.

## Strategic read

au Jibun Bank is a representative example of a "telecom-carrier economic-sphere bank." Rather than a standalone branch network, it is designed to bundle mortgages, deposits, and payments by linking the au / KDDI customer base, smartphone touchpoints, and the card, securities, insurance, and payment businesses within [[megabanks/au-fh|au FH]].

Compared with [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行]] and [[banking/paypay-bank|PayPay 銀行]], au Jibun Bank's strength is the ease of connecting to the telecom carrier's existing contracts, identity verification, and billing relationships. On the other hand, its character differs from high-frequency commerce / payment touchpoints such as Rakuten Ichiba or PayPay.
