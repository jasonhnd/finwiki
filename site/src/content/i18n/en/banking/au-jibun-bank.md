# au Jibun Bank

## TL;DR

au Jibun Bank Corporation is a smartphone-first internet bank wholly owned by [[JapanFG/au-fh|au Financial Holdings]]. It was established on 2008-06-17, has stated capital of 119.0 billion yen, and is positioned as the banking component of the KDDI / au financial ecosystem. As of 2026-04-01, the bank disclosed 650 employees and bank code 0039.

## Structure

- **Company**: au Jibun Bank Corporation.
- **Shareholder**: [[JapanFG/au-fh|au Financial Holdings]] 100%.
- **Regulatory identity**: registered financial institution, Kanto Local Finance Bureau registration No. 652.
- **Rating snapshot**: as of 2026-03-05, the bank disclosed R&I issuer rating AA and JCR issuer rating AA+.

## Strategic read

au Jibun Bank is a representative example of a telecom-carrier ecosystem bank. Its strategic surface is not a branch network, but the au / KDDI customer base, smartphone interface, and cross-sell links to cards, securities, insurance, and payments within [[JapanFG/au-fh|au FH]]. The bank packages deposits, mortgages, and payment functions around that carrier relationship.

Compared with [[banking/rakuten-bank|Rakuten Bank]] and [[banking/paypay-bank|PayPay Bank]], au Jibun Bank benefits from telecom-contract identity, billing, and customer-verification relationships. Its limitation is that the au channel differs from the high-frequency commerce or QR-payment surfaces of Rakuten Marketplace and PayPay.
