# PayPay Bank

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[banking/INDEX|banking index]]. Read it against [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行 (Rakuten Bank)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/cooperative-banking-japan|Cooperative banking in Japan]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

PayPay Bank is a domestic online bank established and launched in 2000 年, and is now the core financial bank of the PayPay Group. Following the 2025-04  share restructuring, it took on a structure in which PayPay Corporation holds 75.53% of the voting rights, strengthening its positioning as the entity that bundles the payment app, cards, securities, and banking under a single brand.

## Structure

- **Company**: PayPay Bank Corporation.
- **Established / launched**: established 2000-09-19, launched 2000-10-12.
- **Capital**: 722 億 1 千万 yen.
- **Major shareholder**: PayPay Corporation 75.53% voting rights as of 2025-04-28; SMBC 21.54%; Fujitsu and insurers hold small residual stakes.
- **Group adjacency**: belongs to the same financial-services bundle as PayPay, PayPay Card, PayPay Securities, and PayPay SC.

## Strategic read

PayPay Bank's role is less that of a standalone online bank and more that of the receiving vessel for deposits, transfers, loans, and salary receipts within the PayPay payment sphere. Just as [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行]] connects to the Rakuten economic sphere and [[banking/au-jibun-bank|auじぶん銀行]] connects to the KDDI / au financial sphere, PayPay Bank has a funnel that descends from the high-frequency touchpoint of QR payments into banking usage.

The 2025 年 share restructuring carries significant meaning in pulling the bank directly under the PayPay brand. It created a structure that overlays the customer touchpoints of the PayPay app onto the legacy of the conventional online bank, the former Japan Net Bank.
