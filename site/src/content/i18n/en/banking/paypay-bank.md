# PayPay Bank (PayPay Bank)

## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[banking/INDEX|banking index]]. Read it against [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行 (Rakuten Bank)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/cooperative-banking-japan|Cooperative banking in Japan]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

PayPay Bank is a domestic net bank established and launched in 2000 年, and is now the core financial bank of the PayPay group. Through the 2025–04  equity restructuring, PayPay Corporation came to hold 75.53% of the voting rights, strengthening its positioning as the entity that bundles the payment app, cards, securities, and banking under the same brand.

## Structure

- **Company**: PayPay Bank Corporation / PayPay Bank Corporation.
- **Established / launched**: established 2000-09-19, opened for business 2000-10-12.
- **Capital**: 722 億 1 千万 yen.
- **Major shareholder**: PayPay Corporation 75.53% voting rights as of 2025-04-28; SMBC 21.54%; Fujitsu and insurers hold small residual stakes.
- **Group adjacency**: belongs to the same financial-services bundle as PayPay, PayPay Card, PayPay Securities, and PayPay SC.

## Strategic read

The role of PayPay Bank is, rather than that of a standalone net bank, the receptacle for deposits, transfers, loans, and salary receipt within the PayPay payment sphere. Just as [[banking/rakuten-bank|楽天銀行]] connects to the Rakuten economic sphere and [[banking/au-jibun-bank|auじぶん銀行]] connects to the KDDI / au financial sphere, PayPay Bank has a funnel that descends from the high-frequency contact points of QR payment into banking usage.

The 2025 年 equity restructuring carries the large significance of pulling the bank directly beneath the PayPay brand. It created a structure that layers the customer contact points of the PayPay app onto the legacy of the conventional net bank, the former Japan Net Bank.
