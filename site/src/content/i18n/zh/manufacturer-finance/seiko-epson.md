# Seiko Epson Corporation

## Wiki 路由

本条目位于 [[manufacturer-finance/INDEX|manufacturing index]] 之下。阅读时可将 [[regional-banks/hachijuni-bank|hachijuni-bank]] 作为 Nagano regional-bank / manufacturer 背景，将 [[manufacturer-finance/toyota-motor|Toyota Motor Corporation]] 作为相邻的 manufacturer-finance anchor，并将 [[INDEX|FinWiki index]] 作为更广的系统边界。

## TL;DR

Seiko Epson 是总部位于长野县诹访市的精密 / 电子设备制造商。官方 company outline 给出 paid-in capital JPY 53,204mn、consolidated employees 74,619，以及截至 2026-03-31 财年的 revenue JPY 1,413.3bn。^[source:epson-company-outline-2026] FY2026 financial results 显示 revenue JPY 1,413,251mn、business profit JPY 83,788mn、total assets JPY 1,534,870mn、equity attributable to owners of the parent JPY 853,503mn、period-end cash / cash equivalents JPY 288,582mn。^[source:epson-fy2026-results-2026] 对 FinWiki 而言，Epson 不是受监管金融公司；它是一个**制造业 anchor**，连接 regional-bank lending、global supply-chain finance、treasury / liquidity、corporate bonds、FX 和 shareholder-return decisions。

## 2. Treasury / balance-sheet anchor

Epson 属于 manufacturer-finance，因为大型制造商即便没有 captive finance company，也会不断产生金融系统接点：

- **Liquidity**：FY2026 cash and cash equivalents at end of period 为 JPY 288,582mn。^[source:epson-fy2026-results-2026]
- **Operating cash generation**：FY2026 net cash provided by operating activities 为 JPY 112,372mn。^[source:epson-fy2026-results-2026][source:epson-cashflow-2026]
- **Investing cycle**：FY2026 investing cash flow 为负 JPY 65,561mn，包括购买 property, plant and equipment 以及 intangible assets；这正是 equipment finance、capex timing 和 supplier financing 重要的地方。^[source:epson-cashflow-2026]
- **Financing cycle**：FY2026 financing cash flow 为负 JPY 39,604mn；cash-flow table 包含 current borrowings、non-current borrowings、bond redemption、lease obligations 和 dividends。^[source:epson-cashflow-2026]
- **Shareholder return**：Epson 表示，会根据股价与资本情况，机动购买 treasury shares 以更积极回报股东。^[source:epson-shareholder-returns-2026]

## 4. 风险 / 政策图

- **Disclosure / listing**：作为上市公司，Epson 适用证券披露和公司治理制度，而不是保险或银行监管。
- **Manufacturing cycle**：Revenue 和 business profit 取决于 printing、robotics、visual products 和 precision-device 需求；这与受监管金融中介不同。
- **Capital allocation**：Dividends、treasury shares、bond redemptions 和 borrowings 使 Epson 可用于分析日本 corporate treasury behavior。
- **Environmental / industrial policy**：Energy efficiency、resource circulation 和 manufacturing-location decisions 会影响区域贷款和 capex finance。
