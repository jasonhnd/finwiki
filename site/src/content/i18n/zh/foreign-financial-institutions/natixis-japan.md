# Natixis 东京分行

## TL;DR

Natixis 是法国 / Groupe BPCE 系 Natixis CIB 在日本的银行分行，列入金融厅外国银行分行名单。它是在 2025 年 宣布银行分行化的较新 P0  外国银行分行锚点，本页将其视为从 lending-only presence 扩展到 broader banking licensed activities 的阶段。

## 2. 运营模式

Natixis CIB announced that its Japan banking branch would be established from 2025-07-01, expanding a Japan setup that had operated under a money-lending license since 2022. The official release frames the branch around capital markets, M&A, investment banking, asset-backed financing, and sector capabilities in infrastructure, energy and renewables, insurance, transportation, healthcare, telecom, and technology.

其在日本的实际角色偏批发 / 机构业务，而非零售：覆盖在日全球客户、日本客户的境内与出海活动、保险公司、银行、金融赞助方、公共部门组织以及跨境 CIB 覆盖。
