# retail domain

## TL;DR

This domain routes retail groups when they matter to finance: store traffic, card issuing, prepaid electronic money, loyalty points, ATM networks, retail media, and embedded financial distribution.

The current active pair is [[retail/aeon-group|AEON Group]] and [[retail/seven-and-i-hd|Seven & i Holdings]].

## Retail Finance Pattern

| Pattern | AEON | Seven & i |
|---|---|---|
| Main physical wedge | Supermarket / mall / shopping-center visits | Convenience-store density |
| Bank layer | [[JapanFG/aeon-bank|Aeon Bank / AFS]] | [[JapanFG/seven-bank|Seven Bank]] |
| Payment / stored value | WAON, AEON Pay, AEON Card | nanaco, Seven Card, ATM services |
| Point / loyalty | WAON POINT | nanaco point |
| Strategic strength | Broad daily-life retail + finance cross-sell | ATM / cash-access platform + convenience frequency |
| Main risk | App complexity and competition from PayPay / Rakuten / telco points | Cashless adoption and dependence on ATM economics |
