This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[card-issuers/jaccs|株式会社ジャックス (Jaccs)]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

SMBCコンシューマーファイナンスは PROMISE ブランドで知られる [[megabanks/smfg|SMFG]] 系 consumer-finance operator。貸金業、保証業、個人向けローンのノウハウが中心で、カード会社の [[card-issuers/smbc-card|三井住友カード]] とは役割を分けて読む。

## 2. Business role

- Provides unsecured consumer-loan and guarantee capabilities within SMFG's retail-finance stack.
- Complements bank account, card, and transaction services with direct lending and credit-scoring know-how.
- Important comparator against [[consumer-finance/acom|ACOM]], [[consumer-finance/aiful|AIFUL]], and [[card-issuers/life-card|Life Card]].

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

Consumer finance has different credit risk, regulation, customer acquisition, and disclosure from card issuing. The standalone page prevents SMFG's consumer lending from being hidden inside the bank page.
