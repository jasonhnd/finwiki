## TL;DR

COINHUB is a registered Japanese crypto asset exchange operator. The FSA list shows Kanto Local Finance Bureau No.00013, registration date 2017-12-26, and BTC as the handled crypto asset. COINHUB's official site presents the business as a crypto-asset exchange service with crypto ATM positioning.

## 2. 業務範囲

COINHUB's public site describes business activities as crypto asset exchange service and emphasizes crypto ATM access. This makes it different from large online order-book exchanges and from bank-backed platforms.

## 3. 戦略動向

- **Crypto ATM / cash interface**: The company is positioned around a crypto ATM network and physical access pattern.
- **Narrow asset scope**: The FSA registry row lists BTC only, making it a focused operator compared with broad-listing domestic exchanges.
- **Completeness role**: Its inclusion closes a direct gap against the FSA registered-operator list.

## Counterpoints

- Public disclosures are limited; volume, account count, and network scale require careful source handling.
- ATM-based crypto access is more exposed to AML / fraud-risk perception than ordinary online exchange UX.
