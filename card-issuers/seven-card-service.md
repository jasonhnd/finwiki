## 2. Business role

- Connects card usage to Seven-Eleven / Ito-Yokado retail customer relationships, nanaco electronic money, and repeat purchase data.
- Complements Seven Bank's ATM and account infrastructure with card / payment services; the company page states that Seven Bank became the parent in 2023 and that the group is focusing on integrated retail financial services.
- The company overview lists credit-card business, electronic-money business, and other businesses, so the page should be read as a retail-payment operator rather than only a co-branded card page.
- A useful case for reading retail payment strategy separately from banking strategy.
