# financial-regulators — Japan financial regulators, SROs and policy-finance index

## Scope

`financial-regulators/` is the standalone top-level domain for Japan's **regulatory, self-regulatory and policy-finance** layer: the Bank of Japan and its operating departments, the Financial Services Agency and its bureaus, policy / government finance institutions, self-regulatory organisations and industry bodies, audit firms, credit rating agencies, financial safety-net / protection corporations, money-market (tanshi) infrastructure, and exchanges acting as self-regulatory bodies. It contains **52 member pages** (excluding this INDEX, 53 total), of which 50 are entity / institution pages and 2 are registries listed under [Registries](#registries).

Use this domain when the question is **"which body regulates, self-regulates, supervises, rates, or backstops Japanese finance, and through what statute or mandate?"**

## Disambiguation

- For the **thematic policy-finance layer** — government finance statutes, export credit, ODA, SME / infrastructure / regional finance as a policy theme — use [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance]]. This domain holds the *institution* pages (JFC, JBIC, DBJ, ODFC, Shoko Chukin) and bridges out to the policy-finance theme.
- For **financial-group entity / operating-company umbrella** pages (banks, securities firms, insurers, card / leasing / consumer-finance companies), use [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]].
- For the **license / statute stack** (which license or law makes an activity possible) use [[financial-licenses/INDEX|financial-licenses]].
