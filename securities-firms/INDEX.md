# securities-firms — Japanese securities brokers / FIEA 第一種 operator index

## Scope

`securities-firms/` is the top-level domain for **Japanese securities broker / dealer operating companies** — the entity layer of the FIEA 第一種金融商品取引業 (Type I Financial Instruments Business) world. It covers independent securities groups (野村 HD, 大和証券グループ), megabank-affiliated securities (SMBC 日興, みずほ証券, MUFG 証券), online / smartphone brokers (SBI証券, 楽天証券, 松井証券, マネックス, PayPay証券, DMM.com証券), FX-focused 第一種 operators (SBI FXトレード, GMOクリック, トレイダーズ, サクソバンク), regional securities subsidiaries (ちばぎん証券, FFG 証券, めぶき証券), the small / mid-cap independent broker tail (丸三証券, 岡三証券グループ, 東海東京, アイザワ証券, 岩井コスモ, 立花証券, 今村証券, 極東証券), and the Japan operating arms of foreign investment banks (Goldman Sachs Japan, Morgan Stanley Japan, ANZ Bank Japan). All of these were split out of the former [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]] umbrella.

These are **firms** (registered legal entities), not thematic market mechanics. Per-entity pages carry FSA registration number (財務局長(金商)第N号), FIEA scope, parent / group boundary, and business role.
