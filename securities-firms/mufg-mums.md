# Mitsubishi UFJ Morgan Stanley Securities

## TL;DR

Mitsubishi UFJ Morgan Stanley Securities is the core MUFG securities company created around the MUFG / Morgan Stanley strategic alliance. Its role is different from a pure online brokerage: it is a bank-affiliated comprehensive securities firm covering wealth management, investment banking, sales and trading, and corporate / institutional securities services.

Use this page as the [[megabanks/mufg|MUFG]] securities-arm route. Use Morgan Stanley MUFG Securities separately when the focus is institutional Morgan Stanley-led Japan activity.

## JV Boundary

The important analytical boundary is the two-company Japan JV structure described by Morgan Stanley:

- Mitsubishi UFJ Morgan Stanley Securities serves individual and corporate clients, including wealth management, investment banking, and sales & trading.
- Morgan Stanley MUFG Securities provides institutional services such as research, sales & trading, and capital markets.

That means MUMSS should not be treated as simply "MUFG's retail brokerage" or simply "Morgan Stanley Japan." It is a Japan-specific bank / global-investment-bank alliance structure.
