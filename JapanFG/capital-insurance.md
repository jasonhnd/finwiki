## Overview

Capital Insurance is a licensed Japanese non-life insurer with public group-support disclosure tied to Mitsubishi HC Capital. In FinWiki it is a specialty non-life route adjacent to [[JapanFG/mitsubishi-hc-capital]] and leasing / finance group insurance.
