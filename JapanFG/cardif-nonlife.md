# Cardif Non-Life Japan / カーディフ損害保険

Wiki route: [[JapanFG/INDEX]] -> [[insurance/INDEX]] -> [[JapanFG/cardif-nonlife]]

## Overview

Cardif Non-Life Japan is the BNP Paribas Cardif non-life insurance entity in Japan. It sits beside [[JapanFG/cardif-life]] as the non-life side of a foreign bancassurance / protection-insurance platform.
