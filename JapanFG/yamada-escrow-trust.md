## Overview

Yamada Escrow Trust's public site describes it as a management trust company handling money trust, securities trust, money-claim trust, and real-estate trust. It presents trust use cases across real-estate sale, leasing, M&A transactions, senior housing, and related services.

The page matters because escrow trust is a concrete transaction-control mechanism. It is useful for FinWiki when an asset transfer or payment flow needs segregation, retention, or bankruptcy-remoteness.
