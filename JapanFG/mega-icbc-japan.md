## TL;DR

兆豐國際商業銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Taiwan / Mega International Commercial Bank の在日銀行支店。Taiwan's major international commercial bank network の Japan branch として、Japan-Taiwan trade, corporate banking, remittance, and treasury flows を担う。

## 2. Operating model

Mega Bank's Tokyo site provides the Japan branch route, while the overseas page lists Tokyo among its global offices. The branch is a Taiwan-Japan cross-border banking platform, especially for Taiwanese corporates, Japanese clients with Taiwan exposure, remittances, and trade-finance related services.
