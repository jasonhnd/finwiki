# CastGlobal Trust / キャストグローバル信託

## Overview

CastGlobal Trust is a management-type trust company tied to real-estate, escrow, and cross-border investment support. In FinWiki it is a professional-services trust route, useful beside [[JapanFG/yamada-escrow-trust]] and [[JapanFG/kyodo-trust]].

## Entity Boundary

This page covers キャストグローバル信託株式会社, not the whole CastGlobal professional-services network. Claims about legal, tax, or international partner services should stay tied to public company materials.
