# スマートバンク (SmartBank)


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/au-payment|auペイメント (au Payment)]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

スマートバンクは B/43 / ワンバンクを運営する household-finance and prepaid wallet fintech。公式会社概要は資金移動業者登録と第三者型前払式支払手段発行者登録を明示しており、家計管理 UX と payment account を組み合わせる事例。

## 2. Business role

- Builds payment and account-like UX around household budgeting problems.
- Uses prepaid / funds-transfer licensing to support shared spending and wallet flows.
- A useful comparator against Kyash, Merpay, and bank app experiences.

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

SmartBank is small compared with megabank or telecom wallets, but it shows how financial UX can start from budgeting rather than banking or commerce.
