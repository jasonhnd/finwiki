## TL;DR

クレディ・アグリコル銀行は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される France / Crédit Agricole CIB の在日銀行支店。[[JapanFG/credit-agricole-life|クレディ・アグリコル生命]] とは別の Banking Act branch として、wholesale banking / CIB / global markets / trade and export finance の日本拠点を扱う。

## 2. Operating model

Crédit Agricole CIB's Japan page describes a Tokyo franchise across investment and corporate banking, global markets, real-asset financing, international trade and transaction banking, corporate and leverage finance, distribution, and financing services. The official Japan network page also describes project, export, trade, aviation, real-estate, JPY financing, derivatives, hedging, debt capital markets, JGB primary-dealer, and JSCC IRS participation.

The FSA list is the source of truth for the Banking Act branch name and current registry inclusion. The 2026 Crédit Agricole CIB office notice confirms the group relocation to Azabudai Hills; branch address details should be checked against the current FSA workbook when address precision matters.
