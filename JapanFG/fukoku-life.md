# 富国生命保険


## Wiki route

## TL;DR

富国生命保険相互会社は、金融庁 life-insurer list と生命保険協会会員一覧に載る mutual life-insurance operating company。大手 4 社ほどの規模 headline はないが、相互会社形態の independent life insurer として、[[asahi-life]]、[[nippon-life]]、[[meiji-yasuda]]、[[sumitomo-life]] と同じ governance category で扱う。

## 2. Operating model

富国生命は、traditional life-insurance products and sales channels を持つ independent mutual insurer。FinWiki では、mutual-company governance, policyholder orientation, solvency, channel productivity を比較するために standalone page とする。

Mutual life insurers are not listed equity plays; analysis should avoid using listed-company metrics alone. Instead, disclosure review should focus on policyholder protection, solvency margin / ESR transition, surplus distribution, and long-term product economics.
