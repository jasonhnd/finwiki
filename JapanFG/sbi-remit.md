## 2. Business role

- Provides Japan-origin international remittance with migrant-worker and resident-foreigner use cases.
- Expands through bank and regional-financial-institution partnerships.
- Important for tracking bank-fintech remittance cooperation after ISO 20022 and AML/CFT pressure.

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

SBI's bank, securities, crypto, and regional-bank pages do not explain the remittance operator. SBI Remit is its own licensed funds-transfer entity.
