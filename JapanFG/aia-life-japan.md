## TL;DR

AIA Group Limited is one of the largest pan-Asian life and health insurance groups, headquartered in Hong Kong and listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HKEX: 1299). With operations across 18 markets in Asia-Pacific, AIA's Japan presence sits within its broader regional strategy. Compared with the major foreign life insurers in Japan such as [[JapanFG/metlife-japan|MetLife Japan]] and [[JapanFG/prudential-japan|Prudential Japan]], AIA's distinctive feature is its pure-play Asia-Pacific positioning and historical roots in the region's life insurance market.

## 2. Operating model

AIA's group business model centers on agency-led life insurance distribution across Asia-Pacific, with strong protection and health insurance focus. In Japan, AIA's positioning has historically been more limited compared with its dominant presence in markets like Hong Kong, Singapore, Thailand, China, and Southeast Asia. The Japan operation reflects AIA's strategic choice to focus capital and management attention on growth markets where the protection gap is wider.

Compared with peers:

- [[JapanFG/metlife-japan|MetLife Japan]]: large agency force, broad retail life and accident & health
- [[JapanFG/prudential-japan|Prudential Japan]] (Gibraltar Life): top-tier foreign life insurer with very large in-force book
- [[JapanFG/aflac-japan|Aflac Japan]]: third-sector (cancer / medical) leader with bancassurance and agency mix
- [[JapanFG/manulife-japan|Manulife Life Insurance Japan]]: mid-tier foreign with foreign-currency and variable life focus
