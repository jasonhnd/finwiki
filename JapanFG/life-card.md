# ライフカード (Life Card)


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG index]]. Read it against [[JapanFG/rakuten-card|楽天カード (Rakuten Card)]] for peer / contrast context and [[payments/INDEX|payments index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

ライフカードは [[JapanFG/aiful|アイフル]] グループのカード issuer。公式会社概要はクレジットカード、プリペイドカード、銀行保証、保険などを事業内容として示しており、consumer-credit と payments の交差点にある。

## 1. 法人・ライセンス境界

| Item | Reading |
|---|---|
| Legal entity | ライフカード株式会社 |
| Group | [[JapanFG/aiful|AIFUL group]] |
| Main lanes | Credit card, prepaid card, bank guarantee, insurance agency |
| Regulatory route | Installment Sales Act, card-number contracting, lending, and association membership shown in official profile. |

## 2. Business role

- Gives AIFUL a card and prepaid payment surface in addition to unsecured lending.
- Bridges consumer credit, guarantee, and everyday card usage.
- Useful comparator against [[JapanFG/acom|ACOM]] and bank-affiliated card issuers.

## 3. Why this standalone page matters

Consumer-finance groups often have both lending and card subsidiaries. Life Card makes that product boundary explicit and prevents AIFUL from being read as loan-only.
