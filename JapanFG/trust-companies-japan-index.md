# Japan trust companies registry index

## TL;DR

FSA's trust-company list contains **38 trust companies as of 2026-03-31**. This page is the registry route for the independent / group trust-company surface. It is separate from licensed trust banks such as [[JapanFG/mitsubishi-ufj-trust-bank]], [[JapanFG/mizuho-trust-bank]], [[JapanFG/smtb]], [[JapanFG/smbc-trust-bank]], [[JapanFG/nomura-trust-bank]], and [[JapanFG/nochu-trust-bank]].

Use this page to avoid creating 38 standalone company pages unless a trust company is strategically material.

## Boundary

| Boundary | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Trust banks | Keep as standalone bank operating-company pages under [[JapanFG/INDEX]]. |
| Trust companies | Keep the full FSA list here; promote standalone pages only for material group, listed-company, fintech, estate, or tokenization relevance. |
| Group-linked trust companies | Route to group pages where the group page already exists; do not imply the group page covers all trust-company details. |
| Long-tail trust companies | Registry-only until there is a public-source reason to promote. |

## Standalone Promotion Rules

Promote a trust company from registry-only to standalone page only if one of these is true:

- it is a listed-company group subsidiary with material strategic relevance;
- it is tied to tokenized securities, estate planning, bankruptcy remoteness, escrow, or real-estate finance infrastructure;
- it appears repeatedly in JapanFG / securities / real-estate finance analysis;
- public filings, official disclosure, or regulator material provide enough content for a separate source-backed page.
