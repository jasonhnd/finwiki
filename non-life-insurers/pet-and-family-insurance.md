## Overview

ペット＆ファミリー損害保険株式会社 is a FSA-listed non-life insurer specializing in pet insurance. Its official company profile identifies T&D Holdings as the 100% shareholder, making it the T&D group route into pet-insurance underwriting.

The page matters because pet insurance is a different specialty line from the group's core life-insurance companies: [[life-insurers/daido-life|Daido Life]], [[life-insurers/taiyo-life|Taiyo Life]], and [[life-insurers/td-financial-life|T&D Financial Life]].

## 2. Operating model

Pet & Family is a specialty non-life insurer rather than a broad auto / fire carrier. The product model is built around pet medical expenses, owner acquisition, claims handling, and animal-hospital usage patterns.

The company's official history shows a small-amount short-term insurance origin, transition into a non-life insurer, and later product development for pet medical-expense policies. That history makes it useful for comparing specialty-insurance scale-up paths against [[non-life-insurers/dai-ichi-ipet-insurance|Daiichi ipet]] and [[non-life-insurers/anicom-insurance|Anicom]].
