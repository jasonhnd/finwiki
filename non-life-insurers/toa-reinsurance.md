## TL;DR

トーア再保険株式会社は、金融庁の損害保険会社免許一覧に掲載される Japanese reinsurance company。Primary insurer ではなく、保険会社からリスクを引き受ける reinsurance operating company として JapanFG に置く。

## 2. Operating model

Reinsurance redistributes catastrophe, mortality / morbidity, liability, and large-loss risks across insurers and global capital. トーア再保険 is important because Japanese primary insurers' risk capacity is partly shaped by domestic and global reinsurance relationships, including the big three non-life insurers [[non-life-insurers/tokio-marine|東京海上 HD]], [[non-life-insurers/msad|MS&AD]], and [[non-life-insurers/sompo|SOMPO HD]] who cede a portion of catastrophe exposure via [[insurance/natcat-reinsurance-japan|natcat reinsurance arrangements]].
