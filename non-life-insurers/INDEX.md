# non-life-insurers — Japanese non-life / P&C insurer + kyosai domain index

## Scope

`non-life-insurers/` is the entity domain for **Japanese non-life (損害 / P&C) insurance carriers and kyosai (共済) mutuals** — every entity licensed under Japan's Insurance Business Act (保険業法) to underwrite non-life risk, plus the cooperative-insurance federations that sit alongside the licensed market. It holds the 大手3社 holding and operating companies, the direct (digital / telco / EC) carriers, the specialty and niche insurers, the foreign-affiliated carriers and reinsurer branches, the Lloyd's Japan platform, and the two largest kyosai federations. This domain was **split out of the former `JapanFG/` umbrella** so that the non-life entity layer routes independently from the entity-group umbrella and from thematic insurance concepts.

Each page is an **entity / operating-company** page. The thematic operating models — catastrophe risk, agency channel, solvency, bancassurance — stay in [[insurance/INDEX|insurance]]. The FSA registry routing surface stays on the registry page below.
