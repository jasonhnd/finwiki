# American Home Insurance Japan / アメリカンホーム医療・損害保険

Wiki route: [[non-life-insurers/INDEX]] -> [[insurance/INDEX]] -> [[non-life-insurers/american-home-insurance-japan]]

## Overview

American Home Medical and General Insurance is an FSA-licensed non-life insurer in Japan. In FinWiki it is a legacy AIG-linked direct / medical-insurance route, separate from the broader [[non-life-insurers/aig-japan]] page and from direct auto insurers such as [[non-life-insurers/sony-insurance]].

## Entity Boundary

This page covers アメリカンホーム医療・損害保険株式会社, not AIG Japan as a whole. The company-brand context matters because "American Home" and "American Home Direct" remain consumer-facing insurance brands in public materials.
