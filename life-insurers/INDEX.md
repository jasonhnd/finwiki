# life-insurers — Japanese life-insurance operating company index

## Scope

`life-insurers/` is a top-level **entity** domain holding **46 entries** (excluding INDEX): the Japanese **life-insurance operating companies** — direct life insurers plus the life / health **reinsurers** registered on the FSA life-insurer list and the Life Insurance Association of Japan (生命保険協会) member directory. These pages were split out of the former **JapanFG** umbrella so each life-insurance legal entity carries its own license, group-boundary, and operating-model anchor. Membership is registry-driven: an entity belongs here when it sits on the FSA 生命保険会社免許一覧 (or its reinsurer extension) and underwrites Japan life / health risk.

### Disambiguation

- **Thematic insurance concepts** (channel mix, solvency / ESR, mutual-vs-stock structure, big-four market mechanics) live in `[[insurance/INDEX|insurance]]`, not here. This domain is the **operating-company** registry; `insurance/` is the **subject-matter** map.
- **Non-life / P&C carriers and general reinsurers** live in `[[non-life-insurers/INDEX|non-life-insurers]]`. Note that several life entities below are *life subsidiaries of P&C groups* (Tokio Marine, SOMPO, MS&AD) — those subsidiaries belong here; their P&C parents belong in non-life-insurers.
- The broader **entity umbrella** these pages were carved out of is `[[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]]`; member pages still cross-link back to it for system-boundary context.
