# Japan fair disclosure and insider trading controls

## Overview

Japan fair disclosure and insider-trading records connect selective disclosure, timely disclosure, unpublished material facts, issuer information management, insider trading restrictions, and exchange disclosure systems. Public routes include FSA fair-disclosure materials, Financial Instruments and Exchange Act text, JPX / TSE timely disclosure pages, and TDnet.

## Fair Disclosure Rule Fields

| Field | Public fact route |
|---|---|
| Statutory basis | FIEA Article 27-36. |
| FSA description | Rule introduced to ensure fair disclosure of information to investors. |
| Trigger route | Listed company provides undisclosed material information to specified business associates. |
| Same-time disclosure route | Disclosure at the same time as the information transmission unless exception conditions apply. |
| Confidentiality / no-trading exception | Recipient is bound by confidentiality and no-trading obligations under law, regulation, or contract. |
| Prompt disclosure route | Prompt disclosure after learning of specified unintentional or non-same-time transmission. |
| Leak / trade route | Prompt disclosure if a confidentiality / no-trading recipient leaks or trades before disclosure. |
| Material information field | Undisclosed precise information likely to have a material influence on the value of securities. |
| Mosaic information field | FSA guideline indicates mosaic information generally is not, by itself, subject information. |
| Internal control field | Internal rules and employee training are listed by FSA guideline as practical control measures. |

## Insider Trading Publication Fields

FIEA Article 166 is the public statutory route for company-insider insider-trading restrictions. Japanese Law Translation materials define `Publicized` through Cabinet Order-specified publication measures or public inspection of specified disclosure documents under Article 25 when the fact is stated in those documents.

JPX's TDnet page states that disclosure through the Company Announcements Disclosure Service / `適時開示情報閲覧サービス` completes legal publication measures for material facts and certain tender-offer facts. The same page states that TDnet public postings are visible on the public disclosure service for 31 days including the disclosure date, while Listed Company Search covers past disclosure materials for 10 years.

## Legal Publication and Practical Information Gap

The statutory publication route is tied to the FIEA / Cabinet Order publication-measure framework and TDnet publication-measure field. JPX insider-trading public materials also record a practical information-gap note for the period immediately after a formal public announcement. This field is recorded as a conduct / fairness note, separate from the statutory publication-measure field.
