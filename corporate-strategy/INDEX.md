# Corporate strategy domain

## TL;DR

This domain routes **tax-driven corporate-restructuring strategy** in Japan — anchored on the **partial spinoff tax deferral** (パーシャルスピンオフ税制) regime, the 2023 reform that lets a parent retain a minority stake in a spun-off subsidiary while still qualifying for tax-deferred treatment, and now spanning the adjacent restructuring tax regimes, an option-selection decision tree, and a flagship going-private case.

The domain currently holds **10 content entries**. The natural cluster is Japanese corporate-restructuring tax regimes (会社分割 / 合併 / 株式交換 / 株式移転 / 株式交付 / 株式分配 / パーシャルスピンオフ) plus the legal structures, the group-tax overlay, and the decision tooling around them, with flagship cases that exercise these regimes end-to-end. Each new entry should answer a *decision-relevant* question about Japanese restructuring — a tax-regime page, a structure-selection guide, or a worked case — not a generic corporate-finance write-up. For broader financial-system context see [[finance/INDEX|finance]] and [[INDEX|FinWiki index]]; for the policy-finance institutions that often co-finance restructurings see [[policy-finance/INDEX|policy-finance]].

## Start here

Start from the decision you face:

- *"Which restructuring structure fits my goal?"* — open [[corporate-strategy/spinoff-decision-tree-japan|the spinoff decision tree]] to choose between 株式分配 / パーシャルスピンオフ / 会社分割 / IPO partial sell-down.
- *"Can a Japanese parent spin off a subsidiary while retaining a minority stake without triggering capital-gains tax?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/japan-kabushiki-bunpai-spinoff-regime|partial spinoff tax deferral]], the specific gap the 2023 reform addresses.
- *"How is a corporate split (吸収分割 / 新設分割) taxed?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/japan-kaisha-bunkatsu-tax-regime|the 会社分割 tax regime]], the broader regime under which partial spinoffs sit.
- *"How do I structure a share-for-share acquisition of a Japanese target?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/kabushiki-koufu-stock-distribution-regime|the 株式交付 regime]].
- *"What does a large-cap going-private squeeze-out look like end-to-end?"* — work through the [[corporate-strategy/toshiba-tob-squeeze-out-2023-2024-case|Toshiba TOB squeeze-out case]].
- *"What's the difference between a full spin-off (株式分配) and a partial one?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/japan-kabushiki-bunpai-spinoff-regime|the 株式分配 spin-off regime]] (0% retained vs パーシャルスピンオフ's 20%).
- *"How is a merger (吸収合併 / 新設合併) structured and taxed?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/japan-gappei-merger-regime|the 合併 merger regime]], the combination-side counterpart to 会社分割.
- *"How does a wholly-owned group net its profits and losses for tax?"* — read [[corporate-strategy/japan-group-tsusan-consolidated-tax-regime|the グループ通算 group-tax regime]], the post-conversion overlay.

Pair any of these with the broader [[finance/INDEX|finance INDEX]] for general capital-markets context.
