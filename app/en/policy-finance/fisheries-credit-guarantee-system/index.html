Fisheries credit guarantee system in Japan

Fisheries credit guarantee system in Japan

Wiki route

This entry sits under policy-finance index. Read it against Japan credit guarantee system for SME-side contrast, agriculture credit guarantee system for the sister sector (same JAFFIC parent), and Japan policy finance system for the broader public-credit boundary. The fisheries deposit-and-lending counterpart is JF Marine Bank system and the cooperative apex is Norinchukin (which serves agriculture, forestry, and fisheries cooperatives jointly).

TL;DR

Japan’s fisheries credit guarantee system (漁業信用保証保険制度) supplements small / medium fisheries operators’ credit through a two-tier structure: (1) prefectural-and-broad-area-level 漁業信用基金協会 (Fisheries Credit Fund Associations) that guarantee fisheries-borrower debt to lending institutions, with the 全国漁業信用基金協会連合会 as national federation, reinsured by (2) the national-level 独立行政法人農林漁業信用基金 (JAFFIC) fisheries-credit-insurance business. The system parallels the agriculture credit guarantee system but with sector-specific borrower targets — fishing vessel finance, aquaculture, seafood processing — and is geographically concentrated in Japan’s coastal prefectures with Hokkaido, Kyushu (Nagasaki / Kagoshima), Tōhoku Pacific coast, and Sanriku coast accounting for the bulk of guarantee volume.

3. 機関概要 — JAFFIC 漁業信用保険業務

Same parent agency as agriculture (independent administrative agency 農林漁業信用基金 / JAFFIC). Separate fisheries credit insurance account within JAFFIC, with separate reinsurance pool and separate prefectural-association interface. See agriculture-credit-guarantee-system for the agriculture-account counterpart.

4. 主要保証商品

  • 漁業近代化資金保証 — modernization-loan guarantee for fishing-vessel construction, engine replacement, gear upgrade, refrigeration, on-board processing.
  • 漁業経営改善支援資金保証 — fisheries-management improvement loan guarantee.
  • 沿岸漁業改善資金保証 — coastal-fisheries improvement loan guarantee.
  • 新規就業者支援資金保証 — new-entrant fisher / aquaculture-operator support guarantee.
  • 養殖関連融資保証 — aquaculture facility / feed / fingerling working-capital guarantee (heavy use in scallop / oyster / yellowtail / kombu / pearl segments).
  • 水産加工 / 流通融資保証 — seafood processing and distribution working-capital guarantee.
  • 災害復旧保証 — disaster-recovery guarantee (typhoon damage, marine pollution, red tide, vessel loss, harbor damage).
  • 燃油価格高騰対策保証 — fuel-price-shock liquidity guarantee.
  • 借換保証 — refinancing guarantee.

5. 製品ミックス characteristics

The fisheries guarantee book has a distinct product-mix profile vs the agriculture book:

  • Fishing vessel finance is heavily collateralized but with depreciating asset values; guarantee terms reflect the vessel-life cycle and resale-market thinness for specialized vessels.
  • Aquaculture finance is highly geographically concentrated (scallop / kombu in Hokkaido and Sanriku; oyster / pearl in Hiroshima and Mie; yellowtail in Kagoshima and Ehime; tuna farming in Wakayama and Nagasaki) and exposed to environmental shocks (red tide, marine heatwave, disease outbreaks).
  • Processing finance overlaps with general SME working-capital needs but with seasonality tied to catch cycles and inventory build for export markets.

6. Geographic concentration

Unlike agriculture, which is geographically distributed across nearly all prefectures, the fisheries guarantee system is heavily concentrated in coastal prefectures:

RegionFisheries characterGuarantee book emphasis
HokkaidoPacific salmon, scallop, kelp, sea urchin, crab — Japan’s largest fisheries prefecture by volume and value.Vessel finance + scallop / kelp aquaculture + processing.
Sanriku coast (Iwate, Miyagi)Coastal fisheries; oyster, scallop, kelp aquaculture; salmon; saury.Post-2011-earthquake reconstruction guarantee + aquaculture.
Kyushu (Nagasaki, Kagoshima, Fukuoka, Saga)Tuna farming, yellowtail aquaculture, coastal fisheries, pelagic fishing fleet.Aquaculture + vessel finance + export-processing.
Sea of Japan (Tottori, Shimane, Toyama)Snow crab, glow squid, yellowtail.Vessel + seasonal-processing guarantee.
Pacific central (Chiba, Shizuoka, Kochi)Bonito, tuna, mackerel; distant-water fleet.Vessel finance for distant-water fleet.
Inland-sea (Hiroshima, Ehime, Mie)Oyster, pearl, yellowtail.Aquaculture-heavy guarantee book.

The geographic concentration means the fisheries guarantee system’s portfolio risk is exposed to regional marine-environment shocks that can simultaneously affect multiple guarantees in a single coastal area.
