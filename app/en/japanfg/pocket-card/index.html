ポケットカードは retail / co-brand credit-card 領域の重要 operator。P-one / ファミマ / 旧流通系カードの文脈を持ち、銀行系・EC系・通信系カードとは異なる提携カード戦略を読むためのページ。

2. Business role

  • Co-brand cards turn retail relationships into revolving credit and payment data.
  • The company is a useful case for non-megabank card competition.
  • Its boundary matters when mapping retail payment alliances and loyalty economics.

3. Why this standalone page matters

The company is easy to miss if the wiki only tracks financial holding companies. It is a named card issuer with its own public profile and a distinct retail-card strategy.
