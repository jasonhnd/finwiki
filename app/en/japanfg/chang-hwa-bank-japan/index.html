Overview

Chang Hwa Bank is a Taiwan commercial bank with an international network that includes Tokyo. Its public overseas-network page lists Tokyo Branch among overseas branches and describes the overseas units as offering deposits, loans, remittance, trade finance, letters of credit, guarantees, FX, money / capital-market transactions, and international syndicated-loan participation.

The branch is worth standalone treatment because the remaining Taiwan registry-only row is not merely a raw address. It adds a legacy Taiwan bank with a Tokyo branch, Osaka sub-branch, and public disclosure surface to FinWiki’s Taiwan corridor cluster.
