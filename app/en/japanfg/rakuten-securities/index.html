Rakuten Securities

Rakuten Securities

TL;DR

Rakuten Securities is the online brokerage arm associated with Rakuten’s financial ecosystem. FSA’s financial instruments business operator list identifies 楽天証券株式会社 as Kanto Local Finance Bureau (FIBO) No. 195. Rakuten’s own April 2024 release said Rakuten Securities surpassed 11 million general securities customer accounts and linked the growth environment to the revamped 2024 NISA program.

Use this page for online brokerage, retail NISA acquisition, point / card / ecosystem distribution, and household asset-building flow.

Strategic Meaning

Rakuten Securities is not a traditional branch brokerage. Its advantage is distribution:

  • Rakuten ID / ecosystem customer acquisition;
  • Rakuten Card and cash-payment routes for investment trusts;
  • point investment and loyalty integration;
  • digital-first NISA onboarding;
  • low-fee online brokerage competition.

The risk is also distribution-linked: online brokerage competition compresses commissions, and ecosystem benefits can become expensive if points / card rewards are used as acquisition subsidies.
