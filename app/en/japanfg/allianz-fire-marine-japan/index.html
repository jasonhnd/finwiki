Overview

Allianz Fire and Marine Japan is an FSA-licensed non-life insurer connected to the Allianz commercial-insurance group route. In FinWiki it is a foreign-affiliated corporate-risk insurer peer to chubb-insurance-japan, aig-japan, and axa-japan.

Entity Boundary

This page covers アリアンツ火災海上保険株式会社 as the Japan non-life insurer. It should not absorb Allianz global asset management, life insurance, or non-Japan commercial insurance operations.
