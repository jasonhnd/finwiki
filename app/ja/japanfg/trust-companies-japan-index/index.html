Japan trust companies registry index

TL;DR

FSA’s trust-company list contains 38 trust companies as of 2026-03-31. This page is the registry route for the independent / group trust-company surface. It is separate from licensed trust banks such as mitsubishi-ufj-trust-bank, mizuho-trust-bank, smtb, smbc-trust-bank, nomura-trust-bank, and nochu-trust-bank.

Use this page to avoid creating 38 standalone company pages unless a trust company is strategically material.

Boundary

BoundaryTreatment
Trust banksKeep as standalone bank operating-company pages under INDEX.
Trust companiesKeep the full FSA list here; promote standalone pages only for material group, listed-company, fintech, estate, or tokenization relevance.
Group-linked trust companiesRoute to group pages where the group page already exists; do not imply the group page covers all trust-company details.
Long-tail trust companiesRegistry-only until there is a public-source reason to promote.

Standalone Promotion Rules

Promote a trust company from registry-only to standalone page only if one of these is true:

  • it is a listed-company group subsidiary with material strategic relevance;
  • it is tied to tokenized securities, estate planning, bankruptcy remoteness, escrow, or real-estate finance infrastructure;
  • it appears repeatedly in JapanFG / securities / real-estate finance analysis;
  • public filings, official disclosure, or regulator material provide enough content for a separate source-backed page.
