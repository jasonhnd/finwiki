Overview

American Home Medical and General Insurance is an FSA-licensed non-life insurer in Japan. In FinWiki it is a legacy AIG-linked direct / medical-insurance route, separate from the broader aig-japan page and from direct auto insurers such as sony-insurance.

Entity Boundary

This page covers アメリカンホーム医療・損害保険株式会社, not AIG Japan as a whole. The company-brand context matters because “American Home” and “American Home Direct” remain consumer-facing insurance brands in public materials.

Public Context

American Home matters because it preserves the old direct-insurance and medical / non-life brand layer in Japan’s foreign-affiliated insurance map. It is not a current megainsurer peer, but it is a distinct licensed entity that should not disappear inside AIG shorthand.
