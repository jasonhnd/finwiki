Overview

Capital Trust is an operating-type trust company whose public materials emphasize alternative-asset trust, repackaging, securitization / liquidation transactions, and project-finance trust / agency functions. In FinWiki it is the corporate-structured-finance trust route, distinct from personal-trust operators such as asahi-trust.
