## TL;DR

中小企業銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Industrial Bank of Korea / IBK の在日銀行支店。Korean SME finance bank の Japan branch として、韓国企業・日系企業・中小企業金融ネットワークの接点を担う。

## 2. Operating model

The FSA list confirms the Tokyo branch as a foreign-bank branch, and JBA's bank database provides the Japan banking-code and public contact route. IBK's global site is the parent-bank official route. The branch is useful for Korea-Japan SME, remittance, trade, and corporate banking context, rather than broad Japanese retail-bank coverage.

For detailed current product or disclosure analysis, prefer FSA/JBA plus IBK branch disclosure where available.
