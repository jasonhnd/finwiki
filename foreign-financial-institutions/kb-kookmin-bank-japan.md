# 國民銀行 東京支店


## Wiki route

This entry sits under [[foreign-financial-institutions/INDEX|foreign-financial-institutions INDEX]]. Read it against [[foreign-financial-institutions/woori-bank-japan|ウリィ銀行 東京支店]] for peer / contrast context and [[banking/INDEX|banking index]] for the broader system / regulatory boundary.

## TL;DR

國民銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Republic of Korea / KB Kookmin Bank の在日銀行支店。Korean retail / commercial banking giant の Japan branch として、韓国・日本間の remittance, deposit, and corporate banking corridor を担う。

## 2. Operating model

KB Kookmin Bank's overseas-office page lists the Tokyo branch address and contact details. JBA also lists the bank as a Japan banking database entry. The Japan role is a foreign-bank branch connection to Korean banking flows and corporate / customer support, not a Japan-incorporated bank.
