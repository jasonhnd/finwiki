韓国産業銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Korea Development Bank の在日銀行支店。Korean policy-bank / development-bank corridor として、project finance, shipping / aircraft finance, syndicated loans, trade finance, and cross-border corporate finance を扱う。

## 2. Operating model

KDB's Tokyo branch page states that the branch was opened in 1991 after a Tokyo office that began in 1969. It describes the branch as important to KDB's global business, providing project finance, ship and aircraft finance, syndicated loans, trade finance, and customized solutions to Korean companies in Japan, Japanese companies, and multinationals.

The page should therefore sit partly in foreign-bank branch analysis and partly in policy-finance adjacency.
