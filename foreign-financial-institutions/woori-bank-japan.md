## TL;DR

ウリィ銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Republic of Korea / Woori Bank の在日銀行支店。韓国系銀行の Japan corridor branch として、預金・送金・法人取引・韓国関連クロスボーダー金融の入口を担う。

## 2. Operating model

Woori Bank's Japan site identifies the Japan branch route and branch information. The branch is not a Japanese domestic bank subsidiary; it is a foreign-bank branch that connects Korean and Japan customers, remittance flows, deposits, and business banking activity through Woori's home-market network.

The FSA list is the primary license source, while JBA's bank database provides the Japan banking-code / contact context for public users.
