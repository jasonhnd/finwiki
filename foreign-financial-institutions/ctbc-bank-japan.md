## TL;DR

中國信託商業銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される Taiwan / CTBC Bank の在日銀行支店。Taiwan private banking group の Japan corporate branch として、台湾・日本間の企業取引、インターネットバンキング、口座・送金・融資関連手続を支える。

## 2. Operating model

CTBC's Japan branch forms and disclosure materials identify the Tokyo Branch route. JBA lists CTBC Bank in the foreign-bank associate-member surface. The wiki should treat CTBC Japan as a foreign-bank branch rather than a Japan-incorporated retail bank.

## 3. #26 deepening: Taiwan private-bank branch corridor

- CTBC Bank's Japan page identifies the Tokyo branch as a cross-border platform connecting CTBC's network with Japanese banks. That makes the branch useful for Taiwan-Japan corporate deposits, remittance, lending, trade finance, and customer onboarding rather than mass-market Japanese retail banking.
- The legal boundary is a foreign-bank branch under Japan's Banking Act, not a Japan-incorporated bank subsidiary. This matters because [[regional-banks/tokyo-star-bank|Tokyo Star Bank]] is CTBC-owned but is a domestic Japanese bank, while CTBC Tokyo Branch is the Taiwan bank's direct branch route.
- FinWiki should therefore keep three CTBC-related layers separate: CTBC Financial Holding / CTBC Bank as parent group, [[regional-banks/tokyo-star-bank|Tokyo Star Bank]] as domestic-bank subsidiary exposure, and this page as foreign-branch corridor exposure. Merging them would hide deposit-insurance, governance, and customer-contract differences.
- Peer comparison belongs inside the Taiwan branch cluster: [[foreign-financial-institutions/mega-icbc-japan|Mega ICBC Japan]], [[foreign-financial-institutions/bank-of-taiwan-japan|Bank of Taiwan Japan]], [[foreign-financial-institutions/esun-bank-japan|E.SUN Bank Japan]], and [[foreign-financial-institutions/taiwan-business-bank-japan|Taiwan Business Bank Japan]].
