## TL;DR

中国農業銀行 東京支店は、金融庁の外国銀行支店一覧に掲載される mainland China / Agricultural Bank of China の在日銀行支店。2013 年に外国銀行支店免許を取得し、corporate lending, trade finance, securities investment, yen settlement, and remittance services を担う。

## 2. Operating model

ABC Tokyo's branch profile states that it obtained the business license for foreign banks in April 2013 and opened in July 2013. It describes departments covering corporate banking, treasury, operations / IT, compliance, risk, finance, audit, and administration, and lists services such as corporate lending, portfolio investment, trade finance, yen settlement, and remittances.

The customer base includes multinationals, Japanese large and medium-sized enterprises, Chinese-funded enterprises in Japan, Japanese-funded enterprises in China, and international financial peers.
