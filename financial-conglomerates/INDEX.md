# financial-conglomerates — 総合商社 親持株会社 (sogo-shosha parent holdings) index

## Scope

`financial-conglomerates/` holds the **sōgō shōsha parent holding companies themselves** — the listed general trading houses that are *not* financial-services firms per se, but are kept here as **entity anchors** for their financial-services subsidiaries and strategic shareholdings. Each parent is the cap-table owner of an inward-facing group-treasury arm (covered under [[trading-company-finance/INDEX|trading-company-finance]]) and, in most cases, a strategic stake in a listed outward-facing leasing / asset-finance platform. These pages give the JapanFG graph a place to hang the商社 ↔ メガバンク ↔ リース ↔ 保険 cross-shareholding lens.

The domain currently holds **7 entries** (excluding this INDEX): the five majors (5 大商社), the Toyota-group captive trading house, and the leading mid-tier (7 大商社) house. All report under IFRS, list on 東証 PRIME, and carry heavy equity-method-investment weightings.

Distinctions to hold:

- **This domain vs [[trading-company-finance/INDEX|trading-company-finance]]** — here are the *parent trading houses* (entity anchors); there are the *finance subsidiaries* they own. Each entry below cross-links to its finance arm.
- **"financial-conglomerates" naming** — these are non-financial parents retained as anchors for their finance arms; the financial-services content lives in the finance arm and leasing pages, not in the parent's core trading business.
- **Restructuring / strategy cases** that exercise these groups (spinoffs, holding-company conversions, going-private such as ローソン / 東芝) → [[corporate-strategy/INDEX|corporate-strategy]].
- **Umbrella** — the operating-company layer lives under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]].
