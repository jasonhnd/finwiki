# asset-managers — Japanese investment managers / 投資運用業 index

## Scope

`asset-managers/` is the top-level domain for **Japanese investment-management operating companies** — the entity layer of the FIEA 投資運用業 (Investment Management Business) world: the *manufacturers* of public investment trusts (公募投信), ETFs, and discretionary / institutional mandates. It covers the megabank- and securities-affiliated giants (アセットマネジメントOne, 野村アセットマネジメント, 大和アセットマネジメント, 三菱UFJアセットマネジメント, 三井住友DSアセットマネジメント), the trust-bank-affiliated managers (三井住友トラスト・アセットマネジメント), insurer-affiliated managers (ニッセイアセットマネジメント, 東京海上アセットマネジメント, T&Dアセットマネジメント), online / low-cost and independent houses (SBIアセットマネジメント, セゾン投信), trading-house-linked managers (三菱商事系アセットマネジメント), and foreign-affiliated managers (Principal Japan). All of these were split out of the former [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]] umbrella.

These are **investment-management firms** (registered legal entities), not the thematic mechanics of funds or markets. Per-entity pages carry FSA registration, FIEA scope (投資運用業 / 投資助言・代理業 / 第二種), parent / group boundary, and product / mandate role.
