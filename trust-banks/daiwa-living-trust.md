## Overview

Daiwa Living Trust's public site describes the company as supporting asset and family succession through real-estate management trust. The site explains trust through the transfer of real estate and other property to a trustee for the benefit of a designated beneficiary.

The page matters because it is a housing / rental-property trust route rather than a capital-markets or private-bank trust route. It is useful for landlord, rental housing, and family succession analysis.
