# FPG信託 (FPG Trust)

## Wiki route

FPG Trust is a standalone trust-company route under [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]] and is promoted from [[trust-banks/trust-companies-japan-index|the Japan trust companies registry index]]. Read it with [[finance/japan-acquisition-finance|Japan acquisition finance]], [[finance/cross-border-m-a-japan|cross-border M&A Japan]], and [[banking/japan-trust-bank-custody-map|Japan trust-bank custody map]].

Use this page when a question involves FPG group trust structures, real-estate / movable-asset trust schemes, asset administration, or independent trust-company positioning.

## Overview

FPG Trust is an operating trust company under the FPG group. Its public company profile states that it holds an operating trust-company license and is a 100% subsidiary of FPG, a Tokyo Stock Exchange Prime listed company.

The page matters because FPG Trust is not only a registry row. Public company material emphasizes trust structures for movable assets, real estate, securities, money claims, and other entrusted assets, making it a useful route for structured asset finance and high-net-worth / SME-adjacent product analysis.

## Public Context

FPG Trust is useful when a transaction needs a trustee / asset-administration layer but does not require a full trust-bank route. The analytical boundary is the trust wrapper: asset title, administration, segregation, and scheme support.

The page should not infer specific investor returns, tax effects, or product risk from the FPG group business model. Those claims need product-level disclosure or securities filings.
