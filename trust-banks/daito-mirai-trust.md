## Overview

Daito Mirai Trust is a Daito Kentaku group management-type trust company focused on real-estate management trust and asset-succession support. In FinWiki it belongs with housing / rental-property trust routes such as [[trust-banks/daiwa-living-trust]] and [[trust-banks/kainuma-trust-real-estate]].

This page covers 大東みらい信託株式会社 as the trust-company entity. It does not cover the full Daito Kentaku property-management business, construction business, or leasing platform.
