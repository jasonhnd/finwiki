## Overview

Pasona IP Trust presents itself as a trust service for intellectual property, supporting filing, rights maintenance, use, and administration of industrial property rights. Its public site describes the company as a specialist in intellectual-property trust services.

The page matters because it expands FinWiki's trust-company coverage beyond money, securities, real estate, and digital securities. IP trust is a distinct route for intangible-asset administration and potential IP-finance analysis.
