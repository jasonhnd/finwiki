# trust-banks — Japanese trust banks & trust companies index

## Overview

`trust-banks/` contains **47 entries** (excluding INDEX, 48 total with the registry page) covering Japanese **trust banks (信託銀行)** and **trust companies (信託会社)** — licensed full-service and asset-administration trust banks, plus the long tail of management-type and operating-type trust companies (real-estate / estate-planning, IP, escrow, tokenization, and group-captive trust vehicles). Each entry is a source-backed entity page that physically lives in `trust-banks/`. Use this domain when a banking, securities, pension, or real-estate-finance page needs the underlying trust-bank custody operator, asset-administration specialist, or independent trust company behind a mandate.

> **Scope / disambiguation.** This domain holds trust **entity** pages. For the thematic *concepts* — trust-bank / custody operating models, master-trust & custody-bank plumbing, pension-trust mandate flow, fee structures, and the banking-system boundary — see [[banking/INDEX|banking]]; for the securities-services and settlement side (JASDEC / JSCC interface, asset management) see [[securities/INDEX|securities]]. For the broader financial-group *entity* umbrella that these trust banks belong to, see [[JapanFG/INDEX|JapanFG]]. The pages here are the trust-entity complement to those homes.
