# Kiriu Real Estate Trust / きりう不動産信託

## Overview

Kiriu Real Estate Trust is a management-type trust company with a real-estate trust and regional revitalization profile. It is useful in FinWiki as the Osaka / Kansai real-estate trust route, distinct from housing-group trust companies such as [[trust-banks/sekisui-house-trust]].

This page covers きりう不動産信託株式会社. It does not cover all real-estate consulting, brokerage, or historical-townscape policy; those should remain in separate real-estate or public-policy notes if expanded.
