## How to use this file

1. Identify the closest matching task below.
2. Fetch the **start file** first. It anchors the topic.
3. Fetch the listed **bedrock follow-ups** to deepen.
4. The **matrix anchor** gives a cross-entity comparison view.
5. If the user's intent does not match any task, fall back to
   [INDEX.md](INDEX.md) → relevant domain INDEX → search by domain slug.

raw GitHub: `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jasonhnd/finwiki/main/<domain>/<slug>.md`.

regulator filings / corporate IR / press releases / public market data are
included. Safe to quote and cite.
